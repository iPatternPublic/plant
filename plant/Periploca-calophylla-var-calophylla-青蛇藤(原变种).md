青蛇藤(原变种) **Periploca calophylla var. calophylla**

> [Asclepiadaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Asclepiadaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Asclepiadaceae.pdf)>>[Periploca](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Periploca?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Periploca.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> The stem is used as medicine for lumbago, traumatic injury, and against snake bites. <P

## Description

Leaf blade elliptic-lanceolate to oblong-elliptic, apex acuminate. Fl. Apr-May, fr. Aug-Sep.

> **Habait** : 
> Thickets, mixed woods along valleys; 0-1000 m. Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Kashmir, Nepal, Sikkim, Vietnam]

### Synonym
*Streptocaulon calophyllum* Wight, Contr. Bot. India 65. 1834.
