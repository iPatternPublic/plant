假蛇尾草**(原变种) **Mnesithea laevis var. laevis**

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf)>>[Mnesithea](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Mnesithea?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Mnesithea.pdf)

## Description

Culms 20–70 cm tall. Sessile spikelet 3–4 mm; lower glume ± smooth. Fl. and fr. autumn.

> **Habait** : 
> Grassy hill slopes, field margins; 100–1000 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Hainan, Taiwan [India, Indonesia, Pakistan, Philippines, Sri Lanka, Thailand, Vietnam; Pacific Islands (Polynesia)].
