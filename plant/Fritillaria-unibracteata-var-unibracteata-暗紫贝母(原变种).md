暗紫贝母 (原变种) **Fritillaria unibracteata var. unibracteata**

> [Liliaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Liliaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Liliaceae.pdf) >> [Fritillaria](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Fritillaria?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Fritillaria.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> The bulbs are used medicinally.

## Description

Flowers narrowly campanulate. Nectaries ca. 2.5 × 2 mm, inconspicuous. 2* n* = 24*.

> **Habait** : 
>* Thickets, meadows; 3200--4500 m. S Gansu, SE Qinghai, NW Sichuan.

### Synonym
* Fritillaria lixianensis* Y. K. Yang & J. K. Wu; * F. sulcisquamosa* S. Y. Tang & S. C. Yueh; * F. unibracteata* var. * ganziensis* Y. K. Yang & J. K. Wu; * F. unibracteata* var. * maculata* S. Y. Tang & S. C. Yueh.
