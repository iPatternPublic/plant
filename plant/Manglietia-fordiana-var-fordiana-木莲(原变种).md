木莲**(原变种) **Manglietia fordiana var. fordiana**

> [Magnoliaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Magnoliaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Magnoliaceae.pdf)>>[Manglietia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Manglietia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Manglietia.pdf)
## Description

Leaf blade leathery, reticulate veins inconspicuous on both surfaces when dry, margin not wavy. Peduncle with reddish brown trichomes. Outer tepals oblong-elliptic. Carpel with a ca. 1 mm beak. Fl. May, fr. Oct. 2*n* = 38.


> Reference: 
> This taxon is used medicinally and for timber.This taxon is used medicinally and for timber.

### Statistics
Hills; ca. 1200 m. S Anhui, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hunan, Jiangxi, Yunnan, Zhejiang [Vietnam].

### Synonym
*Magnolia fordiana* (Oliver) Hu; *M. yuyuanensis* (Y. W. Law) V. S. Kumar; *Manglietia yuyuanensis* Y. W. Law; *Paramanglietia microcarpa* Hung T. Chang.
