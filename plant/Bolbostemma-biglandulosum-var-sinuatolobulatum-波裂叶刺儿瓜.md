## Description

Leaf blade broadly oblong, shorter than broad, 2.5-3.5 × 3-4.5 cm. Calyx- and corolla segments narrowly lanceolate, 6-8 × 0.8-1 mm.

### Statistics
● Thickets under forests; ca. 1000 m. NW Yunnan (Lijiang).
