## Description

Leaf blade glabrous on both surfaces.


> **Habait** : 
> Mountain regions; 200--400 m. Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning [Korea].

### Synonym
*Prunus mandshurica* (Maximowicz) Koehne var. *glabra* Nakai, J. Jap. Bot. 15: 679. 1939.
