## Description

Leaf segments ovate-oblong, acute or obtuse, the basal pair undivided, usually deep-dentate.

> **Habait** : 
> Forests on mountain slopes, grassy places on stream banks; 200–1200 m. Widely distributed from NE to S China; also in S Gansu and S Shaanxi [Japan, Korea, Mongolia, Russia (Siberia); North America].
