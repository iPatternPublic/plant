## Description

Leaf blade linear to linear-oblong, 2.5-3.5 × 0.3-0.4 cm, base rounded, apex obtuse. Legume rhombic to ovoid.

### Statistics
● Seashore sandy areas, along trails. Guangdong, Guangxi, Hu-nan, Taiwan.

### Synonym
*Crotalaria stenophylla* Vogel, Nov. Actorum Acad. Caes. Leop.-Carol. Nat. Cur. 19(Suppl. 1): 7. 1843, not Ecklon & Zeyher.

**36b. 狭叶猪屎豆（变种）**

var. stenophylla (Vogel) Chun-yu Yang in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 20 (4): 479. 1982. ——C. stenophylla Vogel in Nova Acta Nat. Cur. 65, Suppl. 1: 7. 1843.

本变种植物体比较矮小，叶片狭线形或线状长圆形，长 2.5-3.5厘米，宽3-4毫米，先端钝圆，具短尖头，基部圆；叶柄短；荚果卵球形或四角菱形，直径4-6毫米区别于原变种。

产台湾、湖南、广东、广西。生山谷路旁及海滩沙地。
