> [Dennstaedtiaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Dennstaedtiaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Dennstaedtiaceae.pdf)>>[Dennstaedtia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Dennstaedtia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Dennstaedtia.pdf)

## Description

Plants several meters tall, growth indeterminate, scandent. Rhizome long creeping. Fronds scattered; stipe coarse and stiff, brownish, with short spines; spines 1-1.5 mm, slightly curved at apex; lamina brown-green when dried, firmly herbaceous, with dense, short, slender hairs along midrib abaxially, glabrous adaxially; pinnae many, subopposite, 9-13 cm apart, shortly stalked (2-3 mm), oblong-lanceolate, 20-30 × 8-12 cm, tripinnatisect, densely shortly brown hairy at axil, acuminate at apex; pinnules 12-14 pairs, alternate, 1.5-3 cm apart, almost sessile, basiscopic pinnae shortest, oblong, ca. 2 cm × 7-8 mm, pinnatisect; other pinnae broadly lanceolate or subfalcate, 4-5 × 1.5-2.8 cm, bipinnatisect, acuminate at apex; ultimate pinnae 8-12 pairs, oblong, 8-15 × 5-6 mm, oblique at base, slightly truncate above, broadly cuneate below, sessile, disarticulate, pinnatisect 1/2-2/3 to midrib, rounded at apex; lobes elliptic, not serrate, rounded at apex. Veins pinnately furcate up to margin, 2-4 veinlets per lobe; rachis, pinna rachis, and stalk concolorous, grooved adaxially, with sparse short spines, small pinna rachis gray-green, densely shortly brown hairy abaxially. Sori orbicular, located near notch of lobule base; indusium brown-green, bowl-shaped, glabrous.

> **Habait** : 
>200-1000 m. Taiwan [Indonesia, Malaysia, New Guinea, Philippines; Pacific islands (Tahiti)].

### Synonym
*Dicksonia scandens* Blume, Enum. Pl. Javae 2: 240. 1828.

**7.刺柄碗蕨**

Dennstaedtia scandens (Bl.) Moore in Parker's Cat. et Ind. Fil. 307; Christ, Farnkr. d. Erde 312; Diels in Engl. u. Prantl, Nat. Pflanzenfam. Liv 218; C. Chr. Ind. Fil. 218; Cop. Polyp. Philip. 58: H. Ito, Fil. Jap. Illustr. t. 11——Dicksonia scandens Bl. Enum. Pl. Jav. 240。

植株高达数米，无限生长，攀援。根状茎长而横走。叶疏生，柄粗硬，淡褐色，有短刺，刺长1-1.5毫米，尖端略向下弯钩。羽片多数，一般长20-30厘米，宽8-12厘米，长圆披针形，先端渐尖，有短柄（长2-3毫米），下侧腋间密生短棕毛，近对生，相距9-13厘米，三回羽状深裂；一回小羽片12-14对，互生，几无柄，相距约1.5-3厘米，下方基部一片最短，长2厘米，竟7-8毫米，长圆形，羽状深裂；其余的长4-5厘米，宽1.5-2.8厘米，阔披针形或近镰刀形，先端渐尖，二回羽状深裂；末回羽片8-12对，长8-15毫米，宽5-6毫米，长圆形，圆头，基部偏斜，上方略为平截，下方阔楔形，无柄，分离，羽状深裂达中肋1/2-2/3处；裂片椭圆形，圆头，无踞齿。叶脉羽状分又，直达叶边，每裂片有2-4条小脉。叶坚草质，干后褐绿色，上面光滑，下面沿中脉两侧密生短细毛；叶轴、羽轴和叶柄同色，上面有沟，疏生短刺，小羽轴灰绿色，下面密被短棕毛。抱子囊群圆形，生于裂片基部上侧近缺刻处；囊群盖碗形，棕绿色，无毛。

产台湾（台北）、菲律宾及南洋群岛。
