毛稃以礼草**(原变种) **Kengyilia alatavica var. alatavica**

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf)>>[Kengyilia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Kengyilia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Kengyilia.pdf)

## Description

Culms smooth and glabrous or nodes puberulent. Leaf sheath smooth, glabrous; leaf blade abaxial surface smooth, glabrous, adaxial surface and margin scabrous. Lodicules shortly broadly triangular.


> **Habait** : 
> Meadows on mountain slopes; 1500–3000 m. Xinjiang, Xizang [Mongolia, Russia].

### Synonym
*Agropyron alatavicum* Drobow, Repert. Spec. Nov. Regni Veg. 21: 43. 1925; *Elymus alatavicus* (Drobow) ┴. Löve; *Ely-trigia alatavica* (Drobow) Nevski.
