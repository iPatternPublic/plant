## Description

Leaves and inflorescences scattered on branches rather than near apex of branchlets. Leaflet blades oblong-elliptic, 3.5-10 × 1.5-4 cm, abaxially pale green and finely appressed pilose, adaxially pubescent, secondary veins 6 or 7 on each side of midvein, base symmetric or only slightly asymmetric, apex acute.

### Statistics
Thickets on riverbanks; 200-1100 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Guang-xi, Guizhou, Hainan, Hunan, Jiangxi, Yunnan [India].

### Synonym
*Millettia pulchra* f. *laxior* Dunn, J. Linn. Soc., Bot. 41: 151. 1912.
