## Description

Corolla purple, 1-1.1 cm; tube 9-10 mm; lobes ca. 1.3 mm. Style 7-9 mm. Fl. and fr. Jul.

> **Habait** : 
>* Limestone cliffs; ca. 1200 m. Guizhou (Xiuwen Xian).

### Synonym
*Tengia potiflora *S. Z. He in S. Z. He & X. D. Cong, J. China Pharm. Univ. 23: 269. 1992.
