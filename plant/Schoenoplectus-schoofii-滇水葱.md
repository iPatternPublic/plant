## Description

Culms tufted, 30-60 cm tall, 2.5-3.5 mm thick, compressed terete to terete, smooth, clothed at base with 2 or 3 bladeless pale brown leaf sheaths. Leaf sheaths 2-16 cm, mouth obliquely truncate and with a mucro. Involucral bract 1, culmlike, 8-30 cm. Inflorescence a pseudolateral head, with 8-20 spikelets. Spikelets ovoid to oblong-ovoid, 3-10 × 2-3 mm, many flowered. Glumes brownish yellowish to brown, elliptic to subobovate, ca. 3 mm, membranous, obscurely many veined, margin ciliolate, apex rounded and with a green 1-3-veined costa excurrent into a mucro. Perianth bristles very short, glabrous. Stamens 3; anthers nearly narrowly ovate, short; connective apex evident beyond anthers. Style of medium length; stigmas 3. Nutlet black at maturity, broadly obovoid, ca. 1 mm, 3-sided, densely transversely wrinkled. Fl. and fr. Aug-Sep.

### Statistics
● Wet places along lake margins; ca. 2300 m. Jiangsu, Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Scirpus schoofii* Beetle, Amer. J. Bot. 29: 654. 1942.

Scirpus schoofii Beetle in Amer. Journ. Bot. XXIX 654.

具须根。稈丛生，高30-60厘米，直径2.5-3.5毫米，扁圆形或圆柱状，平滑，基部具2-3个叶鞘，鞘长2-16厘米，上端开口处呈斜截形，边缘干膜质，淡棕色，顶端钝圆，具短尖，无叶片。苞片1枚，为稈的延长，长8-30厘米；小穗8-20余个簇生成头状，假侧生，卵形或长圆状卵形，长3-10毫米，宽2-3毫米，具多数花；鳞片椭圆形或近于倒卵形，顶端钝或圆，膜质，长约3毫米，背面绿色，具1-3条明显的脉，中脉延伸出顶端呈短尖，两侧褐黄色或褐色并具多数不明显的脉，边缘具缘毛；下位刚毛极短，无倒刺或顺刺；雄蕊3，花药短，近于狭卵形，药隔稍突出；花柱中等长，柱头3。小坚果宽倒卵形，三稜形，长约1毫米，成熟时呈黑色，具密的横皱纹。花果期8-9月。

产于我国云南省（东川市）；生长在湖边等湿地上，海拔2300米左右。
