## Description

Leaf blade elliptic, 4-10 X 1.5-4 cm. Cymes 1- or 2-flowered. Pistil pubescent. Fl. Jul.


> **Habait** : 
> Rocks or on trees in forests; ca. 2800 m. Yunnan (Zhenkang Xian).
