大花川梨 **Pyrus pashia var. grandiflora** Cardot

> [Rosaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Rosaceae ?t=foc) - [PDF](http://iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Rosaceae.pdf)>>[Pyrus](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pyrus ?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Pyrus.pdf)

## Description

Flowers large, ca. 3 cm in diam., leaf blade initially, petiole, pedicel, and sepals adaxially rusty tomentose.

> **Habait** : 
> Guizhou, Yunnan.
