## Description

Legume broadly elliptic or nearly orbicular, 8-10 × 6-7 mm.

### Statistics
Grassy riverbanks, thickets or sparse forests on mountain slopes; 100-800 m. Hainan [Cambodia, Laos, Thailand, Vietnam].

### Synonym
*Lespedeza lanceolata* Dunn, J. Linn. Soc., Bot. 35: 488. 1903; *Desmodium dunnii* Merrill.
