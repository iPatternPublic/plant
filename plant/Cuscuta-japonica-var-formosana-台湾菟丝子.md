## Description

Corolla 4-7 mm. Stigma lobes quadrangular.

> **Habait** : 
>* Low elevations. Taiwan.

### Synonym
*Cuscuta formosana* Hayata, Icon. Pl. Formosan. 2: 124. 1912.
