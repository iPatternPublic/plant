## Description

Branchlets, petioles, and inflorescence rachises yellow-brown pubescent. Inflorescences paniculate-spicate, ca. 23 cm.


> **Habait** : 
>●  Dense forests along canals. S Yunnan (Malipo, Mengla).

Sageretia hamosa (Wall.) Brongn. var. trichoclada C. Y. Wu ex Y. L. Chen in Bull. Bot. Lab. North-East. Forest. Inst. 5: 75. 1979.

小枝、叶柄和花序轴均被黄褐色密柔毛；花序为圆锥状穗状花序，长达23厘米，与原变种不同。核果当年成熟。

产云南南部（勐腊）。生于沟边密林中。
