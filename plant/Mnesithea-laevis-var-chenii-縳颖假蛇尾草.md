縳颖假蛇尾草** **Mnesithea laevis var. chenii** (Hsu) de Koning & Sosef

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf)>>[Mnesithea](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Mnesithea?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Mnesithea.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> This is a local variant with pitted sessile spikelets. Traces of pits also occur on some specimens of the species from Hong Kong.

## Description

Culms 15–30 cm tall. Sessile spikelet 3.5–5 mm; lower glume sunken and pitted between ribs. Fl. and fr. autumn.

> **Habait** : 
>* By the sea. S Taiwan.

### Synonym
