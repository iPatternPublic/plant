## Description

Stems 5-15 cm tall, often branched; branches procumbent or ascending, densely hairy. Petiole 2-10 mm; leaves small, hairy, base mostly shallowly cordate to truncate. Fl. Jun-Jul.


> **Habait** : 
> Alpine meadows, forests; 2800-4400 m. W to SW Sichuan, S Xizang, NW Yunnan [Bhutan, NW India, Sikkim].

### Synonym
*Veronica capitata* Royle ex Bentham var. *sikkimensis* J. D. Hooker, Fl. Brit. India 4: 295. 1884; *V*. *umbelliformis*Pennell.

**四川婆婆纳多毛亚种**

subsp. sikkimensis(Hook. f.)Hong, comb. nov. Veronica capitata var. sikkimensis Hook. f. Fl. Brit. Ind. 4: 296. 1884. ——V. capitata auct. non Foyle: Hook. f. 1. c. 4: 295. 1884. p. p. quoad specim. Bhutan.; Franch. Bull. Soc. Bot. France, 47: 19. 1900. ——V. umbelliformis Pennell, Monogr. Acad. Nat. sci. Philad. 5: 87. 1943.

植株较矮，高5-15厘米，被毛较密。茎常多分枝，分枝倾卧或上升。叶片较小，基部常浅心形或平截形，少宽楔形，叶柄较短，叶片两面被毛。

分布于云南（西北部）、四川（西部和西南部）、西藏（南部）。不丹，锡金至印度西北部也有。生海拔2800-4400米的高山草地及林下。
