狭叶沙参(原亚种) **Adenophora gmelinii subsp. gmelinii**

> [Campanulaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Campanulaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Campanulaceae.pdf) >> [Adenophora](Adenophora-沙参属.md) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Adenophora.pdf)

## Description

Plants mostly less than 1 m tall, glabrous, sometimes sparsely puberulent at base of stems. Leaves usually linear and entire, less frequently lanceolate or lanceolate-ovate and serrate. Calyx lobes 4.5-6 × 1-1.6 mm. Fl. Jul-Sep, fr. Aug-Oct. 2*n* = 34*, 68.

### Statistics
Meadows; below 1800 m. Hebei (Kangbao, Weichang, Zhangbei), Heilongjiang (Anda), W Jilin (W of Fuyu, Qian’an), Liaoning (Zhangwu), E, N, and SE Nei Mongol [Korea, E Mongolia, Russia (SE Siberia)].
