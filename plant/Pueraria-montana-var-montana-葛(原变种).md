葛(原变种) **Pueraria montana var. montana**

> [Fabaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Fabaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Fabaceae.pdf)>>[Pueraria](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pueraria?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Pueraria.pdf)

## Description

Bracts shorter than bracteoles. Calyx 7-8 mm. Standard ca. 8 mm in diam. Legumes 4-9 cm × 6-8 mm. Fl. Jul-Sep, fr. Oct-Dec.

### Statistics
Open places, thickets, forests. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Gui-zhou, Hainan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, Zhe-jiang [Japan, Laos, Myanmar, Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam].

### Synonym
*Dolichos montanus* Loureiro, Fl. Cochinch. 2: 440. 1790; *Glycine javanica* Linnaeus, not *Pueraria javanica* (Ben-tham) Bentham; *P. lobata* (Willdenow) Ohwi var. *mon-tana* (Loureiro) Maesen; *P. thunbergiana* (Siebert & Zuccarini) Bentham var. *formosana* Hosokawa; *P. tonkinensis* Gagnepain.
