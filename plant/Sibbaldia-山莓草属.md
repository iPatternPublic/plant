山莓草属 **Sibbaldia** Linnaeus

> [Rosaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Rosaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Rosaceae.pdf) >> [Sibbaldia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Sibbaldia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Sibbaldia.pdf)
## Description

Herbs perennial, often woody based. Flowering stems prostrate to erect. Leaves pinnate or palmately 3–5-foliolate; leaflets serrate at apex or margin, rarely entire. Inflorescence a cyme or solitary flower. Flowers usually bisexual, rarely unisexual. Hypanthium saucer-shaped or cupular. Sepals (4 or)5, alternate, persistent; epicalyx segments (4 or)5. Petals (4 or)5, yellow, purple-red, or white. Disk usually markedly broad, rarely not so. Stamens (4 or)5(–10); anthers 2-loculed. Carpels 4–20, free; ovule usually ascending; style sub-basal, lateral, or subterminal. Achenes few, inserted on elevated, dry receptacle. x = 7.



### Statistics
About 20 species: arctic and alpine regions of N hemisphere; 13 species (four endemic) in China.

### Synonym
*Sibbaldianthe* Juzepczuk.

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第37卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/37) >> 335页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/37/335y.pdf)
**37.山莓草属*-Sibbaldia L.**
L. Sp. Pl. 284. 1753; Benth. ＆ Hook. f. Gen. Pl. 1: 621. 1856; Hutch. Gen. Flow. Pl. 1: 199. 1964.
多年生草本。常具木质化根茎。叶为羽状或掌状复叶，小叶边缘或顶端有齿，稀全缘。花通常两性，成聚伞花序或单生；萼筒碟形或半球形，萼片5，稀4；副萼片5，稀4，与萼片互生；花瓣黄色，紫色或白色；花盘通常明显宽阔，稀不明显，雄蕊（4）-5-（10），花药2室，花丝或长或短；雌蕊4-20，彼此分离；花柱侧生近基生或顶生；每心反有1胚珠，通常上升。瘦果少数，着生于干燥凸起的花托上，萼片宿存，种子1颗，染色体基数x=7。
本属的模式种：山莓草Sibbaldia procumbens L.
全世界近20余种，分布于北半球北极及高山地区。我国约15种，分布于华北、西北及西南高山地带。
