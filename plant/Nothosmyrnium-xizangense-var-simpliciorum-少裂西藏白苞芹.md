## Description

Basal leaves 1–2-pinnate. Petals broadly ovate, glabrous, apex acute. Vittae 2 in each furrow, 4 on commissure.


> **Habait** : 
> Grassland at forest margins, riparian weed communities; 3100–3400 m. SE Xizang (Mainling).
