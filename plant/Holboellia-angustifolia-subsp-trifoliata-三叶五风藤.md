## Description

Leaflets 3(--5), lanceolate-oblong to oblanceolate, leathery, base cuneate, margin entire, apex acute. Fl. Apr--May.

> **Habait** : 
>* Forests, hillsides, by streams; 1000--1900 m. W Hubei, E Sichuan.
