海南羽叶金合欢 **Acacia pennata subsp. hainanensis** (Hayata) I. C. Niel-sen Adansonia, n.s. 19: 352. 1980.

## Description

Glands narrowly terete or slightly obconic, 0.3-0.8 mm in diam. Leaflets sharply acute, bent forward. Inflorescences red-dish glandular hairy. Flowers subsessile. Corolla 2-2.5 mm.

### Statistics
Thin forests, thickets. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Hainan, Yun-nan [India, Myanmar, Vietnam].

### Synonym
*Acacia hainanensis* Hayata, Icon. Pl. Formosan. 3: 86. 1913.
