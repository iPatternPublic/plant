## Description

Leaves 2-3.7 cm ×  5-7 mm. Pollen-cone racemes borne up to 10 together, 1.5-5.5 cm. Seed-bearing structures with peduncle circa 2/3 ×  length of subtending leaf

> **Habait** : 
>* Limestone mountains; ca. 900 m. S Guizhou.
