> [Thymelaeaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Thymelaeaceae ?t=foc) - [PDF](http://iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Thymelaeaceae.pdf)>>[Wikstroemia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Wikstroemia ?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Wikstroemia.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> Plants with a more sericeous indumentum have been separated as *Wikstroemia pilosa* var. *kulingensis*.

## Description

Shrubs to 1 m tall. Primary branches turning yellow or brownish in second year, terete, slender, irregularly rugulose, pilose or sericeous, glabrescent. Leaves opposite or subopposite, rarely alternate; leaf blade dark green adaxially, paler abaxially, ovate, elliptic-ovate, or elliptic, 1.5-4 × 0.7-2 cm, membranous, abaxially sericeous, adaxially pilose, base broadly cuneate, rounded, or truncate, margin revolute, apex acute or shortly acuminate; lateral veins 3-5 pairs, raised. Inflorescences terminal and axillary, capitate becoming spicate, ca. as long as or longer than leaves, puberulous; peduncle 0.5-1 cm. Pedicel short. Calyx yellow; tube cylindric, 8-10 mm, 10-ribbed, exterior densely pilose; lobes 5, ovate, 1-1.2 × ca. 0.4 mm, apex rounded. Stamens 10, lower whorl inserted above middle of calyx tube, upper whorl near throat, subsessile; anthers oblong, ca. 1 mm; upper ones almost exserted from calyx tube. Disk scale 1, linear, 1-2 mm. Ovary clavate, ca. 2.5 mm, pilose; style ca. 0.4 mm; stigma capitate. Drupe reddish, fusiform, ca. 8 mm, enclosed in persistent calyx. Fl. autumn, fr. winter.

> **Habait** : 
>● Open bushy slopes, rocks, roadsides. Anhui, Guangdong, Hunan, Jiangxi, Zhejiang.

### Synonym
*Daphne kulingensis* (Domke) Halda; *Wikstroemia kulin**gensis* Domke; *W. pilosa* var. *kulingensis* S. C. Huang; *W.* *sericea* Domke, not Christopherson.

**44.多毛荛花**

Wikstroemia pilosa Cheng in Contr. Biol. Lab. Sci. Soc. China 8: 140. f. 4. 1932; H. L. Li in Journ. Arn. Arb. 25: 423. 1944;蔡少兰, 中山大学学报5 (2): 96. 1956. ——Diplomorpha dolichantha (Diels) Hamaya var. pilosa (Cheng) Hamaya in Acta Hort. Gotoburg. 26: 94. 1963, syn. nov.

**44a.多毛荛花（原变种）图版53：4-6**

var. pilosa

灌木，高达1米；当年生枝纤细，圆柱形，被长柔毛，越年生枝黄色，变为无毛。叶膜质，对生、近对生或互生，卵形、椭圆状卵形或椭圆形，长1.5-3.8厘米，宽0.7-1.8厘米，先端尖，基部宽楔形、圆形或截形，边缘稍反卷，上面暗绿色，下面粉绿色，两面被长柔毛，侧脉每边3-5，凸出，边缘微反卷。总状花序顶生或腋生，密被疏柔毛，长等于叶或稍露出于叶外，具短花序梗；花黄色，具短梗；花萼筒纺锤形，具10脉，外面密被长柔毛，内面无毛，约长10毫米，裂片5，长圆形，端圆，长1-1.2毫米；雄蕊10，2列，上列近喉部着生，下列固着于花萼筒中部以上，花药长圆形，约长1毫米；子房纺锤形，被长柔毛，长约6毫米，柱头头状；花盘鳞片1枚，线形，长约1毫米。果红色。花期秋季，果期冬季。

产浙江、安徽、江西、湖南。凡山坡、路旁、灌丛中均有生长。模式标本采自浙江诸暨。

**44b.绢毛荛花**

var. kulingensis (Domke) S. C. Huang Stat. nov. ——W.ikstroemia kulingensisDomke in Notizbl. Bot. Gart. Berlin 13: 388. 1936. ——W. sericea Domke in Notizbl. Bot. Gart. Berlin 11: 356. 1932, non Christoph. ; Chun in Sunyatsenia 4: 184. 1940. ——W. canescens auct. non Meisn.: Hemsl. in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. 26: 397. 1894.

与原变种的不同在于植株各部均被稠密的灰白色绢状毛。

产江西、广东。模式标本采自江西牯岭。

Hemsley根据F. B. Forbes在江西牯岭所采之标本，定名为W. canescens Meisn.我们根据在牯岭所采到的多数标本及F. B. Forbes标本的图片观察分析的结果，认为牯岭的标本与我们在模式产地西藏吉隆所采的标本相比较，前者的花萼裂片为5数，毛被厚得多而显著不同。
