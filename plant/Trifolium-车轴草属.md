车轴草属 **Trifolium** Linnaeus Sp. Pl. 2: 764. 1753.

> [Fabaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Fabaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Fabaceae.pdf) >> [Trifolium](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Trifolium.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> About 250 species: temperate and subtropical Africa, America, Asia, and Europe; 13 species (nine introduced) in China.

## Description

Annual or perennial herbs, glabrous to pubescent. Stems erect to ascending or prostrate. Leaves pinnately 3-foliolate or palmately 3(or 5-9)-foliolate; stipules entire or toothed, partly adnate to petiole; leaflets toothed or entire. Inflorescences axillary or terminal. Flowers sessile or shortly pedicellate, in heads, short racemes, umbels, or rarely solitary; bracts small or absent, often connate into a dentate and membranous involucre. Calyx 5-10[-30]-veined, regular or bilabiate, after flowering sometimes strongly inflated; teeth equal or unequal. Corolla persistent, white, pink, purple, or yellow, sometimes inflated in fruit. Filaments some or all dilated at apex; anthers uniform. Ovary with 1-12 ovules. Legume indehiscent, enclosed by marcescent calyx and petals. Seeds 1 or 2(-9), ovoid to spherical or reniform.

### Synonym
*Ursia* Vassilczenko.

## Keylist

* 1 Stipules almost completely adnate to petiole——(2)
* ~ Stipules adnate to petiole for 1/2 its length or less——(4)

* 2 (1)Stem erect; leaflets (3-)5(-9); flowers 20-35, inflorescence capitate.——1 [野火球](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium lupinaster?t=foc)
* ~ Stem prostrate or decumbent; leaflets 3; flowers 1-5, inflorescence umbellate——(3)

* 3 (2) Stem prostrate, thick and short; leaflets with 6 pairs of lateral veins.——2 [大花车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium eximium?t=foc)
* ~ Stem decumbent, thin and long; leaflets with 18-24 pairs of lateral veins.——3 [延边车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium gordeievii?t=foc)

* 4 (1) Petioles slender, shorter than or equal to leaflets; flowers less than 8 mm; ovary stipitate——(5)
* ~ Petioles, especially in lower leaves, much longer than leaflets; flowers more than 6 mm; ovary sessile——(7)

* 5 (4) Leaflets all ± sessile; stipules lanceolate, 1-1.5 cm, adnate to petiole for ca. 1/2 their length.——6 [黄车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium aureum?t=foc)
* ~ At least terminal leaflet stalked; stipules ovate to oblong, 4-8 mm, adnate to petiole for less than 1/2 their length——(6)

* 6 (5) Flowers 5-15; pedicels larger than or equal to calyx tube; corolla 3-4 mm, standard boat-shaped, acute, smooth; leaflets 5-10 mm.——8 [钝叶车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium dubium?t=foc)
* ~ Flowers 20-40; pedicels smaller than calyx tube; corolla (3-)5-6 mm, standard broadly flattened, emarginate, striate; leaflets 8-16 mm.——7 [草原车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium campestre?t=foc)

* 7 (4) Calyx inflated in fruit, veins reticulate, 2 upper teeth longer than 3 lower teeth.——9 [草莓车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium fragiferum?t=foc)
* ~ Calyx not inflated in fruit, veins not reticulate, teeth subequal or lower longest——(8)

* 8 (7) Flowers pedicellate, bracts present; calyx throat glabrous inside——(9)
* ~ Flowers ± sessile, bracts absent; calyx throat with a thickened hairy ring inside——(10)

* 9 (8) Stem prostrate, stoloniferous, rooting at nodes; stipules thin and membranous; calyx teeth shorter than tube.——4 [白车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium repens?t=foc)
* ~ Stem erect-ascending; stipules herbaceous; calyx teeth longer than or equal to tube.——5 [杂种车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium hybridum?t=foc)

* 10 (8) Floral heads sessile or very shortly pedunculate, ovoid to globose, involucrate by stipules of upper leaves.——12 [红车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium pratense?t=foc)
* ~ Floral heads pedunculate, subglobose to elongate, involucre of small bracts or none——(11)

* 11 (10) Plants perennial, rhizomatous; stems flexuous; leaf veins arcuate, running into small marginal teeth.——11 [中间车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium medium?t=foc)
* ~ Plants annual, lacking rhizomes; stems upright, straight; leaf veins straight——(12)

* 12 (11) Floral heads conic to cylindric, 3-6 cm, involucre absent; calyx teeth ± equal; corolla red or pink, rarely white; stipules broad, apex blunt, dark green to purple.——10 [绛车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium incarnatum?t=foc)
* ~ Floral heads ovoid or globose, 1.2-2.5 cm, involucre of small fused bracts; calyx teeth unequal, lower much longer than others; corolla creamy white; stipules narrow, apex attenuate, pale green.——13 [埃及车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium alexandrinum?t=foc)

**车轴草属 Trifolium**

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第42(2)卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/42(2)) >> 329页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/42(2)/329y.pdf)

**118. 车轴草属* Trifolium Linn.**

Linn. Sp. Pl. 764. 1753; Hossain Not. Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 23: 387.1961; Hutch. Gen. Flow. Pl. 1: 457. 1964.——Ursia Vass. in Nov. Syst.Pl. Vase. 16: 133. 1979.

一年生或多年生草本。有时具横出的根茎。茎直立、匍匐或上升。掌状复叶，小叶通常3枚，偶为5-9枚；托叶显著，通常全缘，部分合生于叶柄上；小叶具锯齿。花具梗或近无梗，集合成头状或短总状花序，偶为单生，花序腋生或假顶生，基部常具总苞或无；萼筒形或钟形，或花后增大，肿胀或膨大，萼喉开张，或具二唇状胼低体而闭合，或具一圈环毛，萼齿等长或不等长，萼筒具脉纹5、6、10、20条，偶有30条；花冠红色、黄色、白色或紫色，也有具双色的，无毛，宿存，旗瓣离生或基部和翼瓣、龙骨瓣连合，后二者相互贴生；雄蕊10枚，两体，上方1枚离生，全部或5枚花丝的顶端膨大，花药同型；子房无柄或具柄，胚珠2-8粒。荚果不开裂，包藏于宿存花萼或花冠中，稀伸出；果瓣多为膜质，阔卵形、长圆形至线形；通常有种子1-2粒，稀4-8粒。种子形状各样，传布时连宿存花萼或整个头状花序为一单元。

本属约250种，分布欧亚大陆，非洲，南、北美洲的温带，以地中海区域为中心。我国包括常见于引种栽培的有13种，1变种。

属名模式：红车轴草 Trifolium pratense Linn.

## 检索表

* 1 叶柄短。大部分与托叶合生。——(2)
* ~叶柄通常长或明显具柄。——(4)

* 2 茎匍匐或平卧；花1-5朵组成伞形花序；小叶3枚。——(3)
* ~茎直立；花多数集成头状花序；小叶5-7。——[野火球](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium lupinaster?t=z)

* 3 茎粗短，平卧或上升；小叶具侧脉6对。——[大花车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium eximium?t=z)

* ~茎细长，匍匐；小叶侧脉20对左右。——[延边车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium gordejevi?t=z)

* 4 花长6毫米以上，花序、小叶均较大；子房无柄；植株较粗壮。——(5)
* ~花长6毫米以下，花序、小叶均较小，总花梗与叶柄均纤细；子房具柄。——(11)

* 5 萼在果期不膨大，萼齿近等长或下方1齿最长。——(6)
* ~萼的上唇在果期膨大成泡囊状，上方2萼齿比下方3齿略长。——[草莓车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium fragiferum?t=z)

* 6 花较大，通常在12毫米以上，无苞片，近无梗，萼喉内具1多毛的加厚环。——(7)
* ~花较小，不到12毫米，具苞片，有花梗，萼喉无毛。——(10)

* 7 花序具总花梗，在花期中继续伸长。——(8)
* ~花序无总花梗，包于顶生叶的托叶之内；卵球形，长1.5-2.5厘米，无总苞；萼被长柔毛；托叶离生部分渐尖。——[红车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium pratense?t=z)

* 8 花序卵形或球形，长1.2-2.5厘米；托叶离生部分钻形，长渐尖。——(9)
* ~花序长筒形，长3-5厘米，无总苞；萼密被长硬毛；托叶离生部分钝三角形。——[绛车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium incarnatum?t=z)

* 9 花序有总苞；被贴伏绢毛。——[埃及车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium alexandrinum?t=z)

* ~花序无总苞；萼无毛或被稀疏毛。——[中间车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium medium?t=z)

* 10 茎直立，不在节上生根；总花梗长约5厘米，萼齿比萼筒长或等长，脉纹5条。——[杂种车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium hybridum?t=z)

* ~茎平卧或匍匐，节上生根；总花梗长6-20厘米，萼齿比萼筒短，脉纹10条。——[白车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium repens?t=z)

* 11 茎直立或上升，被贴伏柔毛；花序紧密，具花20-40朵，花梗比萼筒短。——(12)
* ~茎平卧，近无毛；花序疏松，具花5-15朵，花梗比萼筒长或等长。——[钝叶车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium dubium?t=z)

* 12 顶生小叶柄较长，托叶明显比叶柄短；花梗长1-1.5毫米，萼长2毫米，无毛。——[草原车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium campestre?t=z)

* ~小叶柄均等长，托叶与叶柄近等长；花梗长不到1毫米，萼长2.5-3.5毫米，被稀疏毛。——[黄车轴草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trifolium strepens?t=z)
