## Description

Perennials. Rhizomes short, hardened. Culms 35-70 cm tall, compressed triquetrous, smooth, winged at angles, with several leaves at basal part. Leaves shorter to slightly longer than culm; leaf blade 0.8-1.2 cm wide, flat, smooth. Involucral bracts 3-5, leaflike, basal 2 or 3 longer than inflorescence, subspreading. Inflorescence a compound or sometimes simple anthela; rays 8-10, to 5.5 cm, each with 2-7(-16) raylets; raylets mostly to 1.5 cm. Glomerules of 5-18 spikelets densely arranged at apex of rays and raylets, 5-8 mm in diam. Spikelets ovoid to oblong-ovoid, 3-5.5 × 1.8-2.5 mm, slightly turgid, 6-10-flowered; rachilla wingless. Glumes brown to brownish on both surfaces, densely imbricate, broadly ovate, 2-2.5 mm, membranous, concave, 13-15-veined, keel abaxially green, apex excurved mucronate. Stamens 3; anthers broadly linear, ca. 0.8 mm. Style ca. 0.2 mm; stigmas 3, ca. 1 mm. Nutlet dark brown, sessile, ovoid, ca. 1.8 mm, 3-sided, densely punctate, apex subacute. Fl. and fr. Jun-Jul.

### Statistics
Shaded places under sparse forests or scrub, grasslands by water, field margins; 100-500 m. Guangdong, Hainan [Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam].

### Synonym
*Scirpus trialatus* Boeckeler, Flora 42: 445. 1859; *Cyperus turgidulus* C. B. Clarke; *Mariscus trialatus* (Boeckeler) Tang & F. T. Wang.

Mariscus trialatus (Bocklr.) Tang et Wang, comb. nov. ——Scirpus trialatusBocklr. in Flora XLII 445 et in Linnaea XXXVI 721——Cyperusbancanus Miq. Fl. Ind. Bat. Suppl. I 599；E. -G. Camus in Lecomte, Fl. Gen.Indo-Chine VII 54——Cyerus diffusus Vahl subsp. β. bancanus (Miq. ) Kukenth. in Engl. Pflanzenr. Heft 101, IV, 20 209——Cyperus turgidulus C. B. Clarke in Journ. Linn. Soc. Bot. XXI 130, in Hook. f. Fl. Brit.Ind. VI 604 et Ill. Cyper. t. 13, f. 4-5——Cyperus diffusus Vahlform. turgidulus Suringar, Het Gesl. Cyper. Mal. Archip. 100, t. 4, f. 17.

根状茎短，木质。稈高38-70厘米，扁锐三稜形，各稜上具翅，平滑，下部具较多叶。叶短于稈或稍长于稈，宽8-10毫米，平张，不粗糙。苞片3-5枚，叶状，下面2-3枚长于花序，向外展开；长侧枝聚繖花序复出，或间有简单，具8-10个第一次辐射枝；辐射枝最长达5.5厘米，每个第一次辐射枝上具2-7（-16）个第二次辐射枝；第二次辐射枝通常很短，最长达1.5厘米；小穗5-18个密集于辐射枝顶端呈球形头状花序，卵形或长圆状卵形，肿胀，长3-5.5毫米，宽1.8-2.2毫米，具6-10朵花；鳞片紧密地复瓦状排列，膜质，扁圆卵形，顶端具外弯短尖，很凹，长2-2.2毫米，淡灰褐色，背面龙骨状突起灰绿色，具多数脉；雄蕊3，花药短，线形；花柱极短，柱头3。小坚果卵形，三稜形，深棕色，长约1.8毫米，表面具密的微突细点。花果期6-7月。

产于广东海南岛：生长于疏林下阳处。也分布于越南、马来亚、菲律宾。
