小花丝颖针茅 **Stipa capillacea var. parviflora** N. X. Zhao & M. F. Li

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf) >> [Stipa](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Stipa?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Stipa.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> This variety is used for forage and fiber.

## Description

Glumes 1.5–2(–2.2) cm. Lemma (7–)8–9 mm. Anthers ca. 2 mm. Fl. Aug.


> **Habait** : 
>* Meadows on lake margins, moist places, field margins; 4200–4400 m. Xizang (LangkazΩ, Tingri).
