## Description

Stem dark green, 1--2 m. Tepals adaxially streaked with purple-red, abaxially white. 2* n* = 24*.
> **Habait** : 
> Forests; 1200--3600 m. Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi; Guizhou, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan [Myanmar].

### Synonym
* Lilium giganteum* Wallich var. * yunnanense* Leichtlin ex Elwes, Gard. Chron., ser. 3, 60: 49. 1916; * L. mirabile* Franchet.
