## Description

Leaflets large, oblong, 15-20 mm, lateral veins obvious, gray-white pilose. Legumes moniliform, long constricted be-tween seeds. Seeds 2 or 3, rarely 5.

### Statistics
● 700-800 m. Sichuan.

### Synonym
Basionym: *Sophora viciifolia* Hance var. *liangshanensis* C. Y. Ma, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 20: 470. 1982.
