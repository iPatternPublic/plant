紫丁香 (原亚种) **Syringa oblata subsp. oblata**

> [Oleaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Oleaceae ?t=foc) - [PDF](http://iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Oleaceae.pdf) >> [Syringa](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Syringa ?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Syringa.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> Widely cultivated in several other provinces.
> White-flowered plants, which are cultivated in provinces north of the Chang Jiang, have been recognized as cultivar alba.

## Description

Shrubs or small trees to 5 m. Leaf blade ovate-orbicular to reniform, 2.5-10(-14) &amp;#215; 2.5-8(-15) cm, base truncate to usually subcordate, apex abruptly acute to acuminate. Panicles congested or lax, 4-16(-20) &amp;#215; 3-7(-10) cm. Calyx ca. 3 mm. Corolla purple or sometimes white; tube 0.6-1.4 cm; lobes ovate-orbicular to obovate-orbicular, 4-6 mm. Anthers inserted 0-4 mm from the corolla mouth. Capsule 1-1.5(-2) cm. Fl. Apr-May, fr. Jun-Oct. 2*n* = 46*.

> **Habait** : 
>* Woods, thickets on slopes, valleys, along streams or roadsides; 300-2600 m. Gansu, Hebei, Henan, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi, NW Sichuan

### Synonym
*Syringa giraldii* Lemoine; *S*. *oblata* var. *alba* Rehder; *S*. *oblata* var. *giraldii* (Lemoine) Rehder; *S. oblata* var. *hupehensis* Pampanini; *S*. *vulgaris* Linnaeus var. *oblata* (Lindley) Franchet.
