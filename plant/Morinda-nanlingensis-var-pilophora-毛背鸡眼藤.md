## Description

Leaf blade obovate, elliptic-oblanceolate, or elliptic, 7-9(-12) × 2-3.5 cm, abaxially densely pilosulous. Flowering heads 5-11-flowered.

### Statistics
● Forests, shady thickets on mountains. Guangxi, Hunan.
