黑药以礼草**(原变种) **Kengyilia melanthera var. melanthera**

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf)>>[Kengyilia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Kengyilia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Kengyilia.pdf)

## Description

Culms 15–25 cm tall. Glumes glabrous or puberulent. Lemma apex with awn 2–4 mm. Fl. and fr. late summer.


> **Habait** : 
>* Mountain slopes, sandy grassy slopes, lake banks. Qinghai.

### Synonym
*Agropyron melantherum* Keng, Sunyatsenia 6: 62. 1941; *Elymus melantherus* (Keng) ┴. Löve; *Kengyilia thoroldiana* (Oliver) J. L. Yang et al. var. *melanthera* (Keng) L. B. Cai; *Roegneria melanthera* (Keng) Keng.
