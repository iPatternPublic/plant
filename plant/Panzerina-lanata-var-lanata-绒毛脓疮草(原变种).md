绒毛脓疮草 **Panzerina lanata var. lanata**

> [Lamiaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Lamiaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Lamiaceae.pdf)>>[Panzerina](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Panzerina?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Panzerina.pdf)

## Description

Stems sparsely minute tomentose. Calyx teeth narrowly triangular, apex long spinescent, anterior teeth to 7 mm. Corolla 3-3.5 cm.

> **Habait** : 
> Stony sandy desert steppes. Gansu, Nei Mongol [Mongolia, Russia].

### Synonym
*Ballota lanata* Linnaeus, Sp. Pl. 2: 582. 1753; *Leonurus lanata* (Linnaeus) Persoon; *Panzeria lanata* (Linnaeus) Bunge, *P. tomentosa* Moench.
