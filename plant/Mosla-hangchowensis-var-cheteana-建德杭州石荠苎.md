## Description

Verticillasters widely spaced; bracts not overlapping. Calyx teeth long subulate, subequal.

> **Habait** : 
>* Roadsides. Zhejiang.

### Synonym
*Orthodon hangchowensis* (Matsuda) C. Y. Wu var. *cheteana* Sun ex C. H. Hu, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 11: 46. 1966.
