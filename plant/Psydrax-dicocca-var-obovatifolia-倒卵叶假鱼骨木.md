## Description

Leaf blade obovate or ovate-elliptic. Inflorescences fasciculate, 4-6-flowered. Stigma entire or often bifid or emarginate. Fruit subglobose or ellipsoid. Fl. May-Jun.

### Statistics
● Broad-leaved forests. Hainan (Tunchang).

### Synonym
Basionym: *Canthium dicoccum* var. *obovatifolium* G. A. Fu, Bull. Bot. Res., Harbin 5(1): 183. 1985.

本变种与原变种（鱼骨木）的区别是：叶倒卵形或倒卵状椭圆形；花4-6朵成束，簇生于叶腋内；柱头常2裂或微缺；果近球形或椭圆形。花期5-6月。

产海南屯昌。生于杂木林内。
