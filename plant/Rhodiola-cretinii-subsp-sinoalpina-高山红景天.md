高山红景天 **Rhodiola cretinii subsp. sinoalpina** (Fröderström) H. Ohba

> [Crassulaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Crassulaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Crassulaceae.pdf) >> [Rhodiola](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Rhodiola?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Rhodiola.pdf)

## Description

Flowering stems 2-5 cm. Leaves elliptic-oblong to spatulate, base decurrent, margin entire, apex rounded. Flowers often 4-merous. Petals linear-oblanceolate to linear-spatulate.

> **Habait** : 
>* 4300-4400 m. NW Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Sedum roseum* (Linnaeus) Scopoli var. *sinoalpinum* Fröderström in Handel-Mazzetti, Symb. Sin. 7: 406. 1931; *Rhodiola sinoalpina* (Fröderström) S. H. Fu.
