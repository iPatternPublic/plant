## Description

Corolla white to pink, 5-7 mm; tube 3.8-4 mm; lobes 1.2-3 mm. Style 4-5 mm. Fl. Aug, fr. Jul-Aug.

> **Habait** : 
>* Shaded, stony cliffs; ca. 300 m. S Guizhou (Pingba Xian), Yunnan.
