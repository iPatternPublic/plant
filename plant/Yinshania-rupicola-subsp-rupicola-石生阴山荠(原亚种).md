## Description

Petals white. Fruit narrowly elliptic, 5-6 × 2-2.5 mm. Fl. May, fr. Jun.


> **Habait** : 
>* Forests, rock crevices in wet shady places; 1000-1200 m. Anhui.

### Synonym
*Cochlearia rupicola* D. C. Zhang & J. Z. Shao, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 24: 404. 1986; *Hilliella rupicola* (D. C. Zhang & J. Z. Shao) Y. H. Zhang.
