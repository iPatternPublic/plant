## Description

Leaves thickly papery; petioles stout. Phyllaries and florets 5, rarely 4.

### Statistics
● Valleys; ca. 1700 m. Shanxi (Huoxian).

### Synonym
*Cacalia ambigua* var. *wangiana* Y. Ling, Contr. Inst. Bot. Natl. Acad. Peiping 5: 14. 1937.

**18b.作宾两似蟹甲草**
var. wangiamm (Ling) Y. L. Chen, comb. nov.——Cacalia wangiana Ling in Contr. Inst. Bot. Nat. Acad. Peiping 5: 14. 1937.
与原变种的区别在于叶质地较厚，纸质；叶柄粗壮；总苞片和小花均为5，稀4。
产山西（霍县、霍峪山）。生于山谷，海拔1700米。模式标本采自山西霍县。
