## Description

Inflorescences solitary or paired, narrow, thyrsoid, and racemelike, up to as long as leaf, rarely longer. Male flowers: petals usually absent. Fl. summer.

### Statistics
● Forests. NW Guangxi, S Guizhou.

**7b. 黔桂轮环藤（广西植物）**

subsp. guangxiensis Lo in Guihaia 6: 57. 1986.

与原亚种的区别是雄花通常无花瓣。花期夏季。

产于广西西北部和贵州南部。生于林中。模式标本采自广西隆林。
