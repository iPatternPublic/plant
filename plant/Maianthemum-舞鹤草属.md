## Description

Herbs perennial, rhizomatous. Stems erect, simple. Leaves alternate, sessile or petiolate, usually elliptic to ovate, sometimes basal leaf solitary and early withered. Inflorescence a terminal raceme or panicle. Flowers bisexual or sometimes unisexual (when plants dioceous), small. Perianth segments 4 or 6, in 2 whorls, free or proximally ± connate, rarely forming a long tube. Stamens 4 or 6, inserted at base of perianth segments or adnate to perianth tube; filaments filiform; anthers dorsifixed. Ovary 2- or 3-loculed; ovules 1 or 2 per locule. Style columnar, relatively short; stigma entire or 2- or 3-lobed. Fruit a berry, globose or subglobose. Seeds 1--3, globose to ovoid.

### Statistics
About 35 species: mainly in E Asia and North America, also in N Asia, Central America, and N Europe; 19 species (nine endemic) in China.

### Synonym
* Oligobotrya* Baker; * Polygonastrum* Moench; * Smilacina* Desfontaines; * Tovaria* Necker ex Baker, not Ruiz & Pavón; * Vagnera* Adanson.

**48.舞鹤草属 Maianthemum Web.**

Web. in Wigg., Prim. Fl. Holsat. 14. 1780.

多年生草本，有匍匐根状茎。茎直立，不分枝。基生叶1枚，早凋萎；茎生叶互生，心状卵形，有柄至无柄。总状花序顶生，小苞片宿存；花小，两性；花被片4,排成2轮，分离，平展至下弯；雄蕊4,着生于花被片基部，花药小，卵形至矩圆形，背着，内向纵裂；子房2室，每室有2胚珠；花柱粗短，与子房近等长，柱头小。浆果球形，熟时红黑色，有种子1-3颗。种子球形至卵形。

约有4种，分布于北半球温带地区。我国有1种。
