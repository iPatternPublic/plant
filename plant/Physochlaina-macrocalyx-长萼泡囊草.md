> **Comment** : 
> This species is known to us only by Pascher's abbreviated original description.

## Description

Herbs 30-60 cm. Leaf blade base subhastate, subdeltate, sometimes attenuate into channeled petiole, lateral angles sometimes protracted. Calyx larger than 6 × 5 mm, tubular, not urceolate, not at all constricted distally. Corolla yellow, not at all violet, campanulate, gradually narrowed into a tube. Fruit not seen.

>* Xizang

**3.长萼泡囊草**

Physochlaina macrocalyx Pascher in Fedde, Repert. Sp. Nov. 7: 167. 1909.

根据帕协尔（A. Pascher）的原记载，本种接近西藏泡囊草，与它不同在于花萼在花时较长，长为花冠的一半或超过一半；叶大多数边缘具若干向前倾的三角形牙齿，最下端的一对更为显著，因而叶成近戟形。

这种植物是根据采自我国西藏的标本描写的，我们尚未看到标本，仅将诊断性特征摘出，以便今后在植物发掘中参考。
