## Description

Leaflets densely pubescent especially abaxially, densely and shortly velutinous-pubescent adaxially. Fruit small, 2.5-4 cm in diam., large wings 1.3-2 cm wide, very sparsely pubescent.

### Statistics
● Dense valley forests, thickets; ca. 1100 m. NW Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Illigera mollissima* W. W. Smith, Notes Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 10: 42. 1917.

**2b.多毛青藤（变种）葛根（云南元谋）**

Illigera cordata Dunn var. mollissima (W. W. Sm.) Kubitzki in Bot. Jahrb. 89: 176. 1969.——I. rnollisshna W. W. Sm. in Notes Bot. Gard. Edinb. 10: 42. 1917; Hand.-Mazz. Symb. 云南元谋民间药用根，作牲畜滋补药。
