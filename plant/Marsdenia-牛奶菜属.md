牛奶菜属**Marsdenia** R. Brown

> [Asclepiadaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Asclepiadaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Asclepiadaceae.pdf)>>[Marsdenia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Marsdenia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Marsdenia.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> About 100 species: Asia, America, tropical Africa; 25 species in China.

## Description

Lianas. Inflorescences terminal or extra-axillary, umbel-like, paniculate, or racemelike, pedunculate. Sepals overlapping, basal glands or scales often present. Corolla urceolate or campanulate, throat often hairy; lobes twisted to right. Corona lobes 5, erect, acuminate, fleshy, attached to gynostegium. Filaments connate into a tube; anthers erect, appendages inflexed; pollinia 2 per pollinarium, often oblong, erect. Stigma head flat, convex, or beaked. Follicles mostly thick, acuminate, smooth or longitudinally winged.

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第63卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/63) >> 442页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/63/442y.pdf)
**32．牛奶菜属——Marsdenia R. Br.**

R. Br. in Mem. Wern. Soc. 1: 28. 1810.

攀援灌木，稀直立灌木或半灌木。叶对生。聚伞花序伞形状，单生或分歧，顶生或腋生；花中等或小形；花萼深5裂，裂片双盖覆瓦状排列，基部内面有腺体，稀缺；花冠钟状、坛状或高脚碟状，裂片狭窄或宽阔，向右覆盖；与雄蕊合生的副花冠，裂片5枚，通常肉质，膨胀，向上端渐狭而成钻状；合蕊柱较短；花药顶端具有透明的膜片；花粉块每室1个，直立，通常长圆形，具花粉块柄；子房由2个心皮所组成，柱头长喙状或凸起，高出于花药之上。蓇葖披针形或匕首状；种子顶端具白色绢质的种毛。

约100种，分布于美洲、亚洲及热带非洲。我国产22种，5变种，分布于华东、华南及西南各省区。

本属模式种为：Marsdenia tinctoria R. Br.
