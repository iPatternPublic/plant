异燕麦属 **Helictotrichon** Besser ex Schultes & J. H. Schultes

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf) >> [Helictotrichon](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Helictotrichon.pdf)


> **Comment** : 
> Most species provide good forage.

## Description

Perennials, tussocky, often rhizomatous. Leaf blades linear to setaceous, flat, folded or rolled; ligule membranous. Inflorescence a panicle, open or often contracted, sometimes without secondary branching. Spikelets with 2 to several fertile florets and 1 or 2 reduced sterile florets above; rachilla pilose, disarticulating below each floret; glumes lanceolate, slightly unequal, usually shorter than spikelet and often shorter than lemmas, hyaline to membranous, lower glume 1–3-veined, upper glume 3–5-veined, keel scaberulous, apex acute; floret callus shortly bearded; lemmas lanceolate, firmly membranous to leathery, rounded or weakly keeled, 5–7(–9)-veined, glabrous, awned from middle of back or slightly above, apex minutely to deeply 2–4-toothed; awn geniculate with twisted column, sometimes weakly so or almost straight; palea slightly shorter than lemma and enclosed within lemma margins, keels scabrid-ciliolate to ciliate. Ovary densely hairy toward apex. Caryopsis with linear hilum; endosperm sometimes liquid.



### Statistics
About 100 species: Europe eastward to Japan, North America, also on tropical mountains; 14 species (seven endemic) in China.

### Synonym
*Avenastrum* Opiz; *Avenula* (Dumortier) Dumortier; *Trisetum* sect. *Avenula* Dumortier.


## Keylist

* 1 Palea keels smooth, glabrous, back deeply sulcate; hairs at apex of each rachilla internode 4–6 mm.——1  [毛轴异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon pubescens?t=foc)
* ~ Palea keels scabrid to ciliate, back flat at maturity; hairs at apex of each rachilla internode 1–3 mm——(2)

* 2 (1)Leaf blades flat or folded, keeled, margins and abaxial midrib prominently white-thickened, adaxial surface not ribbed; leaf sheath margins connate for part of length; floret callus bearded on sides only——(3)
* ~ Leaf blades flat or rolled, margins and midrib not thickened, adaxial surface closely ribbed with deep grooves between; leaf sheath margins free to base; floret callus evenly bearded——(4)

* 3 (2) Rhizomes present, plant forming loose turf; leaf sheaths closed for more than 1/2 of length; leaf blades 5–12 mm wide; spikelets 2–2.5 cm.——2  [大穗异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon dahuricum?t=foc)
* ~ Rhizomes absent, plant forming dense turf; leaf sheaths closed for less than 1/4 of length; leaf blades 2–5 mm wide; spikelets 1–1.8 cm.——3  [异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon hookeri?t=foc)

* 4 (2) Panicle open, sometimes nodding; branches ascending or spreading, up to 10 cm——(5)
* ~ Panicle laxly to densely contracted; branches erect or narrowly ascending, usually less than 4 cm——(10)

* 5 (4) Lemma apex subentire or minutely denticulate——(6)
* ~ Lemma apex 2-toothed——(7)

* 6 (5) Culms 20–45 cm; ligules to 0.5 mm; rachilla internodes hairy throughout length.——4  [冷杉异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon abietetorum?t=foc)
* ~ Culms up to 80 cm tall; ligules ca. 1 mm; rachilla internodes hairy only in upper part.——5  [光花异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon leianthum?t=foc)

* 7 (5) Panicle branches in whorls of up to 8, with branchlets almost to base; lower culm internodes and sheaths villous.——6  [滇异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon yunnanense?t=foc)
* ~ Panicle branches 2–4 per node (up to 6 in *H. altius*), bare in lower part; lower culm internodes glabrous (sheaths sometimes pilose)——(8)

* 8 (7) Nodes of culm pubescent.——7  [高异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon altius?t=foc)
* ~ Nodes of culm glabrous——(9)

* 9 (8) Leaf blades 10–25 cm, 3–5 mm wide; spikelets 1–1.4 cm.——8  [变绿异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon junghuhnii?t=foc)
* ~ Leaf blades 6–11 cm, ca. 2 mm wide; spikelets 0.8–1 cm.——9  [云南异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon delavayi?t=foc)

* 10 (4) Panicle 10–17 cm; lemmas densely scabrid; awn arising from upper 1/3 of lemma, recurved, scarcely twisted.——10  [粗糙异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon schmidii?t=foc)
* ~ Panicle 2–9(–14) cm; lemmas smooth, scaberulous or puberulous; awn arising near middle of lemma, geniculate with twisted column——(11)

* 11 (10) Spikelets reddish violet; anthers 1–1.6 mm.——11  [短药异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon potaninii?t=foc)
* ~ Spikelets brown or tinged violet; anthers 3–4.2 mm——(12)

* 12 (11) Panicle densely contracted; axis and branches densely pubescent; culm pubescent below panicle; spikelets golden brown.——12  [藏异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon tibeticum?t=foc)
* ~ Panicle laxly contracted; axis and branches scabrid or puberulent only in upper part; culm glabrous below panicle; spikelets pale yellowish brown tinged violet——(13)

* 13 (12) Plant densely tufted; sheaths of culm leaves usually pilose; leaf blades scabrid on adaxial surface; anthers ca. 3 mm.——13  [*H. tianschanicum* 天山异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon tianschanicum?t=foc)
* ~ Plant loosely tufted; sheaths of culm leaves usually glabrous; leaf blades densely puberulous on adaxial surface; anthers ca. 4 mm.——14  [*H. mongolicum* 蒙古异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon mongolicum?t=foc)

**异燕麦属 Helictotrichon**

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第9(3)卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/9(3)) >> 154页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/9(3)/154y.pdf)


**11.异燕麦属*——Helictotrichon Bess.**

Bess. in Schult. et Schult. f. Syst. Veg. Mant. 3 (Add. 1) : 526. 1827. ——Avenastrum (Koch) Opiz, Seznam Rostl. Kvet. Ceske 20. 1852; Jessen, Deutschl. Gras. 214. 1863. p. p. ——Avenochloa Holub, Acta Horti Bot. Prag. 1962: 82. 1962.

多年生草本。具开展或紧缩而有光泽的顶生圆锥花序；小穗含2至数小花，小穗轴节间具毛，脱节于颖之上及各小花之间；颖几相等，等长于或短于小花，具1-5脉，边缘宽膜质；外稃成熟时下部质较硬，上部薄膜质，常浅裂为2尖齿，背部为圆形，具数脉，常于中部附近着生扭转膝曲的芒，基盘钝而具毛；内稃2脊具纤毛；雄蕊3;子房有毛。

本属约有80余种，遍布于温带，主产于欧亚大陆及热带非洲与南非洲，北美洲有少数几种分布。我国有14种、2变种。

模式种：Helictotrichon sempervirens (Vill. ) Pilger (Avena sempervirens Vill.) 产欧洲。

本属多数种类可作饲料。

## 检索表

* 1 秆常单生或少数丛生，常具明显的地下茎；叶片扁平或稍内卷；秆生叶舌膜质，披针形，长3-8毫米（高异燕麦H. altius者平截，长1-2毫米）。——(2)
* ~秆丛生或散生；地下茎极短或无；叶片内卷或扁平；秆生叶舌短，平截或齿裂，长0.5-2毫米（短药异燕麦H. potaninii者稍长，为1.5-3.5毫米）。——(5)

* 2 花序较开展，每节具4-6分枝；第一颖具1-3脉，第二颖具3脉；小穗轴节间具长2.5-6毫米的柔毛；秆节无毛或具细短毛。——(4)
* ~花序窄狭，每节具1-2分枝；第一颖3脉，第二颖3-5脉；小穗轴节间具长1-2.6毫米的柔毛；杆节平滑无毛。——(3)

* 3 秆常单生，具长的匐匍地下茎；叶片宽6-12毫米；小穗长20-25毫米，小穗轴节间具长1.5-2.6毫米的柔毛。——[大穗异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon dahuricum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~秆少数丛生，具地下茎，常较短或不明显；叶片宽2-4毫米；小穗长11-17毫米，小穗轴节间具长1-2毫米的柔毛。——[异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon schellianum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 4 秆节被细短毛；叶舌平截，长1-2毫米；小穗轴节间具长2-3毫米柔毛；第一颖长6-10毫米。——[高异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon altius?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~秆节无毛；叶舌披针形，长3-6毫米；小穗轴节间具长4-6毫米的柔毛；第一颖长10-17毫米。——[毛轴异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon pubescens?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 5 花序开展；分枝细长，长达10厘米。——(6)
* ~花序窄狭；分枝短，常不超过4厘米。——(8)

* 6 小穗轴节间全部具柔毛；外稃先端常具1-2毫米长的细齿裂。——(7)
* ~小穗轴节间仅上部具柔毛，下部无毛；外稃先端钝，通常不开裂。——[光花异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon leianthum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 7 小穗长16-19毫米；外稃长12-13毫米；第一颖长7-9毫米。——[长稃异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon polyneurum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~小穗长10-14毫米；外稃长9-11毫米；第一颖长4-6毫米。——[变绿异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon virescens?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 8 花序分枝较短，上升或紧靠主轴；芒自稃体近顶处或1/2以上伸出，芒柱扭转或不扭转；外稃先端常齿裂。——(9)
* ~花序分枝较长，长达4厘米，伸展；芒自稃体顶端1/3以下伸出，芒柱扭转；外稃先端完整。——[冷衫异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon abietetorum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 9 花序窄狭并稀疏，具少数小穗；芒自稃体近顶端1/3以上伸出。——(10)
* ~花序窄狭且紧缩，具多数小穗；芒自稃体顶端1/3-1/2处伸出。——(12)
* 10 花序长10-17厘米，直径1-1.5厘米；分枝短，每节2-3枚，紧贴花序主轴；叶集中茎基部，上部少而短。——(11)
* ~花序长5-8厘米，直径2-3厘米；分枝光滑，长1-2厘米，上升，每节2枚；叶遍生植株上下。——[云南异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon delavayi?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 11 植株细弱；小穗细小，长约7毫米；颖光滑，第一颖长1.5-3.5毫米，第二颖长3-5.5毫米。——[小颖异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon schmidii var. parviglumum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~植株较粗壮，直立；小穗大，长8-10毫米；颖粗糙，第一颖长4-6毫米，第二颖长5.5- 7毫米。——[粗糙异燕麦（原变种）](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon schmidii var. schmidii?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 12 花序稠密，并紧缩似穗状，卵形至长圆形。——(13)
* ~花序较稠密，但不紧缩呈穗状，为狭长圆形或长椭圆形。——(15)
* 13 叶舌极短，长约0.5毫米，顶端具纤毛；花序以下被微毛；花药长约4毫米。——(14)
* ~叶舌长1.5-3.5毫米，顶端齿裂，无毛；花序以下茎光滑；花药长1-1.6毫米。——[短药异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon potaninii?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 14 花序较疏松，长6-14厘米；分枝长达16毫米；小穗常含3-4小花。——[疏花异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon tibeticum var. laxiflorum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~花序紧缩，长2-5厘米；分枝短，长3-13毫米；小穗常含2小花。——[藏异燕麦（原变种）](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon tibeticum var. tibeticum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 15 植株为疏丛；茎基不具残留的叶鞘；叶长达茎的1/2以上；小穗长约12毫米，常褐黄色带紫色。——[蒙古异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon mongolicum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~植株密丛；茎基部具多数残留的叶鞘；叶长达茎的1/8-1/2;小穗长9-10毫米，常褐黄色。——[天山异燕麦](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon tianschanicum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)
