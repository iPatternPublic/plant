等颖落芒草(原变种) **Piptatherum aequiglume var. aequiglume**

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf) >> [Piptatherum](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Piptatherum?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Piptatherum.pdf)
## Description

Ligule ovate, rounded, 2–5 mm. Spikelets (5.5–)6.5–9 mm; lemma 5.5–7.5 mm, pubescent with 0.15–0.3 mm hairs, passing smoothly into awn; awn tardily deciduous, 6–13 mm. Fl. and fr. Aug–Sep.

> **Habait** : 
> Rocky fissures near streams; 1800–2900 m. Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan [E Afghanistan, Bhutan, NW India, Kashmir, Pakistan].

### Synonym
*Oryzopsis aequiglumis* Duthie ex J. D. Hooker, Fl. Brit. India 7: 234. 1896 [\"1897\"]; *O. multiradiata* (Hackel) Handel-Mazzetti; *O. munroi* Stapf f. *multiradiata* Hackel; *Piptatherum sinense* Mez.
