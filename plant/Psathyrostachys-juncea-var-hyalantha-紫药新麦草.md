## Description

Culms with dense, short, soft hairs below spike. Anthers purple, 3–4 mm. Fl. and fr. late summer to autumn.

> **Habait** : 
> Grasslands on mountain slopes, steppes; 1500–2000 m. Xinjiang [Kyrgyzstan, Russia].
