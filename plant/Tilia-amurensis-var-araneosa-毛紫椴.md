## Description

Branchlets white stellate pubescent. Petiole ca. 1 cm, hairy; leaf blade 3-4.5 × 2.5-4.5 cm, abaxially greenish, densely hairy, base cordate, apex caudate.


> **Habait** : 
>●  Mixed *Abies* and *Picea* forests; 1300-1400 m. Jilin.
