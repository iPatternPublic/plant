喜马拉雅嵩草**(原亚种) **Kobresia royleana subsp. royleana**

> [Cyperaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Cyperaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Cyperaceae.pdf)>>[Kobresia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Kobresia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Kobresia.pdf)

## Description

Culms rigid, acutely trigonous, or subterete in lower part, 5-35 cm tall, 1.5-2.5 mm in diam. Leaves basal, blade flat, 2-4 mm wide. Inflorescence a dense panicle, lower branches sometimes slightly distant, 1-3.5 × 0.6-1.2 cm. Terminal spikelets of inflorescence branches male, lateral ones bisexual or unisexual. Lower spikelets of branches usually bisexual with 1 basal female flower and 2 or 3(-6) distal male flowers. Glumes ovate-oblong, 2.6-4.5 × 3.2-3.5 mm. Prophylls 3-5 mm. Nutlets 2.4-4 mm, beak to 1 mm.


> Reference: 
> Records of *Kobresia caricina* Willdenow (FRPS 12: 22-23. 2000) and *K. persica* Kükenthal & Bornmüller (loc. cit.: 35-36) are based on misidentifications of *K. royleana* subsp. *royleana*.Records of *Kobresia caricina* Willdenow (FRPS 12: 22-23. 2000) and *K. persica* Kükenthal & Bornmüller (loc. cit.: 35-36) are based on misidentifications of *K. royleana* subsp. *royleana*.

### Statistics
Moist habitats; 700-5200 m. Gansu, Qinghai, Sichuan, Xinjiang, Xizang, Yunnan [Afghanistan, India (Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand), Kashmir, Kyrgyzstan, Nepal, Pakistan, Tajikistan].
