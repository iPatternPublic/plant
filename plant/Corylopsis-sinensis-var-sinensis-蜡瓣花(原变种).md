## Description

Young branches and buds pubescent. Leaf blade obovate or obovate-rounded, abaxially gray-brown stellately pubescent, adaxially glabrous or pubescent only along veins, base asymmetrical, cordate, apex subacute or obtuse. Fl. May–Jul, fr. Jul–Sep.


> **Habait** : 
> Forests, mountains; 1000--1500 m. Anhui, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Zhejiang.
