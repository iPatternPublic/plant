刺续断(原亚种) **Acanthocalyx nepalensis subsp. nepalensis**

> [Morinaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Morinaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Morinaceae.pdf)>>[Acanthocalyx](Acanthocalyx-刺续断属.md) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Acanthocalyx.pdf)

## Description

Ovary and achene usually glabrous.

### Statistics
Grassy slopes; 2800-4200 m. S Xizang [Bhutan, N India, Nepal].

### Synonym
*Morina nepalensis* D. Don, Prodr. Fl. Nepal. 161. 1825; *M. betonicoides* Bentham; *M. nana* Wallich ex Bentham.
