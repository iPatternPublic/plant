鞭打绣球(原变种) **Hemiphragma heterophyllum var. heterophyllum**

> [Scrophulariaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Scrophulariaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Scrophulariaceae.pdf)>>[Hemiphragma](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Hemiphragma?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Hemiphragma.pdf)

## Description

Leaves 0.8-2 cm, as wide a long, margin serrately 5-7-toothed. Pedicel lacking.


> **Habait** : 
> Alpine grassland, rock crevices; 2000-4000 m. Fujian, Gansu, Guizhou, Hubei, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Indonesia (Sulawesi), Nepal, Philippines, Sikkim].
