## Description

Stem green, 1.5--3 m. Tepals adaxially streaked with purple, abaxially greenish. 2* n* = 24*.

> **Habait** : 
> Forests, hillsides; 2300--2900 m. S Xizang [Bhutan, NE India, Myanmar, Nepal, Sikkim].

### Synonym
* Lilium giganteum* Wallich, Tent. Fl. Napal. 21. 1824.
