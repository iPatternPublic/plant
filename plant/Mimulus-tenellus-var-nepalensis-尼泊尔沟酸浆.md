## Description

Stems suberect. Pedicel nearly as long as whole leaf. Calyx cylindric, ca. 1 cm or longer, apex truncate.

> **Habait** : 
> Beside water, wet places; 500-3000 m. Gansu, Guizhou, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Xizang, Yunnan, Zhejiang [India, (Assam), Japan, Nepal, Vietnam].

### Synonym
*Mimulus assamicus* Griffith; *M*. *formosanus* Hayata; *M. nepalensis*Bentham, Scroph. Ind. 29. 1835; *M*. *tenellus* subsp. *nepalensis* (Bentham) D. Y. Hong ex H. P. Yang.

**尼泊尔沟酸浆（变种）**

var. nepalensis (Benth.) Tsoong, comb. nov. ——M. nepalensis Benth. Scroph. Ind. 29. 1835. ——M. tenellus Bunge subsp. epalensis(Benth.)Hong,中国高等植物图鉴4: 13. 1975.

这一变种与前一变种之别，在于其植株较大，近直立；花梗与叶近等长；花萼较大，长1厘米或更长。

分布于秦岭淮河以南，广西、福建以外各省区；尼泊尔，印度同日本也有。生于海拔800-2200米的水边、湿地。
