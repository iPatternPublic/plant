## Description

Lianas woody, to 5 m, glabrous throughout. Branchlets grayish, thick, hollow, lenticellate. Petiole 2-3 cm; leaf blade ovate-oblong to oblong, 11-19 &amp;#215; 4.5-11 cm, thin papery, base rounded to truncate, apex acute; lateral veins 4 or 5 pairs, flattened. Inflorescences 8-13 cm; peduncle ca. 5.5 cm; rachis zigzag with 2 or more separated cymules; cymules with 2-5 flowers open, eventually racemelike. Pedicel 3-8 mm. Sepals triangular, ca. 1 &amp;#215; 1 mm; glands 10. Corolla brownish purple; tube ca. 1 mm long; lobes rotate, ovate-oblong, ca. 3 &amp;#215; 2 mm, longitudinally veined, mealy inside. Corona stellate spreading, apparently dark brown; lobes oblong-ligulate, ca. 1 mm, posterior angle obtuse. Gynostegium yellow tipped, constricted below apex. Anthers square, appendages rounded; pollinia circular to almost square. Stigma head subdepressed, obtuse. Fl. Jun.

**7. 海南醉魂藤（海南植物志）**

Heterostemma sinicum Tsiang in Sunyatsenia 3: 190, Pl. 21, 1936; 海南植物志, 3: 284. 1974.

高攀援木质藤本；小枝粗壮，干后中空，有皮孔，全株无毛。叶薄纸质，长圆形或卵状长圆形，长11.5-25厘米，宽4.5-11厘米，顶端急尖，基部圆形；叶脉两面扁平，侧脉远离，每边4-5条，弧形上升；叶柄长2-4厘米，顶端丛生3-5个小腺体。聚伞花序假伞形状，腋生；花序梗曲折，长10-13厘米，上面有花梗凋落的遗痕很多；花梗不等长，长3-8毫米；小苞片长1毫米；花蕾钝头；花萼钟状，内面基部有10个小腺体，花萼裂片卵状三角形，长和宽约1毫米；花冠紫褐色，辐状，花冠筒长1毫米，裂片卵状长圆形，长3毫米，宽2毫米，顶端钝头，外面无毛，内面粉状；副花冠星芒状，裂片舌状长圆形，展开，长1毫米，外角钝；合蕊冠极短，花药顶端有圆形膜片；花粉块近圆球状，直立，内角顶端有透明膜边；柱头膨大，顶端平坦。花期6月。

产于广东（海南）。生长于山地林中。模式标本采自保亭。
