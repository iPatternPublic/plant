## Description

Culm internodes 31–36 cm, initially white powdery; leaf blade 21–26 × (3.2–)4–5 cm, secondary veins 7 or 8 pairs.


> **Habait** : 
>* Broad-leaved forests; ca. 1400 m. Sichuan (Leibo).

### Synonym
Basionym: *Qiongzhuea macrophylla* f. *leiboensis* Hsueh & D. Z. Li, Acta Bot. Yunnan. 10: 51. 1988; *Chimonobambusa macrophylla* f. *leiboensis* (Hsueh & D. Z. Li) T. H. Wen & Ohrnberger; *Q. macrophylla* var. *leiboensis* (Hsueh & D. Z. Li) Hsueh & D. Z. Li.
