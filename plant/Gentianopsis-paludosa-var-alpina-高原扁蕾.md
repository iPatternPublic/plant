## Description

Stem branched from base. Stem leaf blades oblong to elliptic-lanceolate. Corolla pale yellow-white to yellow.


> **Habait** : 
>* Stream banks, swamps, alpine meadows, forests; 2800-4000 m. Qinghai, Xizang.
