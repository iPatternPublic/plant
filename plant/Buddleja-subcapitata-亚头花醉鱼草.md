> **Comment** : 
> Name appeared after publication of the family treatment for the Flora of China.

## Description

Fl. Jul-Sep, fr. Aug-Oct.

> **Habait** : 
>* Roadsides at forest margins; ca. 2200 m. Sichuan (Yanbian Xian).
