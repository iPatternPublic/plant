## Description

Dry fruits 1.2-1.6(-1.7) × 1.2-1.7 mm; wing 0.1-0.4 mm wide.

> **Habait** : 
> Lakes, still water. NE Nei Mongol (Da Hinggan Ling) [pan-boreal].

### Synonym
*Callitriche autumnalis* Linnaeus; *C. bifida* Morong.
