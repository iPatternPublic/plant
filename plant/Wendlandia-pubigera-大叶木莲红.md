## Description

Shrubs, to 2 m tall; branches pubescent. Leaves opposite or ternate; petiole 0.8-1.8 cm, pubescent; blade drying thinly leathery, obovate-oblong or elliptic, 14.5-18 × 6-7 cm, adaxially glabrous or sparsely puberulent along principal veins, abaxially sparsely pubescent with pubescence denser along principal veins, base cuneate, apex shortly acuminate; secondary veins 10 or 11 pairs; stipules generally persistent, pandurate, 7.5-9 × 2.5-3 mm, nearly as wide as to slightly wider than branches, apex spreading, rounded. Inflorescence paniculate, pyramidal in outline, ca. 12 × 12-15 cm, branched to 2 or 3 orders, yellowish brown pubescent, sessile and tripartite or pedunculate; peduncle 1.8-3 cm. Flowers sessile. Calyx pubescent; hypanthium portion ca. 1.8 mm; limb lobed nearly to base; lobes ovate to deltoid, ca. 1 mm. Corolla white, tubular-salverform, outside glabrous; tube 2-3 mm, white villous inside upper part; lobes rounded to spatulate, ca. 1 mm. Anthers elliptic, ca. 0.7 mm, subsessile, partially exserted. Stigma 2-lobed. Capsules not seen. Fl. Mar.

**20. 大叶木莲红（广西上思）**

Wendlandia pubigera W. C. Chen in Acta Phytotax. Sinica 21 (4): 395, fig. 7. 1983.

灌木，高约2米；小枝被短柔毛。叶薄革质，倒卵状长圆形或椭圆形，长14.5-18厘米，宽6-7厘米，顶端短渐尖，基部楔形，上面无毛或仅脉上有极疏的微柔毛，下面被疏短柔毛，脉上较密；侧脉10-11对，在下面凸起；叶柄长0.8-1.8厘米，被短柔毛，托叶顶端圆形，不反折，与小枝近等宽。花序顶生，长12厘米，宽15厘米，被黄褐色柔毛；花无花梗；花萼长约2毫米，被柔毛，裂片与萼管近等长；花冠管状，白色，长3-4毫米，冠管内面上部被白色长柔毛，裂片圆形，长1毫米，开放时下弯；花药椭圆形，长0.7毫米；柱头2裂，伸出。果未见。花期3月。

产于广西上思；生于密林中。模式标本采自广西上思县十万大山。
