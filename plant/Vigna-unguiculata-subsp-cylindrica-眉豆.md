## Description

Legumes 7.5-13 cm, erect or spreading. Fl. Apr-May, fr. Sep.


> Reference: 
> This taxon is used as a vegetable.This taxon is used as a vegetable.

### Statistics
Widely cultivated in China [Cambodia, Japan, Korea, Laos, Viet-nam; Africa, America (cultivated)].

### Synonym
*Phaseolus cylindricus* Linnaeus, Herb. Amboin. 23. 1754; *Dolichos catjang* N. L. Burman; *Vigna catjang* (N. L. Burman) Walpers; *V. cylindrica* (Linnaeus) Skeels; *V. sinensis* var. *cat-jang* (N. L. Burman) Chiovenda; *V. sinensis* subsp. *cylindrica* (Linnaeus) Van Eseltine; *V. unguiculata* var. *cylindrica* (Lin-naeus) H. Ohashi.

一年生直立草本，高20-40厘米。荚果长10-16厘米；种子颜色种种。花期7-8月，果期9月。

我国各省都有栽培。日本、朝鲜、美国亦有栽培。

种子供食用，可掺入米中做豆饭、煮汤、煮粥或磨粉用。
