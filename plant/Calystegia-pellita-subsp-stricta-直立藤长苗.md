## Description

Stems &amp;#177; erect, with crowded leaves. Leaves broadly oblong, 1.5--2.5 cm wide, 3--4 X as long as wide, base truncate; major lateral veins 3 or 4 per side. Fl. Jun-Jul.


> **Habait** : 
> Mountains. Jilin [N Korea, E Russia].
