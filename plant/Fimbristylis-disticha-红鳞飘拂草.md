红鳞飘拂草 **Fimbristylis disticha** Boeckeler Linnaea. 38: 393. 1874.

> [Cyperaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Cyperaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Cyperaceae.pdf) >> [Fimbristylis](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Fimbristylis?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Fimbristylis.pdf)

## Description

Annuals. Culms tufted, 10-30 cm tall. Leaf 1/4-1/3 as long as culm; sheath apex obliquely split, mouth margins membranous and pale brown; leaf blade linear, 1-2.5 mm wide, slightly curved, both surfaces scabrous with appressed short pubescence or glabrescent, apex acute. Involucral bracts 3 or 4, leaflike, to 2.8 cm, basalmost ca. 3 × as long as glumes, apically attenuate. Inflorescence a compound anthela; rays many. Spikelets solitary, ovoid to oblong-ovoid, 3-6 × 1.5-2.5 mm, slightly compressed, with basal 1 or 2 glumes empty. Glumes reddish brown, broadly ovate, ca. 2.5 mm, not shiny, glabrous, 1-veined forming an abaxial keel, apex mucronate. Stamens 3. Style 3-sided, not ciliate, basally inflated; stigmas 3. Nutlet white, obovoid, ca. 0.7 mm, 3-sided, verruculose. Fl. and fr. Aug-Sep.

### Statistics
Grasslands, slopes. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi [Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos, Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam; Indian Ocean islands].

### Synonym
*Fimbristylis rufoglumosa* Tang & F. T. Wang.

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第11卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/11) >> 104页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/11/104.pdf)

**40.红鳞飘拂草**

Fimbristylis rufoglumosa Tang et Wang, sp. nov.

稈丛生，无毛，高10-300厘米。叶线形，略弯曲，长为稈的1/4-1/3, 宽1-2.5毫米，两面被糙伏毛，或毛脱落而仅留存于叶片末端与边缘，顶端急尖，鞘上端斜裂，裂口处有淡棕色的膜质边缘。苞片叶状，3-4枚，最下面的苞片通常为鳞片长的3倍以上，最长的可达28毫米，向顶端渐狭；长侧枝聚繖花序复出，有较多的辐射枝；小穗单生于辐射枝顶端，卵形或长圆卵形，稍扁，长3-6毫米，宽1.5-2.5毫米，下面有1-2片鳞片内无花；鳞片宽卵形，无毛，长约2.5毫米，红褐色，无光泽，背面有一条中脉呈龙骨状隆起，顶端具硬尖，上部鳞片的短尖较下部的为短；雄蕊3；花柱三稜形，基部膨大，无缘毛，柱头3。小坚果三稜状倒卵形，长约0.7毫米，白色，有疣状突起。花果期8-9月。

产于广东、广西；生长在路旁。
