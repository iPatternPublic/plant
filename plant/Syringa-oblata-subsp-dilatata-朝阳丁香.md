## Description

Shrubs 1-3 m, many branched. Leaf blade ovate to ovate-orbicular, 3-10 &amp;#215; 2.5-8 cm, base truncate to broadly cuneate, or rarely subcordate, apex short to long acuminate. Panicles lateral, lax, 5-10 &amp;#215; ca. 8 cm. Calyx ca. 2 mm. Corolla lilac or red-lilac to violet-lilac, sometimes white; tube 1-1.7(-2.2) cm; lobes oblong-elliptic, 5-8(-10) mm. Anthers inserted at middle of corolla tube. Capsule 7-12(-15) mm. Fl. May-Jun, fr. Sep.

> **Habait** : 
> Gravely mountains; 100-700 m. Jilin (Jian Xian), Liaoning [Korea].
