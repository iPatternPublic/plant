## Description

Glumes 10–15 mm, apex with awn 4–8 mm. Lemma ca. 12 mm, apex with awn 4–8 mm. Fl. and fr. Jun–Aug.

> **Habait** : 
>* Valleys, field margins; 500–1900 m. Xinjiang.
