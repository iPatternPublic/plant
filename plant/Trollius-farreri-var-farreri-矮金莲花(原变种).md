## Description

Leaves basal or subbasal; leaf blade 1.4--2.6 cm wide; central segment rhombic-obovate or cuneate, with 2 or 3 irregular triangular teeth.

> **Habait** : 
>* Grassy slopes; 3500--4700 m. S Gansu, E and S Qinghai, S Shaanxi, W Sichuan, NE Xizang, NW Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Trollius kansuensis* (Brühl) Mukerjee; *T. pumilus* D. Don subsp. *normalis* Brühl var. *kansuensis* Brühl.
