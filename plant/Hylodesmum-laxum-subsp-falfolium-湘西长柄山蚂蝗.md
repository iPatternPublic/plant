## Description

Leaves approximate at apex of stem; terminal leaflet broadly elliptic or broadly ovate, 4-5 × 2.7-3.2 cm. Legume stipe 1.2-1.4 cm.

> Reference: 
> Only one individual was found.Only one individual was found.
