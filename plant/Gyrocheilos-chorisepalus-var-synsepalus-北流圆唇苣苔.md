## Description

Leaf blade margin double dentate to deeply crenate. Calyx 2-4-lobed; tube 1-2 mm; lobes unequal, largest 2- or 3-lobed, 1-3 mm wide.


> **Habait** : 
>* Rocky hills; 700-900 m. W Guangdong, E Guangxi (Beiliu Xian).
