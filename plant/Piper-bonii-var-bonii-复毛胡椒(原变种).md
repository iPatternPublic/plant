> [Piperaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Piperaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Piperaceae.pdf) >> [Piper](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Piper?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Piper.pdf)
## Description

Leaf blade ovate or ovate-lanceolate, 4.5-9 ×  2.2-5 cm, apex acuminate. Peduncle 5-10 mm. Fl. Feb-Apr.

> **Habait** : 
> Thickets or forests, on trees; 300-1000 m. NW and SW Guangxi, SE Yunnan [N Vietnam]
