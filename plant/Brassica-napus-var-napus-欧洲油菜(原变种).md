欧洲油菜**(原变种) **Brassica napus var. napus**

> [Brassicaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brassicaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Brassicaceae.pdf)>>[Brassica](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brassica?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Brassica.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> The source of canola oil.

## Description

Plants annual. Taproots slender, not fleshy. 2n = 38*.

> **Habait** : 
> Cultivated. Throughout China [widely cultivated and naturalized elsewhere].

Léveillé; *B. oleracea* var. *pseudocolza* H. Léveillé.
