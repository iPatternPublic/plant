## Description

Subshrubs much branched. Branchlets opposite, jointed. Leaves opposite, undeveloped, scale-like. Inflorescences sessile, spicate; bracts peltate, opposite; bractlets absent. Flowers axillary, (2 or)3 per bract, bisexual. Perianth 3-parted; segments broadly ovate, apex obtuse. Stamen 1. Ovary compressed ovoid; ovule anatropous; stigmas 2, subulate, papillate. Fruit a utricle. Seed vertical; embryo semi-annular; perisperm present.

### Statistics
One species: N Africa, Asia, S Europe.

**6. 盐节木属 *——Halocnemum Bieb**

Bieb. Fl. Taur.-Cauc. 3: 3. 1819.

半灌木。多分枝，小枝对生，有关节。叶不发育，叶片成鳞片状，对生。花序穗状，无柄；花两性，腋生，每3朵花极少为2朵花生于1苞片内，苞片为盾状的鳞片，对生，无小苞片；花被3深裂，花被片宽卵形，顶端钝；雄蕊1，子房卵形，两侧扁；柱头2，钻状，有乳头状小突起；胚珠倒生。果实为胞果；种子直立；胚半环形，有胚乳。

本属为单种属，分布于欧洲南部、亚洲西部及北部、非洲北部。

* 中文异名：盐节草属（中国植物科属检索表）
