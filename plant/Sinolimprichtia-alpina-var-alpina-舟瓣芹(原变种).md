舟瓣芹(原变种) **Sinolimprichtia alpina var. alpina** (C. B. Clarke) Ridley

> **Comment** : 
> This variety has reputed medicinal value.

## Description

Bracteoles linear or linear-lanceolate, 8–17 × 0.4–0.7 mm, almost as long as umbellule, apex entire, obtuse.


> **Habait** : 
> Alpine sandy areas, screes, rock crevices; 3300–5000 m. Qinghai, SW Sichuan, SE Xizang, NW Yunnan.
