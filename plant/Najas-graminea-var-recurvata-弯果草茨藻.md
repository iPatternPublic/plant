## Description

Leaves less than 0.5 mm wide; auricles shortly lanceolate. Fruit recurved at apex. Seeds with apparently oblong areoles.

### Statistics
● Paddy fields. Hubei, Zhejiang.

**9b. 弯果草茨藻（变种）（植物研究）**

Najas graminea Del. var. recurvata J. B. He et al. in Bull. Bot. Res. 8(3): 126. 1988.

与原变种区别在于：叶鞘顶端裂片为短披针形；叶宽不及0.5毫米；果先端向背脊弯曲；外种皮细胞明显为长方形。

产湖北利川和咸丰。生于稻田中。
