## Description

Rootstock moniliform. Leaflets not 2-pinnatifid, obovate-elliptic to elliptic, apex acuminate, rarely long acuminate.

> **Habait** : 
> Forests; 1700-3600 m. Gansu, Guizhou, Henan, Hubei, Shanxi, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan [N Myanmar, Nepal, N Vietnam].

### Synonym
