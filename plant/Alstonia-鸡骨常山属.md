> **Comment** : 
> About 60 species: tropical Asia, Africa, C America, N Australia, Pacific Islands; eight species in China.

## Description

Trees or shrubs, latex present. Branches whorled, mostly 4 or 5 together. Leaves whorled, rarely opposite; lateral veins numerous, ending in a marginal vein. Cymes terminal, usually 1-5\nin thyrses or compound umbels, terminal. Flowers white, yellow, or pink. Calyx without glands inside, lobes connate at base. Corolla salverform, tube cylindric, dilated in distal half,\npubescent inside, lobes overlapping to right or left. Stamens included, inserted near or above middle of corolla tube; anthers ovate, free from pistil head, not caudate; disc absent or of\nscales. Ovaries 2, distinct or connate, ovules numerous. Follicles 2, free or connate. Seeds oblong or linear, long bearded at both ends; endosperm thin; cotyledons up to twice as long\nas radicle.

### Synonym
*Blaberopus* A. de Candolle; *Winchia* A. de Candolle.

## Keylist

* 1 Trees; disc absent or small annular.——(2)
* ~ Shrubs; disc of 2 scales alternating with ovaries.——(4)

* 2 (1)Lateral leaf veins widely spaced, (3-)5-12 mm apart, ascending; corolla lobes in bud overlapping to right; seeds acuminate at 1 end——2 [Alstonia macrophylla 大叶糖胶树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Alstonia macrophylla?t=foc)
* ~ Lateral leaf veins close together, 1-6 mm apart, horizontal or almost so; corolla lobes in bud overlapping to left; seeds obtuse or rounded at both ends.——(3)

* 3 (2) Leaf apex long acuminate; ovary syncarpous; follicles solitary——1 [Alstonia rostrata 盆架树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Alstonia rostrata?t=foc)
* ~ Leaf apex rounded to short acuminate; ovary of 2 separate carpels; follicles paired——3 [Alstonia scholaris 糖胶树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Alstonia scholaris?t=foc)

* 4 (1) Leaves sessile, blade thick, papery to ± leathery, lateral veins at almost 90° to midvein.——(5)
* ~ Leaves petiolate, blade thin, membranous, lateral veins at 45° to midvein.——(6)

* 5 (4) Leaves pubescent abaxially; corolla to 3 cm——5 [Alstonia neriifolia 竹叶羊角棉](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Alstonia neriifolia?t=foc)
* ~ Leaves glabrous abaxially; corolla ca. 1.1 cm——6 [Alstonia rupestris 岩生羊角棉](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Alstonia rupestris?t=foc)

* 6 (4) Leaves pubescent on both surfaces; corolla pink to red——4 [Alstonia yunnanensis 鸡骨常山](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Alstonia yunnanensis?t=foc)
* ~ Leaves glabrous; corolla white or yellow.——(7)

* 7 (6) Colleters few and inconspicuous; corolla white, to 3 cm, tube 1-2 cm; disc lower than ovary——7 [Alstonia mairei 羊角棉](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Alstonia mairei?t=foc)
* ~ Colleters numerous, becoming ± indurated at base and persisting; corolla yellow, ca. 1 cm; tube ca. 0.8 cm; disc as long as ovary——8 [Alstonia henryi 黄花羊角棉](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Alstonia henryi?t=foc)

**16.鸡骨常山属——Alstonia R. Br.**

R. Br. Mem. Wern. Soc. 1: 75. Apr. 1810.

乔木、灌木，具乳汁；枝轮生。叶通常为3-4（-8）片轮生，稀对生；侧脉多数，密生而平行。花白色、黄色或红色，由多朵花组成伞房状的聚伞花序，顶生或近顶生；花萼短，萼片为双盖覆瓦状排列，内面无腺体；花冠高脚碟状，冠筒圆筒形，中部以上膨大，喉部无副花冠，而被柔毛至近无毛，花冠裂片在花蕾时向左覆盖（中国种）；雄蕊与柱头离生，内藏，花药长圆形；子房由2枚离生心皮组成，每心皮有胚珠多颗，花柱丝状，柱头棍棒状，顶端2裂；花盘由2枚舌状鳞片组成，与心皮互生。蓇葖2个，离生，叉开或并行；种子扁平，两端被长缘毛。

约50种，分布于热带非洲、亚洲和澳大利亚。我国产6种，分布于广东、广西和云南等省区。

本属模式种为：Alstonia scholaris（L.）R. Br.

## 检索表

* 1 乔木；花盘环状；子房密被柔毛。——(2)
* ~直立灌木；花盘由2枚舌状鳞片组成；子房无毛。——(3)

* 2 叶背被柔毛，黄绿色。 ——[大叶糖胶树](Alstonia-macrophylla-大叶糖胶树.md)

* ~叶无毛，灰绿色。 ——[糖胶树](Alstonia-scholaris-糖胶树.md)

* 3 花白色或黄色；柱头和叶均无毛。——(4)
* ~花紫红色；柱头和叶均被短柔毛。 ——[鸡骨常山](Alstonia-yunnanensis-鸡骨常山.md)

* 4 叶薄纸质，有柄，柄长0.5-1.5厘米。——(5)
* ~叶厚纸质，无柄，叶缘下延抱茎。 ——[岩生羊角棉](Alstonia-rupestris-岩生羊角棉.md)

* 5 花黄色；花冠筒粗短，长0.8厘米；花盘与子房等高。 ——[黄花羊角棉](Alstonia-henryi-黄花羊角棉.md)

* ~花白色；花冠筒细长，长1-2厘米；花盘高达子房一半。 ——[羊角棉](Alstonia-mairei-羊角棉.md)
