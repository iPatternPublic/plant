## Description

Leaves narrowly lanceolate, 13-27 × 1.5-4.5 cm; secondary veins distinct or often indistinct on adaxial surface. Fl. May, fr. Jul.


> Reference: 
> Plants that match this variety are uncommonly collected and are provisionally separated here.Plants that match this variety are uncommonly collected and are provisionally separated here.

### Statistics
Wet places, forest understories; 100-2000 m. Guangdong, Guangxi, Hainan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bangladesh, Bhutan, Borneo, Cambodia, E and NE India (including Andaman Islands), Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, Singapore, Sri Lanka, Thailand, Vietnam].

### Synonym
*Psychotria longifolia* Dalzell, Hooker’s J. Bot. Kew Gard. Misc. 2: 133. 1850, not Hoffmannsegg ex Roemer & Schultes, nor Sprengel; *Chassalia longifolia* K. M. Wong.
