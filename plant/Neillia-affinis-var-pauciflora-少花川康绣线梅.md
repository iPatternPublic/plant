## Description

Raceme 3–4 cm, 5–10-flowered. Carpel 1; ovules 8–10.
> **Habait** : 
>2000--2300 m. SE Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Neillia pauciflora* Rehder in Sargent, Pl. Wilson. 1: 437. 1913.
