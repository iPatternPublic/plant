## Description

Usually shrubs, 1-2 m tall. Leaf blade 4.5-7.5 × 1-2 cm, midrib elevated distally adaxially. Inflorescences subracemose, all parts sparsely pubescent to puberulent.

### Statistics
● Limestone hills. SW Guizhou (Xingyi).

**33b. 兴义楠（变种）**

var. cavaleriei Liou, l. c.

与白楠（原变种）不同在于叶较狭小，长4.5-7.5厘米，宽1-2厘米，中脉上面突起；花序近总状；各部分毛被均较稀疏。

产贵州兴义。
