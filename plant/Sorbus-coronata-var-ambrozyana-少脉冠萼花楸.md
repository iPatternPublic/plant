少脉冠萼花楸 **Sorbus coronata var. ambrozyana** (C. K. Schneider) L. T. Lu

> [Rosaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Rosaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Rosaceae.pdf) >> [Sorbus](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Sorbus?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Sorbus.pdf)
## Description

Petiole 1–2 cm; leaf blade ovate-lanceolate, abaxial surface and pedicels usually persistently pubescent; lateral veins 9 or 10 pairs.

> **Habait** : 
> Mountain regions; 3000--3200 m. NW Yunnan (Lijiang Naxizu Zizhixian).

### Synonym
*Sorbus ambrozyana* C. K. Schneider, Bot. Gaz. 63. 401. 1917.
