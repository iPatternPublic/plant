## Description

Leaf blade margin double dentate. Calyx 5-sect; tube absent; segments equal, ca. 1 mm wide.


> **Habait** : 
>* Rocky streamsides, shaded cliffs in valleys. S Guangxi (Shanglin Xian, Wuming Xian).
