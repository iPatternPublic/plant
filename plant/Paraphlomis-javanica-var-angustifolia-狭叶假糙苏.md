## Description

Leaf blade ovate-lanceolate to narrowly lanceolate, margin inconspicuously crenulate. Calyx acicular, hispi-dulous.

> **Habait** : 
> Evergreen and mixed forests; 500-1600 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hunan, Sichuan, Yunnan [Vietnam].

### Synonym
*Paraphlomis rugosa* (Bentham) Prain var. *angustifolia* C. Y. Wu, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 8: 38. 1959.

**1c. 狭叶变种 鬼灯笼树（广东海南），土结香（四川峨眉）**
Paraphlomis javanica (Bl.) Prain var. angustifolia (C. Y. Wu) C. Y. Wu et H. W. Li in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 13 (1): 73. 1975——Paraphlomis rugosa (Benth.) Prain var. angustifolia C. Y. Wu in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 8 (1): 38. 1959; H. W. Li in ibid. 10 (1): 61. 1965.
这一变种与原变种不同在于叶卵圆状披针形直至狭长披针形，具极不显著的细圆齿；萼齿尖明显针状，具细刚毛。
产云南，四川，贵州，广西，广东，福建及湖南；生于亚热带常绿林或混交林林荫下，海拔500-1600米。越南北部也有。选模式标本采自四川峨眉。
