短裂阔蕊兰 **Peristylus lacertifer var. taipoensis** (S. Y. Hu & Barretto) S. C. Chen S. W. Gale & P. J. Cribb, Fl. China. 25: 143. 2009.

## Description

Flowers white. Lateral lobes of lip always shorter than mid-lobe.


> Reference: 
> After publication of the family treatment for the Flora of China, this was treated as *Peristylis intrudens* (Ames) Ormerod (Taiwania 56: 46. 2011).After publication of the family treatment for the Flora of China, this was treated as *Peristylis intrudens* (Ames) Ormerod (Taiwania 56: 46. 2011).

### Statistics
● Forests, grasslands; 100-800 m. Hong Kong, C Taiwan.

### Synonym
Basionym: *Peristylus spiranthes* (Schauer) S. Y. Hu var. *taipoensis* S. Y. Hu & Barretto, Chung Chi J. 13(2): 2. 1976; *P. taipoensis* (S. Y. Hu & Barretto) T. C. Hsu & S. W. Chung.
