## Description

Leaflets glabrous. Legumes almost glabrous.

### Statistics
● Forests, valleys; 1000-2000 m. Guangxi, Guizhou, Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Sophora cavaleriei* H. Léveillé, Fl. Kouy-Tchéou, 242. 1914-1915; *S. velutina* subsp. *cavaleriei* (H. Léveillé) Yakovlev.
