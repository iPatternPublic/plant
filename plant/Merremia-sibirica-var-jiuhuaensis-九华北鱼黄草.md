九华北鱼黄草** **Merremia sibirica var. jiuhuaensis** B. A. Shen & X. L. Liu

> [Convolvulaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Convolvulaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Convolvulaceae.pdf)>>[Merremia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Merremia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Merremia.pdf)
## Description

Inflorescences 7-20-flowered. Corolla pale purplish. Capsule 5-6 mm. Seeds ca. 3.5 mm, &amp;#177; densely whitish puberulent. Fl., fr. Oct.

> **Habait** : 
>* Thickets; 800-1000 m. Anhui (Jiuhua Shan).
