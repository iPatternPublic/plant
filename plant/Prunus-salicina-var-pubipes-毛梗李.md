## Description

Branchlets, petioles, abaxial surface of leaf blades, pedicels, and outside base of hypanthia densely pubescent.


> **Habait** : 
> Forest margins, thickets; 1600--2000 m. Gansu, Sichuan, Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Prunus triflora* Roxburgh var. *pubipes* Koehne in Sargent, Pl. Wilson. 1: 280. 1912.

**6b. 毛梗李（变种）**

Prunus salicina L. var. pubipes (Koehne) Bailey in Rhodora 18:155. 1916; Nakai in Bot. Mag. Tokyo 47:250. 1933;陈嵘, 中国树木分类学467. 1937. ——Prunus triflora Roxburgh var. pubipes Koehne in Sarg. Pl. Wils. 1:280. 1912.

本变种的主要特征为小枝、叶片下面和叶柄以及花梗和萼筒基部均密被短柔毛。

产甘肃、四川和云南。生于灌丛中或林边，海拔1600-1800米。
