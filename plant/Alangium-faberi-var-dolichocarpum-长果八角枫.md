## Description

Leaf blade oblong-oblanceolate, 14-19.5 × 2.5-5.5 cm, base very oblique, lateral veins 8-10. Drupe 1.3-1.4 cm, 5-7 mm in diam.


> **Habait** : 
>● Among shrubs; ca. 1200 m. SE Xizang.
