## Description

Herbs, 70-100 cm tall. Stem pubescent. Terminal leaflet broadly ovate or ovate, 3.5-12 × 2.5-8 cm, both surfaces ad-pressed pubescent, base broadly cuneate or suborbicular, apex acuminate or caudate-acute. Fl. Aug-Nov. 2*n* = 22.


> Reference: 
> The whole plant is used medicinally for treating dysentery and rheumatism. As well, it is used as forage for livestock.The whole plant is used medicinally for treating dysentery and rheumatism. As well, it is used as forage for livestock.

### Statistics
Roadsides on mountain slopes, thickets, sparse forests, forest mar-gins; 300-1400 m. Anhui, Fujian, Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Gui-zhou, ?Hainan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Shanxi, Si-chuan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [Japan, Korea].

顶生小叶宽卵形或卵形，长3.5-12厘米，宽2.5-8厘米，先端渐尖或急尖，基部阔楔形或圆，是与原变种主要区别之点。

产东北、华北（除山东）至陕、甘以南各省（除西藏）。生于山坡路旁、灌丛中疏林中，海拔300-1350米。朝鲜、日本也有分布。

全草供药用，可祛风、活血、止痢，并可作家畜饲料。
