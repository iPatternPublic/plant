> [Brassicaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brassicaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Brassicaceae.pdf)>>[Yinshania](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Yinshania?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Yinshania.pdf)

## Description

At least some of the lowermost leaves trifoliolate. Fruit 6-13 mm. Seeds 1.2-1.3 mm. Fr. May.


> **Habait** : 
>* Forests; 200-700 m. Jiangxi.

### Synonym
*Hilliella sinuata* (K. C. Kuan) Y. H. Zhang & H. W. Li var. *qianwuensis* Y. H. Zhang, Acta Bot. Yunnan. 8: 405. 1986.
