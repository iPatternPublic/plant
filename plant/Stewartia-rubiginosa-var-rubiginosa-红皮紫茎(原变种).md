## Description

Seeds narrowly winged. Fl. May-Jun, fr. Oct-Nov.

> **Habait** : 
>●  Forests; 1100-1300 m. NW Guangdong, S Hunan.

### Synonym
*Stewartia mangshanica* C. X. Ye; *S. oblongifolia* Hu ex S. Z. Yan.
