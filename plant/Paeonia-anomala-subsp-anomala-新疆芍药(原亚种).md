新疆芍药(原亚种) **Paeonia anomala subsp. anomala**

> [Paeoniaceae](http://iplant.cn/info/Paeoniaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Paeoniaceae.pdf) >> [Paeonia](http://iplant.cn/info/Paeonia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Paeonia.pdf)
## Description

Flowers solitary, terminal, rarely 1 or 2 underdeveloped flower buds also present in axils of distal leaves. Fl. Jun--Jul, fr. Sep. 2n = 10.


> **Habait** : 
> Populus or coniferous forests in valleys; 1200--1800 m. N Xinjiang [NE Kazakhstan, N Mongolia, Russia (NE European part, Siberia)].

### Synonym
*Paeonia altaica* K. M. Dai & T. H. Ying; *P. anomala* var. *nudicarpa * Huth; *P. sinjiangensis* K. Y. Pan; *P. veitchii* subsp. *altaica * (K. M. Dai & T. H. Ying) Halda.
