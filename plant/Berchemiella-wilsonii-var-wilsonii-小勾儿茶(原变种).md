小勾儿茶**(原变种) **Berchemiella wilsonii var. wilsonii**

> [Rhamnaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Rhamnaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Rhamnaceae.pdf)>>[Berchemiella](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Berchemiella?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Berchemiella.pdf)

## Description

Petiole glabrous; leaf blade with barbate vein axils abaxially.


> **Habait** : 
>●  Forests, valley forests; ca. 1300 m. W Hubei (Xingshan).

### Synonym
*Chaydaia wilsonii* C. K. Schneider in Sargent, Pl. Wilson. 2: 221. 1914; *Berchemia wilsonii* (C. K. Schneider) Koidzumi.
