## Description

Central spikelet: caryopsis free from lemma and palea. Fl. and fr. Jul–Aug. 2*n* = 14.


> **Habait** : 
> Cultivated as a fodder plant, or a weed in fields of *Hordeum vulgare*. NW and SW China [widely cultivated in temperate regions].

### Synonym
*Hordeum distichon* convar. *nudum* (Linnaeus) Tzvelev; *H. distichon* subsp. *nudum* (Linnaeus) Rothmaler; *H. nudum* (Linnaeus) Arduino; *H. sativum* Jessen var. *nudum* (Linnaeus) Vilmorin; *H. vulgare *var. *nudum* (Linnaeus) J. D. Hooker.
