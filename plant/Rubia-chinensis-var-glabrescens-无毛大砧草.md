无毛大砧草 **Rubia chinensis var. glabrescens** (Nakai) Kitagawa Lin. Fl. Manshur. 405. 1939.

> [Rubiaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Rubiaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Rubiaceae.pdf) >> [Rubia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Rubia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Rubia.pdf)

## Description

Leaves glabrous except aculeolate adaxially along midvein, abaxially glabrous, margin scaberulous.

### Statistics
NE China [Japan, Korea].

### Synonym
*Rubia mitis* f. *glabrescens* Nakai, J. Jap. Bot. 14: 115. 1938; *R. chinensis* f. *glabrescens* (Nakai) Kitagawa.

据记载我国见于东北南部。分布于日本和朝鲜。
