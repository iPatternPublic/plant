## Description

Ovaries pubescent. Follicles transversely plicate-corrugate, sometimes almost transversely winged. Fl. Mar-Aug.


> **Habait** : 
>* Bushland. Gansu, Guizhou, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan

### Synonym
*Dregea corrugata* C. K. Schneider in Sargent, Pl. Wilson. 3: 353. 1916; *Wattakaka corrugata* (C. K. Schneider) Stapf; *W. sinensis* var. *corrugata* (C. K. Schneider) Tsiang.
