山里红 **Crataegus pinnatifida var. major** N. E. Brown
family : [Rosaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Rosaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Rosaceae.pdf)>>[Crataegus](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Crataegus?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Crataegus.pdf)


> **Comment** : 
> This taxon has been long cultivated as an important fruit tree.

## Description

Pedicel and peduncle pubescent, glabrate. Fruit relatively large, ca. 2.5 cm in diam.


> **Habait** : 
> Cultivated in N, NE, and NW China [of cultivated origin].

### Synonym
*Crataegus pinnatifida* var. *korolkowii* (Ascherson & Graebner) Yabe; *Mespilus korolkowii* Ascherson & Graebner.

本变种果形较大，直径可达2.5厘米，深亮红色；叶片大，分裂较浅；植株生长茂盛。

在河北山区为重要果树，果实供鲜吃、加工或作糖胡芦用。一般用山楂为砧木嫁接繁殖。
