## Description

Sepals 12--17 × 1.9--3.2 cm. Labellum 12--17 × 2--2.3 cm; lateral petals 2.8--3.2 cm.

> **Habait** : 
>* Mountain valleys; ca. 400 m. S Guangxi.

### Synonym
* Orchidantha longisepala* D. Fang in D. Fang & D. H. Qin, Guihaia 16: 4. 1996.
