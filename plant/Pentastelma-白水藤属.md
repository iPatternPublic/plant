白水藤属 **Pentastelma** Tsiang & P. T. Li

> [Asclepiadaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Asclepiadaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Asclepiadaceae.pdf) >> [Pentastelma](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pentastelma?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Pentastelma.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> One species: endemic to China.
> The protologue describes the pollinia as pendulous, but *Pentastelma* otherwise shows many similarities to *Tylophora*, suggesting that the anther morphology needs reexamination.

## Description

Lianas. Leaves opposite, petiolate. Cymes axillary. Flower buds ovoid, apex acute. Calyx with basal glands. Corolla subcampanulate; lobes longer than tube, overlapping to right. Corona lobes 5, adnate to gynostegium, fleshy, with lateral vertical processes. Filaments connate into a tube; anthers appressed against stigma, apical appendages incurved; pollinia 2 per pollinarium. Stigma head discoid, apex slightly convex.

**白水藤属 Pentastelma**

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第63卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/63) >> 392页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/63/392y.pdf)
**19. 白水藤属——Pentastelma Tsiang et P. T. Li**

Tsiang et P. T. Li in Fl. Hainan. 3: 262, 577. 1974.

攀援灌木，具乳汁。叶对生，具柄，羽状脉。聚伞花序伞形状，腋生；花蕾卵圆形，顶端急尖；花萼5深裂，内面基部具腺体；花冠近钟状，5深裂，花冠裂片向右覆盖；副花冠5片，与合蕊柱合生，而在其背部直立隆起成侧面平板状；雄蕊5枚，与雌蕊贴生，花药顶端具膜片，覆盖着柱头，花丝合生成筒；花药2室，每室藏有花粉块1个，圆球状，下垂；子房由2枚离生心皮所组成，花柱圆柱状，柱头盘状五角形，顶端略凸起；每心皮有胚珠多颗，着生于腹缝线胎座上。

1种，我国特产，产于广东海南。
