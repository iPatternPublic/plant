> [Apocynaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Apocynaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Apocynaceae.pdf)>>[Ichnocarpus](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ichnocarpus?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Ichnocarpus.pdf)


> **Comment** : 
> Twelve species: SE Asia, N Australia, Pacific Islands; four species in China.

## Description

Plants scramblers or woody lianas, with latex. Leaves opposite. Inflorescences cymose, terminal and/or axillary. Flowers small. Calyx with basal glands inside, lobes free. Corolla white, yellowish, or red, salverform; tube widened near base, throat hairy; lobes oblong, falcate, overlapping to right, in bud with inflexed distal halves. Stamens included, inserted at or below middle of corolla tube; filaments very short; anthers sagittate, adherent to pistil head, cells spurred at base; disc entire, 5-crenate or 5-denate, or deeply divided into 5 erect segments. Ovaries adnate basally to disc, pubescent; ovules numerous. Pistil head ovoid or cup-shaped. Follicles 2, spreading or divaricate. Seeds numerous, linear, compressed, not or hardly beaked, comose; endosperm copious; cotyledons long, flat, radicle superior.

### Synonym
*Lamechites* Markgraf; *Micrechites* Miquel; *Otopetalum* Miquel; *Springia* Heurck & M&amp;uuml;ller Argoviensis.

**40. 腰骨藤属——Ichnocarpus R. Br.**

R. Br. Mem. Wern. Soc. 1: 61. Apr. 1810.

木质藤本，具乳汁。叶对生。花多朵组成顶生或腋生的总状聚伞花序；花萼5裂，内面的腺体有或无；具很多小苞片；花冠高脚碟状，花冠筒喉部稍缩小，被柔毛，无副花冠，花冠裂片5枚，长圆形，向右覆盖；雄蕊5枚，着生在花冠筒中部或稍下，花药箭头状，内藏而不伸出花喉外，基部具耳；花盘5深裂，或5浅裂，通常比子房长；花柱短，柱头圆锥状；子房由2枚离生心皮组成，被毛，胚珠多颗。蓇葖双生，叉开，近圆箸状，一长一短；种子顶端具种毛。

约18种，分布于中国、印度、马来西亚和大洋洲。我国产2种、2变型，分布于广东、广西、云南等省区。

本属模式种为：Ichnocarpus frutescens（L.）W. T. Aiton

## 检索表

* 1 小枝、叶背、叶柄及总花梗无毛，仅幼枝上被短柔毛或枝条和叶背被短柔毛；花白色，花序长3-8 厘米；花萼腺体时有时无；花盘5深裂，长过子房；蓇葖外皮被短柔毛。 ——(2)
