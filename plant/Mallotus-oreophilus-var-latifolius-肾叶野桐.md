## Description

Shrubs or small trees, 1.5-13 m tall. Leaf blade subreniform to transversely oblong, 6-14 × 6.5-20 cm, apex rounded to subtruncate, abruptly shortly acuminate. Male pedicels 6-7 mm; females 2.5-4 mm. Fl. May-Jun, fr. Jun-Aug.


> **Habait** : 
>● Mountain valleys, roadsides, forests; 600-2000 m. W Sichuan, NE Yunnan.

### Synonym
Basionym: *Mallotus oreophilus* Müller Argoviensis subsp. *latifolius* Boufford & T. S. Ying, J. Arnold Arbor. 71: 575. 1990.

**3.肾叶野桐（新拟）**

Mallotus oreophilus Muell. Arg. subsp. latifolius Bouffoid et T. S. Ying in journ. Arn. Arb. 71: 575. 1990, cam fig.

据原始记载：产于四川的小乔木。叶近肾形或扁圆形，长6-14厘米，宽6.5-20厘米，嫩叶密被星状短绒毛。雌花序不分枝，雌花和果均被星状短绒毛。未见雄花及模式标本，暂作存疑。

产四川。
