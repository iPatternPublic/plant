## Description

Trees or small trees; buds yellow tomentose. Leaf rachis terete; leaflets 9-15; leaflet petiolule 2-5 mm; leaflet blade 5-16 × 1-5.5 cm, sparsely pubescent on both surfaces. Fr. Jun-Sep.


> **Habait** : 
>● Lowland and hill forests; 500-600 m. S Jiangxi (Anyuan).
