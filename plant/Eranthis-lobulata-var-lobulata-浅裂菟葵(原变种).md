## Description

Scape 8.5--14.5 cm; bracts 1.1--1.9 × 0.5--1.1 cm. Carpels 5, persistent style ca. 0.5 mm at fruiting.

> **Habait** : 
>* Grassy slopes; ca. 3100 m. NC Sichuan (Wenchuan Xian).
