> [Rutaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Rutaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Rutaceae.pdf)>>[Zanthoxylum](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Zanthoxylum?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Zanthoxylum.pdf)

## Description

Leaves 3- or 5-foliolate; margin of leaflet blades with spines.


> **Habait** : 
>● Hillside open forests and thickets; 400-2100 m. Guizhou, SW Henan (Funiu Shan), W Hubei, Hunan, S Shaanxi (Qin Ling), Sichuan.

### Synonym
*Zanthoxylum dissitum* Hemsley var. *spinulosum* Z. M. Tan; *Z. ovalifolium* Wight var. *spinifolium* (Rehder & E. H. Wilson) C. C. Huang.
