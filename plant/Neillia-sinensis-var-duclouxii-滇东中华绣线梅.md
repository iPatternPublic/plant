滇东中华绣线梅** c.**Neillia sinensis var. duclouxii** (Cardot ex J. E. Vidal) T. T. Yü

> [Rosaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Rosaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Rosaceae.pdf)>>[Neillia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Neillia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Neillia.pdf)

## Description

Leaf blade lobed; lobes obtuse or acute at apex. Pedicels 1–2 mm. Hypanthium 7–8 mm, abaxially sparsely glandular. Sepals acute at apex.
> **Habait** : 
> About 2000 m. NE Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Neillia thibetica* var. *duclouxii* Cardot ex J. E. Vidal, Adansonia 3: 163. 1963.
