鸢尾蒜 (原变种) **Ixiolirion tataricum var. tataricum**

> [Amaryllidaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Amaryllidaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Amaryllidaceae.pdf)>>[Ixiolirion](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ixiolirion?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Ixiolirion.pdf)

## Description

Perianth azure to dark violet, segments 1--7 mm wide, not connivent proximally into a pseudotube. Fl. Apr--Jun.


> **Habait** : 
> Valleys, sandy places, waste grasslands. N Xinjiang [Afghanistan, Kazakhstan, Pakistan, Russia, Turkmenistan].

### Synonym
* Amaryllis tatarica *Pallas, Reise Russ. Reich. 3: 727. 1776.
