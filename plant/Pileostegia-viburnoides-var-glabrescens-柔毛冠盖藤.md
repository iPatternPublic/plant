## Description

Branchlets, leaves, and inflorescences sparsely stellate pubescent. Fl. May.

> **Habait** : 
>* 800-1000 m. Hainan.

### Synonym
*Pileostegia tomentella* Handel-Mazzetti var. *glabrescens* C. C. Yang, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 20: 477. 1982.
