## Description

Leaf blade oblong, ovate-oblong, narrowly elliptic, or lanceolate, 5–12(–15) × 1.5–4(–6) cm, papery, margin glandular serrate. Fruit not seen.


> **Habait** : 
> Woods by streams, valleys, slopes, roadsides; 100–2500 m. Taiwan, Zhejiang.
