## Description

Glumes hispid; anthers usually 0.4–0.6 mm.


> **Habait** : 
> Streamsides, moist meadows; below 3000 m. NE China [E Russia].

### Synonym
*Beckmannia hirsutiflora* (Roshevitz) Probatova; *B. sy-zigachne* subsp. *hirsutiflora* (Roshevitz) Tzvelev.
