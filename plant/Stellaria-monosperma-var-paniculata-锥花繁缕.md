锥花繁缕 **Stellaria monosperma var. paniculata** (Edgeworth) Majumdar

> [Caryophyllaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Caryophyllaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Caryophyllaceae.pdf) >> [Stellaria](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Stellaria?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Stellaria.pdf)
## Description

Leaves variously shaped, oblong-lanceolate to elliptic, adaxially rough, margin usually glabrous, base narrowed into short or long petiole, apex long acuminate. Female flowers small. Sepals lanceolate, ca. 4 mm. Petals 1/2--1 × as long as sepals. Stamens 5.

> **Habait** : 
> Mixed forests, grassland slopes; 1500--2900(--3300) m. Xizang, Yunnan [Afghanistan, Bhutan, India, Kashmir, Myanmar, Nepal, Sikkim, Thailand, Vietnam].

### Synonym
*Stellaria paniculata* Edgeworth, Trans. Linn. Soc. London 20: 35. 1846.

叶片形状多变化，长圆状披针形至椭圆形，顶端通常长渐尖，基部渐狭，下延成柄状，边缘通常无毛，上面及边缘粗糙；叶柄长或短。雌花通常较小；萼片披针形，长约4毫米；花瓣比萼片稍短或为其1/2；雄蕊5。

产云南（除滇中外）、西藏（聂拉木、波密、察隅、墨脱）。生于海拔1500-2850(-3300)米的杂木林内或草坡上。印度、尼泊尔、锡金、不丹、缅甸、泰国、越南也有。
