须药藤属 **Stelmocrypton** Baillon

> [Asclepiadaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Asclepiadaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Asclepiadaceae.pdf) >> [Stelmocrypton](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Stelmocrypton?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Stelmocrypton.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> One species: India and China.

## Description

Lianas. Cymes extra-axillary, short pedunculate. Sepals stout; basal glands 5. Corolla &amp;#177; campanulate; tube short; lobes overlapping to right. Corona lobes 5, short, ovate, inserted at base of corolla. Stamens inserted at base of corolla; filaments free, broad at base; anthers oblong-ovate, pilose on back, long pilose at apex, exserted; pollen carriers spatulate, pollen tetrads in masses, translator oblong, retinaculum ovoid. Styles short; stigma head slightly projected. Follicles divaricate.
