## Description

Leaves rather narrow, elliptic-oblong or oblong-lanceolate, pilosulous abaxially. Calyx densely grayish white hirsute, &amp;#8209;strigose, -tomentulose, or -strigillose. Fl. Mar-Apr, fr. Apr-Jul.

### Statistics
● Forests or thickets in ravines or on hill slopes; below 100-600 m. Guangdong, Guangxi, Hainan.

### Synonym
*Wendlandia chinensis* Merrill, Philipp. J. Sci. 15: 257. 1919.

**15b. 中华水锦树（亚种）（中国高等植物图鉴）黄麖木（海南）**

subsp. chinensis (Merr.) Cowan in Not. Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 16: 288. 1932 et 18: 185. 1934, pro parte: How in Sunyatsenia 7 (1-2): 50. 1948, pro parte; 海南植物志3: 292. 1974; 中国高等植物图鉴4: 203. 1975; W. C. Chen in Acta Phytotax. Sinica 21 (4): 392. 1983.——W. chinensis Merr. in Philip. Journ. Sci. 15: 257. 1919.

本亚种与原亚种不同的是叶通常较狭，常为长圆形或长圆状披针形，叶下面被疏柔毛。花期3-4月，果期4-7月。

产于广东、广西、海南；生于山坡、山谷溪边、丘陵的林中或灌丛中，海拔65-540米。模式标本采自广东高州县石角圹。
