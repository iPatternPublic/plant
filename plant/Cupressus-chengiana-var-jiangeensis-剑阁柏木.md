## Description

Branchlets subterete. Seed cones oblong-ovoid; cone scales 10-14.

> **Habait** : 
>*  About 800 m. N Sichuan (Jiange Xian)

### Synonym
*Cupressus jiangeensis* N. Zhao, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 18: 210. 1980.
