## Description

Spike rachis easily disarticulating. Glumes adnate to floret, very hard, distinctly keeled. Fl. and fr. Apr–Aug. 2*n* = 42*.


> **Habait** : 
>* Cultivated for food in SW Yunnan; 1500–3000 m.
