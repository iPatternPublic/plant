## Description

Sepals 0.9--1.3 cm, ca. 2 × as long as petals.

> **Habait** : 
>* Forests, shaded places in valleys; 500--2000 m. Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, S Shaanxi, Sichuan.

### Synonym
*Coptis teeta* var. *chinensis * (Franchet) Finet & Gagnepain.
