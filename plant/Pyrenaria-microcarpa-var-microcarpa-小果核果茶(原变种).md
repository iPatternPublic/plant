小果核果茶(原变种) **Pyrenaria microcarpa var. microcarpa**

> [Theaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Theaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Theaceae.pdf) >> [Pyrenaria](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pyrenaria?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Pyrenaria.pdf)
## Description

Leaf blade elliptic, oblong-elliptic, or oblanceolate, apex acuminate to acute. Fl. Apr-Jul, fr. Aug-Nov.


> **Habait** : 
> Forests in mountain valleys or along streams; 300-1000 m. Anhui, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hainan, Jiangxi, Taiwan, Zhejiang [Japan (Ryukyu Islands), Vietnam].
