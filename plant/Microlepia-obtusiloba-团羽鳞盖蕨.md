> **Comment** : 
> Fraser-Jenkins (Taxon. Revis. Indian Subcontinental Pteridophytes, 85. 2008) included *Microlepia taiwaniana* as a synonym of the C Indian species *M. hallbergii* (d’Almeida) C. Christensen, the inclusion of which would extend the distribution significantly westward.*Microlepia subpinnata* Hayata (Icon. Pl. Formosan. 4: 209. 1914), described from Taiwan, was treated as a synonym of *M. hainanensis* in FRPS (2: 224. 1959).

## Description

Plants terrestrial. Rhizome creeping, ca. 4 mm in diam., with dense red-brown bristles. Fronds remote; stipe brown-straw-colored, ca. 40 cm, ca. 3 mm in diam., with spreading, gray, long, acicular hairs, abscising to leave scabrous marks; rachis and rachillae with spreading brown bristles; lamina brown-green when dried, 3-pinnatifid to 3-pinnate at base, 2-pinnate at middle, narrowly ovate in outline, 40-60 × 15-30 cm, thickly papery, glabrous adaxially, costa and veinlets abaxially with brown long bristles, apex long acuminate; pinnae upswept, with stalk ca. 10 mm, lanceolate in outline, 10-20 × 2-5 cm, basal pair longest, base symmetrical, rounded-cuneate, apex acuminate; pinnules ca. 9 pairs, oblong, ca. 2.5 × 1.2 cm, with thick teeth, base asymmetrical, basiscopically cuneate, acroscopically erect-truncate and parallel to pinna rachis, slightly auriculate, margin pinnatisect nearly to costule, apex obtuse; ultimate lobules 3 or 4 pairs, oblong, apex rounded, with few teeth; pinnae progressively shorter toward apex, merging to form long acuminate terminal segment. Veins obvious on both surfaces, 2 or 3 pairs in ultimate lobules, upswept, simple. Sori orbicular, small, at base of notch on lobules; indusium brown, hemitelioid, hairy.

> **Habait** : 
> Near streams in forests; below 100-1200 m. Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hainan, Taiwan, Yunnan [N Vietnam].

### Synonym

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第2卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/2) >> 225页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/2/225.PDF)
**26.团羽鳞盖蕨**
Microlepia obtusiloba Hay. in Bot. Mag. Tokyo XXII 27; C. Chr. Ind. Fil. Suppl. I 51——Microlepia pseudo-strigosa var. tripinnata Tard.-Blot et C. Chr. in Fl. Indo-Chine VII, u 96——Microlepia parastrigosa Ching in Bull. Fan Mem. Inst. Biol. new ser. I 302.
根状茎横走，粗红4毫米，密被暗红棕色刚毛。叶远生，柄长40厘米，粗约3毫米，棕禾稈色，通体有开展的灰色长针状毛并有粗糙的痕；叶片长40-45厘米，宽约2.2厘米，长卵形，先端急长渐尖头，并为一回羽状；下部为三回羽状深裂，中部为二回羽状，基部一对较长，斜向上，有柄（长约1厘米），长达20厘米，宽约5厘米，披针形，渐尖头，基部相等，圆楔形，二回羽状深裂；羽状深裂的小羽片在一回羽状的渐尖头顶部约为9对，长2.5厘米，宽1.2厘米，长圆形，钝头，有粗齿牙，基部不对称，下侧楔形，上侧直截井与羽轴并行，略呈耳状，羽状深裂几达小羽轴；末回裂片3-4对，长圆形，圆头，有少数齿牙。上部的羽片为披针形，宽约2厘米，一回羽状，小羽片为长圆形，圆钝头，密接。叶脉两面明显，在末回裂片为2-3对，斜向一上，单一。叶为厚纸质，干后为褐色，上面无毛，下面沿中脉及小脉有相当多的棕色长刚毛；叶轴及羽轴上有较多开展的棕色刚毛。孢子囊群圆形，小，生于裂片上侧的缺刻底部，每裂片一个，基部，上侧裂片较大，常有2个；囊群盖杯形，棕色，有毛。
产台湾、云南东南部（屏边县大围山）及西南部。越南北部接近云南交界处也有分布。 
台湾鳞盖蕨 Microlepia taiwaniana Tagawa in Acta Phytotax. et Geobot. X 199. 未见标本。据说同本种有关系，但按其原靓载：中部的羽片长达50厘米，宽15厘米，羽柄长1厘米，小羽片羽状深裂……的情况看，同本种并无共同之点。
