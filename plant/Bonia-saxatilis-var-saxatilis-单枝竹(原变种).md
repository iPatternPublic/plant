单枝竹**(原变种) **Bonia saxatilis var. saxatilis**

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf)>>[Bonia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Bonia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Bonia.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> The culms are used for papermaking.

## Description

Culm sheaths shortly tomentose, also with longer, stiff, dull brown hairs.

> **Habait** : 
>* Limestone mountains. Guangdong, Guangxi.

### Synonym
*Monocladus saxatilis* L. C. Chia et al., Acta Phytotax. Sin. 26: 213. 1988.
