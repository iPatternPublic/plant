## Description

Umbels 1-flowered. Capsule 1.4-2.4 cm in diam.

> **Habait** : 
> Cultivated in Fujian, Guangxi, Guizhou, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, and Zhejiang [native to extreme SE Australia and Tasmania].
