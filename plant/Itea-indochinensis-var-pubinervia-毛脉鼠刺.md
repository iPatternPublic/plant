## Description

Leaf blade abaxially barbellate only at vein axils.

> **Habait** : 
>* Sparse forests, streamsides, roadsides; 1000-2100 m. Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Itea chinensis* var. *pubinervia* H. T. Chang, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 2: 126. 1953.
