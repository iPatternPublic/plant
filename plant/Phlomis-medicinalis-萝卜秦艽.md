萝卜秦艽 **Phlomis medicinalis** Diels

> [Lamiaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Lamiaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Lamiaceae.pdf) >> [Phlomis](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Phlomis?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Phlomis.pdf)
## Description

Herbs perennial. Stems 20-75 cm tall, purple-red, stellate pilose. Petiole of basal leaves 6-23 cm, others 0.8-7 cm; basal leaf blades ovate to ovate-oblong, 4.5-14 &amp;#215; 4-11 cm, base deeply cordate, margin coarsely crenate, apex rounded; stem leaf blades 5-6 &amp;#215; 2.5-4 cm, adaxially strigose, abaxially densely stellate pubescent, base &amp;#177; truncate, margin coarsely dentate, apex acute or obtuse, longer than verticillasters. Verticillasters 1-4, many flowered, widely spaced; bracts linear-subulate, 6-10 mm, spinescent, ciliate, glandular puberulent, outer bracts reflexed, others &amp;#177; spreading. Calyx tubular-campanulate, ca. 9 &amp;#215; 5 mm, sparsely stellate puberulent, bristly outside; teeth triangular, ca. 2 mm, margin puberulent, double dentate, tufted villous inside, apical spines 3-5 mm, obliquely reflexed or spreading. Corolla purple-red or reddish, ca. 2 cm, base glabrous; limb densely stellate tomentose, silky outside, villous annulate inside; upper lip ca. 1 cm, margin irregularly denticulate, bearded inside; lower lip spreading, ca. 8 &amp;#215; 6 mm, red lineate; middle lobe obovate, ca. 5 mm in diam.; lateral lobes small, broadly ovate. Posterior filament appendages reflexed, lingulate, at base above annulus. Nutlets apex scaly. Fl. May-Jul.

> **Habait** : 
>* Slopes; 1700-3600 m. Sichuan, Xizang.

### Synonym
*Phlomis wangii* Hu & Tsai.

**17. 萝卜秦艽 白秦艽（四川）**
Phlomis medicinalis Diels in Engler, Bot. Jahrb. 29: 554. 1900; Dunnn in Notes Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 6: 186. 1915; Kudo in Mem. Fac. Sci. Agr. Taihoku Univ. 2: 216. 1929; Hand. -Mazz. in Act. Hort. Gothob. 13: 346. 1939——Phlomis wangii Hu et Tsai, 静生汇报2: 261. 1931——Phlomis tuberosa auct. non. Linn.: Hand. -Mazz. in l. c. 9: 81. 1934, p. p.
多年生草本。茎高20-75厘米，具分枝，不明显的四棱形，常染紫红色，被星状疏柔毛。墓生叶卵形或卵状长圆形，长4.5-14厘米，宽4-11厘米，先端圆形，基部深心形，边缘为粗圆齿，茎生叶卵形或三角形，长5-6厘米，宽2.5-4厘米，先端急尖或钝，基部浅心形至几截形，边缘为不整齐的圆牙齿，苞叶卵状披针形至狭菱状披针形，长3.2-9厘米，宽1.8-3.5厘米，先端渐尖，基部截状阔楔形至截形，边缘为粗牙齿状，超过花序许多，叶片均上面橄榄绿色，被糙伏毛，下面较淡，密被星状短柔毛，基生叶叶柄长6-23厘米，茎生叶叶柄长0.8-7厘米，苞叶叶柄长0.7-2厘米。轮伞花序多花，通常1-4个生于主茎及分枝上部，彼此分离；苞片线状钻形，先端刺状，长6-10毫米，外部的常倾斜向下，其余的平展或斜向上，被具节缘毛及腺微柔毛。花萼管状钟形，长约9毫米，宽约5毫米，外面疏被星状微柔毛及极疏的具节刚毛，齿因先端作三角状凹陷至基部而形成每一齿间有2个三角状小齿，此小齿长约2毫米，先端丛生长柔毛，边缘被微柔毛，在萼齿先端凹陷处仅存有长3-5毫米的常斜向下或平展的刺尖。花冠紫红色或粉红色，长约2厘米，外面在唇瓣及冠筒近喉部密被星状绒毛及绢毛，余部无毛，内面在冠筒下部1/3具斜向间断的毛环，冠檐二唇形，上唇长约1厘米，边缘具不整齐的齿缺，自内面被髯毛，下唇平展，长约8毫米，宽约6毫米，具红条纹，3圆裂，中裂片倒卵形，直径约5毫米，侧裂片阔卵形，较小。后对雄蕊花丝基部在毛环上具舌状向上反折的附属器。小坚果顶端被微鳞毛。花期5-7月。
产四川西部，西藏东部；生于山坡上，海拔1700-3600米。模式标本采自四川理县。
