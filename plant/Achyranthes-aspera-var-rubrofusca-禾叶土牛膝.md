禾叶土牛膝 **Achyranthes aspera var. rubrofusca** (Wight) J. D. Hooker

> [Amaranthaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Amaranthaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Amaranthaceae.pdf)>>[Achyranthes](Achyranthes-牛膝属.md) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Achyranthes.pdf)

## Description

Leaves brownish red when dried, elliptic-ovate, 2-9 × 1-4 cm, both surfaces hairy, densely so abaxially, base cuneate, margin slightly undulate, apex acute.

> **Habait** : 
> Roadsides. Fujian, Hunan, Taiwan, Yunnan [India].

### Synonym
*Achyranthes* *rubrofusca* Wight, Icon. Pl. Ind. Orient. 5(2): 3, t. 1778. 1852.
