## Description

Small trees. Branchlets subverticillate, glabrous. Leaves alternate or clustered at apex of branchlet; petiole 1-1.5 cm, glabrous; leaf blade ovate-lanceolate to lanceolate, 6-9 × 1.8-2.5 cm, glabrous on both surfaces, pinninerved or subtriplinerved, lateral veins 5 pairs, base obtuse or cuneate, apex acuminate to caudate-acuminate. Umbels axillary or lateral, sessile or subsessile, male umbel 4-flowered. Pedicel 4-5 mm. Perianth segments 4, rotund. Male flowers: fertile stamens 6; filaments pubescent at base, of 3rd whorls each with 2 reniform glands at base; rudimentary pistil ovoid. Fruit ovoid, 7-10 mm; fruiting pedicel ca. 1 cm, pubescent to glabrous. Fr. Oct-Nov.

### Statistics
● Evergreen broad-leaved forests on high mountains. Taiwan.

### Synonym
*Tetradenia acuminatissima* Hayata, Icon. Pl. Formosan. 3: 166. 1913.

Neolitsea acuminatissima (Hay.) Kanehira et Sasaki in Trans. Nat. Hist. Soc. Formosa 20: 381. 1930; Kanehira, Formos. Trees 224. f. 168. 1936; H. L. Li, Woody Fl. Taiwan 219. 1963; 刘业经, 台湾木本植物志160. 1972; C. E.Chang in Fl. Taiwan 2: 449, Pl. 369. 1976.——Tetradenia acuminatissima Hay.Icon. Pl. Formos. 3: 166. 1913; 5: 172. f. 61d. 1915.

小乔木。小枝纤细，近轮生，无毛。叶互生或聚生于枝顶成轮生状，卵状披针形至披针形，长6-9厘米，宽1.8-2.5厘米，先端渐尖至尾状渐尖，基部钝或楔形，薄革质，两面均无毛，羽状脉或近离基三出脉，侧脉约5条，纤细，中脉与侧脉在叶上面隆起，在下面微突或近于平滑；叶柄长1-1.5厘米，纤细，无毛。伞形花序腋生或侧生，无总梗或近于无梗；苞片近圆形，外面有短柔毛；每一雄花序有雄花4朵；花梗长4-5毫米；花被裂片近圆形，长2.5毫米，外面有柔毛，内面无毛；能育雄蕊6，花丝长2毫米，基部有柔毛，第三轮基部的腺体肾形；退化子房卵形，近无毛或有稀疏柔毛，花柱下弯，无毛，柱头宽漏斗形；每一雌花序有雌花3-4朵；花被裂片卵状长圆形，长2毫米，外面有柔毛；退化雄蕊基部有柔毛；子房倒卵形，花柱有毛，柱头斜2裂。果球状卵形，长7毫米；果梗长约1厘米，初时被柔毛，以后脱落变无毛。

产我国台湾。为台湾樟科植物分布海拔最高的一种，生于常绿阔叶林中。
