## Description

Culm nodes only slightly swollen. Culm sheath densely setose; auricles falcate; oral setae prominent. Leaf sheath auricles prominent; oral setae absent or many.

> **Habait** : 
> Coniferous and mixed subalpine forests; 2500–2900 m. S Xizang [Bhutan, NE and NW India, Nepal].
