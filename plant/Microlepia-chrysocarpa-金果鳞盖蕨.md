## Description

Plants terrestrial, 60-80 cm tall. Rhizome long creeping, 3-4 mm in diam., with dense, long, brown-red hairs. Fronds distant; stipe brown, slightly shiny, 32-40 cm, as thick as rhizome, base with dense long brown-red hairs, distally with short hairs; rachis and rachillae with dense short hairs; lamina brown-green when dried, 3-pinnatisect, oblong-ovate in outline, 35-40 × 10-15 cm, herbaceous, both surfaces glabrous, small rachillae sparsely shortly hairy, apex acuminate; pinnae 14-17 pairs, alternate, usually overlapping, obliquely spreading, with stalk ca. 5 mm, 2-pinnatisect, broadly lanceolate in outline, 10-12 × 4-5 cm, base unequal, basiscopically cuneate, acroscopically truncate, apex acuminate; pinnules 14-17 pairs, adjacent, obliquely spreading, shortly stalked, pinnatisect, oblong-lanceolate, 2-2.5 × 0.7-1 cm, base unequal, basiscopically cuneate, acroscopically truncate and parallel to rachis, apex acuminate; pinnules 6 pairs, compact, obliquely spreading, oblong, 5-7 × 3-4 mm, margin crenate, apex obtuse. Veins in lobules pinnate, 4 or 5 pairs, veinlets furcate. Sori small, distant from margin, terminal on one of furcate veinlets; indusium brown, hemitelioid, almost glabrous.


> **Habait** : 
>● Wet limestone areas; 300-500 m. Chongqing, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hunan.

**13.金果鳞盖蕨　黄胞鳞蕨（中国蕨类植物图谱，第一卷）**

Microlepia chrysocarpa Ching in Sinensia I 3; Ic. Fil. Sinic. I t. 18; C. Chr. Ind. Fil. Suppl. III 127.

植株高60厘米。根状茎长而横走，直径3-4毫米，密被褐红色长毛。叶远离；叶柄长32-40厘米，粗与根状茎同，褐色，稍有光泽，基部密被褐红色长毛，上部被短毛；叶片长圆卵形，长35-42厘米，宽10-12厘米，先端渐尖，三回羽状深裂；羽片14－17对，互生，接近，常呈复瓦状，斜展，有柄（长约5毫米），阔披针形，长10-12厘米，宽4-4.5厘米，先端渐尖，基部不等，上侧截形，下侧楔形，二回羽状深裂；一回小羽片14－17对，接近，斜展，有小短柄，长圆披针形，长2-2.5厘米，宽7－10毫米，先端渐尖，基部不等，上侧截形而与羽轴并行，下侧楔形，羽状深裂；小裂片6对，密接，斜展，长圆形，长5-7毫米，宽3-4毫米，先端钝，边缘有圆齿状齿牙。叶脉在小裂片上羽状，4－5对，细脉叉分，两面明显。叶草质，干后呈褐绿色；叶轴及羽轴密被短毛，叶两面光滑无毛，惟小羽轴上下两面略被短毛。孢子囊羣小形，每小裂片上4-6枚，顶生于分又细脉的向顶的一条上；囊羣盖棕色，浅杯状，几无毛，离叶缘远。

产广西西部（河池）及西北部（罗城）。生石灰岩脚阴湿地，海拔300-500米。
