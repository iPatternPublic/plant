## Description

Stems, leaf blade, inflorescences, and calyx sparsely pubescent. Fl. Apr-Jul, fr. not seen from China.

> Reference: 
> Ninh’s varietal combination was not known to W. C. Ko (loc. cit.: 66), who intended to publish it himself but was precluded.Ninh’s varietal combination was not known to W. C. Ko (loc. cit.: 66), who intended to publish it himself but was precluded.

### Statistics
Broad-leaved forests at middle elevations. Yunnan (Hekou) [India, Indonesia, Malaysia, Vietnam].

### Synonym
*Oldenlandia capitellata* var. *mollis* Pierre ex Pitard in Lecomte, Fl. Indo-Chine 3: 137. 1922.

**疏毛头状花耳草 Hedyotis capitellata var. mollis**

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第71(1)卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/71(1)) >> 066页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/71(1)/066.PDF)

**47b. 疏毛头状花耳草（变种）（新拟）**

var. mollis (Pierre ex Pitard) Ko, comb. nov. ——Oldenlandia capitellata (Wall. ex G. Don) Kuntze var. mollis Pierre ex Pitard in Lecomte Fl. Gen. Indo-Chine 3: 137. 1922.

本变种与原变种不同为小枝、花序、花萼和叶下面或两面均被疏短柔毛。花期4-7月。

产于云南河口；生于中海拔杂木林内。分布于印度、越南、印度尼西亚、马来西亚。

**47c. 绒毛头状花耳草（变种）（新拟）**

var. mollissima (Pitard) Ko, comb. nov. ——Oldenlandia capitellata (Wall. ex G. Don) Kuntze var. mollissima Pitard in Lecomte, Fl. Gen. Indo-Chine 3: 138. 1922.

本变种与原变种不同为全株植物均被浓密、浅黄色绒毛。花期几乎全年。

产于云南西双版纳；生于中海拔至高海拔阳光充足的山坡上，少见。国外分布于越南。
