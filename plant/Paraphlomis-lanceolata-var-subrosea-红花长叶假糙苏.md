Paraphlomis lanceolata Hand. -Mazz. var. subrosea Hand. -Mazz. Symb. Sin. 7: 922. 1936; C. Y. Wu in Act. Phytota x. Sin. 8(1): 34. 1959; H. W. Li in ibid. 10 (1): 69. 1965.
