## Description

Flower-bearing part of spikes very condensed, capitate, not longer than 0.5 cm. Calyx tube ca. 7 mm. Petal limb obovate. Fl. Apr.

> **Habait** : 
> Thickets, scrub; 1100-1500 m. SW Yunnan [Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam].
