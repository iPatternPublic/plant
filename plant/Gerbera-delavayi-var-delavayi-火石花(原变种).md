## Description

Leaf blade lanceolate or oblong-lanceolate. Fl. Nov-Feb.

### Statistics
Open places, mountain slopes, forest margins; 1800-3200 m. Sichuan, Yunnan [N Vietnam].

### Synonym
*Gerbera uncinata* Beauverd.
