## Description

Lateral spikelets: lemma apex cuspidate or with very short awn. Fl. and fr. Apr–Jun.


> **Habait** : 
> Roadsides, waste places, as a weed in cultivated fields of *Hor-deum*. Sichuan, Xizang [C and SW Asia].
