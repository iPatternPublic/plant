## Description

Leaf blade ± thin, 10–18 × 4–7 cm. Fruiting pedicel 5–7 mm.

> **Habait** : 
> Foothill mixed forests, thickets on slopes; 900--1200 m. Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan.
