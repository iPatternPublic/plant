草沙蚕属 **Tripogon** Roemer & Schultes

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf) >> [Tripogon](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Tripogon.pdf)

## Description

Perennials, often small. Culms densely tufted, slender, unbranched. Leaf blades mainly basal, filiform to setaceous, usually involute; ligule a narrow membrane fringed with hairs. Inflorescence a solitary unilateral terminal raceme. Spikelets subsessile, biseriate, broadside to rachis, linear to elliptic, laterally compressed, florets 2 to several, rachilla disarticulating above glumes and between florets; glumes narrow, shorter than lemmas or upper glume exceeding lowest lemma, unequal, membranous, 1-veined or upper glume sometimes 3-veined, keeled, acute to emarginate and mucronate, lower glume often with a lobe or tooth on one side; lemmas lanceolate to ovate, membranous, 3-veined, glabrous, lightly keeled or rounded, 2-dentate, midvein produced into an awn, teeth usually also mucronate or awned; palea keels scabrid or ciliolate, often winged. Floret callus bearded. Stamens 1–3. Caryopsis narrow, trigonous to subterete.

### Statistics
About 30 species: Old World tropics, one species in tropical America; 11 species (five endemic) in China.

## Keylist

* 1 Central awn much shorter than lemma; anthers 3——(2)
* ~ Central awn slightly shorter to distinctly longer than lemma; anthers 1–3——(4)

* 2 (1)Lemma midvein extended into 0.2–0.5 mm mucro; lateral veins not extended.——1 [玫瑰紫草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon purpurascens?t=foc)
* ~ Lemma midvein extended into 0.5–2 mm awn; lateral veins extended into 0.2–0.7 mm mucros——(3)

* 3 (2) Culms 5–8 cm; racemes 2–4 cm, purple-brown.——2 [矮草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon humilis?t=foc)
* ~ Culms 10–30 cm; racemes 6–15 cm, gray-green.——3 [中华草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon chinensis?t=foc)

* 4 (1) Anthers 3 (rarely 2)——(5)
* ~ Anther 1——(8)

* 5 (4) Central awn 1.8–3.3 mm; lower glume without lateral lobe——(6)
* ~ Central awn 5–11 mm; lower glume lobed on one side——(7)

* 6 (5) Racemes erect or slightly curved; upper glume 3.5–4.3 mm, apex subacute, mucronate; awn 1.8–3.3 mm.——4 [四川草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon sichuanicus?t=foc)
* ~ Racemes drooping; upper glume 4–5 mm, apex 2-denticulate; awn 3–4 mm.——5 [柔弱草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon debilis?t=foc)

* 7 (5) Plant robust, up to 50 cm; lemmas with lateral awns arising from tips of teeth; anthers 1.4–1.7 mm.——6 [三裂草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon trifidus?t=foc)
* ~ Plant slender, up to 35 cm; lemmas with apical teeth between awns; anthers 0.5–0.9 mm.——7 [岩生草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon rupestris?t=foc)

* 8 (4) Central awn distinctly longer than its lemma, flexuose or reflexed; lateral awns 1–2 mm——(9)
* ~ Central awn slightly shorter to slightly longer than its lemma; lateral awns 0–1 mm——(10)

* 9 (8) Raceme with closely imbricate spikelets; central awn flexuose, at most gently reflexed.——8 [小草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon filiformis?t=foc)
* ~ Raceme with spaced spikelets; central awns all strongly and stiffly reflexed.——9 [长芒草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon longearistatus?t=foc)

* 10 (8) Spikelets their own length apart or slightly imbricate; lemmas 3.3–4.5 mm; central awn 2.5–4 mm.——10 [云南草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon yunnanensis?t=foc)
* ~ Spikelets closely imbricate; lemmas 2.2–2.6 mm; central awn 1.8–2.8 mm.——11 [丽藕草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon liouae?t=foc)

**草沙蚕属 Tripogon**

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第10(1)卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/10(1)) >> 058页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/10(1)/058y.pdf)

**11. 草沙蚕属——Tripogon Roem. et Schult.**

Roem. et Schult. Syst. Veg. 2: 34. 1817.

多年生细弱草本，密丛。叶片细长，通常内卷。穗状花序单独顶生；小穗含少数至多数小花，几无柄，成2行排列于纤细穗轴之一侧，小穗轴脱节于颖之上及各小花之间；颖具1脉，不等长，第一颖较小，通常紧贴穗轴之槽穴，窄狭，膜质，先端尖或具小尖头；外稃卵形，背部拱形，先端2-4裂，具3脉，中脉自裂片间延伸成芒，侧脉自外侧裂片顶部延伸成短芒或否，基盘具柔毛；内稃宽或狭窄，褶叠，与外稃等长或较之为短；雄蕊3；花柱很短。

本属约有30种，多数分布于亚洲和非洲，大洋洲有1种，美洲有2种；我国现有6种。

本属植物多数可作饲料。

模式种：草沙蚕 T. bromoides Roem. et Schult.

* 4 小穗铅绿色，长5-8（10）毫米，含3-5小花；外稃具芒长1-2毫米。——[中华草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon chinensis?t=z)

* ~小穗带紫色，长约5毫米，常有2-3小花；外稃具芒长约0.5毫米。——[玫瑰紫草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon purpurascens?t=z)

* 5 植株高15-30厘米；花序长6-13厘米；第二颖先端2裂，裂齿间伸出长0.5-0.8（1.2）毫米的短芒。——(6)
* ~植株高7.5-12.5厘米；花序长2-4厘米；第二颖先端不裂，具小尖头。——[小草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon nanus?t=z)

* 6 小穗长5-8毫米，第二颖长3.5-4.5毫米。——[草沙蚕（原变种）](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon bromoides var. bromoides?t=z)

* ~小穗长（10）14-22毫米，第二颖长4.5-5.5毫米。  ——[云南草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon bromoides var. yunnanensis?t=z)

* 7 颖下常具有长不及1.5毫米的小苞片而使小穗有三颖片；外稃主芒长5-8毫米，侧芒长1-3毫米。 ——[线形草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon filiformis?t=z)

* ~颖下不具小苞片；外稃主芒长约4毫米，侧芒仅为长约1毫米的小尖头。——[长芒草沙蚕](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tripogon longe-aristatus?t=z)
