> **Comment** : 
> This taxon should perhaps be treated at specific rank.

## Description

Stem prostrate. Leaves compressed, 0.3-1.2 cm × 1.5-2 mm.


> **Habait** : 
> River banks, sandy places on lake shores. Xizang [C Asia (Pamir mountains)].

### Synonym
*Suaeda* *olufsenii* Paulsen, Vidensk. Meddel. Dansk Naturhist. Foren. Kjøbenhavn 6(5): 194. 1903.
