## Description

Leaf blade glabrous except tomentose at margin and apex.


> **Habait** : 
>* Dense forests. Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Jiangxi, Yunnan.

### Synonym
* Alpinia densibracteata* T. L. Wu & S. J. Chen.
