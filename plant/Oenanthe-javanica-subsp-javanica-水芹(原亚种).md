水芹(原亚种) **Oenanthe javanica subsp. javanica** (Batalin) H. Hara

> [Apiaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Apiaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Apiaceae.pdf) >> [Oenanthe](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Oenanthe?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Oenanthe.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> The shoots and leaves comprise the dietary herb “shui qin” of traditional Chinese medicine.

## Description

Plants slender. Umbels 3–5 cm across. Rays subequal, 1–3 cm. Bracteoles linear. Fruit subglobose. Fl. Jun–Jul, fr. Aug–Sep. 2n = 20.

> **Habait** : 
> Marshlands, lakeshores, muddy stream banks, shallow water; 600–3000 m. Throughout China [India, Indonesia, Japan, Korea, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Nepal, New Guinea, Pakistan, Philippines, Russia, Thailand, Vietnam].
