## Description

Panicles 8-15 cm, each branch with only 1 male head. Anthers ca. 0.5 mm. Fruit ca. 3.5 mm.

### Statistics
● Nei Mongol.

### Synonym
Basionym: *Sparganium choui* D. Yu, Bull. Bot. Res., Harbin 12: 257. 1992.
