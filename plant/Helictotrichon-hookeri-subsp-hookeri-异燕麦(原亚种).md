异燕麦(原亚种) **Helictotrichon hookeri subsp. hookeri**

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf) >> [Helictotrichon](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Helictotrichon?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Helictotrichon.pdf)

## Description

Plant 20–50 cm tall. Panicle 4–8 cm, dense, branches usually bearing a single spikelet. Spikelets golden brown.


> **Habait** : 
> Mountain meadows and rocky mountain slopes; below 3500 m. Qinghai, Sichuan, Xinjiang, Yunnan [Mongolia, Russia; North America].

### Synonym
*Avena hookeri* Scribner, True Grasses 123. 1890; *A. ten-toensis* Honda; *Avenastrum asiaticum* Roshevitz; *A. tentoense* (Honda) Kitagawa; *Helictotrichon asiaticum* (Roshevitz) Grossheim.
