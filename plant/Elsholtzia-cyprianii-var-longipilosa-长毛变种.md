长毛变种 **Elsholtzia cyprianii var. longipilosa** (Handel-Mazzetti) C. Y. Wu & S. C. Huang

> [Lamiaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Lamiaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Lamiaceae.pdf)>>[Elsholtzia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Elsholtzia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Elsholtzia.pdf)

## Description

Upper part of stems, branches, and petioles densely white villous.


> **Habait** : 
>* Valleys, open slopes; 1600-2800 m. Sichuan, Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Elsholtzia communis* (Collett & Hemsley) Diels var. *longipilosa* Handel-Mazzetti, Acta Horti Gothob. 13: 357. 1939.
