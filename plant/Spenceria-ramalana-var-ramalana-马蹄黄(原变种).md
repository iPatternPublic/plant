## Description

Flowers 1.5–2 cm in diam. Sepals 4–6 × 1.2–1.8 mm; epicalyx segments 2–3.5 × 1.5–3 mm. Petals 6–10 × 4–6 mm.

> **Habait** : 
> Alpine meadows, limestone mountain slopes; 3000--5000 m. Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan.
