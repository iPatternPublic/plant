狸藻(原亚种) **Utricularia vulgaris subsp. vulgaris**

> [Lentibulariaceae](http://iplant.cn/info/Lentibulariaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Lentibulariaceae.pdf) >> [Utricularia](http://iplant.cn/info/Utricularia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Utricularia.pdf)

## Description

Spur conspicuously shorter than corolla lower lip, ± straight, with internal glands on dorsal surface only. 2*n* = (36-) 40, 44.

### Statistics
Lakes, pools; 2900-3700 m. Xizang [Afghanistan, Kazakhstan, Pakistan, Russia, Uzbekistan; N Africa, SW Asia, Europe].

### Synonym
*Lentibularia vulgaris* (Linnaeus) Moench.
