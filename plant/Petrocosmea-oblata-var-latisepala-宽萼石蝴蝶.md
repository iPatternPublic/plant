## Description

Bracts narrowly triangular, ca. 1.1 mm. Calyx segments triangular-ovate, 2-3 X 1-1.7 mm, glabrous. Staminodes 3. Fl. and fr. Aug-Sep.

> **Habait** : 
>* Limestone soils; ca. 2200 m. NE Yunnan (Huize Xian).

### Synonym
*Petrocosmea latisepala *W. T. Wang, Bull. Bot. Res., Harbin 1(4): 37. 1981.
