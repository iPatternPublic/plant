## Description

Leaves elliptic-obovate, broadly ovate, or suborbicular, apex subrounded, mucronate. Lobes of bracts usually dentate.

> **Habait** : 
>*  Thickets on mountain slopes; 500-2500 m. Anhui, S Gansu, Guizhou, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Shandong, NE Sichuan (Chengkou Xian), Zhejiang

### Synonym
*Corylus heterophylla* var. *cristagallii* Burkill; *C. kweichowensis* Hu.
