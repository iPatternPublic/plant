## Description

Herbs perennial. Rhizomes long, creeping. Stems scapiform. Basal leaves petiolate, exstipulate, palmatiparted. Inflorescence cymose, many flowered; bracts conspicuous. Sepals 5. Petals 5. Stamens 5; filaments short. Disc 10-lobed. Carpels 2 or 3, connate; ovary inferior, 2- or 3-loculed; placentation axile; ovules many; styles 2 or 3, short. Fruit a capsule. Seeds numerous, small.
