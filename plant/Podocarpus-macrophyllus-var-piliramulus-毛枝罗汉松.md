## Description

Crown not columnar; branches spreading or erect-spreading; branchlets densely blackish brown pubescent. Leaf blade 1.7-7 cm ×  2-4.5 mm, apex mucronate.


> **Habait** : 
>* Roadsides. NW Hubei (Zhushan Xian).
