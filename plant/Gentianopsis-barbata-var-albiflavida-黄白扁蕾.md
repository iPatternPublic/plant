## Description

Calyx ca. 1/2 as long as corolla, 3-4 mm in diam., lobes linear-subulate. Corolla pale yellow.


> **Habait** : 
>* Swamp meadows, meadow slopes, *Juniperus* forests; 3200-4100 m. Qinghai.
