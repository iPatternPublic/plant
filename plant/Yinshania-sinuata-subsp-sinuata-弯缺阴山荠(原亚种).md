弯缺阴山荠(原亚种) **Yinshania sinuata subsp. sinuata**

> [Brassicaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brassicaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Brassicaceae.pdf)>>[Yinshania](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Yinshania?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Yinshania.pdf)

## Description

Lowermost leaves always simple. Fruit 5-10 mm. Seeds 0.8-0.9 mm. Fl. Mar, fr. May. 2n = 42*.

> **Habait** : 
>* Dense forests, mountain slopes, rock crevices, shady moist sand; near sea level to 200 m. Anhui, Guangdong, Hunan, Jiangxi.

### Synonym
*Cochlearia sinuata* K. C. Kuan, Bull. Bot. Lab. N. E. Forest. Inst., Harbin 1980(8): 39. 1980; *Hilliella sinuata* (K. C. Kuan) Y. H. Zhang & H. W. Li.
