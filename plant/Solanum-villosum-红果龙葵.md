## Description

Herbs erect, 40-60 cm tall, much branched; pubescence of simple, sometimes glandular hairs. Stems pubescent, often angular. Petiole 5-10 mm, winged; leaf blade ovate to elliptic, 3-7 &amp;#215; 2-4 cm, sparsely pubescent, base cuneate, decurrent, margin entire, sinuate, or coarsely dentate, apex acute. Inflorescences extra-axillary, umbellate; peduncle 1 cm, unbranched. Pedicel 4-6 mm. Calyx 2 &amp;#215; 1-1.5 mm, puberulent; lobes obtuse, less than 1 mm, ciliate, sinuses rounded. Corolla white or purplish, sometimes drying yellowish, 5-7 &amp;#215; 8-10 mm; lobes ovate-lanceolate, ca. 3 mm, ciliate, strongly spreading and reflexed. Filaments 1.5-1.8 mm, pubescent; anthers ca. 2 mm. Style 3-4.5 mm, pilose at base. Fruiting pedicel 1-1.5 cm, pubescent. Berry red, orange, or yellow, often bright, globose, 6-8 mm in diam. Seeds discoid, ca. 1 mm. Fr. Jul-Sep, fr. Sep-Nov.

> **Habait** : 
> Slopes, valleys, near roads, shady places; 100-1300 m. Gansu, Qinghai, Shanxi, Xinjiang, occasionally cultivated in Hebei  [Afghanistan, India, Nepal; SW Asia, Europe].

### Synonym
*Solanum alatum* Moench; *S. humile* Bernhardi ex Willdenow; *S. miniatum *Bernhardi ex Willdenow; *S. nigrum *var. *humile* (Bernhardi ex Willdenow) C. Y. Wu & S. C. Huang.

**4b.矮株龙葵（变种）**
var. humile (Bernh. ) C. Y. Wu et. C. Huang植物分类学报, 16 (2) : 72. 1978. S. humile Bernh. in Willd, Enum. hort. Berol. 236. 1809; Hand.-Mzt. Symb. Sin. 7: 287. 1936.
