钟花清风藤(原变种) **Sabia campanulata subsp. campanulata** (Batalin) H. Hara

> [Sabiaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Sabiaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Sabiaceae.pdf) >> [Sabia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Sabia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Sabia.pdf)
## Description

Pedicel 1.5-3 mm. Petals persistent, green or yellowish green, enlarged to 12 mm at fruiting. Disk shorter than broad.


> **Habait** : 
> Sparse forests on mountain slopes; 2200-2800 m. S Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, N India, Nepal].

### Synonym
*Sabia campanulata* subsp. *metcalfiana* (L. Chen) Y. F. Wu; *S. metcalfiana* L. Chen.
