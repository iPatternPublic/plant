小花马蹄黄 **Spenceria ramalana var. parviflora** (Stapf) Kitamura

> [Rosaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Rosaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Rosaceae.pdf)>>[Spenceria](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Spenceria?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Spenceria.pdf)

## Description

Flowers ca. 1 cm in diam. Sepals 3–4 × ca. 1 mm; epicalyx segments 1.2–1.8 × 1–1.5 mm. Petals 4–4.5 × 2.5–3 mm.


> **Habait** : 
> Alpine meadows; 3000--5000 m. Xizang [Bhutan].

### Synonym
*Spenceria parviflora* Stapf, Bot. Mag. 149: t. 9007. 1923.
