## Description

Lemma densely pilose throughout.


> **Habait** : 
> Frigid high pastures, sandy places; ca. 2700 m. Nei Mongol, Qinghai, Xinjiang [Mongolia].

### Synonym
*Agropyron desertorum* f. *pilosiusculum* Melderis in Norlindh, Fl. Mongol. Steppe 1: 121. 1949; *A. sinkiangense* D. F. Cui.
