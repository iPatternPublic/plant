## Description

Leaves lanceolate to oblong-lanceolate, 1.5-4 × 0.6-1 cm, margin entire, glabrous to sparsely villous.

> **Habait** : 
>* Grassy slopes, forest margins, thickets; to 3200 m. Sichuan, Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Prunella pennsylvanica* Willdenow var. *lanceolata* Barton, Fl. Philadelph. Prod. 64. 1815; *P. vulgaris* var. *elongata* Bentham.

**1c. 狭叶变种**

var. lanceolata (Barton) Fernald in Rhodora 15: 182. 1913——Prunella lanceolata Barton, Comp. Fl. Philoci 2: 37. 1818——Brunella vulgaris Linn. var. elongata Benth. in DC. Prodr. 12: 411. 1848.

这一变种与原变种不同在于叶全缘，披针形至长圆状披针形，长1.5-4厘米，宽6-10毫米，无毛或疏生柔毛。

产云南，四川；生于路旁，草坡，灌丛及林缘等处，海拔达3200米。
