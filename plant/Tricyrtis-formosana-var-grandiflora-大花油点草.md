大花油点草 **Tricyrtis formosana var. grandiflora** S. S. Ying

> [Liliaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Liliaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Liliaceae.pdf)>>[Tricyrtis](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tricyrtis?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Tricyrtis.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> No specimens have been seen by the present authors.

## Description

Stem 45--70 cm. Inflorescence glabrous. Tepals oblong to broadly lanceolate, 3--4 cm.

> **Habait** : 
>* Roadsides; ca. 1500m. E Taiwan (Hualian Xian).
