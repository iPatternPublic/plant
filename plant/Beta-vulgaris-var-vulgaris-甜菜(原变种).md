> **Comment** : 
> The roots are used as a vegetable (red beet).

## Description

Root purple-red, tuberlike, fusiform to globose. Leaf veins purple-red.

> **Habait** : 
> Cultivated mostly.  in Beijing [of cultivated origin].

### Synonym
*Beta* *vulgaris* var. *rosea* Moquin-Tandon.
