## Description

Flowers many in corymbs, rarely solitary, bisexual, longer than subtending leaf.

> **Habait** : 
> Forest margins, alpine meadows, rock crevices near snow line; 3600--4700 m. Shaanxi, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, Sikkim].

产陕西、四川、云南、西藏。生高山草地、高冷林缘、雪线附近石砾间或岩石缝中，海拔3600-4700米。
