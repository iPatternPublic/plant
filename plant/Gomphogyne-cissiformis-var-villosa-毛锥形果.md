## Description

Fruit tomentose.

### Statistics
Forests on mountainsides; ca. 2300 m. S and SW Yunnan (Menghai, Yongde) [NE India (Sikkim), Nepal].

### Synonym
*Gomphogyne cissiformis* f. *villosa* (Cogniaux) Mizushima.
