马肠子树(原变种) **Pentapanax tomentellus var. tomentellus** (C. B. Clarke) Ridley

> [Araliaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Araliaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Araliaceae.pdf) >> [Pentapanax](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pentapanax?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Pentapanax.pdf)
## Description

Leaflets abaxially subglabrous. Inflorescence ca. 30 cm. Styles united to middle.


> **Habait** : 
>● Shaded thickets, along streams, rocky areas, often on limestone; 1200-2600 m. SW Sichuan, Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Aralia tomentella* Franchet, J. Bot. (Morot) 10: 304. 1896; *Pentapanax henryi* Harms var. *larium* (Handel-Mazzetti) Handel-Mazzetti; *P. larium* Handel-Mazzetti.
