## Description

Leaflets tomentose at first, glabrescent, subglabrous when old abaxially.

> **Habait** : 
> Broad-leaved evergreen forests, near water courses; 2000--2200 m. Chongqing (Jinfo Shan), S Sichuan (Leibo Xian).
