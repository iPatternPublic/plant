长阳铁杉 **Tsuga chinensis var. patens** (Downie) L. K. Fu & Nan Li

## Description

Branchlets brownish yellow or brown, 1.5-2 mm in diam. Seed cones ovoid-globose, 2-2.5 × 1.8-2 cm. Seed scales almost square, thick, exposed part smooth and shining.


> **Habait** : 
>* Mountains; 2000-2300 m. W Hubei (Changyang Xian).

### Synonym
*Tsuga patens* Downie, Notes Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 14: 16. 1923; *T. chinensis* subsp. *patens* (Downie) E. Murray.
