> **Comment** : 
> Reviewer M. G. Gilbert is of the opinion that *Microlepia marginata* var. *villosa* is only arbitrarily distinguished from *M. marginata* var. *marginata* and that it would be better to merge these two varieties.

## Description

Fronds 1-pinnate or bipinnatifid, both surfaces densely hairy. Indusium densely hairy.


> **Habait** : 
> Forests, shrublands, usually in limestone regions; 100-1800 m. Anhui, Chongqing, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hainan, Hubei, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [N India, Japan, Nepal, Papua New Guinea, Sri Lanka, Vietnam].

### Synonym
*Microlepia villosa* C. Presl, Epimel. Bot. 95. 1851.
