西蜀丁香 (原亚种) **Syringa komarowii subsp. komarowii**

> [Oleaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Oleaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Oleaceae.pdf) >> [Syringa](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Syringa?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Syringa.pdf)
## Description

Inflorescences usually &amp;#177; compact. Corolla somewhat deep purple-red, red, to lilac-red outside; lobes mostly &amp;#177; erect. Fl. May-Jul, fr. Jul-Oct. 2*n* = 46*, 48.


> **Habait** : 
>* Thickets, woods, near rivers; 1000-3400 m. S Gansu, S Shaanxi, Sichuan, N Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Syringa glabra* (C. K. Schneider) Lingelsheim; *S*. *komarowii* var. *sargentiana* (C. K. Schneider) C. K. Schneider; *S*. *sargentiana* C. K. Schneider; *S. villosa* Vahl var. *glabra* C. K. Schneider.
