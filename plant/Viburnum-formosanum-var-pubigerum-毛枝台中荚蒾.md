## Description

Young branchlets, petioles, and inflorescences densely yellow-brown stellate pubescent. Calyx tube stellate-pubescent.

### Statistics
● Sparse or dense forests, scrub; 100-1000 m. N Guangdong, S Hunan, Jiangxi.
