## Description

Bark yellow-gray, smooth. Petiolules shorter. Samarium 7-8 mm wide.

> **Habait** : 
>●  Mainly in alpine forests. N Taiwan.

### Synonym
*Ailanthus glandulosa* var. *tanakae* Hayata, Icon. Pl. Formos. 4: 2. 1914 [*\"tanakai\"*].
