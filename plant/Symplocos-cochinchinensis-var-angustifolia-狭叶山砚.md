## Description

Shrubs to 2 m tall. Young branchlets green, angled, glabrous; old branchlets brown. Petiole 4--8 mm. Fl. May, fr. Jul-Nov.

> **Habait** : 
> Mixed forests; 300--500 m. Hainan [Vietnam].
