田麻(原变种) **Corchoropsis crenata var. crenata**

> [Sterculiaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Sterculiaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Sterculiaceae.pdf)>>[Corchoropsis](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Corchoropsis?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Corchoropsis.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> *Corchoropsis tomentosa* var*. micropetala* was described as having much smaller flowers, with petals only ca. 3 mm, but otherwise fits here.

## Description

Ovary densely tomentose. Capsule very densely to sparsely hairy.

> **Habait** : 
> Anhui, Fujian, Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hebei, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Shanxi, Sichuan, Zhejiang [Japan, Korea].

### Synonym
*Corchoropsis tomentosa* Makino var*. micropetala* Y. T. Zhang; *C. tomentosa* var. *tomentosicarpa* P. L. Chiu & G. R. Zhong.
