水毛茛**(原变种) **Batrachium bungei var. bungei**

> [Ranunculaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ranunculaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Ranunculaceae.pdf)>>[Batrachium](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Batrachium?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Batrachium.pdf)

## Description

Flower 1--1.8 cm in diam. Sepals 5, 4--5.5 mm. Petals 5, white with yellow base, 5--9 × 4.5--7 mm. Stamens (12--)15--20. Fl. May--Sep.


> **Habait** : 
>* Ponds, lakes, streams; near sea level to 4800 m. Gansu, N Guangxi, Hebei, SW Hubei, S Jiangsu, Liaoning, E Qinghai, Shanxi, W Sichuan, Xizang, NW Yunnan, Zhejiang.

### Synonym
*Ranunculus bungei* Steudel, Nomencl. Bot., ed. 2, 2: 432. 1841, based on *R. hydrophilus* Bunge, Enum. Pl. China Bor. 2. 1833, not Gaudichaud-Beaupré ex Mirbel; *R. hydrocharis* f. *bungei * (Steudel) Hiern; *R. trichophyllus* var. *chanetii * H. Léveillé.
