> **Comment** : 
> About six species: tropics and subtropics of Asia; four species (one endemic) in China.

## Description

Perennial twining or climbing herbs or subshrubs. Leaves pinnately 3-foliolate; stipules and stipels deciduous or persistent; leaf-lets sessile or shortly petiolulate. Raceme axillary; flowers small, paired, clustered, or sparsely arranged; bracts and bracteoles 2, small, acute, persistent. Calyx campanulate; lobes shorter than tube, unequal, upper 2 connate. Corolla usually purple, light purple, or red, longer than calyx, 7-8 mm in diam.; standard ovate or broadly ovate, almost erect, without auricles, longer than other petals; wings narrow, oblique, with auricles, longer than keels and connate with them; keels straight and with blunt auricles and stipe. Sta-mens diadelphous; anthers small, uniform. Ovary subsessile or shortly stipitate; style inflexed, filiform, glabrous; stigma terminal, capitate. Legume linear, compressed, slightly curved, 4-5 cm, shortly stipitate or sessile, 4-11-ovuled, septate. Seeds suborbicular, oblong, or reniform; hilum short, without strophiole.

**宿苞豆属 Shuteria**

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第41卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/41) >> 243页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/41/243.pdf)
**59. 宿苞豆属 Shuteria Wight et Arn.**

Wight et Arn. Prodr. 207. 1834. (nom. cons.), non Shutereia Choisy Mem Soc. Phys. Geneve 6: 485. 1833. (Convolvul.); Benth. et Hook. f., Gen. Pl. 1: 529. 1865; Baker in Hook. f. Fl. Brit. Ind. 2: 181. 1876; Taub. in Engl. et Prantl. Nat. Pflanzenfam. 3(3): 360. 1894; Gagnep. in Lecomte, Fl. Gen. Indo-Chine 2: 402. 1916; Hutch. Gen. Fl. Pl. 1: 448. 1964; Thuan in Adansonia ser. 2, 12:291. 1972. et Aubrev. Fl. Camb. Laos Vietn. 17: 63 1979; Polhill in Polhill et Raven, Adv. Leg. Syst. 319. 1981.

多年生草质藤本，茎缠绕。羽状复叶具3小叶；托叶和小托叶具纵条纹，早落或宿存；小叶具柄或于花序轴基部小叶退化或缩小，无柄或具短柄。总状花序腋生，花小，成对、密集簇生或稀疏；苞片和小苞片均2，小，尖锐，具纵条纹，宿存；萼管钟状，具条纹，裂齿较萼管短，不等，在上部2裂片合生；花冠伸出萼外，红色、淡紫色或紫色，径7-8毫米；旗瓣卵圆形或宽卵形，近直立，无耳，具瓣柄，比其他花瓣长，翼瓣狭小，偏斜，有耳，具瓣柄，比龙骨瓣长并与其紧贴合生；龙骨瓣直而有钝头，有耳，具瓣柄；雄蕊二体（1+9），花药小，一式；子房近无柄或延长具短柄，花柱内弯，丝状，无毛，柱头头状，顶生。荚果线形，压扁，稍弯曲，长4-5厘米，具短果颈或近无，有隔膜，有种子4-11颗，种子近圆形、长圆形或肾圆形，无种阜，种脐小。

约6种，分布于亚洲热带和亚热带。我国产2种2变种，主要分布于南部至西南部。

属模式种：西南宿苞豆 Shuteria vestita Wight et Arn. (typ. cons.)

Thuan于Adansonia ser. 2，12: 299. 1972. 记载了我国云南有Shuteria suffulta Benth. 小叶近圆形，托叶宿存，其根据标本是Forrest 1358号，我们未见到标本，现志于此，留待考订。
