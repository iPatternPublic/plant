## Description

Lianas. Leaves petiolate; leaf blade oblong or obliquely oblong, 15-24 × 5-9 cm, leathery, base obtuse, margin entire, apex acuminate; lateral veins 8 or 9 pairs, ascending, nearly parallel. Peduncles 6-9 cm. Spathe convolute, ca. 8 × 2 cm. Spadix sessile, shorter than spathe, ca. 6 cm, ca. 1 cm in diam. Anthers ovoid, acuminate. Ovary hexagonal-cylindric, ca. 5 mm.

> Reference:

Taiwan 5: 813. 1978) are based on misidentifications of *R. liukiuensis* (see Fl. Taiwan, ed. 2, 5: 686. 2000).
