## Description

Leaf blade base broadly cuneate, margin remotely serrate, adaxially sparsely puberulent. Corolla red.

> **Habait** : 
>* Forests. Sichuan.

### Synonym
*Teucrium tochauense* Kudo, Mem. Fac. Sci. Taihoku Imp. Univ. 2: 296. 1929.
