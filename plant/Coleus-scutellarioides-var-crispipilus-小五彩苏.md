## Description

Middle lobe of upper calyx lip shorter than lobes of lower lip.

> **Habait** : 
> Streamsides, open areas, hills, fields, forests. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Taiwan [Philippines].

### Synonym
*Coleus macranthus* Merrill var. *crispipilus* Merrill, Philipp. J. Sci. 1: suppl. 235. 1906; *C. crispipilus* (Merrill) Merrill; *C. formosanus* Hayata; *C. pumilus* Blanco.
