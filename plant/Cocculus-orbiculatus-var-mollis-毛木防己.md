## Description

Sepals puberulent abaxially.

### Statistics
Sparse forests, shrublands. NW Guangxi, SW Guizhou, Sichuan, S Yunnan [E India, Nepal].

### Synonym
*Cocculus mollis* Wallich ex J. D. Hooker & Thomson, Fl. Ind. 1: 193. 1855; *C. lenissimus* Gagnepain; *C. mokiangensis* W. Y. Lien.
