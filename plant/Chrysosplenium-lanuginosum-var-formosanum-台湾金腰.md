台湾金腰 e.**Chrysosplenium lanuginosum var. formosanum** (Hayata) H. Hara
family : [Saxifragaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Saxifragaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Saxifragaceae.pdf) >> [Chrysosplenium](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Chrysosplenium?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Chrysosplenium.pdf)

## Description

Basal leaf blade ovate to orbicular-ovate, 1-1.8 × 0.7-1.3 cm, both surfaces and margin sparsely villous, base often broadly cuneate, margin 8-10-crenate. Bracteal leaf blade orbicular-ovate, margin obtusely 4-6-crenate. Sepals not ciliate at margin, apex obtuse.


> **Habait** : 
>* Forests. Taiwan.

### Synonym
*Chrysosplenium formosanum* Hayata, Icon. Pl. Formos. 3: 103. 1913.
