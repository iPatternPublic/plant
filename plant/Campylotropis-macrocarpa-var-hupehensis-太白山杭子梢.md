## Description

Calyx lobes almost as long as tube, 2.2-3 mm. Lateral sur-face of legumes pubescent. Fl. and fr. Jun-Oct(-Nov).

### Statistics
● Mountain slopes, thickets, open places, forest margins, forests, valleys; 200-2000 m. Gansu, Guangdong, Guizhou, Hebei, Henan, Hu-bei, Shaanxi, Shanxi, Sichuan, Taiwan.
