> **Comment** : 
> Twelve species: mainly in China, extending to Vietnam, Laos, Thailand, Myanmar, and NE India; 12 species (seven endemic) in China.

## Description

Herbs, epiphytic, monopodial, small to moderate-sized. Stems enclosed by persistent leaf sheaths, rooting mostly from base or lower part. Leaves many, distichous, terete, subterete, or broadly subterete, fleshy, adaxially channeled, jointed and dilated into sheathing base. Inflorescences axillary, racemose, few to many flowered; rachis usually purple; floral bracts much shorter than pedicel and ovary. Flowers usually opening widely. Sepals subsimilar, dorsally usually ± carinate; lateral sepals often slightly larger, oblique. Petals similar to dorsal sepal; lip saccate or spurred, 3-lobed; lateral lobes erect beside entrance of spur, spotted; mid-lobe rather larger, often narrowed and with appendages at base; spur often cylindric and curved, attenuate toward tip, interior usually hispid papillose. Column short, thick, winged, with a very short but distinct foot; rostellum distinct, sometimes reduced, bilobed; pollinia 2, waxy, globose, porate, attached by a common, short, linear stipe to a broad viscidium.




## Keylist

* 1 Leaves broadly subterete, 5-10 mm wide.——1  [大根槽舌兰](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Holcoglossum amesianum?t=foc)
* ~ Leaves terete or subterete, 2-3(-4) mm wide——(2)

* 2 (1)Spur broadly saccate; mid-lobe of lip with an incurved margin, concave or shallowly cucullate at base.——2  [凹唇槽舌兰](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Holcoglossum subulifolium?t=foc)
* ~ Spur cylindric to hornlike; mid-lobe of lip flat, not concave nor cucullate at base——(3)

* 3 (2) Mid-lobe of lip entire or nearly entire, with fleshy and thickened callus at base; spur 7-8(-12) mm——(4)
* ~ Mid-lobe of lip with a denticulate or undulate margin, with crested lamellae at base; spur (8-)12-20 mm——(8)

* 4 (3) Plants erect; inflorescence 6-10-flowered.——9  [滇西槽舌兰](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Holcoglossum rupestre?t=foc)
* ~ Plants pendulous or slanting; inflorescence 1-3-flowered——(5)

* 5 (4) Flowers very small (sepals 9-10 × 4-5 mm, petals ca. 8 × 4 mm), spur unornamented inside.——11  [中华槽舌兰](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Holcoglossum sinicum?t=foc)
* ~ Flowers medium to large, spur hispid papillate inside——(6)

* 6 (5) Leaves 4-8.5 cm × 3-4 mm; floral bracts 5-8 mm.——8  [短距槽舌兰](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Holcoglossum flavescens?t=foc)
* ~ Leaves 20-30 cm × ca. 2 mm; floral bracts ca. 2 mm——(7)

* 7 (6) Mid-lobe of lip semicircular.——10  [维西槽舌兰](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Holcoglossum weixiense?t=foc)
* ~ Mid-lobe of lip rhombic.——12  [怒江槽舌兰](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Holcoglossum nujiangense?t=foc)

* 8 (3) Spur cylindric, not narrowed toward end; mid-lobe of lip ca. 2 × as long as spur.——7  [筒距槽舌兰](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Holcoglossum wangii?t=foc)
* ~ Spur narrowed toward end; mid-lobe of lip nearly as long as spur——(9)

* 9 (8) Mid-lobe of lip suborbicular, purple.——3  [管叶槽舌兰](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Holcoglossum kimballianum?t=foc)
* ~ Mid-lobe of lip ligulate to obovate-rhombic, white——(10)

* 10 (9) Lateral lobes of lip entire; mid-lobe obovate-rhombic; column foot indistinct.——4  [槽舌兰](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Holcoglossum quasipinifolium?t=foc)
* ~ Lateral lobes of lip bilobed, with 2 lobules; mid-lobe ligulate; column foot distinct, 1.5-2 mm——(11)

* 11 (10) Mid-lobe of lip ca. 2 × as long as wide, lower lobule of lateral lobe triangular.——5  [峨眉槽舌兰](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Holcoglossum omeiense?t=foc)
* ~ Mid-lobe of lip ca. 3 × as long as wide, lower lobule of lateral lobe ovate.——6  [舌唇槽舌兰](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Holcoglossum lingulatum?t=foc)

**槽舌兰属 Holcoglossum**

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第19卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/19) >> 420页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/19/420y.pdf)


**164．槽舌兰属 Holcoglossum Schltr.**

Schltr. in Fedde Repert. Sp. Nov. Beih. 4: 285. 1919; Garay in Bot. Mus. Leafl. Harvard Univ. 23 (4): 181. 1972.

附生草本。茎短，被宿存的叶鞘所包，具许多长而较粗的根。叶肉质，圆柱形或半圆柱形，近轴面具纵沟，或横切面为V字形的狭带形，先端锐尖，基部具关节并且扩大为彼此套迭的鞘。花序侧生，不分枝，总状花序具少数至多数花；花苞片比花梗和子房短；花较大，萼片在背面中肋增粗或呈龙骨状突起；侧萼片较大，常歪斜；花瓣稍较小，或与中萼片相似；唇瓣3裂；侧裂片直立，中裂片较大，基部常有附属物；距通常细长而弯曲，向末端渐狭；蕊柱粗短，具翅，无蕊柱足或具很短的足；蕊喙短而尖，2裂；花粉团蜡质，2个，球形，具裂隙；粘盘柄狭窄，向基部变狭；粘盘比粘盘柄宽。

8种，分布于东南亚至越南、老挝、泰国、缅甸、印度东北部。我国有8种。

本属模式种：Holcoglossum quasipinifolium (Hayata) Schltr. (Saccolabium quasipinifolium Hayata).
