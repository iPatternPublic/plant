蔓菁**(原变种) **Brassica rapa var. rapa**

> [Brassicaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brassicaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Brassicaceae.pdf)>>[Brassica](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brassica?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Brassica.pdf)

## Description

Plants biennial. Taproot fleshy, napiform. Basal leaves rarely up to 10, not rosulate or obscurely rosulate; petiole slender, neither fleshy nor winged; leaf blade lyrately pinnatifid or rarely sinuate-dentate. Fl. Mar-May, fr. May-Jul. 2n = 20*.

> **Habait** : 
> Cultivated for its fleshy roots. Throughout China [widely cultivated elsewhere].

### Synonym
*Barbarea derchiensis* S. S. Ying; *Brassica campestris* Linnaeus subsp. *rapa* (Linnaeus) J. D. Hooker; *B. campestris* subsp. *rapifera* (Metzger) Sinskaya; *B. campestris* var. *rapa* (Linnaeus) Hartman; *B. rapa* subsp. *rapifera* Metzger; *Raphanus rapa* (Linnaeus) Crantz.
