迟花郁金香 **Tulipa kolpakovskiana** Regel

> [Liliaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Liliaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Liliaceae.pdf) >> [Tulipa](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tulipa?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Tulipa.pdf)

## Description

Bulb ovoid, 1.5--2 cm in diam.; tunic black, leathery, appressed hairy inside distally. Stem usually glabrous. Leaves 3, spaced, linear-lanceolate, varying in size, usually overtopping flower, 0.5--1.5 cm wide, glabrous, margin crisped. Bracts absent. Flowers solitary or paired, nodding in bud. Tepals yellow, rarely orange-red, ± tinged with violet, oblong to rhomboidal-oblong, 3--6 × 0.5--2 cm. Stamens equal, 1--2 cm; filaments glabrous, gradually attenuate from base. Style very short. Fl. May. 2* n* = 24.


> **Habait** : 
> Semideserts. NW Xinjiang [Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan].
