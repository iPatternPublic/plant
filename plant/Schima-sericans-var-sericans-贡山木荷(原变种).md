## Description

Current year branchlets, petioles, pedicels, and outside of sepals gray puberulent. Fl. Aug-Sep, fr. Oct-Nov.

> **Habait** : 
>●  Forests; 1600-1900 m. SE Xizang, NW Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Schima khasiana* Dyer var. *sericans* Handel-Mazzetti, Anz. Akad. Wiss. Wien, Math.-Naturwiss. Kl. 61: 108. 1924.
