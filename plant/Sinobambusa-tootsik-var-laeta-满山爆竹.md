满山爆竹 **Sinobambusa tootsik var. laeta** (McClure) T. H. Wen

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf)>>[Sinobambusa](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Sinobambusa?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Sinobambusa.pdf)

## Description

Culm sheath ligule truncate, short; blade purple or purple-green. Leaf sheath auricles and oral setae developed; blade abaxially pubescent. Lodicules with a few vertical veins.

> **Habait** : 
>* Fujian, Guangdong.

### Synonym
*Sinobambusa laeta* McClure, Lingnan Univ. Sci. Bull. 9: 63. 1940; *Semiarundinaria tootsik* var. *laeta* (McClure) T. H. Wen.
