## Description

Stem 45--80 cm. Inflorescence pubescent on rachis and pedicels. Tepals lanceolate or oblanceolate, 2--2.5 cm. 2* n* = 26*.

> **Habait** : 
>* Forests, thickets, shaded places; near sea level to 3000 m. Taiwan.
