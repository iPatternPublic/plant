## Description

Petiole markedly pilose; leaf blade cordate-ovate, rarely triangular-ovate, base usually subcordate, margin usually densely serrate, rarely slightly lobed, markedly ciliate. Fl. May–Jun.

> **Habait** : 
> Cliffs on shady slopes; 1000--1200 m. S Hebei (Wu’an Xian).

本变种与原变种不同在于，叶片呈心状卵形，稀三角卵形，大多数基部呈微心形，边缘锯齿常较多而深，稀有时微浅裂，显著具缘毛，叶柄显著被疏柔毛。花期5-6月。

产河北南部（武安）。生阴坡山崖石壁上，海拔1000-1200米。
