二柱繁缕 **Stellaria bistyla** Y. Z. Zhao

> [Caryophyllaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Caryophyllaceae ?t=foc) - [PDF](http://iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Caryophyllaceae.pdf) >> [Stellaria](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Stellaria ?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Stellaria.pdf)
## Description

Herbs perennial. Stems densely tufted, diffuse, purplish, subterete, 10--30 cm tall, dichotomously branched, densely glandular hairy. Leaves oblong-lanceolate, 1--2 cm × 2--10 mm, margin hairy or glabrous, midvein conspicuous, raised abaxially, impressed adaxially, base narrowed, apex acute. Flowers in terminal dichotomous cymes; bracts lanceolate, 1.5--3 mm, both surfaces glandular hairy. Pedicel 0.3--2 cm. Sepals 5, oblong-lanceolate, 4--5 × ca. 1 mm, outside shortly hairy or glabrous, margin broadly membranous, apex acute. Petals 5, obovate, ca. 3 × 2 mm, shorter than sepals, apex 2-cleft to 1/4--1/3 length, base cuneate. Stamens 10, ca. 3 mm. Ovary globose, 1-loculed; ovules 4 or 5; styles 2(or 3), 2--3 mm. Capsule obovoid, ca. 2.5 mm, shorter than persistent sepals, 4(or 6)-valved. Seeds 1(or 2), black-brown, ovoid to obovoid, 1.5--1.8 mm, tuberculate. Fl. Jul, fr. Aug.

> **Habait** : 
>* Dry gullies; 2000--2600 m. Nei Mongol, Ningxia.

### Synonym
*Stellaria bistylata* W. Z. Di & Y. Ren.

**63. 二柱繁缕（植物研究）**
Stellaria bistyla Y. Z. Zhao in Bull. Bot. Research 5 (4): 142. fig. 1. 1985; C. Y. Wu in Act. Bot. Yunnan. 13 (4): 367. 1991. ——St. bistylata W. Zh. Di et Y. Ren in Act. Bot. Bor. -Occid. sin. 5 (3): 231. 1985.
多年生草本，高10-30厘米。茎密丛生，铺散，叉状分枝，近圆柱形，带紫色，密被腺毛。叶片长圆状披针形，长1-2厘米，宽2-10毫米，顶端急尖，基部渐狭，边缘有毛或无毛，中脉明显，上面下凹，下面凸起。二歧聚伞花序顶生，稀疏；苞片披针形，长1.5-3毫米，两面被腺毛；花梗长3-20毫米；萼片5，长圆状披针形，长4-5毫米，宽约1毫米，顶端尖，边缘宽膜质，外面被短毛或无毛；花瓣5，白色，倒卵形，长约3毫米，宽约2毫米，比萼片短，顶端2浅裂至1/4-1/3处，基部楔形；雄蕊10，长约3毫米；子房球形，1室，具4-5胚珠；花柱2（极少3），长2-3毫米。蒴果倒卵形，长约2.5毫米，短于宿存萼，顶端4（稀6）齿裂，含1 (-2) 种子；种子卵形至倒卵形，长1.5-1.8毫米，黑褐色，表面具小瘤状凸起。花期7月，果期8月。
产宁夏、内蒙古（贺兰山）。生于海拔2 000-2 600米的干沟石缝中。模式标本采自贺兰山。
