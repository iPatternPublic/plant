## Description

Leaves abaxially gray pubescent.


> **Habait** : 
>●  Forests or shrublands on hillsides; ca. 1300 m. Hunan, Shaanxi, Sichuan.

### Synonym
*Ampelopsis heterophylla* (Thunberg) Siebold & Zuccarini var. *cinerea* Gagnepain in Sargent, Pl. Wilson. 1: 101. 1911; *A. micans* var. *cinerea* (Gagnepain) Rehder; *Vitis lyjoannis* H. Léveillé.
