## Description

Spike usually branched at base, dense. Glumes obviously shorter than lemma, papery, keeled. Fl. and fr. Jun–Jul. 2*n* = 28.

> **Habait** : 
> Frequently cultivated for food. Beijing, Gansu, Henan, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Xinjiang, Xizang, Yunnan; probably other provinces [cultivated in C and SW Asia and S Europe].
