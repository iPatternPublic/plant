### Statistics
Forests; up to 1000 m. S mainland China, N & C Taiwan; Japan (S Kyushu, N Ryukyus), Vietnam.Name appeared after publication of the family treatment for the Flora of China.

### Synonym
*Entada tonkinensis* Gagnepain, Notul. Syst. (Paris) 2: 60. 1911.
