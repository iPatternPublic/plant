## Description

Terminal leaflets ovate to ovate-orbicular, 3--5.5 × 2.4--4.5 cm, length:width ratio (1--)1.2--1.8(--2.2):1; terminal lobes length:width ratio (1--)1.3--2.4(--3):1. Lateral leaflets ovate to orbicular, 1.8--5.5 × 1.4--4.5 cm. Carpels (2 or)3 or 4(or 5). 2n = 10*.

> **Habait** : 
>* Sparse Cupressus chengiana forests, young secondary deciduous broad-leaved forests, well-developed thickets; 2000--3100 m. NW Sichuan (valley of Ming Jiang).
