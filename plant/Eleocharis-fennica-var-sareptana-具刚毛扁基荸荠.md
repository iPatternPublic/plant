## Description

Perianth bristles 4 or 5, longer than nutlet.

### Statistics
Shallow water at lakeshores; ca. 3300 m. Qinghai, Xinjiang [Kazakhstan, Mongolia, Russia; Europe].

### Synonym
*Eleocharis sareptana* G. Zinserling, Trudy Glavn. Bot. Sada 40: 279. 1929; *E. fennica* f. *sareptana* (G. Zinserling) Tang & F. T. Wang; *E. paradoxa* Y. D. Chen.
