## Description

Leaf blade base truncate-cordate to rounded, apex obtuse. Cymes lax, branched 4 , ca. 35-flowered. Fl. Aug-Sep.


> **Habait** : 
>* Rocks in caves in limestone hills. SW Guangdong (Yangchun Xian).
