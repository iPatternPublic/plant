> **Comment** : 
> Parasitic on species of *Artemisia* Linnaeus.
> Orobanche cernua is highly variable in plant size, division of calyx, size and color of flower, and pubescence of filaments. It requires further studies, and the account adopted here is\nprovisional.

## Description

Corolla pale yellow, tube distinctly enlarged and turned into a rounded curve. Filaments glabrous. 2*n* = 24.
> **Habait** : 
> Grasslands, slopes, forests, roadsides; 500-3000 m. Gansu, Hebei, W Jilin, Nei Mongol, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shanxi, Xinjiang [Afghanistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Mongolia, Nepal, Russia, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan; SW Asia, Europe].

### Synonym
*Orobanche cumana* Wallroth, Orob. Gen. Diask. 58. 1825; *O. bicolor* C. A. Meyer; *O. cernua* subsp. *cumana *(Wallroth) So&amp;oacute;.
