## Description

Leaflets (3--)5(--7), elliptic, ovate, or obovate-oblong, 5--11 × 2.5--7 cm, papery, base round or widely cuneate, margin entire, apex acute, main veins impressed.

> **Habait** : 
> Mixed forests by streams or other shady moist sites; 1800--3000 m. S Xizang, NW Yunnan [Bhutan, Sikkim].
