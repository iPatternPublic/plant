## Description

Fronds 1-pinnate or bipinnatifid, thickly papery or herbaceous, sparsely hairy on both surfaces. Indusium hairy.

> **Habait** : 
> Forests, shrublands; 100-1500 m. Anhui, Chongqing, Fujian, Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hainan, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Taiwan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [India, Indonesia, Japan (including Ryukyu Islands), Nepal, Papua New Guinea, Sri Lanka, Vietnam].

### Synonym
*Polypodium marginatum* Panzer in Christmann, Vollst. Pflanzensyst. 13(1): 199. 1786; *Davallia marginalis* Baker; *D. scabra* D. Don; *Dicksonia marginalis* Swartz; *Microlepia caudipinnata* B. S. Wang; *M. marginalis* Beddome; *M. scabra* (D. Don) J. Smith; *Polypodium marginale* Thunberg, not Linnaeus.
