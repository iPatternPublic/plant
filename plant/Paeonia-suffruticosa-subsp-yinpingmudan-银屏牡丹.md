银屏牡丹 **Paeonia suffruticosa subsp. yinpingmudan** D. Y. Hong

> [Paeoniaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Paeoniaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Paeoniaceae.pdf) >> [Paeonia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Paeonia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Paeonia.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> At present only two individuals of this subspecies are known. The plant in Anhui grows on a cliff and is said by local people to be at least 500 years old; the plant in Henan is cultivated but is said by its owner to have been collected on a nearby mountain during the early 1960s (Hong et al., Acta Phytotax. Sin. 36: 517. 1998).
> For an update on the two entities, see Hong, D. Y. & K. Y. Pan. 2007. Acta Phytotax. Sin. 45: 285-288.

## Description

Flowers single; petals white or pale red-purple. Fl. Apr--May.


> **Habait** : 
>* Cliffs; ca. 300 m. C Anhui (Yinping Shan), W Henan (Song Xian).
