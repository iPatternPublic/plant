 **Crawfurdia sinkuensis** (Marquand) H. Smith

> [Gentianaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Gentianaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Gentianaceae.pdf) >> [Crawfurdia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Crawfurdia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Crawfurdia.pdf)


> **Comment** : 
> Name appeared after publication of the family treatment for the Flora of China.
> Ho Ting-nong examined the specimens of this species (Feng Guo-mei 7891 at PE and Yu Te-Tsun 2022 at PE). The species is similar to *Crawfurdia delavayi* Franchet and *C. gracilipes* H. Smith by having small flowers 2--3.5 cm and short leaf petioles 3--5 mm, but differs as follows:&#x0D;\n<br>&#x0D;\n1a. Inflorescences cymes; calyx tube distinctly split on one side, spathelike, lobes much shorter than tube ... *C. sinkuensis*&#x0D;\n<br>&#x0D;\n1b. Inflorescences 1-, rarely 2-flowered; calyx tube tubular, not split on one side or occasionally split, lobes ca. as long as or shorter than tube.&#x0D;\n<br>&#x0D;\n2a. Calyx lobes ca. as long as tube; corolla 2--2.5 cm ... *C. gracilipes*&#x0D;\n<br>&#x0D;\n2b. Calyx lobes shorter than tube; corolla 3--3.5 cm ... *C. delavayi*
