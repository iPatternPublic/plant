## Description

Stems erect, 15-30 cm tall, simple or branched, winged, glabrous. Leaves alternate, subsessile to short petiolate; leaf blade broadly linear to elliptic, 4-11 X 0.5-2 cm, glabrous, base attenuate, margin serrate to dentate and with ascending teeth tips, apex acute to acuminate. Racemes terminal on main stems and leafy branches, 1.5-4.5 cm; bracts subulate-lanceolate, 3-5 X ca. 1 mm. Calyx glabrous; lobes subulate-lanceolate, 3-4 X ca. 1 mm. Corolla greenish to yellowish or purplish, ca. 3 mm, pubescent; tube as long as limb; limb actinomorphic. Stamens nearly as long as corolla; filaments hairy. Capsule ovoid-globose, 3-4 mm, glabrous. Fl. Jul-Aug.

> **Habait** : 
>* 2000-3000 m. Taiwan (Hualian Xian).

### Synonym
*Veronica formosana* Masamune, J. Soc. Trop. Agric. 2: 241. 1930; *Calorhabdos kitamurae* Ohwi; *Veronicastrum kitamurae* (Ohwi) T. Yamazaki.

**11. 台湾腹水草**

Veronicastrum formosanum (Masamune) Yamazaki, Journ. Fac. Sci. Univ. Tokyo sect. 3. 7: 127. 1957. ——Veronica formosana Masamune, Journ. Soc. Trop. Agr. 2: 241. 1930. ——Calorhabdos formosana(Masamune)Ohwi, Rep. Nov. sp. 36: 54. 1934.

未见到标本，根据文献，与前一种极为相近，区别仅仅是本种植株较矮，高达30厘米；叶无柄，叶片较狭窄；花梗较长，果期长2-3毫米；花萼较长，近4毫米；花冠略短，长近3毫米。

产我国台湾。生山地。

照文献，这个种与四方麻的区别都是量上的，而且这种差异也不明显，因而很可能它们实际上就是一个种的两个亚种，而后面的那个直立腹水草只不过是台湾腹水草的宽叶类型而已。
