## Description

Leaf segments pinnatipartite, lobes irregularly incised again.

### Statistics
Forest understories; 1000-2000 m. Hubei, Shaanxi, Sichuan [Japan].

### Synonym
*Stylophorum japonicum* var. *dissectum* Franchet & Savatier, Enum. Pl. Jap. 1: 27. 1873.
