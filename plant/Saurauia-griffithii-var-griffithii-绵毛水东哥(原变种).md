## Description

Leaf blade broadly elliptic-oblong, ca. 40 × 17 cm, base rounded, symmetrical. Abaxial surface of leaves, inflorescences, and sepals densely tomentose.


> **Habait** : 
> Broad-leaved forests; 600-1200 m. SE Xizang [Bhutan, NE India].
