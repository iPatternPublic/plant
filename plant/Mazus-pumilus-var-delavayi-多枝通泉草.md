## Description

Plants more than 10 cm. Stems numerous, much branched. Petiole of basal leaves shorter than blade. Racemes elongated, 10-20-flowered. Pedicel short, less than 5(-7) mm. Calyx not enlarged in fruit.

> **Habait** : 
> Trailsides, wet grassland; 1200-3800 m. Guangxi, W Sichuan, Yunnan [Bhutan, NW India, Kashmir, Nepal, Sikkim].

### Synonym
*Mazus delavayi* Bonati, Bull. Herb. Boissier 2: 530. 1908; *Mazus japonicus* (Thunberg) Kuntze var. *delavayi* (Bonati) P. C. Tsoong.
