## Description

Leaf blade ca. 30 cm, thick, wrinkled. Syncarp greenish white to purple when mature.


> **Habait** : 
>* Cultivated. Jiangsu, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Zhejiang.

### Synonym
*Morus* *multicaulis* Perrottet, Mém. Soc. Linn. Paris 3: 129. 1823–1824 [“1825”]; *M*. *alba* var. *latifolia* (Poiret) Bureau; *M*. *chinensis* Loddiges ex Loudon; *M*. *latifolia* Poiret.

江苏、浙江、四川及陕西等地栽培。

因叶大，肉厚多汁，为家蚕的良好饲料。
