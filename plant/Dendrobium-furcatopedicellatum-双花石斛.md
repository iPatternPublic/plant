## Description

Stem erect, cylindric, 30-40 cm or longer, ca. 2 mm in diam., upper part with opposite leaves, internodes 3-5 cm. Leaves linear, ca. 11 × 0.4 cm, leathery, 3-veined, base slightly contracted and then dilated into sheath, apex acuminate; leaf sheath tubular, ca. 3.5 cm, tightly embracing internodes. Inflorescence umbellate, lateral, 2-flowered, spreading at right angle outward; peduncle ca. 13 mm, with 1 or 2 basal sheaths. Pedicel and ovary ca. 1 cm. Flowers opening slightly, pale yellow; sepals with purple spots on both sides at middle. Sepals narrowly lanceolate, ca. 3 cm, base ca. 3.5 mm wide; mentum ca. 5 mm, ± curved. Petals ca. as long as sepals, but relatively narrow; lip 3-lobed; lateral lobes erect, small, obtuse; mid-lobe triangular-lanceolate, somewhat large, 1-1.5 cm, margin fimbriate-toothed, apex recurved; disk pubescent.

### Statistics
&amp;#9679; Mountain forests. C and S Taiwan.

### Synonym
*Grastidium furcatopedicellatum* (Hayata) Rauschert.

**2．双花石斛（台湾植物志）**
Dendrobium furcatopedicellatum Hayata, Icon. Pl. Formos. 4: 39, t. 14. 1914; Schltr. in Fedde Repert. Sp. Nov. Beih. 4: 209. 1919; S. Y. Hu in Quart. J. Taiwan Mus. 26 (1, 2): 155. 1973; 台湾植物志 5: 961. 1978; Z. H. Tsi in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 18 (4): 434. 1980.
茎圆柱形，直立，长30-40厘米或更长，粗2毫米，上部互生叶，节间长3-5厘米。叶薄革质，线形，长约11厘米，宽4毫米，先端渐尖，基部稍收窄然后扩大为鞘，具3条脉；叶鞘筒状，长3.5厘米，紧紧围抱于节间。伞状花序侧生，具2朵花，呈90度角向外伸展；花序柄长13毫米，基部被1-2枚鞘；花梗和子房长约1厘米；花稍张开，淡黄色；萼片在中部两侧具紫色斑点，狭披针形，长约3厘米，基部宽3.5毫米，先端丝状卷曲；萼囊长约5毫米，多少弯曲的；花瓣与萼片等长，但较窄；唇瓣3裂，侧裂片小，直立，先端钝；中裂片较大，三角状披针形，长1-1.5厘米，先端反卷，边缘具流苏状的齿，唇盘被短柔毛。花期5月。
产台湾（中部和南部）。生于山地林中。
本种我们没有见到标本，以上描述来自原始文献和《台湾植物志》。应绍舜在《台湾兰科植物彩色图鉴》 和《台湾兰科植物彩色图志》 中认为本种与大洋洲的 Dendrobium biflorum Sw. 同种，我们认为尚须进一步研究。
