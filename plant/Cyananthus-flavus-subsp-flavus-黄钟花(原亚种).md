## Description

Leaves broadly ovate, apex obtuse. Flowers yellow, pale yellow, or greenish yellow.

### Statistics
● Grassy slopes; 3100-3600 m. NW Yunnan (Lijiang, Zhongdian).

### Synonym
*Cyananthus flavus* var. *glaber* C. Y. Wu.
