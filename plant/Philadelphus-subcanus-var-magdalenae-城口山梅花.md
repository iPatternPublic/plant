城口山梅花 **Philadelphus subcanus var. magdalenae** (Koehne) S. Y. Hu

> [Saxifragaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Saxifragaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Saxifragaceae.pdf) >> [Philadelphus](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Philadelphus?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Philadelphus.pdf)
## Description

Shrubs to 4 m tall. Leaf blade ovate, 3-6 × 1-3 cm, abaxially ± appressed villous only along veins. Calyx tube sparsely gray-yellow villous. Petals glabrous. Style apically divided for ca. 2/3 its length. Fl. Jun.


> **Habait** : 
>* Thickets; 1200-2000 m. Hubei, Sichuan.

### Synonym
*Philadelphus magdalenae* Koehne, Mitt. Deutsch. Dendrol. Ges. 13: 83. 1904.
