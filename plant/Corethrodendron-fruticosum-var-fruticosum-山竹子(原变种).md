## Description
 "
Articles pubescent and prickly.

### Statistics
Sandy areas in steppes; ca. 1100 m. Heilongjiang, Jilin, W Liao-ning, NE Nei Mongol [E Mongolia, E Russia].

### Synonym
*Hedysarum fruticosum* Pallas, Reise Russ. Reich. 3: 753. 1776.
