> **Comment** : 
> One species: Cambodia, China, Laos, Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam.

## Description

Trees. Leaves opposite, 2- or 3-pinnately compound; leaflets ovate, entire. Inflorescences cymose-paniculate, terminal. Calyx cupular, small, apex subtruncate, with very short teeth. Corolla bilabiate, tube long and narrow, upper lip 2-lobed, lower lip 3-lobed; lobes valvate, ovate-lanceolate, densely pubescent adaxially along margin. Stamens 4, didynamous, inserted high in corolla tube; 1 anther cell fertile and ellipsoid, another caudate and appendagelike. Disc annular-cupular. Ovary sessile, ovoid. Style long; stigma lingulate, compressed, 2-lobed, slightly exserted from corolla tube. Capsule dehiscing septicidally, long linear, compressed. Seeds in several rows, minute, compressed, winged.

**老鸦烟筒花属 Millingtonia**

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第69卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/69) >> 008页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/69/008y.pdf)
**4．老鸦烟筒花属——Millingtonia L. f.**

L. f. Suppl. 45: 291. 1781; Benth. et Hook. f. Gen. Pl. 2: 1041. 1876; Schum. in Engler u. Prantl, Nat. Pflanzenfam. 4(3b) : 226. fig. 68. 1894; Santisuk et Vidal in Fl. Camb. Laos et Viet. 22: 12. 1985

直立乔木。叶对生，为2-3回羽状复叶；小叶卵形，全缘。顶生聚伞状大圆锥花序。花萼很小，杯状，顶端近平截，具极短小的5齿。花冠呈二唇形，花冠筒细长，檐部上唇裂片2，下唇裂片3，镊合状排列，卵状披针形，内面沿边缘密被极细柔毛。雄蕊4，2强，着生于花冠筒近顶端，花药1室为椭圆形，另一室似一尾状附属体。花盘环状杯形。子房无柄，卵形，花柱细长，柱头舌状，扁平，2裂，与雄蕊微伸出花冠管外。蒴果细长，线形，压扁。种子多列，极细小，扁平具翅。

单种属，分布于我国云南，以及越南、老挝、泰国、缅甸、印度、马来西亚、印度尼西亚。各地多栽培供庭园观赏。
