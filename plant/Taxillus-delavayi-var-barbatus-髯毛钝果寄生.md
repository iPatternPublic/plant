> **Comment** : 
> This taxon is parasitic on species of *Salix*.

## Description

Bracts glabrous, with long-bearded tip. Pedicel 4-6 mm. Corolla lobes 6-9 mm. Anthers 3-4 mm.

> **Habait** : 
>* Forests, mountain slopes; 2000-2700 m. SE Xizang (Nyingchi).
