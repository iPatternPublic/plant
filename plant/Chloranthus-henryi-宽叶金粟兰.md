**Chloranthus henryi** Hemsley 宽叶金粟兰

![](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/illast/Chloranthus%20multistachys.jpg)

* Herbs perennial, 40-65 cm high. 多年生草本，高40-65厘米
* Rhizomes strong, blackish brown, with many slender, long, brown, fibrous roots. 根状茎粗壮，黑褐色，具多数细长的棕色须根
* Stems erect, **1 or several** in a fascicle, evidently 6- or 7-noded; internodes 0.5-3 cm, basal nodes with 1 pair of scalelike leaves. 茎直立，单生或数个丛生，有6-7个明显的节，节间长0.5-3厘米，下部节上生一对鳞状叶。
* Scalelike leaves ovate-triangular, membranous. 鳞状叶卵状三角形，膜质。
* Leaves opposite, usually 4 on apical part of stem; stipules subulate; 叶对生，通常4片生于茎上部，纸质
* petiole 5-**12** mm; 叶柄长0.5-1.2厘米
* leaf blade broadly elliptic, ovate-elliptic, obovate, or suborbicular, 9-18 × 5-9 cm, papery, glandular mucronate, base cuneate to broadly cuneate, margin serrate or crenate, apex acuminate; 宽椭圆形、卵状椭圆形或倒卵形，长9-18厘米，宽5-9厘米，顶端渐尖，基部楔形至宽楔形，边缘具锯齿，齿端有一腺体，背面中脉、侧脉有鳞屑状毛
* lateral veins 6-8 pairs. 叶脉6-8对
* Spikes terminal or terminal and axillary, usually dichotomously or racemosely branched; 穗状花序顶生，通常两歧或总状分枝
* peduncle **(2.5-)10-16 cm**; 连总花梗长10-16厘米，总花梗长5-8厘米
* bracts usually ovate-triangular or nearly semiorbicular. 苞片通常宽卵状三角形或近半圆形
* Flowers white. 花白色
* Stamens 3, **base nearly free**, only insides connected; 雄蕊3枚，基部几分离，仅内侧稍相连
* central connective ca. 3 mm, with a 2-loculed anther; 中央药隔长3毫米，有1个2室的花药
* lateral connectives slightly shorter, each with a 1-loculed anther; 两侧药隔稍短，各有1个1室的花药
* thecae at **base** of connectives. 药室在药隔的基部
* Ovary ovoid; style absent; stigma subcapitate. 子房卵形，无花柱，柱头近头状。
* Drupes globose, ca. 3 mm; stalk short. 核果球形，长约3毫米，具短柄。
* Fl. Apr-Jun, fr. Jul-Aug.花期4-6月，果期7-8月。
* 托叶小，钻形。

* Wet places, thickets, forests; 800-2000 m. Anhui, Fujian, Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hainan, Hubei, Hunan, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Zhejiang

## Description

Herbs perennial, 40-65 cm high. Rhizomes strong, blackish brown, with many slender, long, brown, fibrous roots. Stems erect, 1 or several in a fascicle, evidently 6- or 7-noded; internodes 0.5-3 cm, basal nodes with 1 pair of scalelike leaves. Scalelike leaves ovate-triangular, membranous. Leaves opposite, usually 4 on apical part of stem; stipules subulate; petiole 5-12 mm; leaf blade broadly elliptic, ovate-elliptic, obovate, or suborbicular, 9-18 ×  5-9 cm, papery, glandular mucronate, base cuneate to broadly cuneate, margin serrate or crenate, apex acuminate; lateral veins 6-8 pairs. Spikes terminal or terminal and axillary, usually dichotomously or racemosely branched; peduncle (2.5-)10-16 cm; bracts usually ovate-triangular or nearly semiorbicular. Flowers white. Stamens 3, base nearly free, only insides connected; central connective ca. 3 mm, with a 2-loculed anther; lateral connectives slightly shorter, each with a 1-loculed anther; thecae at base of connectives. Ovary ovoid; style absent; stigma subcapitate. Drupes globose, ca. 3 mm; stalk short. Fl. Apr-Jun, fr. Jul-Aug.
