## Description

Shrubs epiphytic, glabrous except for pedicels and corolla. Stems stout, climbing to 3 m; branches pendulous. Petiole 1-2.5 cm, stout; leaf blade linear to narrowly oblanceolate or oblong, 8-20(-25) &amp;#215; (0.4-)2-3 cm, base narrowly acute or rounded, apex acuminate or acute; lateral veins obscure. Pseudumbels extra-axillary, globose, many flower-ed; peduncle 1.5-5 cm. Pedicel 1.5-3 cm, pubescent. Sepals 1-1.5 mm, apex acute. Corolla white tinged with pink, 2-4 cm in diam.; lobes ovate-triangular, stellate spreading, pubescent. Corona lobes broadly ovate, flat, with a central boss, convex basally, inner angle acute and shorter than anther apices. Follicles straight, 10-30 cm &amp;#215; 5-8 mm. Seeds ovoid, 1-1.5 mm; coma 1.2-1.5 cm. Fl. Jul-Aug. 2*n* = 22.

> **Habait** : 
> Dense forests; 1400-2400 m. Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Kashmir, Nepal, Pakistan, Sikkim, Thailand].

**长叶球兰 Hoya kwangsiensis**

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第63卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/63) >> 488页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/63/488a.pdf)

**19. 长叶球兰（植物分类学报）**

Hoya kwangsiensis Tsiang et P. T. Li in Acta Phytotax. Sinica 12: 122. 1974.

攀援灌木，在树上生根，除花萼裂片有缘毛外，其余无毛。叶薄肉质或革质，长披针形或披针状长圆形，长8-17厘米，宽2-4厘米；中脉及侧脉两面近于扁平，侧脉有时不明显，叶缘略为背卷；叶柄粗壮，上面有槽，长1-2厘米，顶端具少数黑色腺体。聚伞花序具短花序梗，在一侧的叶腋中生出，着花少；花萼裂片卵形，长3毫米，宽2毫米，边缘有缘毛；花冠白色，辐状，长1.5厘米，直径3厘米；副花冠裂片肉质，外角圆形；雄蕊与雌蕊与属的特征相同。花期8月。

产于广西西北部。生长于海拔300米山谷中，攀援在树上。模式标本采自广西天峨。
