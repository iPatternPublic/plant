> [Sapindaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Sapindaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Sapindaceae.pdf)>>[Sapindus](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Sapindus?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Sapindus.pdf)

## Description

Leaf axis and abaxial surface of leaflets glabrous.

> **Habait** : 
> Sparse forests; 500-1700 m. Taiwan, Yunnan [Bhutan, Cambodia, India, Indonesia, Laos, W Malaysia, Myanmar, Sri Lanka, Thailand, Vietnam].

### Synonym
*Dittelasma rarak* (Candolle) Hiern.
