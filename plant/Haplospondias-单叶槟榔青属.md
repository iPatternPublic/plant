## Description

Medium-sized trees. Leaves pedicellate, blade simple. Inflorescence paniculate, terminal. Flowers 4- or 5-merous, bisexual, pedicellate. Stamens 10. Disk shallowly cup-shaped, margin undulate. Ovary 1-locular; style short and thick. Fruit not seen. Fl. Apr.

### Statistics
Two species: tropical Asia; one species in China.
