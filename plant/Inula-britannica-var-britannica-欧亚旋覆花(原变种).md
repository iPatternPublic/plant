## Description

Capitula usually sparsely arranged.

### Statistics
Roadsides, wastelands; 300-1700 m. Hebei, Nei Mongol, Xinjiang [Russia; Europe].

### Synonym
*Conyza britannica* (Linnaeus) Ruprecht.
