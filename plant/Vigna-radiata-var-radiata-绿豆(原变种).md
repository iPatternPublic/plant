## Description

Stems erect. Leaflets entire, acuminate at apex.

### Statistics
Cultivated throughout China [widely cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions].

### Synonym
*Phaseolus radiatus* Linnaeus, Sp. Pl. 2: 725. 1753; *Azukia radiata* (Linnaeus) Ohwi; *Candelium nigrum* Medikus; *P. aureus* Roxburgh; *Rudua aurea* (Roxburgh) Maekawa.
