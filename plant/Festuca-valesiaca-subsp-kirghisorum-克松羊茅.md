## Description

Leaf sheaths on tillers closed 1/6–1/3 their length; leaf blades (0.5–)0.6–0.9 mm wide. Spikelets (7–)8–14 mm, brownish green; lemmas 4.8–6.5 mm.

> **Habait** : 
> Stony hillsides, *Juniperus* forests. ?China [Kyrgyzstan (Tien Shan)].

### Synonym
*Festuca rupicola* Heuffel subsp. *kirghisorum* Kashina ex Tzvelev, Bot. Zhurn. 56: 1255. 1971.
