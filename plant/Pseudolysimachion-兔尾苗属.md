穗花属 **Pseudolysimachion** (W. D. J. Koch) Opiz

> [Scrophulariaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Scrophulariaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Scrophulariaceae.pdf) >> [Pseudolysimachion](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pseudolysimachion?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Pseudolysimachion.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> About 20 species: Asia, Europe; ten species in China.

## Description

Herbs, perennial. Roots glabrous. Rhizomes usually long. Stems 1 or cespitose, base sometimes woody. Leaves opposite or whorled, rarely alternate. Inflorescences terminal, dense racemes or spikes; bracts small, narrow. Calyx 4-lobed; lobes subequal. Corolla 4-lobed; tube rarely less than 1/3 of corolla length, villous inside; limb slightly zygomorphic; upper lobe widest. Stamens 2; filaments adnate to posterior part of corolla tube; anther locules apically confluent. Style persistent; stigma capitate. Capsule subglobose, slightly laterally compressed, apex rounded-obtuse and emarginate, loculicidal. Seeds numerous per capsule, flattened, smooth.

### Synonym
*Veronica* sect. *Pseudolysimachia* W. D. J. Koch, Syn. Fl. Germ. Helv. 527. 1837.

* 1 Leaves all alternate or at least apically on stem (rarely all opposite in *P. linariifolium*).——(2)
* ~ Leaves opposite or whorled (sometimes alternate upward in *P. incanum*).——(3)
* 2 (1)Stems 1, very rarely 2; rhizomes herbaceous; corolla tube ca. 1/3 of corolla length——1  [Pseudolysimachion linariifolium 细叶穗花](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pseudolysimachion linariifolium?t=foc)
* ~ Stems cespitose; rhizomes woody; corolla tube 1/3-1/2 of corolla length——2  [Pseudolysimachion pinnatum 羽叶穗花](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pseudolysimachion pinnatum?t=foc)
* 3 (1) Pedicel absent or less than 2 mm; ovary and capsule apically hairy.——(4)
* ~ Pedicel 2 mm or more; ovary and capsule glabrous (ovary sometimes hairy in *P. kiusianum*).——(6)
* 4 (3) Stems mostly densely white woolly; corolla lobes ovate to ovate-orbicular——3  [Pseudolysimachion incanum 白兔儿尾苗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pseudolysimachion incanum?t=foc)
* ~ Stems mostly villous and green, gray-green, or gray; corolla lobes ovate-lanceolate to lanceolate.——(5)
* 5 (4) Leaf blade deeply cut to dentate; leaf axils often with sterile branches; calyx lobes glandular ciliate; corolla white, lobes apically slightly thickened and reflexed——4  [Pseudolysimachion alatavicum 阿拉套穗花](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pseudolysimachion alatavicum?t=foc)
* ~ Leaf blade crenate or entire; leaf axils without branches; calyx lobes eglandular ciliate, otherwise often glandular hairy; corolla blue, purple, rarely white, lobes apically not thickened and erect to slightly patent——5  [Pseudolysimachion spicatum 穗花](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pseudolysimachion spicatum?t=foc)
* 6 (3) Leaves sessile, amplexicaul at least basally or to middle of stem; corolla tube less than 1/3 of corolla length.——(7)
* ~ Leaves petiolate; corolla tube at least 1/3 of corolla length.——(8)
* 7 (6) Stems usually with white and curly hairs; leaves opposite, petiolate——10  [Pseudolysimachion rotundum 无柄穗花](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pseudolysimachion rotundum?t=foc)
* ~ Stems glabrous or puberulent; leaves alternate, sessile or subsessile——1  [Pseudolysimachion linariifolium 细叶穗花](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pseudolysimachion linariifolium?t=foc)
* 8 (6) Leaf margin deeply cut to pinnately parted; corolla white or pink——7  [Pseudolysimachion dauricum 大穗花](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pseudolysimachion dauricum?t=foc)
* ~ Leaf margin dentate to double dentate or serrate to double serrate, sometimes deeply serrate; corolla purple to blue.——(9)
* 9 (8) Stems glabrous or apically very sparsely villous; leaf blade lanceolate; stamens conspicuously exserted——8  [Pseudolysimachion longifolium 兔儿尾苗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pseudolysimachion longifolium?t=foc)
* ~ Stems pubescent, sometimes densely so, rarely apically glabrous; leaf blade narrowly oblong, deltoid-ovate, ovate-lanceolate, or elliptic; stamens included.——(10)
* 10 (9) Lower leaves subsessile; leaf blade narrowly oblong to elliptic——6  [Pseudolysimachion spurium 轮叶穗花](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pseudolysimachion spurium?t=foc)
* ~ Lower leaves petiole 1-2.5 cm; leaf blade deltoid-ovate to ovate-lanceolate——9  [Pseudolysimachion kiusianum 长毛穗花](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Pseudolysimachion kiusianum?t=foc)
