## Description

Stems, peduncles, abaxial surface of leaves, and involucre densely lanate-villous.

### Statistics
Heilongjiang, Nei Mongol, Xinjiang [Russia].

### Synonym
*Inula britannica* f. *sublanata* (Komarov) Kitagawa.
