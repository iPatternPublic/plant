> **Comment** : 
> About six species: E Asia, North America; four species (three endemic) in China.

## Description

Lianas, deciduous. Winter buds ovoid; scales 3-5. Stipules present. Leaves imparipinnate; leaflet blades opposite, margin entire; stipels present. Racemes terminal, elongate, pendulous; nodes scattered on rachis, with 1 flower. Flowers 1.5-2.5 cm. Corolla stan-dard orbicular, large, reflexed, glabrous or outside with some trichomes at base, with 2 basal calluses; wings free from keel. Stamens diadelphous; vexillary stamen distinct from other 9 or slightly connate at middle of sheath. Ovary stipitate, with trichomes, with 5-16 ovules. Legume linear to oblanceolate, leathery, convex over seeds. Seeds 1-3[or more] per legume, reniform to lenticular, without a strophiole; radicle folded.

> The Japanese species *Wisteria floribunda* (Willdenow) Candolle is commonly cultivated as a garden plant in China.The Japanese species *Wisteria floribunda* (Willdenow) Candolle is commonly cultivated as a garden plant in China.

## Keylist

* 1 Mature leaflet blades glabrescent or with sparse trichomes; corolla purple, occasionally white——(2)
* ~ Mature leaflet blades with trichomes on both surfaces but abaxially especially dense; corolla violet or white——(3)

* 2 (1)Flowers 2-2.5 cm; pedicel 2-3 cm; corolla standard glabrous, apex truncate; abaxial calyx tooth longer than others.——1  [紫藤](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Wisteria sinensis?t=foc)
* ~ Flowers ca. 1.5 cm; pedicel 0.6-1.2 cm; corolla standard outside sparsely villous, apex emarginate; abaxial calyx tooth as long as others.——2  [短梗紫藤](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Wisteria brevidentata?t=foc)

* 3 (1) Racemes 30-35 cm, densely gray villous; flowers 2.2-2.5 cm, blooming in sequence from base to apex along raceme rachis; corolla violet; leaflet blades villous but abaxially especially dense.——3  [藤箩](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Wisteria villosa?t=foc)
* ~ Racemes 10-20 cm, yellow tomentose; flowers ca. 2 cm, blooming almost at same time along whole raceme rachis; corolla white; leaflet blades abaxially sericeous, adaxially appressed pubescent.——4  [白花藤箩](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Wisteria venusta?t=foc)

**15. 紫藤属 Wisteria Nutt.**

Nutt. Gen. Amer. 2: 115. 1818, nom. conserv.; Taub. in Engl. u. Prantl, Nat. Pflanzenf. 3(3): 217. 1894; Rehd. Man. Cult. Trees & Shrubs ed. 2, 506. 1940; 中国主要植物图说·豆科 294. 1955; Hitch. Gen. Fl. Pl. 1: 378. 1964.

落叶大藤本。冬芽球形至卵形，芽鳞3-5枚。奇数羽状复叶互生；托叶早落；小叶全缘；具小托叶。总状花序顶生，下垂；花多数，散生于花序轴上；苞片早落，无小苞片；具花梗；花萼杯状，萼齿5，略呈二唇形，上方2枚短，大部分合生，最下1枚较长，钻形；花冠蓝紫色或白色，通常大，旗瓣圆形，基部具2胼胝体，花开后反折，翼瓣长圆状镰形，有耳，与龙骨瓣离生或先端稍粘合，龙骨瓣内弯，钝头；雄蕊二体，对旗瓣的1枚离生或在中部与雄蕊管粘合，花丝顶端不扩大，花药同型；花盘明显被密腺环；子房具柄，花柱无毛，圆柱形，上弯，柱头小，点状，顶生，胚珠多数。荚果线形，伸长，具颈，种子间缢缩，迟裂，瓣片革质，种子大，肾形，无种阜。

> 本属模式种: Wisteria frutescens (Linn.) Poir.

约10种，分布于东亚、北美和大洋洲。我国有5种，1变型，其中引进栽培1种。
