## Description

Rhizomes short. Culms slightly tufted, 0.7-1.5 m tall, obtusely 3-angled, 7- or 8-nodose, smooth but slightly scabrous near inflorescence. Leaves cauline, shorter than inflorescence; sheath ± long; leaf blade 7-10 mm wide, abaxial midvein and margin slightly scabrous. Involucral bracts 2 or 3, leaflike, overtopping inflorescence. Inflorescence a terminal large compound anthela, with many rays; first rays to 9 cm, glabrous. Spikelets solitary, oblong-ovoid to narrowly ovoid, 5-8 × ca. 2 mm, many flowered, apex acuminate. Glumes densely arranged, oblong, ca. 2 mm, membranous, both surfaces dark grayish black, midvein pale yellow, base sometimes straw-colored, margin apically ciliate, apex rounded. Perianth bristles 6, usually 2-4 × as long as nutlet, prominently flexuose, retrorsely scabrous only near apex. Anthers ca. 1 mm, linear-oblong. Style slightly short; stigmas 3. Nutlet pale yellow, obovoid, ca. 1 mm, compressed 3-sided. Fl. and fr. Jun-Sep. 2*n* = 56.

### Statistics
In water, swampy places; 400-900 m. Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol [Japan, Kazakhstan, Korea, Mongolia, Russia; Europe].

### Synonym
*Nemocharis radicans* (Schkuhr) Beurling; *Scirpus sylvaticus* Linnaeus var. *radicans* (Schkuhr) Willdenow; *Seidlia radicans* (Schkuhr) Opiz.

Scirpus radicans Schk. in Usteri, Ann. IV 48; Maxim. , Prim. Fl.Amur. 299; Kom. , Fl. Mansh. I 340; Kitagawa, Lineam. Fl. Mansh. 123; Ohwi, Cyper. Jap. II in Mem. Coll. Sci. , Kyoto Imper. I(1798)308——Seidlia radicans (Schkuhr) Opiz, Nat. -Tausch. 349——Nemocharisradicans (Schkuhr) Beurl. in Bot. Notiser. 52.

散生草本。稈粗壮，高65-90厘米，有节，近花序部分为三棱形，稍粗糙，具基生叶与稈生叶。叶较花序短，宽7-10毫米，平滑、叶鞘很长。叶状苞片2-3枚，下面1-2枚长于花序；多次复出长侧枝聚繖花序顶生，大，具多数辐射枝，第一次辐射枝长可达9厘米；小穗多为单生或2-4个簇生于辐射枝顶端，长圆状卵形，顶端渐尖，长6-7毫米，宽2毫米，具多数花；鳞片排列紧密，长圆形，顶端钝圆，长约2毫米，背面具1条淡黄色中肋，两边黑色，有时基部呈麦稈色，上部边缘稍呈齧蚀状；下位刚毛6条，细长，通常约为小坚果长的4倍，多次弯曲隐藏于鳞片内，近顶端部分稍有倒刺；花药长约1毫米，线状长圆形；花柱稍短，柱头3，具乳头状小突起。小坚果倒卵形，扁三稜形，长约1毫米，淡黄色。花期6-7月。

产于我国东北各省；生长在水里，常和水葱 Scirpus validus Vahl 长在一起。也分布于朝鲜、日本、苏联远东地区。

本种与Scirpus sylvaticus Linn. var. maximowizii Rgl.外表上很相近，但本种的各次辐射枝和小穗柄平滑；鳞片背面只有1条脉；下位刚毛弯曲，只在顶部有稀疏的刺，长为小坚果长的3-4倍。
