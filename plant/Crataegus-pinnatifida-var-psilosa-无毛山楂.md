## Description

Pedicel and peduncle glabrous. 2n = 34*.

> **Habait** : 
> Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning [Korea].

### Synonym
*Crataegus coreana* H. Léveillé; *C. pinnatifida* f. *psilosa* (C. K. Schneider) M. Kitagawa.
