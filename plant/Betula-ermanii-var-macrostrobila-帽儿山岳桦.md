## Description

Leaves caudate-acuminate at apex. Female inflorescence ovoid; peduncle obscure; lateral lobes of bracts erect, apex acute, without a lobule at base.

> **Habait** : 
>*  Mixed forests. Heilongjiang (Mao’ershan, Shangzhi Xian)
