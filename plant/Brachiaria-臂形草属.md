臂形草属 **Brachiaria** (Trinius) Grisebach

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae ?t=foc) - [PDF](http://iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf)>>[Brachiaria](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brachiaria ?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Brachiaria.pdf)


> **Comment** : 
> *Brachiaria* is sometimes included within *Urochloa*, but usually they can be distinguished without difficulty.

## Description

Annual or perennial. Leaf blades linear to lanceolate, often with cartilaginous margins. Inflorescence composed of racemes along a central axis; raceme rachis triquetrous or flattened, sometimes winged; spikelets sessile or pedicelled, single or paired, rarely in fascicles or on secondary racemelets. Spikelets plump, usually elliptic, florets 2; lower glume adaxial, varying in length from very small and veinless to many-veined and subequaling spikelet, base sheathing, sometimes extended downward as a short stipe; upper glume and lower lemma similar, as long as spikelet, membranous or cartilaginous; upper lemma coriaceous, smooth, striate or rugose, margins inrolled, apex obtuse to acute, occasionally minutely mucronate; upper palea apex tucked within lemma. *x* = 7, 9.

### Statistics
About 100 species: tropics and subtropics mainly of the Old World, especially Africa; nine species (one endemic) in China.

### Synonym
*Panicum* sect. *Brachiaria* Trinius, Gram. Panic. 51, 125. 1826.


## Keylist

* 1 Culms robust, up to 2 m tall; inflorescence with 10–20 racemes.——1 [巴拉草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brachiaria mutica?t=foc)
* ~ Culms slender, up to 60 cm tall; inflorescence with less than 10 racemes——(2)

* 2 (1)Inflorescence with erect appressed racemes; fertile floret smooth and shiny, obtuse, readily falling from spikelet.——2 [臂形草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brachiaria eruciformis?t=foc)
* ~ Inflorescence with spreading racemes; fertile floret rugulose, acute to mucronate, persistent within the spikelet——(3)

* 3 (2) Spikelets 1.5–2.7 mm; leaf blades broadly lanceolate, 1–4 cm long——(4)
* ~ Spikelets 2.5–4 mm; leaf blades lanceolate or linear-lanceolate, 4–15 cm long——(5)

* 4 (3) Spikelets elliptic, 2–2.7 mm.——3 [毛臂形草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brachiaria villosa?t=foc)
* ~ Spikelets ovate, gibbous, 1.5–2 mm.——4 [短颖臂形草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brachiaria semiundulata?t=foc)

* 5 (3) Spikelets paired, one subsessile and one pedicelled.——5 [多枝臂形草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brachiaria ramosa?t=foc)
* ~ Spikelets single (except sometimes at raceme base)——(6)

* 6 (5) Rachis of racemes triquetrous, 0.2–0.4 mm wide; spikelets herbaceous, pubescent or glabrous——(7)
* ~ Rachis of racemes flat on back, 0.4–0.5 mm wide; spikelets cartilaginous, glabrous or almost so——(8)

* 7 (6) Spikelets contiguous, 3.8–4 mm, pubescent.——6 [细毛臂形草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brachiaria fusiformis?t=foc)
* ~ Spikelets distant, 2.5–3.5 mm, glabrous.——7 [无名臂形草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brachiaria kurzii?t=foc)

* 8 (6) Lower glume 1/3–1/2 spikelet length, separated from upper glume by a short internode; upper lemma subacute.——8 [四生臂形草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brachiaria subquadripara?t=foc)
* ~ Lower glume 1/2–3/5 spikelet length, not separated from upper glume; upper lemma mucronate.——9 [尾稃臂形草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brachiaria urochlooides?t=foc)

**臂形草属 Brachiaria**

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第10(1)卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/10(1)) >> 263页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/10(1)/263y.pdf)


**15.臂形草属——Brachiaria Griseb.**

Griseb. in Ledeb. Fl. Ross. 4: 469. 1869.

一年生或多年生草本。叶片平展。圆锥花序顶生，由2至数枚总状花序组成；小穗背腹压痛，具短柄或近无柄，单生或孪生，交互成两行排列于穗轴之一侧，有1-2小花；第一小花雄性或中性；第二小花两性；第一颖长大都为小穗之半，向轴而生，基部包卷小穗；第二颖与第一外稃等长，同质同形；第二外稃骨质，先端不具小尖头或具小尖头，背部突起，背面离轴而生；尤以单生小穗为明显，边缘稍内卷，包着同质的内稃；鳞被2，折叠，具5-7脉，花柱基分离；种脐点状。染色体X=7，9。叶片表皮脉间细胞微形态有4种类型（国产种类），矽细胞大都为哑铃形。节结形或十字形；有大毛和大刺毛；气孔副卫细胞为圆屋顶形。

约50种，广布全世界热带地区。我国7种（包括引种），4变种，主产长江以南地区。

模式种：臂形草 B. eruciformis (J. E. Smith) Griseb.

## 检索表

* 1 植株高10-80厘米；花序分枝在10枚以下。——(2)
* ~植株高1.5-2.5米，花序分枝10-20枚。——[巴拉草](Brachiaria-mutica-巴拉草.md'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 2 小穗单生，稀在基部李生。——(3)
* ~小穗李生或有时在上部单生，疏被短硬毛。——[多枝臂形草](Brachiaria-ramosa-多枝臂形草.md'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 3 小穗狭长圆形、椭圆形或倒卵形，长3-4毫米。——(4)
* ~小穗卵形，长2-2.5毫米。——(7)

* 4 第二外稃顶端不具小尖头，第一颖长为小穗的1/3-1/2。——(5)
* ~第二外稃顶端具小尖头，第一颖长为小穗的1/2-3/5。 ——[尾稃臂形草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brachiaria urochloaoides?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 5 小穗长圆形，中部最宽，先端渐尖。——(6)
* ~小穗椭圆形至倒卵形，中部以上最宽，先端锐尖。 ——[锐头臂形草](Brachiaria-subquadripara-var-miliiformis-锐头臂形草.md'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 6 小穗及小穗轴无毛。 ——[四生臂形草（原变种）](Brachiaria-subquadripara-var-subquadripara-四生臂形草(原变种).md'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~小穗被毛，小穗轴被长刺毛。 ——[刺毛臂形草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brachiaria subquadripara var. setulosa?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 7 叶片卵状披针形；第一颖长为小穗1/2-2/3，具3脉；第二外稃先端尖。——(8)
* ~叶片线状披针形；第一颖微小，长为小穗的1/10，无脉，第二外稃先端钝。——[臂形草](Brachiaria-eruciformis-臂形草.md'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 8 第二颖短于小穗，第二外稃顶端稍露出。 ——[短颖臂形草](Brachiaria-semiundulata-短颖臂形草.md'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~第二颖与小穗等长。——(9)

* 9 植株密被柔毛。——(10)
* ~植株无毛或疏生短毛。——[无毛臂形草](Brachiaria-villosa-var-glabrata-无毛臂形草.md'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 10 第二颖和第一外稃先端无毛。——[毛臂形草 (原变种) ](Brachiaria-villosa-var-villosa-毛臂形草(原变种).md'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~第二颖和第一外稃先端被银白色髯毛。 ——[髯毛臂形草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Brachiaria villosa var. barbata?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)
