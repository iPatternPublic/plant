## Description

Leaf blade margin denticulate to serrulate. Fl. Mar-Sep.


> **Habait** : 
> Shady areas in valley forests; near sea level to 1700 m. S Fujian, E Guangdong, Hainan, Taiwan [Japan, Philippines].

### Synonym
*Isanthera discolor *Maximowicz, Bull. Acad. Imp. Sci. Saint-Petersbourg 19: 538. 1874.
