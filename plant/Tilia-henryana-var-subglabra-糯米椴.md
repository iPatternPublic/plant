## Description

Branchlets and bud glabrous or nearly so. Leaf blade abaxially hairy in vein axils only, marginal teeth 3-5 mm. Bracts adaxially glabrous.

> **Habait** : 
>●  Anhui, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Zhejiang.
