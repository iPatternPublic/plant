银毛土牛膝 **Achyranthes aspera var. argentea** C. B. Clarke

> [Amaranthaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Amaranthaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Amaranthaceae.pdf)>>[Achyranthes](Achyranthes-牛膝属.md) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Achyranthes.pdf)

## Description

Stem quadrangular, pilose or glabrescent. Leaves green, broadly obovate or elliptic-oblong, abaxially silvery sericeous, apex acute.


> **Habait** : 
> Hillsides. Sichuan [India; Africa, SW Asia, Europe].

### Synonym
*Achyranthes* *argentea* Thwaites, Enum. 249. 1861, not Lamarck.
