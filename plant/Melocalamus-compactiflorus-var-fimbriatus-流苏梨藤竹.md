> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf)>>[Melocalamus](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Melocalamus?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Melocalamus.pdf)

## Description

Plant shorter, with thicker culms. Culm sheath ligule much longer, fimbriate; leaves larger.


> **Habait** : 
>* Forested hills; 1000–1700 m. S Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Melocalamus fimbriatus* Hsueh & C. M. Hui, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 30: 167. 1992.
