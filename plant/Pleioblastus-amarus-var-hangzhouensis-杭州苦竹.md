> **Comment** : 
> The plants are used for fencing and supporting vegetables and are grown as ornamentals. The culms are not suitable for weaving.

## Description

Internodes 28–32(–38) cm; culms green-purple, purple spotted, roughly hairy, hairs dense and inverse. Culm sheath purple-green, glossy, not glaucous; auricles absent; blade linear-lanceolate. New shoots late Apr–May.


> **Habait** : 
>* Lowlands, small hills. Zhejiang.

**5e.杭州苦竹（变种）（植物分类学报）**

var. hangzhouensis S. L. Chen et S. Y. Chen in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 21(4): 408. 1983.

本变种的新竿密披倒生白色糙毛及紫色细点，且呈紫绿色，节间长28-32 (38)厘米，箨鞘绿带紫色，有光泽，无粉，无箨耳，箨片线状披针形等特征与原变种有别。笋期4月底至5月。

产浙江。生于低山坡或平原。模式标本采自杭州植物园。

本变种篾性脆，竿壁薄，不宜劈篾编织，仅整竿常作篱笆和农作物的支架。但其枝叶较秀丽，亦可为园林绿化树种之一。
