稗属 **Echinochloa** P. Beauvois

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae ?t=foc) - [PDF](http://iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf)>>[Echinochloa](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa ?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Echinochloa.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> The reflexed upper palea tip, although a very tiny character, is nevertheless important for distinguishing *Echinochloa* from neighboring genera, especially *Brachiaria*. The absence of a ligule is also a good spot character for recognition of the genus in China.
>
> Species of *Echinochloa* typically grow in aquatic or moist situations. Several have become widespread weeds, especially of irrigated crops, and two are sometimes cultivated as minor cereals.

## Description

Annual or perennial. Culms often coarse and robust. Leaf blades flat, linear or broadly linear; ligule absent (ciliate in some species outside China). Inflorescence composed of racemes along a central axis; racemes simple or compound, densely spiculate, spikelets paired in 4 rows, or congested on secondary racemelets. Spikelets narrowly elliptic to subrotund, plump, plano-convex, often hispidulous or spinulose, acute to awned; lower glume triangular, 1/3–1/2(–3/5) spikelet length, sheathing; upper glume and lower lemma equaling the spikelet or upper glume somewhat shorter in cultivated forms, prominently 5–7-veined, acute to rostrate or lower lemma extended into a stout awn; upper lemma coriaceous, smooth, shining, terminating in a short, laterally compressed, incurving beak; upper palea acute, apex briefly reflexed. *x* = 9.

### Statistics
About 35 species: tropical and warm-temperate regions of the world; eight species (at least one introduced) in China.

### Synonym
*Tema* Adanson, nom. rej.

## Keylist

* 1 Grain persisting at maturity; spikelets plumply ovate; branches of inflorescence incurving; cultivated plants——(2)
* ~ Grain readily deciduous at maturity; spikelets ovate, ovate-lanceolate or ovate-elliptic; branches of inflorescence not incurving; wild plants——(3)

* 2 (1)Spikelets dark greenish when mature, awnless; racemes rather spaced, simple.——1  [湖南稗子](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa frumentacea?t=foc)
* ~ Spikelets purplish brown when mature, acute to awned, awn 0.5–2 cm; racemes very dense, closely packed, usually branched——2  [紫穗稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa esculenta?t=foc)

* 3 (1) Lower lemma convex, hard and shining.——6  [硬稃稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa glabrescens?t=foc)
* ~ Lower lemma flat on the back, herbaceous——(4)

* 4 (3) Spikelets ovate, 3.8–6 mm; culms erect, forming narrow tuft.——3  [水田稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa oryzoides?t=foc)
* ~ Spikelets elliptic-ovate, mostly 2–4 mm; culms spreading, forming loose tuft——(5)

* 5 (4) Racemes neatly 4-rowed, simple, openly spaced, often erect; spikelets awnless, acute, 2–3 mm.——4  [光头稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa colona?t=foc)
* ~ Racemes untidily 2- to several-rowed, at least the longer often with short branchlets; spikelets acuminate to shortly awned, 2–4 mm——(6)

* 6 (5) Racemes distinctly compound with many short branchlets; spikelets 2–3 mm; awn of lower lemma 1–1.5 cm.——5  [孔雀稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa cruspavonis?t=foc)
* ~ Racemes simple or only inconspicuously branched; spikelets 2.5–4 mm; lower lemma acute to long-awned——(7)

* 7 (6) Inflorescence green or purple-tinged, moderately dense; spikelets 3–4 mm; lower lemma acuminate or awned——7  [稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa crusgalli?t=foc)
* ~ Inflorescence dark purple, very dense; spikelets 2.5–3 mm; lower lemma awned, awn 3–5 cm.——8  [长芒稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa caudata?t=foc)

**稗属 Echinochloa**

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第10(1)卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/10(1)) >> 250页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/10(1)/250y.pdf)

**14.稗属——Echinochloa Beauv.**

Beauv. Ess. Agrost. 53: 161. 1812.

一年生或多年生草本。叶片扁平，线形。圆锥花序由穗形总状花序组成；小穗含1-2小花，背腹压扁呈一面扁平，一面凸起，单生或2-3个不规则地聚集于穗轴的一侧，近无柄；颖草质；第一颖小，三角形，长约为小穗1/3-1/2或3/5；第二颖与小穗等长或稍短；第一小花中性或雄性，其外稃革质或近革质，内稃膜质，罕或缺；第二小花两性，其外稃成熟时变硬，顶端具极小尖头，平滑，光亮，边缘厚而内抱同质的内稃，但内稃顶端外露；鳞被2，折叠，具5-7脉；花柱基分离；种脐点状。染色体x=9。

本属约30种，分布全世界热带和温带。我国有9种，5变种。

本属大都为田间杂草；有的为优良牧草；其颖果含淀粉，尤其栽培种，可食用或为制糖及酿酒的原料。

模式种：稗E．crusgalli（L.）Beauv.

## 检索表

* 1 第二颖等长于小穗；小穗卵形、卵伏披针形或卵状椭圆形，谷粒易脱落；花序分枝不弯曲；叶片宽0.3-2厘米，野生种。——(2)
* ~第二颖稍短于小穗，小穗阔卵形，谷粒不易脱落；花序分枝弯曲；叶片宽1.5-3厘米；栽培种。——(14)

* 2 第二外稃草质；植株基部常向外开展；常生于沟边、沼泽地，也见于水稻田中。——(3)
* ~第二外稃近革质或至少中间变革质，硬而光亮，无芒或具芒；植株直立，基部不向外开展，常生长在水稻田中。——(12)

* 3 圆锥花序直立或稍点头；叶片上下表皮细胞结构近相似。——(4)
* ~圆锥花序柔软，下垂或点头；叶片上下表皮细胞结构不相似。——(10)

* 4 小穗长超过3毫米；叶片宽5-12毫米；圆锥花序开展。——(8)
* ~小穗长不超过3毫米；叶片宽不超过1厘米；圆锥花序狭窄，其分枝不再具小枝。——(5)

* 5 小穗卵状椭圆形，顶端渐尖而具短芒；花序轴上具密生疣基长刚毛，第一颖长为小穗的1/3。——(6)
* ~小穗阔卵形或卵形，顶端急尖或无芒；花序轴上无疣基长刚毛（有时分枝交接处偶而有毛1-2根），第一颖长为小穗的1/2。 ——[光头稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa colonum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 6 叶片宽4-10毫米；圆锥花序上的分枝上举。——(7)
* ~叶片宽2-5毫米；圆锥花序上分枝常贴向主轴，小穗长2.5-3毫米，无芒或具短芒。——[小旱稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa crusgalli var. austrojaponensis?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 7 植株及小穗通常绿色，小穗长约3毫米，有长不逾，毫米的芒。 ——[短芒稗 ](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa crusgalli var. breviseta?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~植株基部及小穗常带紫色，小穗长2.5-3毫米，通常无芒。 ——[细叶旱稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa crusgalli var. praticola?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 8 小穗卵形，可具短芒，花序分枝常具小枝。——(9)
* ~小穗卵状椭圆形，无芒，花序分枝不具小枝。 ——[西来稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa crusgalli var. zelayensis?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 9 芒长0.5-1.5厘米；花序分枝柔软。 ——[稗（原变种）](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa crusgalli var. crusgalli?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~芒长不超过0.5毫米；花序分枝挺直。——[无芒稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa crusgalli var. mitis?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 10 小穗长2.5-4毫米，芒长1.5-5厘米。——(11)
* ~小穗长4-6毫米，芒长0.5-1.5厘米。 ——[旱稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa hispidula?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 11 小穗卵状椭圆形，长3-4毫米，芒长1.5-5厘米。——[长芒稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa caudata?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~小穗卵状披针形，长2.5-3毫米，芒长1-1.5厘米——[孔雀稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa cruspavonis?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 12 小穗长3.5-5毫米，第二颖长为小穗的1/2-3/5。——(13)
* ~小穗长3-3.5毫米，第二颖长为小穗的1/3-1/2。——[硬稃稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa glabrescens?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 13 全部叶鞘及叶片均光滑无毛；小穗无芒或仅具长达0.5厘米的芒。 ——[水田稗](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Echinochloa oryzoides?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)
