大叶筇竹(原变种) **Chimonobambusa macrophylla var. macrophylla**

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf) >> [Chimonobambusa](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Chimonobambusa?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Chimonobambusa.pdf)

## Description

Culm internodes 18–29 cm, not white powdery; leaf blade 11–18 × 1.6–3.2 cm, secondary veins 5- or 6-paired.


> **Habait** : 
>* Broad-leaved forests; 1500–2200 m. Sichuan (Leibo, Mabian).

### Synonym
*Qiongzhuea macrophylla* Hsueh & T. P. Yi, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 23: 398. 1985.
