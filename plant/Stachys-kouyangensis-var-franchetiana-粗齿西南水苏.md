## Description

Leaf blade hastate-triangular, base subtruncate, margin coarsely crenate. Calyx teeth linear, subpatent.


> **Habait** : 
>* Grassy slopes, streamsides, field margins; 2400-3800 m. Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Stachys franchetiana* H. Léveillé, Repert. Spec. Nov. Regni Veg. 9: 246. 1911.

**17e. 粗齿变种 黄狼鼠花（四川黑水）**

var. franchetiana (Levl.) C. Y. Wu in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 10(3): 228. 1965 Stachys franchetiana Levl. in Fedde, Repert. Sp. Nov. 13: 246. 1911——Stachys chinensis Hand.-Mazz. in Act. Hort. Gothob. 13: 348. 1939, p. p. quoad syn. S. franchetiana Levl.——Stachys kouyangensis (Vaniot) Dunn in Notes Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 6: 180. 1915, p. p. quoad syn. St. franchetiana Levl.

这一变种与原变种不同在于萼齿线形，具刺尖，近开张；叶戟状三角形，基部近平截，边缘具粗大圆齿。

产四川西部，云南西北部及西藏东部；生于草坡、沟边及水田边，海拔2360-3800米。模式标本采自四川康定。
