## Description

Basal leaves decussate; middle stem leaves opposite; distal stem leaves alternate; Inflorescence paniculiform with cymose branches.


> **Habait** : 
>* Moist rocks on valley slopes; 1300-1700 m. NC Sichuan (Maowen Qiang Zu Zizhixian, Wenchuan Xian).

### Synonym
*Sedum aliciae* var. *komarovii* Raymond-Hamet, Russk. Bot. Zhurn. 1913: 175. 1913.
