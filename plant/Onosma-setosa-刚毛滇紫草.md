## Description

Herbs perennial, 20-40 cm tall. Stems single or several and cespitose, erect or ascending. Basal leaves oblanceolate, 10-25 cm &amp;#215; 5-10 mm, base attenuate, apex obtuse; stem leaves sessile, linear or lanceolate, 2.5-6.5 cm &amp;#215; 5-7 mm. Inflorescences terminal, 3-4 cm wide at anthesis, to 15 cm in fruit, racemose, many flowered; bracts lanceolate, 1-2 cm &amp;#215; 3-6 mm. Pedicel short, ca. 5 mm, elongated in fruit, spreading yellow hispid, short strigose. Flowers crowded. Calyx 1-1.5 cm, to 3 cm in fruit, densely hirsute, parted nearly to base; lobes linear-lanceolate. Corolla yellow; tubular-campanulate, base 2-2.5 mm wide, gradually expanded upward; throat 5-7 mm wide, glabrous on both sides; nectary ringlike, ca. 0.5 mm, glabrous; lobes broadly triangular, 1-2 mm, recurved at apex. Filaments 3-5 mm; anthers united at base, 8-10 mm, included, sterile apex 1-1.5 mm. Style 2.5-2.8 cm, exserted from corolla. Nutlets yellow-brown, ca. 5 mm, wrinkled. Fl. and fr. Jun. 2*n* = 12.

> **Habait** : 
> Steppes, stony slopes. Xinjiang [Kazakhstan, Mongolia, Russia]
