小石积属 **Osteomeles** Lindley

> [Rosaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Rosaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Rosaceae.pdf) >> [Osteomeles](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Osteomeles?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Osteomeles.pdf)

## Description

Shrubs deciduous or evergreen; buds small, with several narrow scales. Leaves imparipinnate; stipules linear to lanceolate; rachis narrowly winged; leaflets opposite, sessile or shortly petiolulate, small, margin entire. Corymb terminal, numerous flowered; bracts caducous. Hypanthium campanulate. Sepals 5. Petals 5, white. Stamens 20. Ovary inferior; 5-loculed, with 1 ovule per locule; styles 5, free. Fruit a small pome, with persistent erect sepals; seeds erect; cotyledons plano-convex.

### Statistics
About five species: E Asia; three species (one endemic) in China.

**13. 小石积属——Osteomeles Lindl.**

Lindl. in Trans. Linn. Soc. 13: 98. 1822; Benth. & Hook. f. Gen. Pl. 1: 628. 1865; Focke in Engler & Prantl, Nat. Pflanzenfam. 3 (3): 22. 1888; Hutch. Gen. Flow. Pl. 1: 213. 1964.

落叶或常绿灌木；冬芽小，卵形，有数枚外露鳞片。奇数羽状复叶，互生；小叶片全缘，对生，近于无柄；叶轴上有窄叶翼；托叶早落。顶生伞房花序，多花；苞片早落；萼筒钟状，具5裂片；花瓣5，白色；雄蕊15-20；花柱5，离生，子房下位，5室，每室具1胚珠。梨果，小形，果肉坚硬，萼片宿存，内含5骨质小核；种子直立，子叶平凸。

本属的模式种：小石积 Osteomeles anthyllidifolia Lindl.

本属约有5种，分布亚洲东部及太平洋岛屿，我国产3种。

栽培供观赏，适宜作绿篱和岩石园植物。
