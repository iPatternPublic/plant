## Description

Branchlets, petioles, abaxial veins of leaves, peduncles, and pedicels pubescent. Fl. Jun, fr. Aug-Nov.


> **Habait** : 
>●  Forests in valleys; 2300-2600 m. Xizang, Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Tetrastigma hypoglaucum* Planchon var. *puberulum* W. T. Wang & Z. Y. Cao; *T. napaulense* var. *puberulum* (W. T. Wang) C. L. Li; *T. serrulatum* var. *puberulum* (W. T. Wang & Z. Y. Cao) C. L. Li, not W. T. Wang; *T. serrulatum* var. *pubinervium* C. L. Li.
