> **Comment** : 
> No specimens have been seen by the present authors.

## Description

Stem less than 30 cm. Inflorescence glandular. 2* n* = 26*.
> **Habait** : 
>* 800--1400 m. C and NE Taiwan.

### Synonym
* Tricyrtis formosana* f. * glandosa* Simizu, Bot. Bull. Acad. Sin., n.s., 3: 37. 1962.
