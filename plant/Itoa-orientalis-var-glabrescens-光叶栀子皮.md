## Description

Branchlets, petioles, and leaf blades abaxially glabrous. Fl. Mar-Jun, fr. Jul-Dec.


> **Habait** : 
>● Evergreen broad-leaved forests in mountains; 500-1700 m. Guangxi, Guizhou, Yunnan.
