## Description

Plants evergreen. Perianth single, orange to reddish orange. 2* n* = 22, 33.


> **Habait** : 
> Thickets, grasslands, streamsides; 300--1000 m. Guangdong, Guangxi, Taiwan [Japan, Korea].

### Synonym
* Hemerocallis aurantiaca* Baker, Gard. Chron., ser. 3, 8: 94. 1890.
