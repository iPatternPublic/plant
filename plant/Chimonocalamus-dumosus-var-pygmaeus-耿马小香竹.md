## Description

Slightly smaller in size. Culms basally hollow; root thorns obtuse and densely arranged on basal nodes. Leaves relatively smaller, 5–11 × 0.5–0.9 cm.
> **Habait** : 
>* Montane evergreen forests. SW Yunnan (Gengma).
