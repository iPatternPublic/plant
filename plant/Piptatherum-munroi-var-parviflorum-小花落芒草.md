小花落芒草 **Piptatherum munroi var. parviflorum** (Z. L. Wu) S. M. Phillips & Z. L. Wu

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf)>>[Piptatherum](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Piptatherum?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Piptatherum.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> This taxon differs very little from *Piptatherum tibeticum*, which is reported to have a broader floret and more branches at the panicle nodes.

## Description

Glumes 4.5–5 mm; lemma 2.5–3 mm; awn 7–9 mm; anthers 1–1.8 mm. Fl. and fr. Jul–Sep.

> **Habait** : 
> Alluvial fans; ca. 2700 m. Gansu, Qinghai [NW India].

### Synonym
*Oryzopsis munroi* var. *parviflora* Z. L. Wu, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 30(2): 174. 1992; *O. stewartiana* Bor.
