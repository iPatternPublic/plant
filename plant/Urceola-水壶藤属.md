水壶藤属 **Urceola** Roxburgh

> [Apocynaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Apocynaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Apocynaceae.pdf) >> [Urceola](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Urceola?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Urceola.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> Fifteen species: SE Asia, eight species in China.

## Description

Lianas woody, latex white. Leaves opposite. Cymes paniculate, terminal or axillary, 3-branched. Flowers small. Calyx deeply divided, with basal glands inside. Corolla suburceolate, throat without scales; lobes short, overlapping to right. Stamens included, inserted at base of corolla tube; filaments short; anthers narrowly oblong, sagittate, connivent, adherent to pistil head, cells spurred at base; disc ringlike, entire or 5-lobed. Ovaries 2, longer than disc, villous at apex; ovules numerous in each locule. Style short; pistil head ovoid, conical or oblong, apex 2-cleft. Follicles cylindric or narrowly ellipsoid, spreading, thick, acuminate. Seeds numerous, oblong or linear, compressed, pubescent, coma long; endosperm scanty; cotyledons oblong or ovate, leaflike, radicle short.

### Synonym
*Chunechites* Tsiang; *Ecdysanthera* Hooker & Arnott; *Parabarium* Pierre in Spire; *Xylinabariopsis* L&amp;yacute;.

## Keylist

* 1 Leaf blade pubescent abaxially, denser along veins.——(2)
* ~ Leaf blade glabrous, sometime with hairy domatia in axils of lateral veins.——(3)

* 2 (1)Leaf blade very narrowly elliptic, lateral veins 5-7 pairs; corolla glabrous outside, lobe margin 1-toothed; fruit linear or nearly so, ca. 0.5 cm in diam.——1  [Urceola xylinabariopsoides 乐东藤](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Urceola xylinabariopsoides?t=foc)
* ~ Leaf blade ovate or narrowly elliptic, lateral veins ca. 10 pairs; corolla pubescent outside, lobe margin entire; fruit narrowly ovoid, 1.5-2 cm in diam.——2  [Urceola huaitingii 毛杜仲藤](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Urceola huaitingii?t=foc)

* 3 (1) Petiole puberulent; corolla lobes 1-toothed near base; fruit ovoid——3  [Urceola micrantha 杜仲藤](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Urceola micrantha?t=foc)
* ~ Petiole glabrous; corolla lobes entire; fruit sublinear or oblong, if ovoid then long beaked.——(4)

* 4 (3) Leaf blade papillate abaxially, petiole 3-5 mm——4  [Urceola quintaretii 华南杜仲藤](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Urceola quintaretii?t=foc)
* ~ Leaf blade not papillate abaxially, petiole 6-25 mm.——(5)

* 5 (4) Leaf blade narrowly oblong, 11-18 cm; fruit ca. 2 cm in diam.——5  [Urceola tournieri 云南水壶藤](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Urceola tournieri?t=foc)
* ~ Leaf blade broadly elliptic, elliptic, or narrowly ovate, 3-10 cm; fruit 0.4-1 cm in diam.——(6)

* 6 (5) Inflorescences glabrous——8  [Urceola linearicarpa 线果水壶藤](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Urceola linearicarpa?t=foc)
* ~ Inflorescences pubescent or puberulent.——(7)

* 7 (6) Sepals ca. 4 mm; corolla lobes &#177; symmetrical, slightly longer than tube; follicles to 15 cm, terete, not stipitate——6  [Urceola rosea 酸叶胶藤](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Urceola rosea?t=foc)
* ~ Sepals ca. 0.8 mm; corolla lobes strongly asymmetrical, ca. 2 longer than tube; follicles 5-7 cm, strongly stipitate——7  [Urceola napeensis 华南水壶藤](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Urceola napeensis?t=foc)
