## Description

Stems distinctly angled. Fruit globose, rarely globose-ovoid, minutely papillate or glabrous. Seeds 2-4(-6) per fruit, 0.8-1(-1.1) mm. Fl. Jul-Aug, fr. Aug-Oct. 2n = 12*.

> **Habait** : 
>* Valleys, roadsides; 1400-3000 m. Gansu, Sichuan.

### Synonym
*Cochlearia henryi* (Oliver) O. E. Schulz var. *wilsonii* O. E. Schulz, Repert. Spec. Nov. Regni Veg. 38: 108. 1935; *Yinshania qianningensis* Y. H. Zhang; *Y. qianningensis* var. *brachybotrys* Y. H. Zhang.
