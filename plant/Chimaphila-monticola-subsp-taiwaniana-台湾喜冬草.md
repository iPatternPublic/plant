## Description

Plant usually to 10 cm tall. Largest leaf narrowly elliptic, 1.5–2.5 cm × 6–11 mm. Inflorescence 1- or 2-flowered.
> **Habait** : 
> Mossy ground beneath Tsuga canopies; 2600–3000 m. Taiwan.
