## Description

Leaf rachises, petiolules, and leaflet blades abaxially densely bright brown hirsute. Leaflet blades leathery, adaxially rough and dark green.

### Statistics
● Evergreen broad-leaved forest margins; 1100-1300 m. Guangxi, S Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Whitfordiodendron filipes* var. *tomentosum* Z. Wei, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 27: 75. 1989.
