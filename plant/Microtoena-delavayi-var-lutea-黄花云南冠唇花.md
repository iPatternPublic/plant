## Description

Leaf blade cordate to oblong-ovate. Corolla ca. 2.6 cm, yellow.

> **Habait** : 
>* Shaded wet areas in evergreen broad-leaved forests; ca. 2000 m. Yunnan.

**6c. 黄花变种**
var. lutea C. Y. Wu et Hsuan in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 10 (1): 48. 1965.
这一变种与大花变种极相近，仅花全为黄色与之相异。
产云南中南部；生于常绿阔叶林下阴湿处，海拔约2000米。模式标本采自云南景东。
