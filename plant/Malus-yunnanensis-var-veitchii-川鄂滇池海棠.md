> [Rosaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Rosaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Rosaceae.pdf)>>[Malus](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Malus?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Malus.pdf)
## Description

Leaf blade ovate, abaxially subglabrous, base usually cordate, lobes acute at apex.

> **Habait** : 
> Guizhou, Hubei, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Xizang.

### Synonym
*Pyrus yunnanensis* var. *veitchii* Osborn, Gard. Chron., ser. 3, 78: 227. 1925.
