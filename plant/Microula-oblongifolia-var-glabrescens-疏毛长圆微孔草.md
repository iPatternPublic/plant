## Description

Stems glabrous on lower parts, sparsely strigose at apex. Abaxial surface of leaf blade glabrous or sparsely short strigose along midvein only. Calyx glabrous except for pubescent midvein outside. Nutlet attachment scar below middle on adaxial surface.

> **Habait** : 
>* Forests, forest margins; 3400-3700 m. Yunnan (Zhongdian Xian).

Microula oblongifolia Hand. -Mazz. var. glabrescens W. T. Wang, 植物分类学报18(3): 275. 1980.

与长圆微孔草的区别：茎下部近无毛或无毛，上部有稀疏伏毛，叶下面无毛或沿中脉几疏被短伏毛；花萼外面只延中脉有短柔毛，其他部分无毛；小坚果着生面位于腹面基部之上。

产云南（中甸）。生海拔3400-3700米山地林边或林中。
