## Description

Petiole ca. 7 cm; leaf blade ca. 15 × 12 cm, leathery, abaxi-ally puberulent, adaxially glabrous, veins 7, base cordate. Petals red. Legume dark brown, ca. 20 × 8-10 cm.

### Statistics
● Open forests; ca. 2000 m. Yunnan (Hekou).
