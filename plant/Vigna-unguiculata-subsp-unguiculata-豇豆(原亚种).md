## Description

Legumes 20-30 cm, pendulous. Fl. Jun-Jul, fr. Aug.


> Reference: 
> The legumes and seeds are edible.The legumes and seeds are edible.

### Statistics
Widely cultivated in China [native to Africa; widely cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions].

### Synonym
*Dolichos unguiculatus* Linnaeus, Sp. Pl. 2: 725. 1753; *D. sinensis* Linnaeus; *Vigna sinensis* (Linnaeus) Hasskarl.

荚果长20-30厘米，下垂。花期6-7月，果期8月。

我国各地常见栽培。全球热带、亚热带地区广泛栽培。

嫩荚作蔬菜食用。
