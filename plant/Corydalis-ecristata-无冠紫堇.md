## Description
 "
Herbs, perennial, 5-10 cm above soil surface; stems and petioles of radical leaves attenuate to filiform underground base. Storage roots few, fascicled, 12-30 × 2-4 mm, tapering distally. Bulb (resting bud) broadly ovoid, 5-8 × 5-10 mm, of several pale fleshy scales. Stems erect, thin, simple, with 1 leaf in upper 1/3, or leaf lacking. Radical leaves few; petiole 2-6 cm, thin; blade 6-15 × 8-15 mm, bi-(tri-)ternate, primary leaflets petiolulate, ultimate leaflets sessile, deeply divided into obovate to elliptic lobes. Cauline leaf sessile; blade 0.8-2.5 cm, deeply bi- to triternatisect into narrowly linear lobes 0.5-1(-1.5) mm wide. Raceme umbellate, 1-3 cm, 2-6-flowered, not elongating in fruit; bracts 8-13 mm, deeply cut into 3-13 linear lobes. Pedicel 8-25 mm, thin, erect in fruit. Sepals minute. Petals clear blue, inner petals pale but darker at tip; upper petal narrowly navicular, 13-19 mm, subacute, crest narrow or absent; spur straight or downcurved apically, cylindric, 6-11 mm; nectary ca. 2/3 as long as spur; lower petal 10-13 mm, claw distinct, 4-5 mm, limb orbicular, much surpassing upper petal, 7-10 mm wide, apex truncate; inner petals 7-9 mm. Stigma broader than long, median marginal papillae distinct, lateral broad and diffuse; geminate papillae lateral (submarginal) and on basal lobes. Capsule reflexed from straight pedicel, lanceolate, 5-8 × 1.5-2 mm, ca. 10-seeded; style ca. 2.5 mm. Fl. and fr. Jun-Sep.

### Statistics
Mossy rocks, fine scree; 4000-5000 m. S Xizang (Yadong) [Bhutan, India (Sikkim), E Nepal].

### Synonym
*Corydalis cashmeriana* Royle var. *ecristata* Prain, J. Asiat. Soc. Bengal, Pt. 2, Nat. Hist. 65: 22. 1896; *C. cashmeriana* var. *brevicornu* Prain.

**134a.无冠紫堇（原亚种）**

ssp. ecristata

矮小草本，高5-10厘米。须根多数成簇，纺锤状肉质增粗，长0.4-1.5厘米，粗2-3毫米末端线状延长，具纤维状细根。根茎短，具鳞茎；鳞片数枚，宽卵形，长4-6毫米，宽3-4毫米，覆瓦状排列，近肉质，干时褐色。茎柔弱，不分枝，裸露，近基部变细。基生叶少数，叶柄纤细，长4-6厘米，基部变细，叶片二回三出分裂，第一回裂片具短柄，第二回深裂片狭椭圆形，长4-8毫米；茎生叶1枚，生于茎上部，无叶柄，叶片羽状深裂，裂片条形，长0.5-1.3厘米。总状花序顶生，长2-3.5厘米，有2-5（-6）花，近伞房状排列；苞片羽状深裂至3浅裂，裂片条形；花梗纤细，明显长于苞片。萼片极小，边缘撕裂状；花瓣蓝色，上花瓣长1.3-1.8厘米，花瓣片舟状卵形，鸡冠状突起无或极矮，距圆筒形，略长于花瓣片，稍向下弯曲，下花瓣长1-1.4厘米，花瓣片近圆形，直径0.6-1.1厘米，先端具钝尖头，无鸡冠状突起，爪楔状条形，内花瓣提琴形，长0.7-1厘米，花瓣片长圆形，具1侧生囊，爪狭楔形，比瓣片短；雄蕊束长6-8毫米，花药极小，花丝下部狭椭圆形，上部线形；子房线形，长4-6毫米，胚珠数枚，排成1列，花柱纤细，稍短于子房，先端弯曲，柱头双卵形，具8个乳突。蒴果披针形，长5-7毫米，粗1.6-2毫米，约具10枚种子，成熟时自果梗先端反折。花果期6-9月。

产西藏南部（错那、亚东、吉隆），生于海拔3300-4700米的山顶石缝或山坡岩石上。不丹、锡金至尼泊尔东部常见。

**134b.长距无冠紫堇（新拟）（亚种）**

ssp. longicalcarata (D. G. Long) C. Y. Wu, comb. nov.——Corydalis ecristata (Prain) D. G. Long var. longicalcarata D. G. Long in Not. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 42 (1): 93. 1984.

与原亚种的区别是花较大，上花瓣长1.8-2.2厘米，距纤细，长1.1-1.6厘米，明显向下弯曲，下花瓣超出上花瓣片5-8毫米。

产西藏南部（察隅、拉萨、亚东），海拔3200-3900米。不丹、锡金有分布。

按照D. G. Long, Liden的概念，我国境内无Corydalis cashmeriana Royle的分布，该种仅见于克什米尔、巴基斯坦、印度西北部等喜马拉雅山脉的西北部分，最东到尼泊尔西部，以东均为上一种及其亚种。
