## Description

Calyx and standard purplish black, wings and keel partly purplish black or red; corolla standard callus blunt at apex.

### Statistics
Mountain slopes, roadsides, streamsides; 300-1300 m. Natural-ized in Yunnan [probably native to India].

### Synonym
*Sesbania aegyptiaca* Poiret var. *bicolor* Wight & Arnott, Prodr. Fl. Ind. Orient. 214. 1834.

产云南（元江）。生于山坡、路边、水沟旁，海拔450-500米。印度、塞内加尔及苏丹也有分布。
