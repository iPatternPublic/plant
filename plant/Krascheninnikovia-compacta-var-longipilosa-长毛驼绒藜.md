## Description

Female floral tube abaxially 4-fascicular long villous in fruit.


> **Habait** : 
>* High cold desert communities; 4300-4800 m. Qinghai, Xizang.

### Synonym
*Certoides* *compacta* var. *longipilosa* C. P. Tsien & C. G. Ma, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 16(1): 117. 1978.
