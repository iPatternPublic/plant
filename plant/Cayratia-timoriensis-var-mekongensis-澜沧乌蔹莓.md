## Description

Leaflets abaxially pilose on veins. Fl. and fr. Jul-Aug.
> **Habait** : 
>●  Forests in valleys; 1100-1200 m. Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Cayratia mekongensis* C. Y. Wu ex W. T. Wang, Acta Phytotax. Sin. 17(3): 79. 1979.
