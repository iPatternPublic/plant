## Description

Leaflets (19-)25-35(-41), elliptic or lanceolate-elliptic, 1-1.5(-2.5) cm × 5-6 mm, glabrous adaxially or pilose near mid-vein. Inflorescence terminal. Fruit neck 2-4 cm or more.

### Statistics
● Mountain forests; 1100-1600 m. Yunnan (Menghai).

产云南（勐海地区）。生于山地林下或较干燥地方，海拔1100-1600米。模式标采自勐海。
