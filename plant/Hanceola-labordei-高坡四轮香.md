## Description

Plants glabrous. Stems elongated, slender, grooved. Leaves lanceolate, ca. 10 × 2.5 cm, base cuneate-attenuate, margin dentate except entire basally, apex acuminate. Flowers rose. Calyx striate, teeth linear. Corolla pink, ca. 4 cm, swollen on 1 side. Nutlets ovoid, ca. 2 mm (specimens not seen).

> **Habait** : 
>* Guizhou (Qingyan).

**7.高坡四轮香**

Hanceola labordei (Levl.) Sun, 科学社生物所论文集12(3): 123. 1942, in nota——Hancea labordei Levl. in Fedde, Repert. Sp. Nov. 12: 22. 1913.

植株极无毛。茎伸长，纤细，具沟。叶披针形，先端渐尖。基部楔状渐狭至具翅的叶柄，边缘除基部楔状全缘外均具牙齿，长10厘米，宽2.5厘米。花美丽，玫瑰红色，花萼具线形齿，但具条纹，花冠一侧肿胀，长4厘米。种子褐色，卵球形，大，长2毫米。（标本未见，摘自原描写）

产贵州。模式标本采自贵州青岩高坡山洞石场。注：尚有2种，即高坡四轮香H. labordei（Levl.）Sun及龙街四轮香H. mairei（Levl.）Sun,
