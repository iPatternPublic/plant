## Description

Leaflet blades oblong, 3.5-10 cm, adaxially pubescent, secondary veins 6 or 7 on each side of midvein, adaxially sunken, and densely yellow villous, base conspicuously asym-metric, apex acute.

### Statistics
Thickets, sparse woodlands; 500-1200 m. Yunnan [Myanmar].

### Synonym
*Millettia yunnanensis* Pampanini, Nuovo Giorn. Bot. Ital., n.s., 17: 26. 1910.

本变种与疏叶崖豆、印度崖豆近似，不同之处为：小叶7-9对，长圆形，先端锐尖，基部两侧不等大，侧脉6-7对，上面凹陷，下面隆起，脉上毛更密，萼齿渐尖。
产云南。缅甸北部也有分布。
