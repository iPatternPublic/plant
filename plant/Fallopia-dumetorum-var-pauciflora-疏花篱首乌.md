> **Comment** : 
> One of us (Park) notes that this appears to be a poorly developed form of the typical variety that is often found in harsh environments, and that it can hardly be considered a variety.

## Description

Flowers few; bracts each 1- or 2(or 3)-flowered. Fl. Jun-Jul, fr. Jul-Aug.

> **Habait** : 
>* Mixed forests in valleys, thickets; 400-1000 m. Hebei, Heilongjiang, Shandong.

**3b. 疏花篱蓼（变种）**

Fallopia dumetorum (L.) Holub var. pauciflora (Maxim.) A. J. Li st. nov.——Polygonum pauciflora Maxim. Ind. Sem. Hort. Petr. 3. 1866. ——Fallopia pauciflora (Maxim.) Kitag. Neo-Lin. Fl. Mansh. 231. 1979.

本变种与原变种的主要区别是花梗中部具关节，花排列稀疏。

产黑龙江，生山沟路旁。朝鲜、俄罗斯（远东）也有。
