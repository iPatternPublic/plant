牯岭山梅花 **Philadelphus sericanthus var. kulingensis** (Koehne) Handel-Mazzetti

> [Saxifragaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Saxifragaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Saxifragaceae.pdf) >> [Philadelphus](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Philadelphus?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Philadelphus.pdf)
## Description

Leaf blade ovate-elliptic, adaxially glabrous or subglabrous, margin sharply and prominently 9-12-serrate. Racemes 5-10-flowered; rachis 3-10 cm. Corolla 2-2.5 cm. Fl. Jun.

> **Habait** : 
>* Mixed forests; ca. 1200 m. Jiangxi, ?Zhejiang.

### Synonym
*Philadelphus incanus* var. *sargentianus* f. kulingensis Koehne, Repert. Spec. Nov. Regni Veg. 10: 126. 1911.
