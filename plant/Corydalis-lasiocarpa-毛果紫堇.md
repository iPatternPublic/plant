## Description

Roots not seen. Stems ascending, 30-50 cm, upper part with 3 or 4 leaves, usually branched. Blade of lower cauline leaves shortly petiolate, triangular-ovate, 4-8 × 2.5-7 cm, bipinnate; primary leaflets 2 or 3 pairs, shortly petiolate; secondary leaflets sessile, entire or ± split into 2 or 3 acute ovate segments. Upper leaves smaller, sessile. Racemes subsecund, densely 13-22-flowered, at fruiting elongated; bracts 3-4 mm, often slightly dentate apically. Pedicel slender, 5-10 mm, at fruiting to 10-20 mm. Flowers pale mauve. Sepals ovate, 3-5 × ca. 2 mm, dentate, acuminate. Outer petals with short dentate crest, apex acuminate, laterally with distinct auricles; upper petal 21-25 mm; spur cylindric, 9-11 mm; nectary 1/2-3/5 as long as spur; lower petal with much broader claw than in *Corydalis kingii*, apically with distinct rounded auricles; inner petals 10-11 mm. Stigma wider than long, with 2 large flat deltoid apical papillae; geminate papillae indistinct or absent. Capsule linear, densely papillose-puberulent. Fl. and fr. Jul-Sep.

### Statistics
● Alpine grasslands; ca. 4500 m. C Xizang (N of Lhasa).

### Synonym
*Corydalis kingii* Prain var. *megalantha* C. Y. Wu & Z. Y. Su.

**毛果紫堇 Corydalis lasiocarpa",

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第32卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/32) >> 369页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/32/369a.pdf)


**209.毛果紫堇 图版86：1-3**

Corydalis lasiocarpa Liden et Z. Y. Su in Edinb. J. Bot. 54 (1): 82, Figs. 4b, 5B, 1997.

根未见。茎高30-40厘米或更高，上部具3-4叶，分枝。下部的叶长4-8厘米，宽2.5-7厘米，三角状卵圆形，具短柄，近二回羽状全裂，一回羽片具短柄，二回羽片无柄，全缘或多少分裂成具短尖的卵圆形裂片。上部的叶较小，无柄。总状花序不分枝或近分枝，具13-22花，密集，果期伸长。苞片长3-4毫米，卵圆形，多少具齿。花梗直立，纤细，长5-10毫米，果期伸长达10-20毫米。花淡紫红色，平展。萼片卵圆形，渐尖，具齿。外花瓣具短齿状鸡冠，顶端渐尖，具突出的侧生裂瓣或耳状体。上花瓣长2-2.2厘米；距粗壮，圆筒形，轻微向下弯曲，长约9毫米；蜜腺稍超过距长的1/2。下花瓣基部具小囊，爪较宽。内花瓣长1-1.1厘米；爪线形，长约7毫米。柱头近四方形，宽度大于长度，顶端具2大的三角形乳突和不明显的侧生乳突。蒴果线形，密被乳突状微柔毛。

产西藏中部（拉萨），生于海拔4500米左右的高山草地。模式标本采自拉萨以北60公里的当雄。
