囊谦蝇子草 **Silene nangqenensis** C. L. Tang

> [Caryophyllaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Caryophyllaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Caryophyllaceae.pdf) >> [Silene](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Silene?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Silene.pdf)
## Description

Herbs perennial, 15--30 cm tall, densely glandular hairy throughout. Roots spiniform or conical, slightly fleshy. Stems sparsely clustered or solitary, erect, simple. Basal leaves elliptic-oblanceolate, rarely saccate, 4--8 × 1--1.5 cm, midvein prominent, base cuneate or attenuate, apex acute, rarely rounded; cauline leaves often 1--3 pairs, smaller than basal leaves. Flowers few, slightly nutant, erect in late anthesis. Pedicel 1--5 cm, densely glandular hairy; bracts lanceolate, 5--10 mm, glandular hairy. Calyx broadly campanulate, slightly saccate, ca. 1.5 × 1 cm, rounded at base, open at apex; longitudinal veins slightly robust, dark violet, glandular hairy; calyx teeth broadly triangular-ovate, ca. 3 mm, margin membranous, white, shortly ciliate, apex acute. Androgynophore ca. 2 mm, glabrous. Petals slightly exserted beyond calyx; claws nearly cuneate, ciliate at base, auricles obtuse; limbs broadly obovate, deeply bifid; lobes narrowly elliptic, apex obtuse, emarginate or shallowly bifid, shortly 1-lobed or weakly toothed on two sides; coronal scales flabellate, laciniate. Stamens slightly exserted; filaments hairy. Styles included. Capsule ovoid, ca. 1.2 cm, 10-toothed. Seeds brown, globose-reniform, ca. 1 mm. Fl. Jul, fr. Aug.

> **Habait** : 
>* Alpine meadows; 4200--4600 m. S Qinghai, E Xizang.

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第26卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/26) >> 324页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/26/324.pdf)
**40. 囊谦蝇子草（云南植物研究） 图版80：1-5**

Silene nangqenensis C. L. Tang in Act. Bot. Yunnan. 2: 433. fig. 4. 1980.——Melandrium glandulosum (Maxim.) Williams var. longistylum Y. W. Tsui et L. H. Zhou in Bull. Bot. Lab. North-East. Forest. Inst. 9: 60. fig. 6: 1-5. 1980, syn. nov. ——M. glandulosum (Maxim.) Williams var. hexapetalum Y. W. Tsui et L. H. Zhou. l. c. 9: 61. fig. 6: 6-10. 1980, syn. nov.

多年生草本，高15-30厘米，全株密被腺毛。根纺缍形或圆锥形，稍肉质。茎疏丛生或单生，直立，不分枝。基生叶叶片椭圆状倒披针形，稀近匙形，长4-8厘米，宽10-15毫米，基部楔形或渐狭成柄状，顶端急尖，稀圆钝，中脉明显；茎生叶常1-3对，较基生叶小。花序呈总状，具少数花；花微俯垂，花后期直立，花梗长1-5厘米，密被腺毛；苞片披针形，长5-10毫米，被腺毛；花萼宽钟形，微呈囊状，长约15毫米，宽约10毫米，口张开，基部圆形，纵脉微粗，带暗紫色，沿脉被腺毛，萼齿宽三角状卵形，长约3毫米，顶端急尖，边缘膜质，白色，具短缘毛；雌雄蕊柄无毛，长约2毫米；花瓣紫色（？）或黄白色，微露出花萼，爪近楔形，基部具缘毛，耳钝，瓣片轮廓宽倒卵形，深2裂，裂片长椭圆形，顶端钝，微凹缺或浅2裂，瓣片两侧近基部各具1小裂片或细齿，副花冠片摺扇状，具缺刻；雄蕊微露花冠喉部，花丝具毛；花柱内藏。蒴果卵形（未成熟），长约12毫米，10齿裂；种子圆肾形，长约1毫米，褐色。花期7月，果期8月。

产青海（囊谦、玉树、杂多、曲麻、久治）、西藏（昌都）。生于海拔4 200-4 600米的高山草地。模式标本采自青海囊谦。
