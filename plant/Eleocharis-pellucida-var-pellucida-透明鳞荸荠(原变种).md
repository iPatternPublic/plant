透明鳞荸荠(原变种) **Eleocharis pellucida var. pellucida**

> [Cyperaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Cyperaceae ?t=foc) - [PDF](http://iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Cyperaceae.pdf)>>[Eleocharis](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Eleocharis ?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Eleocharis.pdf)

## Description

Culms 5-30 cm tall, 0.5-1 mm thick. Spikelet narrowly ovoid, oblong-ovoid, or rarely globose, (3-)5-8 mm. Perianth bristles ca. 1.5 × as long as nutlet, densely retrorsely spinulose. Stigmas 3. Nutlet obovoid, ca. 1.2 × 0.7 mm; persistent style base pyramidal, ca. 1/4 as long as nutlet, not spongy-thickened, apex attenuate. Fl. and fr. Apr-Nov.

### Statistics
Rice paddies, pond margins, lake banks; 300-1000 m. Anhui, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hainan, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Liaoning, Shaanxi, Shanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [India Indonesia, Japan, Korea, Malaysia, Myanmar, Philippines, Russia (Far East), Sri Lanka].

### Synonym
*Eleocharis afflata* Steudel; *E. shimadae* Hayata; *Scirpus afflatus* (Steudel) Bentham.
