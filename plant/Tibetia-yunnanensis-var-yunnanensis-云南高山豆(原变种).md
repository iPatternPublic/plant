云南高山豆(原变种) **Tibetia yunnanensis var. yunnanensis**

## Description

Ovary with long appressed hairs.

### Statistics
● Hilly areas, grassy places at streamsides, disturbed forests, thickets, N-facing ravines; above 2500 m. Sichuan, Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Gueldenstaedtia yunnanensis* Franchet, Pl. Delavay. 164. 1890; *Amblytropis yunnanensis* (Franchet) C. Y. Wu ex H. P. Tsui.
