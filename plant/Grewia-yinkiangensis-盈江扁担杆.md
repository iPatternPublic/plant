盈江扁担杆 **Grewia yinkiangensis** Y. C. Hsu & R. Zhuge

> [Tiliaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tiliaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Tiliaceae.pdf)>>[Grewia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Grewia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Grewia.pdf)

## Description

Shrubs. Branchlets striate, gray-yellow stellate pilose. Petiole very short, 3-4 mm, gray-yellow pilose; leaf blade nearly orbicular, 8-12 × 7.5-10 cm, papery, densely stellate tomentose abaxially, sparsely minutely hairy adaxially, basal veins 5, lateral veins 2-3 pairs, ascending, base rounded, margin entire basally, glandular dentate distally, apex obtuse or acute. Cymes 2-4 per leaf axil, slender, 3-flowered. Infructescence stipe 1.8-2.5 cm; stipe 8-12 mm, densely pilose. Drupe subglobose, 4-5 mm in diam., sparsely stellate pilose; drupelets 1 or 2. Fl. Apr-May, fr. Jun-Jul.

> **Habait** : 
>●  Open woodlands, scrub; ca. 1000 m. SW Yunnan.
