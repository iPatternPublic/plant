苹婆(原变种) **Sterculia monosperma var. monosperma**

> [Sterculiaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Sterculiaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Sterculiaceae.pdf) >> [Sterculia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Sterculia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Sterculia.pdf)
## Description

Bark brownish black. Leaf blade base rounded or obtuse.

> **Habait** : 
> Often cultivated. SE Fujian, S Guangdong, S Guangxi, Taiwan, S Yunnan [India, Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam].

### Synonym
*Sterculia nobilis* Smith.
