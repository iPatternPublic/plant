轮环藤属 **Cyclea** Arnott ex Wight Ill. Ind. Bot. 1: 22. 1840.

> [Menispermaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Menispermaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Menispermaceae.pdf) >> [Cyclea](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Cyclea?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Cyclea.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> About 29 species: S and SE Asia; 13 species (five endemic) in China.

## Description

Vines. Petiole usually long; leaf blade peltate, palmately veined. Inflorescences axillary, terminal or on old stems, thyrsoid, usually narrow, rarely broad and sparse; bracts small. Male flowers: sepals usually 4 or 5(or 6), usually connate, 4- or 5-lobed, rarely free; petals 4 or 5, usually connate, entire or 4-8-lobed, rarely free, sometimes absent; stamens 4 or 5, connate into a peltate synandrium, anthers dehiscing transversely. Female flowers: sepals and petals 1 or 2, opposite to each other, rarely absent; carpel 1, stigma short, 3- or many lobed. Drupes obovate-globose to rotund, often slightly flat, style scar near base; endocarp bony, horseshoe-shaped, abaxially bearing 2 or 3 rows of tubercles on both surfaces of rib; condyle usually 1 or 2 cavities, often perforate between style scar and base. Seed horseshoe-shaped; embryo terete, embedded in endosperm.

### Synonym
*Paracyclea* Kud? & Yamamoto.

**19. 轮环藤属 Cyclea Arn. ex Wight**

Arn. ex Wight, Ill. Ind. Bot. 1: 22. 1840; Miers, Contr. Bot. 3: 234. 1871; Diels in Engler, Pflanzenreich IV. 94: 309. 1910; Formanin Kew Bull. 14: 68. 1960; Lo et S. Y. Zhao in Guihaia 6: 55. 1986.

藤本。叶具掌状脉，叶柄通常长而盾状着生。聚伞圆锥花序通常狭窄，很少阔大而疏松，腋生、顶生或生老茎上；苞片小；雄花：萼片通常4-5，很少6，通常合生而具4-5裂片，较少分离；花瓣4-5，通常合生，全缘或4-8裂，较少分离，有时无花瓣；雄蕊合生成盾状聚药雄蕊，花药4-5，着生在盾盘的边缘，横裂；雌花：萼片和花瓣均1-2，彼此对生，很少无花瓣；心皮1个，花柱很短，柱头3裂或较多裂。核果倒卵状球形或近圆球形，常稍扁，花柱残迹近基生；果核骨质，背肋二侧各有2-3列小瘤体，具马蹄形腔室，胎座迹通常为1-2空腔，常于花柱残迹与果梗着生处之间穿一小孔；种子有胚乳；胚马蹄形，背倚子叶半柱状。

约29种。分布亚洲南部和东南部；我国有12种，1亚种，1变型，分布于长江流域及其以南各省区。

本属模式种：C. peltata (Lam.) Diels

* 9 叶缘的毛短；雄花萼片倒卵形或匙形。——[海岛轮环藤](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Cyclea insularis?t=z)

* ~叶缘有长缘毛；雄花萼片长圆形或椭圆形；花瓣4。——[弄岗轮环藤](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Cyclea longgangensis?t=z)

* 10 雄花花萼非碟状，裂片比萼筒短很多；叶和小枝被柔毛或近无毛；叶柄多少盾状着生。——(11)

* 11 雄花花萼钟状，无毛。——(12)
* ~雄花花萼坛状，被毛。——[南轮环藤](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Cyclea tonkinensis?t=z)

* 12 聚药雄蕊伸出；花较小，雄花花萼长约2毫米或不及。——[纤花轮环藤](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Cyclea debiliflora?t=z)

* ~聚药雄蕊内藏；花较大，雄花花萼长2.5-4毫米。——[轮环藤](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Cyclea racemosa?t=z)
