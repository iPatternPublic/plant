**寡毛变种**

var. dahurica Kitag. in Rep. Inst. Sci. Res. Manch. 1: 297, 1937; S. Y. Hu in Quart Journ. Taiwan, Mus. 19: 161, 1966. ——Solidago dahurica Kitag. l. c.; Juz. in Fl. URSS 25: 42, 1959.
