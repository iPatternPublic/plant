## Description

Leaf blade membranous, abaxially gray puberulent, hairs uniform, twisted, adaxially minutely pilose; blades on proximal part of stem ovate, 6-8 × 4-5 cm, base rounded, those on distal part of stem elliptic, attenuate at both ends. Epicalyx and sepals puberulent. Petals ca. 1 cm.

> **Habait** : 
>●  Hubei.

**1c. 湖北地桃花（变种）**

Urena lobata Linn. var. henryi S. Y. Hu Fl. China Family 153: 75, Pl. 18-2, 1955.

叶膜质，下部的叶卵形，长6-8厘米，宽4-5厘米，基部圆或钝，上部的叶椭圆形，两端狭，上面被不明显疏柔毛，下面被灰色绒毛。小苞片和萼片均被绒毛；花瓣长约1厘米。

特产湖北省。
