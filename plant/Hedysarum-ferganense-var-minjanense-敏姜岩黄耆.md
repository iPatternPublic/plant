## Description

Shrublets, 5-6 cm tall. Stems densely caespitose, strongly ligneous at base. Leaves 3-5 cm. Racemes capitate.

### Statistics
Gravelly areas; ca. 4500 m. W Xinjiang (Pamir Shan) [Afghani-stan, Kashmir, N Pakistan, Tajikistan].

### Synonym
*Hedysarum minjanense* K. H. Rechinger, Biol. Skr. 9: 185. 1958.

产新疆（帕米尔）。生于高寒草原砾石质坡地和干河滩沙地，海拔高度达4 500米。一般分布于塔吉克斯坦、阿富汗东北部和克什米尔地区。
