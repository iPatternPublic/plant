## Description

Leaflets thinly leathery. Sepals (4 or)5(or 6), adaxially more subsessile glandular hairy; veins arcuate, confluent at apex.

> **Habait** : 
> Forests, forest margins, scrub, alpine meadows; 2300-3800 m. Xizang, W Yunnan [Myanmar].

### Synonym
*Astilbe henrici* Franchet in Prince Henri d'Orleans, Du Tonkin aux Indes, 378. 1896; *Rodgersia henrici* (Franchet) Franchet.
