> **Comment** : 
> This variety has reputed medicinal value (in Sichuan).

## Description
 "
Plants 80–150 cm, stout. Ultimate leaf segments lanceolate or ovate-lanceolate, margins irregularly laciniate.

> **Habait** : 
> Montane forests, grasslands; 900–2500 m. Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hubei, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan.
