> **Comment** : 
> *Ostericum* is closely allied to *Angelica*; see the taxonomic comment under that genus.

## Description

Herbs, perennial. Stem hollow, ribbed. Petiole sheaths inflated; leaf blade 2–3-ternate-pinnate. Umbels compound, terminal and lateral; bracts few, lanceolate or linear-lanceolate; bracteoles several, linear to linear-lanceolate. Calyx teeth conspicuous, triangular or ovate. Fruit oblong-ovoid, base cordate, flattened dorsally; surface covered with many convex and shining dots; dorsal ribs prominent, the lateral broadly thin-winged; vittae 1–3 in each furrow, 2–8 on commissure; mesocarp thin, hollow in mature fruit. Seed face plane. Carpophore 2-cleft to base.

### Statistics
About ten species: C and E Asia, E Europe; seven species (three endemic) in China.

* 1 Ultimate leaf segments linear or oblong-lanceolate, entire or inconspicuously serrulate.——(2)
* ~ Ultimate leaf segments ovate to broad-elliptic, margin serrate, crenate or incised.——(3)

* 2 (1)Ultimate leaf segments entire; bracts 1–3, 5–8 mm.——1  [Ostericum maximowiczii 全叶山芹](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum maximowiczii?t=foc)
* ~ Ultimate leaf segments margin inconspicuously serrate; bracts 6–8, ca. 4 mm.——2  [Ostericum citriodorum 隔山香](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum citriodorum?t=foc)

* 3 (1) Central umbels with short peduncles, lateral umbels opposite or cyclic with long peduncles; petals green.——3  [Ostericum viridiflorum 绿花山芹](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum viridiflorum?t=foc)
* ~ Central umbels with long peduncles, lateral umbels not opposite nor cyclic, with short peduncles; petals white.——(4)

* 4 (3) Fruit vittae 1–3 in each furrow, 4–8 on commissure.——(5)
* ~ Fruit vittae 1 in each furrow, 2 on commissure.——(6)

* 5 (4) Bracts 2–5; fruit suborbicular, 5–7 mm.——4  [Ostericum scaberulum 疏毛山芹](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum scaberulum?t=foc)
* ~ Bracts 1–2; fruit ellipsoid, 4–5.5 mm.——5  [Ostericum sieboldii 山芹](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum sieboldii?t=foc)

* 6 (4) Petioles acute-triangular; bracts 1–4, unequal, apex long-aristate.——6  [Ostericum huadongense 华东山芹](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum huadongense?t=foc)
* ~ Petioles rounded; bracts 4–8, equal, apex acute.——7  [Ostericum grosseserratum 大齿山芹](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum grosseserratum?t=foc)

**77．山芹属——Ostericum Hoffm.**

Hoffmann, Gen. Umbell. 162. 1814.——Gomphopetalum Turcz. in Bull. Soc. Nat. 14: 547. 1941. proparte.——Angelica sect. Ostericum Maxim. Mel. Biol. 9: 249. 1873. proparte; Hiroe, Umbell. Asia 1: 156-157. 1958. ——Angelica subgen. Ostericum Maxim. ex Drude Engl. U. Prantl, Pflanzenfam. 3 (8): 220. 1898.

二年生或多年生草本。茎直立，中空，具细棱槽或棱角。叶二至三回羽抹分裂，末回裂片宽或狭，叶下面淡绿色，细脉不明显。复伞形花序；总苞片少数，披针形或线状披针形；小总苞片数个，线形至线状披针形；花白色、绿色或黄白色；萼齿明显，三角状或卵形，宿存。果实卵状长圆形，扁平；分生果背棱稍隆起，侧棱薄，宽翅状，果皮薄膜质，透明，有光泽，外果皮细胞向外凸出，于扩大镜下明显可见呈颗粒状或点泡状突起，棱槽内有油管1-3，合生面有油管2-8；果实成熟后，中果皮处出现空隙，内果皮和中果皮紧密结合而与中果皮分离。种子扁平、胚乳腹面平直，心皮柄2裂。

本属约10种，主产于中国东北、朝鲜、日本和苏联远东地区；少数种类分布于东欧和中亚地区。我国有6种，5变种。

本属有不少种类可供作药用、饲料及提取挥发油。

本属模式种：草山芹 Ostericum pretense Hoffm.

## 检索表

* 1 叶的末回裂片线形或长圆状披针形，全缘或边缘有极细的锯齿。——(2)
* ~叶的末回裂片卵形，广椭圆形，边缘有锯齿、圆齿或缺刻。——(6)

* 2 叶的末回裂片全缘，干后边缘不皱缩。——(3)
* ~叶的末回裂片边缘密生极细的齿，干后波状皱缩。——[隔山香](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum citrodorum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 3 具细长的地下匐枝；伞辐10-17。——(4)
* ~具细长的根，有分枝；伞辐在10以下。——[高山全叶山芹](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum maximowiczii var. alpinum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 4 叶的第一次裂片不具长的小柄，小柄及末回裂片不膝曲。——(5)
* ~叶的第一次裂片不具长的小柄，长柄及末回裂片木膝曲。——[丝叶山芹](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum maximowiczii var. filisectum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 5 叶的末回裂片阔披针形至卵状披针形，宽5-9毫米。——[大全叶山芹](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum maximowiczii var. australe?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~叶的末回裂片线形或线状针形，渐尖，宽0.1-0.4毫米。——[全叶山芹（原变种）](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum maximowiczii var. maximowiczii?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 6 叶的末回裂片的锯齿内曲，分生果棱槽内有油管1-3，合生面油管4-8。——(7)
* ~叶的末回裂片卵形、广椭圆形，边缘锯齿不内曲。——(10)

* 7 叶的末回裂片菱状卵形，或卵状披针形，顶端渐尖或急尖；果实长4-6毫米，宽3-4毫米，侧棱与果体近等宽，分生果棱槽中有油管1-3，合生面油管4-6，少为8。——(8)
* ~叶的末回裂片卵圆形或长椭圆形，顶端有长尖或尾尖；果实长5-7毫米，宽4-6.5毫米，分生果棱槽中有油管2，合生面油管4，侧翅远较果体为宽。——[疏毛山芹](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum scaberulum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 8 茎光滑或基部稍有短柔毛；叶两面均无毛。——(9)
* ~茎下部被白色长毛；小叶两面脉上及边缘被糙毛。——[毛山芹](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum sieboldii form. hirsutum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 9 叶排列较紧密，大部分较狭，最下部的羽片显著变短，末回裂片通常无柄或有短柄，长2.5-8厘米，宽1-3厘米。——[狭叶山芹](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum sieboldii var. praeteritum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~叶排列较疏散，下部和上部的羽片近等长，末回裂片长5-10厘米，宽3-6厘米。——[山芹（原变种）](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum sieboldii var. sieboldi?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 10 茎有钝而较浅的沟纹；叶缘有缺刻状浅齿，常裂至主脉的1/2-2/3；花瓣白色；分生果合生面有油管2-4。——[大齿山芹](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum grosseserratum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~茎有突出而尖锐的深沟纹；叶有粗锯齿或缺刻；花瓣绿色；分生果合生面有油管2。——[绿花山芹](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ostericum viridiflorum?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)
