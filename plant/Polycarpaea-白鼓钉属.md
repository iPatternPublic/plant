## Description

Herbs or subshrubs, annual or perennial. Stems erect or diffuse. Leaves opposite or falsely verticillate with axillary clusters of leaves, sessile or petiolate; leaf blade linear to obovate; stipules membranous or hyaline. Inflorescence a cyme, terminal. Flowers numerous, 5-merous. Sepals hyaline, midvein not raised. Petals usually small, entire or 2-lobed. Stamens (2--)5. Ovary 1-loculed with few to many ovules; style undivided at apex. Fruit a capsule, 3-valved, few to several seeded. Seeds reniform, slightly flat.

### Statistics
About 50 species: mostly in the Old World tropics and subtropics, a few in the New World tropics; two species in China.

### Synonym
*Polia* Loureiro, nom. rej.

## Keylist
* 1 Leaves linear or acicular, ca. 1 mm wide, longer than internodes at least near base, erect or recurved; sepals 2--3 mm; style short, 1/3--1/2 as long as ovary——1  [P. corymbosa 白鼓钉](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Polycarpaea corymbosa?t=foc)
* ~ Leaves narrowly oblong to oblong, 1--3.5 mm wide, shorter than internodes, often mostly sharply reflexed at base; sepals ca. 4 mm; style 1--2 × as long as ovary——2  [P. gaudichaudii 大花白鼓钉](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Polycarpaea gaudichaudii?t=foc)

**7. 白鼓钉属* Polycarpaea Lam.**

Lam. in Journ. Hist. Nat. Paris 2: 8. 1792.

一年生或多年生草本。茎直立或铺散。叶对生或假轮生，叶片狭线形或长圆形；托叶膜质。花多数，排成密聚伞花序；萼片5，膜质，全透明，无脊；花瓣5，小，全缘或2齿裂；雄蕊5；子房1室，具多数胚珠；花柱合生，伸长，顶端不分裂。蒴果3瓣裂；种子肾形稍扁；胚弯。

约50种，分布于热带和亚热带地区。我国有2种。产南部和西南部。

属模式种：P. teneriffiae Lam.
