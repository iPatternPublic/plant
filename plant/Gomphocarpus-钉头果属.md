> **Comment** : 
> About 50 species: tropical Africa, two in China.

## Description

Shrubs or subshrubs, erect. Leaves opposite or whorled, short petiolate. Cymes extra-axillary, umbel-like, pendent, many flowered. Calyx glands 5 or more. Corolla rotate, deeply lobed; lobes patent or reflexed, valvate. Corona lobes 5, inserted on gynostegium, erect, hood-shaped, apex with 2 recurved or straight adaxial cusps, hood not appendaged. Filaments connate into a tube; anther appendages incurved; pollinia 2 per pollinarium, oblong, pendulous. Stigma head depressed. Follicles broadly ovoid, inflated, pericarp usually with long, soft spines or bristles. Seeds oblong.

**8. 钉头果属——Gomphocarpus R. Br.**

R. Br. in Mem. Wern. Soc. 1: 37. 1810.

灌木或半灌木，具乳汁。叶对生或轮生，线形或长圆形，叶缘常反卷，具柄，羽状脉。聚伞花序枝顶腋生，着花多朵；花萼5深裂，裂片内面基部有腺体；花冠5深裂，辐状，或反折，裂片在花蕾时镊合状排列；副花冠通常红色，兜状或舟形或凹形；花药顶端具薄膜片；花粉块每室1个，长圆形，下垂；子房由2枚离生心皮组成，柱头顶端五角状，肉质。蓇葖肿胀，外果皮有软刺；种子长圆形，顶端具白色绢质的种毛。

约50种，产于热带非洲。我国栽培1种。

本属模式种为：Gomphocarpus arborescens（L.）R. Br.
