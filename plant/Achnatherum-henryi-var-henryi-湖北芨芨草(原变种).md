湖北芨芨草(原变种) **Achnatherum henryi var. henryi**

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf) >> [Achnatherum](Achnatherum-芨芨草属.md) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Achnatherum.pdf)

## Description

Glumes 3–4.5 mm, apex apiculate or denticulate; lemma 2.5–3.5 mm, apex emarginate or entire; awn 0.8–1.2 cm. Anther apex bearded or glabrous. Fl. Apr–Jun.


> **Habait** : 
>* Forests on mountain slopes, shady roadsides, among rocks; 100–2300 m. Gansu, Hubei, Shaanxi, Sichuan.

### Synonym
*Stipa henryi* Rendle, J. Linn. Soc., Bot. 36: 382. 1904; *Oryzopsis henryi* (Rendle) Keng ex P. C. Kuo.
