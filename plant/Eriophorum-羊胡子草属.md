> **Comment** : 
> About 25 species: mostly in cool temperate, alpine, and arctic regions of N Hemisphere; seven species (one endemic) in China.

## Description

Herbs, perennial. Rhizomes creeping. Culms tufted or nearly scattered, obtusely 3-angled, clothed at base with bladeless leaf sheaths. Leaves basal and cauline; leaf blade of basal leaves slender, elongate, flat or 3-angled; cauline leaves usually reduced to a sheath. Involucral bracts spathelike or glumelike, rarely leaflike. Inflorescence a terminal simple or compound anthela or reduced to a single spikelet. Spikelets with few to many spirally arranged deciduous glumes. Glumes each subtending a bisexual flower. Perianth bristles 10-30, white or brownish red, elongating extremely after anthesis, smooth, silky. Stamens 3. Style base not distinct, not or slightly thickened; stigmas 3. Nutlet obovoid to oblong, 3-sided, smooth, apex beaked.

**2．羊胡子草属——Eriophorum Linn.**

Linn. Sp. Pl. ed. l 52.

多年生草本，丛生或近于散生，具根状茎，有时兼具匍匐根状茎。稈钝三稜柱状，具基生叶和稈生叶，稈生叶有时只有鞘而无叶片。苞片叶状、佛焰苞状或鳞片状；长侧枝聚繖花序简单或复出，顶生，具几个至多数小穗，或只一个小穗；花两性；鳞片螺旋状排列，通常下面几个鳞片内无花；下位刚毛多数，丝状，极少只有6条，开花后延长为鳞片的许多倍；雄蕊2-3个；花柱单一，基部不膨大，柱头3。小坚果三稜形。

本属在我国见诸记载的共6种，多分布于东北、西北和西南各省。

## 检索表

* 1 小穗单生或少数，一般排列为简单长侧枝聚繖花序；苞片为佛焰苞状或鳞片状或鞘状；鳞片顶端钝或渐尖，不具短尖。——(2)
* ~小穗多数排列为复出长侧枝聚繖花序；鳞片顶端具短尖。叶平张，近顶端三稜形。苞片叶状。丛毛羊胡子草 E. comosum Nees——[南羊胡子草亚属](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Subgen. Erioscirpus?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 2 小穗数个，顶生。——(3)
* ~小穗单个，顶生。叶三稜形或圆柱状，较狭，宽1-1.5毫米。——(5)

* 3 下位刚毛6条，上半部具细刺。日羊胡子草E. japonicum Maxim.——[日羊胡子草组](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Sect. Japonica?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~下位刚毛极多数，不具细刺。——(4)

* 4 鳞片具多条脉。稈较细弱。叶片扁三稜形，宽1-1.5毫米。——[细稈羊胡子草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Eriophorum gracile?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~鳞片只有1条脉（有时下部具不明显的2-3条脉）。稈较粗壮。叶片平张，近顶端三稜形，宽3-9毫米。——[宽叶羊胡子草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Eriophorum latifolium?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)
