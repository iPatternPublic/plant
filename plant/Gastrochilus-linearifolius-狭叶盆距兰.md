## Description

Stems pendulous, 15-32 cm, ca. 5 mm in diam., slender, usually branched, internodes 1.5-2 cm. Leaves distichous, well spaced; blade linear, falcate, 8-15 × 0.5-0.8 cm, apex long acuminate and with 2 or 3 awns. Inflorescence subumbellate, 3- or 4-flowered; peduncle 1-1.5 cm, slender; floral bracts ovate, ca. 2 mm, apex acute; pedicel and ovary ca. 5 mm. Flowers small, widely opening. Dorsal sepal subovate, ca. 5 × 2.5 mm, apex obtuse; lateral sepals similar to dorsal sepal, slightly narrower. Petals oblong, smaller than sepals, apex obtuse; lip with an epichile and a saccate hypochile; epichile suborbicular, ca. 2.2 × 4 mm, adaxially finely papillate on central cushion, margin irregularly fimbriate, inside villous at apex, apex rounded; hypochile subcupular, ca. 3.5 mm tall, ca. 2.5 mm in diam., with a ridge, rounded at apex. Fl. Sep.

### Statistics
On tree trunks in forests; ca. 1600 m. SE Xizang [India (Sikkim)].

**10. 狭叶盆距兰（新拟）**

Gastrochilus linearifolius Z. H. Tsi et Garay in Guihaia 12 (2): 146, fig. 1 (a-b). 1996.——G. intermedius auct. non (Griff. ex Lindl.) Kuntze: 西藏植物志5: 784, fig. 424. 1987.

茎下垂，细长，稍高，长15-32厘米，粗约5毫米，通常分枝，节间长1.5-2厘米。叶二列，彼此疏离，线形或狭镰刀状，长8-15厘米，宽5-8毫米，先端长渐尖并且具2-3条似刚毛状、不等长的裂片。伞形花序侧生于茎，具3-4朵花；花序柄纤细，长1-1.5厘米；花苞片卵形，长约2毫米，先端锐尖；花梗和子房长5毫米；花小，开展；中萼片近卵形，长约5毫米，宽2.5毫米，先端钝；侧萼片与中萼片相似而较狭；花瓣长圆形，比萼片小，先端钝；前唇半圆形，长2.2毫米，宽约4毫米，先端圆形，边缘具不规则的流苏，前端内侧具长柔毛，上面中央的垫状物被细乳突；后唇近杯状，长3.5毫米，宽2.5毫米，末端圆形而外侧具1条脊突，上端口缘抬起而比前唇高，前端具1个凹口，其两侧边缘几乎垂直。花期9月。

产西藏东南部（墨脱）。生于海拔1600米的山坡林中树干上。也产锡金（模式标本产地）。

本种很近细茎盆距兰 Gastrochilus interinedius (Griff. ex Lindl.) Kuntze 区别于后者主要在于前唇边缘具密密的流苏，前端内侧具长柔长，后唇较小。
