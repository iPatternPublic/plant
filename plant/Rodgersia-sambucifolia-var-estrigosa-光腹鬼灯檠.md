## Description

Leaflets glabrous adaxially. Fl. and fr. May-Aug.

> **Habait** : 
>* Forests, rock clefts on slopes; 2000-3700 m. SW Sichuan (Yanbian Xian), N Yunnan (Huize Xian, Lijiang Naxi Zu Zizhixian).
