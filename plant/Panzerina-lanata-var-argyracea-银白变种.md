银白变种 **Panzerina lanata var. argyracea** (Kuprianova) H. W. Li

> [Lamiaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Lamiaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Lamiaceae.pdf)>>[Panzerina](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Panzerina?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Panzerina.pdf)

## Description

Leaves palmatipartite for 1/2-2/3 their length; lobes broadly cuneate, 4-10 mm wide. Calyx teeth short subulate-spinescent, anterior teeth 3.5-4 mm. Corolla 3-3.5 cm.

> **Habait** : 
> Nei Mongol [Mongolia, Russia].

### Synonym
*Panzeria argyracea* Kuprianova, Bot. Mater. Gerb. Bot. Inst. Komarova Akad. Nauk SSSR 15: 364. 1953.
