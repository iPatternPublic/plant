## Description

Leaves 1.3-2.7 X 1-2.2 cm, conspicuously and coarsely dentate. Corolla 1.8-2.2 cm. Staminode obovoid.

> **Habait** : 
>* Among rocks; 4000-4600 m. Xizang (Nyingchi Xian).
