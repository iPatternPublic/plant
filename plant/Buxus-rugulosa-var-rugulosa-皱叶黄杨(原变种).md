皱叶黄杨**(原变种) **Buxus rugulosa var. rugulosa** (C. B. Clarke) Ridley

> [Buxaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Buxaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Buxaceae.pdf)>>[Buxus](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Buxus?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Buxus.pdf)

## Description

Shrubs, 1-2 m tall; young branches pubescent. Petiole pubescent; leaf blade rhomboid-oblong, oblong, or narrowly oblong, 1.5-2.5(-3.5) × 0.6-1.2 cm, adaxially puberulent along midrib. Fl. Mar-May, fr. Jun-Sep.


> **Habait** : 
>● Thickets on mountain slopes; 1900-3500 m. W Sichuan (Barkam, Jinchuan), NW Yunnan (Dali, Dêqên, Lijiang, Weishan).

### Synonym
*Buxus microphylla* Siebold & Zuccarini var. *platyphylla* (Schneider) Handel-Mazzetti.
