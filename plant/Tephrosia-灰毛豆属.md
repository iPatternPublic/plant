灰毛豆属 **Tephrosia** Persoon Syn. Pl. 2: 328. 1807.

> [Fabaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Fabaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Fabaceae.pdf) >> [Tephrosia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tephrosia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Tephrosia.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> More than 400 species: tropical and subtropical regions of the world; 11 species (one endemic, three introduced) in China.

## Description

Herbs, perennial or rarely annual, often suffrutescent, usually sericeous. Stipules caducous. Leaves imparipinnate; stipels absent; leaflet blades opposite, abaxially often sericeous, secondary veins to ca. 30 on each side of midvein and closely parallel, margin entire. Pseudoracemes terminal or axillary, sometimes opposite a leaf; bracts usually caducous. Bracteoles absent. Corolla white, cream-colored, or mauve, occasionally orange or red; standard reflexed, suborbicular, outside villous or sericeous. Stamens monadel-phous; vexillary stamen somewhat distinct from other 9. Ovary sessile, with trichomes, with numerous ovules. Legume flat, occa-sionally inflated, dehiscent, apex often beaked; valves twisted. Seeds 5-16 per legume, oblong, ellipsoid, or occasionally globose; radicle folded.

### Synonym
*Cracca* Linnaeus.

## Keylist

* 1 Flowers 1-2.5 cm; legume 5.5-13 cm × 5.5-14 mm——(2)
* ~ Flowers ca. 1 cm; legume 2-5 cm × 3.5-5(-6) mm——(6)

* 2 (1)Flowers 1-1.7 cm; legume 5.5-6 cm; leaflet blades obovate-elliptic, narrowly elliptic, linear-oblanceolate, or oblong-oblanceolate——(3)
* ~ Flowers 2-2.5 cm; legume 8-13 cm; leaflet blades oblong-elliptic, lanceolate-elliptic, oblong, or oblong-lanceolate——(4)

* 3 (2) Inflorescences 3-7 cm, with congested flowers; corolla white; leaflet blades abaxially velutinous, secondary veins 15-20 on each side of midvein.——4 [黄灰毛豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tephrosia vestita?t=foc)
* ~ Inflorescences to 25 cm, with scattered flowers; corolla red; leaflet blades abaxially silvery sericeous, secondary veins 9 or 10 on each side of midvein.——5 [狭叶红灰毛豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tephrosia coccinea var. stenophylla?t=foc)

* 4 (2) Pedicel 2-2.3 cm, densely rusty villous; legume 10-13 cm × ca. 14 mm, densely creamy villous.——1 [西非灰毛豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tephrosia vogelii?t=foc)
* ~ Pedicel 0.5-1 cm, densely white villous or sericeous; legume 8-10 cm × 6-8.5 mm, brown tomentose or yellow sericeous——(5)

* 5 (4) Calyx teeth ca. 1 mm, apex rounded; leaflet blades 0.6-1.4 cm in width, oblong, secondary veins 30-50 on each side of midvein; pedicel ca. 10 mm, villous; corolla white, rarely yellow or pale pink; legume brown tomentose.——2 [白灰毛豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tephrosia candida?t=foc)
* ~ Calyx teeth ca. 5 mm, apex acute; leaflet blades 1.5-2 cm in width, oblong-lanceolate, secondary veins 20 on each side of midvein; pedicel ca. 5 mm, sericeous; corolla red; legume yellow sericeous.——3 [银灰毛豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tephrosia kerrii?t=foc)

* 6 (1) Inflorescences 10-25 cm (but only ca. 2 cm in T. purpurea var. glabra), flowers scattered——(7)
* ~ Inflorescences 1-5 cm, flowers congested——(8)

* 7 (6) Calyx teeth ± equal; legume with sparse appressed trichomes, ca. 6-seeded.——6 [灰毛豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tephrosia purpurea?t=foc)
* ~ Calyx teeth unequal, most abaxial tooth longer and narrow, other ones shorter and broad; legume densely brown tomentose, 7-9-seeded.——7 [长序灰毛豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tephrosia noctiflora?t=foc)

* 8 (6) Legume with 7-14 seeds——(9)
* ~ Legume with 5-7 seeds——(10)

* 9 (8) Inflorescences with 1-3 flowers; legume apex with curved or hooked beak.——8 [矮灰毛豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tephrosia pumila?t=foc)
* ~ Inflorescences with 4-8 flowers; legume apex with a short straight beak.——9 [西沙灰毛豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tephrosia luzonensis?t=foc)

* 10 (8) Leaves 13-17-foliolate; leaflet blades oblanceolate, abaxially sparsely pubescent, secondary veins ca. 9 on each side of midvein, mauve, conspicuous; legume slightly curved at tip, 3.5-4 cm.——10 [台湾灰毛豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tephrosia ionophlebia?t=foc)
* ~ Leaves 11- or 13-foliolate; leaflet blades obovate, both surfaces densely sericeous, secondary veins 5-8 on each side of midvein, almost inconspicuous; legume straight, 2-2.5 cm.——11 [卵叶灰毛豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tephrosia obovata?t=foc)

**灰毛豆属 Tephrosia**

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第40卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/40) >> 212页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/40/212y.pdf)

**19.灰毛豆属Tephrosia Pers.**

Pers. Syn. Pl. 2: 328. 1807, nom. conserv.: Baker in Hook. f. Fl. Brit. Ind. 2: 110. 1876; Taub. in Engl. u. Prantl, Nat. Pflanzenf. 3 (3): 269. 1894; Merr. Philip. Journ. Sci. Bot. 5: 68. 1910; 中国主要植物图说·豆科264. 1955; Hutch. Gen. Fl. Pl. 1: 396. 1964;海南植物志2: 258. 1965; Gillett et al. Fl. Trop. E. Afr. Leg. 3, Pap. 1: 157. 1971; Ali in Fl. W. Pakistan 100: 58. 1977; Geesink in Polhill & Raven, Adv. Leg. Syst. 1: 260. 1981.

一年或多年生草本，有时为灌木状，奇数羽状复叶；具托叶，无小托叶；小叶多数（我国不产单叶和掌状复叶类型），对生，全缘，通常被绢毛，下面尤密，侧脉多数，与中脉成锐角平行伸向叶缘，连结成边缘脉序。总状花序顶生或与叶对生和腋生，有时花序轴缩短成近头状或伞状；具苞片，小苞片常缺；花具梗；花萼钟状，萼齿5，近等长或下方1齿较长，上方2齿多少连合；花冠多为紫红色或白色，旗瓣背面具柔毛或绢毛，瓣柄明显，瓣片圆形，常后反，翼瓣和龙骨瓣无毛，多少相粘连；雄蕊二体，对旗瓣的1枚花丝与雄蕊管分离，但中部常接触，花丝基部扩大并弯曲，具疣体，其余9枚花丝略等长，三分之二到五分之四连合成管，顶端不扩大，花药同型；花盘浅皿状，具1裂口；子房线形，无柄，具柔毛，胚珠通常多数，花柱向上弯，线形或锥形，扁平或有时扭曲，被绢毛或被稀疏柔毛，柱头头状或点状，无毛或具画笔状簇毛。荚果线形或长圆形，扁平或在种子处稍凸起，种子间无真正的隔膜，爆裂，果瓣扭转，果顶端具喙，直或沿腹缝线下弯；种子长圆形呈椭圆形，珠柄短，有时具小种阜。

约400种。广布热带和亚热带地区，多数产非洲，欧洲不产。我国有11种，3变种。

本属模式种: Tephrosia villosa (Linn.) Pers.
