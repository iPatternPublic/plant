## Description

Whole plant densely dull brown long spreading villous. Stipules 1.5-3 cm; petiole wingless.

> Reference: 
> This taxon was misidentified as *Maughania* (*\"Moghania\"*) *fer-ruginea* (Wallich ex Bentham) H. L. Li in Fl. Hainan. (2: 311. 1965).This taxon was misidentified as *Maughania* (*\"Moghania\"*) *fer-ruginea* (Wallich ex Bentham) H. L. Li in Fl. Hainan. (2: 311. 1965).

**8b. 海南千斤拔（变种）**

var. hainanensis Y. T. Wei et S. Lee in Guihaia 5:169. 1985.

本变种与原变种的区别在于全株各部密被灰褐色开展的长绒毛；叶柄无翅，托叶长1.5-3厘米。

产海南。模式标本采自海南白沙。
