糙叶五加(原变种) **Eleutherococcus henryi var. henryi**

> [Araliaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Araliaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Araliaceae.pdf)>>[Eleutherococcus](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Eleutherococcus?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Eleutherococcus.pdf)

## Description

Leaflets abaxially ± pubescent, especially on secondary veins. Umbels 2-3 cm in diam. Pedicels glabrous or sparsely pubescent.

> **Habait** : 
>● Scrub fields, forest margins, roadsides, mountain slopes; 800-3200 m. Anhui, Henan, Hubei, Jiangxi, Shaanxi, Shanxi, Sichuan, Zhejiang.

### Synonym
*Acanthopanax henryi* (Oliver) Harms.
