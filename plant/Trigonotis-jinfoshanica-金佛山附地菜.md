 **Trigonotis jinfoshanica** W. T. Wang

> [Boraginaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Boraginaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Boraginaceae.pdf) >> [Trigonotis](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Trigonotis?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Trigonotis.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> Name appeared after publication of the family treatment for the Flora of China.

> **Habait** : 
>* Dry places near mouth of cave, shaded moist places, shaded rock fissures; 1900-2000 m. Chongqing: Nanchuan: Jinfo Shan, Gufodang.
