浙贝母 (原变种) **Fritillaria thunbergii var. thunbergii**

> [Liliaceae](http://iplant.cn/info/Liliaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Liliaceae.pdf) >> [Fritillaria](http://iplant.cn/info/Fritillaria?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Fritillaria.pdf)


> **Comment** : 
> Widely cultivated in China for its bulbs, which are used medicinally.

## Description

Bulb of 2(or 3) scales, 1.5--3 cm in diam. Stem 50--80 cm. Leaves mostly alternate. 2* n* = 24*.


> **Habait** : 
>* Bamboo forests, shady and moist places; near sea level to 600 m. Anhui, Jiangsu, Zhejiang.

### Synonym
* Uvularia cirrhosa* Thunberg, Fl. Jap. 136. 1784; * Fritillaria austroanhuiensis* Y. K. Yang & J. K. Wu; * F. collicola* Hance; * F. verticillata* Willdenow var. * thunbergii* (Miquel) Baker.
