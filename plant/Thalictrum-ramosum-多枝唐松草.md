多枝唐松草 **Thalictrum ramosum** B. Boivin

> [Ranunculaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Ranunculaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Ranunculaceae.pdf) >> [Thalictrum](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Thalictrum?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Thalictrum.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> This species is similar to * Thalictrum fortunei,* but differs in having small sepals and many narrowly ovoid achenes; it is also similar to * T. javanicum,* which differs in being taller and having inflorescences much branched and many flowered, sepals long, and filaments broader than anthers.
>
> * Thalictrum ramosum * is used medicinally.

## Description

Plants 12--45 cm tall, glabrous. Rootlets never enlarged. Stems branched, sulcate. Leaves both basal and cauline; petiole 7--9 cm; leaf blade 2- or 3-ternate, 7--15 cm; leaflet blade broadly ovate, broadly obovate, or orbicular, 0.7--2 × 0.5--1.5 cm, herbaceous, base rounded or subcordate, apex obtuse, inconspicuously 3-lobed; lobes acute at apex; veins raised abaxially, flat adaxially. Inflorescence monochasial. Pedicel filiform, 5--10 mm. Sepals 4, early deciduous, white, tinged purplish, ovate, ca. 2 mm. Filament base filiform, 4--6 × as long as anther, apex narrowly oblanceolate; anther tinged yellow, ca. 0.7 mm. Carpels (6--)8--16; style recurved, slender, ca. 2 mm, slightly longer than ovary. Achenes sessile; body narrowly ovoid or lanceolate, 3.5--4.5 mm; veins ca. 8. Fl. Apr, fr. May--Jun.

> **Habait** : 
>* Scrub, hills; 500--1000 m. Guangxi, Hunan, Sichuan.

**多枝唐松草 Thalictrum ramosum**

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第27卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/27) >> 523页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/27/523.pdf)

**4．多枝唐松草（中国药用植物志）水黄连、软杆子、软水黄连（四川）图版121：5；图版119，120：3；图版122：4-6**

Thalictrum ramosum Boivin in Journ. Arn. Arb. 26: 115, pl. 1, f. 12-15. 1945; 中国药用植物志7: 图328. 1964; 中国高等植物图鉴1: 672, 图1343. 1972.

植株全部无毛。茎高12-45厘米，有细纵槽，自基部之上分枝。基生叶数个，与茎下部叶有长柄，为二至三回三出复叶；叶片长7-15厘米；小叶草质，宽卵形、近圆形或宽倒卵形，长0.7-2厘米，宽0.5-1.5厘米，顶端钝，有短尖，基部圆形或浅心形，不明显三浅裂，边缘有疏钝齿，脉在表面平，在背面稍隆起，脉网明显，小叶柄长0.6-1.5厘米；叶柄长7-9厘米，基部有膜质短鞘。复单歧聚花序圆锥状；花梗丝形，长5-10毫米；萼片4，淡堇色或白色，卵形，长约2毫米，早落；花药淡黄色，长圆形，长约0.7毫米，花丝长为花药的4-6倍，比花药窄，上部狭倒披针形，下部变为丝形；心皮（6-）8-16，长约2毫米，花柱细，比子房稍长，向外弯曲，沿腹面生柱头组织。瘦果无柄，狭卵形或披针形，长3.5-4.5毫米，有8条细纵肋，宿存花柱长0.3-0.5毫米，拳卷。4月开花，5-6月结果。

分布于四川、湖南西部、广西西北部（凤山）。生海拔540-950米间丘陵或低山灌丛中。模式标本采自四川灌县。

全草供药用，治目赤、热痢、黄疸等症（中国药用植物志）。

本种最近华东唐松草T. fortunei，区别在于萼片较小，心皮数目较多，瘦果为狭卵球形。也近爪哇唐松草T. javanicum，但后者较高大，花序分枝较多，有较多花，萼片较长，花丝比花药宽，可以区别。
