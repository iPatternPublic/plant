## Description

Petiole ca. 5 mm, densely stellate pilose; leaf blade orbicular, 0.5-1.3 cm in diam., both surfaces stellate hirsute, margin crenate. Flower solitary. Pedicel ca. 3 cm. Calyx stellate tomentose, lobes apically ciliate. Filament tube long hirsute.


> **Habait** : 
>●  Guangdong.
