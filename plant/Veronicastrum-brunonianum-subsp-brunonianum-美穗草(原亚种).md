美穗草 (原亚种) **Veronicastrum brunonianum subsp. brunonianum**

> [Scrophulariaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Scrophulariaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Scrophulariaceae.pdf) >> [Veronicastrum](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Veronicastrum?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Veronicastrum.pdf)

## Description

Stems terete, wingless. Filaments hairy. Fl. Jul-Sep.

> **Habait** : 
> Ravines, shaded slopes with grasses, under forests; 1500-3000 m. Guizhou (Fangjing Shan, Panxian Tequ), W Hubei, Sichuan (E of Erlang Shan), Xizang (Mainling Xian), Yunnan [Bhutan, Nepal, Sikkim].

### Synonym
*Calorhabdos brunoniana* Bentham, Bot. Reg. 21: ad t. 1770. no. 72. 1835.
