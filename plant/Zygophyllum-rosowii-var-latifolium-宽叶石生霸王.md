宽叶石生霸王 **Zygophyllum rosowii var. latifolium** (Schrenk) Popov

> [Zygophyllaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Zygophyllaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Zygophyllaceae.pdf)>>[Zygophyllum](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Zygophyllum?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Zygophyllum.pdf)

## Description

Plants 15-20 cm tall. Leaflet blades rotund to oblong, 1.5-2.5 cm, apex obtuse. Capsule 3-5 × 0.5-0.7 cm.

> **Habait** : 
> Rocky areas with gravel and sand. Gansu, Nei Mongol, Xinjiang [Kazakhstan, Mongolia].

### Synonym
*Zygophyllum latifolium* Schrenk, Bull. Cl. Phys.-Math. Acad. Imp. Sci. Saint-Pétersbourg 2: 198. 1844.
