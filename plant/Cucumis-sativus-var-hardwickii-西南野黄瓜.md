## Description

Fruit oblong or subglobose, 5-6 cm, smooth.

### Statistics
Forests, thickets, mountain slopes; 700-2000 m. Guangxi, Guizhou, Yunnan [NE India, Myanmar, Nepal, Thailand].

### Synonym
*Cucumis hardwickii* Royle, Ill. Bot. Himal. Mts. 1: 220. 1835; *C. sativus* f. *hardwickii* (Royle) W. J. de Wilde & Duyfjes; *C. sativus* [unranked] *hardwickii* (Royle) Alefeld.

**3b. 西南野黄瓜（变种）**

var. hardwickii (Royle) Alef., Landwirthsch. Fl. 196. 1866. ——C. hardwickii Royle, Ill. Himal. 220. t. 47, f. 3. 1835; Cogn. u. Harms in Engl., Pflanzenr. 88 (IV. 275. 2): 143. 1924.

与原变种的区别在于果实较小，长圆形或近球形，长5-6厘米，径4-5厘米，外面平滑，无瘤状突起。

产云南、贵州和广西。常生于海拔700-2000米的山坡、林下、路旁及灌丛中。印度东北部、尼泊尔、缅甸、泰国也有。
