## Description

Leaves abaxially waxy white papillate.

> **Habait** : 
> Forest margins, thickets, meadows on mountain slopes, valleys; 800--2700 m. Guizhou, Hubei, Hunan, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Fragaria mairei* H. Léveillé, Repert. Spec. Nov. Regni Veg. 11: 300. 1912.
