## Description

Spikelets obviously hairy with clavate hairs, pubescence varying from short and appressed to longer, dense and spreading; upper glume 1/4–1/2 as long as spikelet.


> **Habait** : 
> Grasslands; ca. 1800 m. Yunnan [Bhutan, India, Myanmar, Nepal, Pakistan, Sri Lanka].

### Synonym
*Agrostis pilosa* Retzius; *Digitaria puberula* Link; *D. roy-leana* (Nees ex J. D. Hooker) Prain; *Paspalum royleanum* Nees ex J. D. Hooker; *Setaria stricta* (Roth ex Roemer & Schultes) Kunth.
