## Description

Leaf blade ovate to oblong-ovate, 2–3.5 × 1–2 cm, abaxially densely tomentose. Inflorescences 3–7-flowered.


> **Habait** : 
> Slopes, river banks, thickets; 1000--3000 m. Gansu, Hubei, Shaanxi, Sichuan.
