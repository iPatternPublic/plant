## Description

Leaf blade abaxially sparsely pilose, adaxially similar or glabrous. Capitula small. Ray florets small; lamina ca. 1 mm.

### Statistics
● Mountain slopes; ca. 2700 m. Sichuan (Kangding).

### Synonym
*Dendranthema lavandulifolium* var. *discoideum* (Handel-Mazzetti) C. Shih.
