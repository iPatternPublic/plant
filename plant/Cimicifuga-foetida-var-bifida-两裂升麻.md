## Description

Leaflets not becoming black when dried, usually 7--10 × 4--7 cm, abaxially white pubescent. Bracts subulate, ca. 1 mm, caducous. Petals 2-parted nearly to middle.


> **Habait** : 
>* About 3300 m. Xizang (Mainling Xian).
