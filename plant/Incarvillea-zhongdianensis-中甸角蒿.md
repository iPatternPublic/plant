 **Incarvillea zhongdianensis** Grey-Wilson

> [Bignoniaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Bignoniaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Bignoniaceae.pdf)>>[Incarvillea](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Incarvillea?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Incarvillea.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> * ca. 3200 m. Yunnan (Zhongdian).
> Widely cultivated as an ornamental by botanical gardens worldwide.
> 
> Name appeared after publication of the family treatment for the Flora of China.
