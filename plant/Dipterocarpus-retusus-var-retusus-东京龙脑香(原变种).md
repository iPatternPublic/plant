## Description

Parts glabrescent.

> **Habait** : 
> Humid valley rain forests, dense forests on limestone; below 1000 m. SE Xizang, SE and W Yunnan [India, Indonesia, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam].
