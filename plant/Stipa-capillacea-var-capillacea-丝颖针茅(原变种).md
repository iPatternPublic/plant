丝颖针茅(原变种) **Stipa capillacea var. capillacea**

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf) >> [Stipa](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Stipa?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Stipa.pdf)
## Description

Glumes 2.5–3 cm. Lemma ca. 10 mm. Anthers ca. 4 mm. Fl. and fr. Jul–Sep.

> **Habait** : 
> Alpine scrub, meadows, hills, montane plains, river banks; 2900–5000 m. Gansu, Qinghai, Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan [Bhutan, N India, Kashmir, Nepal].

### Synonym
*Stipa koelzii* R. R. Stewart.
