## Description

Branchlets and ovary glabrous.

### Statistics
Weed-tree forests in valleys, mixed forests of coniferous and deciduous broad-leaved trees, *Tsuga* forests; 2000-2600 m. Yunnan [Bhutan, India].

### Synonym
*Dodecadenia griffithii* J. D. Hooker, Fl. Brit. India 5: 181. 1886.
