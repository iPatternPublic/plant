## Description

Culms ca. 50 cm. Leaf sheaths and blades glabrous except for ciliolate sheath margins. Spikelets glabrous, apex without white beard (but pedicels loosely hirsute).


> **Habait** : 
>* Roadsides, grassy places; ca. 800 m. Yunnan.
