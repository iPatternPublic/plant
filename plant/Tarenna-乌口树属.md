> [Rubiaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Rubiaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Rubiaceae.pdf) >> [Tarenna](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Tarenna.pdf)


> **Comment** : 
> About 370 species: tropical and subtropical Africa, Asia, Madagascar, and Pacific islands; 18 species (12 endemic) in China.

## Description

Shrubs or trees, unarmed, often drying blackened. Raphides absent. Leaves opposite, sometimes with domatia; stipules persistent or occasionally tardily deciduous, interpetiolar or united around stem, triangular, often aristate. Inflorescences terminal, sometimes displaced to pseudoaxillary by subsequent growth, cymose to corymbiform, few to many flowered, sessile or pedunculate, bracteate or bracts reduced. Flowers pedicellate or sessile, bisexual, monomorphic, often fragrant. Calyx limb 5-lobed. Corolla white, pale green, or yellow, funnelform or salverform, inside glabrous or pubescent in throat; lobes 5, convolute in bud, usually strongly reflexed at anthesis. Stamens 5, inserted in corolla throat, exserted; filaments short or reduced; anthers dorsifixed. Ovary 2-celled, ovules 1 to numerous in each cell on axile placentas; stigma fusiform or linear, sulcate or striate, shortly 2-lobed at apex, exserted. Fruit baccate, leathery or thinly fleshy, globose to ellipsoid, black or perhaps sometimes white, with calyx limb deciduous; seeds several, medium-sized, plano-convex or concavo-concave, testa membranous, leathery, or crustaceous; endosperm fleshy or corneous; embryo small; cotyledon small, leaflike.


> Reference: 
> The name *Chomelia* Linnaeus was applied to these plants for many years; however, the later homonym *Chomelia* Jacquin, which applies to a wholly neotropical genus, is now conserved against the Linnaean name, so the Old World plants formerly known under *Chomelia* are now correctly known under *Tarenna*.The name *Chomelia* Linnaeus was applied to these plants for many years; however, the later homonym *Chomelia* Jacquin, which applies to a wholly neotropical genus, is now conserved against the Linnaean name, so the Old World plants formerly known under *Chomelia* are now correctly known under *Tarenna*.

**55. 乌口树属 Tarenna Gaertn.**

Gaertn. Fruct. 1: 139, t. 28. 1788; DC. Prodr. 4: 395. 1830. ——Chomelia L. (non Jacq.) Opera Varia 210. 1758; K. Schum. in Engl. u. Prantl, Nat. Pflanzenfam. IV (4): 74. 1891. ——Webera Schreher, Gen. Pl. 794. 1791; Hook. f. in Benth. et Hook. f. Gen. Pl. 2: 86. 1873.

灌木或乔木。叶对生，具柄，干时常呈黑褐色；托叶生在叶柄间，基部合生或离生，常脱落。花组成顶生、多花或少花、常为伞房状的聚伞花序，有或无小苞片；萼管的形状各式，顶部5裂，裂片脱落，很少宿存；花冠漏斗状或高脚碟状，冠管短或长，喉部无毛或被毛，顶部5裂，稀4裂，裂片广展或外反，旋转排列；雄蕊与花冠裂片同数，着生于花冠喉部，花丝短或无，花药背着；花盘环状；子房2室，花柱伸长，无毛或被柔毛，柱头纺锤形或线形，有槽纹，伸出，胚珠每室数颗至多颗，很少2(-1)颗，生于肉质的中轴胎座上。浆果革质或肉质，2室，具1至多数种子；种子平凸或凹陷，种皮膜质、革质或脆壳质，胚乳肉质或骨质；胚小，子叶小，叶状。

约370种，分布于亚洲的热带和亚热带、大洋洲和非洲热带地区。我国有17种、1变型，产于西南部至东部。

本属模式种：Tarenna asiatica (Linn.) Kuntze ex K. Schun.

## 检索表

* 1 种子少至多数；子房每室有胚珠2至多数。——(2)
* ~种子1-2颗；子房每室有胚珠1颗。——(16)

* 2 花萼裂片非钻状，长在2毫米以下。——(3)
* ~花萼裂片钻状，长4-5毫米；果有种子8-32颗；叶披针形，长5-32.5厘米，宽1-5厘米，两面多少被毛。——[广西乌口树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna lanceolata?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 3 花冠管与花冠裂片近等长或短于花冠裂片。——(4)
* ~花冠管比花冠裂片长。——(13)

* 4 叶两面无毛。——(5)
* ~叶两面被毛或仅下面被毛。 ——(8)

* 5 枝非上述颜色；中脉仅在叶下面凸起；托叶常宿存。——(6)
* ~枝淡黄白色或灰白色；中脉在叶两面凸起；托叶脱落。——[白皮乌口树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna depauperata?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 6 侧脉7-13对，较粗，明显；叶柄长2-5厘米；花序较大，长10-15厘米，宽15-20厘米；叶较大，长13-25厘米，宽5-12厘米。——(7)
* ~侧脉4-6对，纤细，不明显或稍明显；叶柄长0.8-2厘米；花序较小，长宽均4-9厘米；叶较小，长5-15厘米，宽1.5-5厘米。——[披针叶乌口树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna lancilimba?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 7 侧脉8-13对；花萼被短柔毛；花序近无毛或被短柔毛，长约15厘米，宽约20厘米；子房每室有胚珠4颗。——[宽序乌口树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna laticorymbosa?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~侧脉7-8对；花萼无毛；花序无毛，长约10厘米，宽约15厘米；子房每室有胚珠2颗。——[锡兰玉心花](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna zeylanica?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)


* 8 叶两面被毛。——(9)
* ~叶仅下面被毛。——(11)

* 9 花冠外面被毛。——(10)
* ~花冠外面无毛；种子1-6颗。——[滇南乌口树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna pubinervis?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 10 花冠长1.7-1.9厘米，花冠裂片长约1.2厘米，远较冠管长；叶侧脉较疏，6-8对。——[崖州乌口树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna laui?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~花冠长约1.2厘米，花冠裂片较冠管稍长或近等长；叶侧脉较密，8-12对。——[白花苦灯笼](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna mollissima?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 11 花冠管外面无毛；叶下面被毛。——(12)
* ~花冠管外面被短柔毛；叶下面仅在中脉和侧脉上有短柔毛；叶披针形或披针状长圆形，长12-29厘米，宽4.5-10.5厘米。——[长叶乌口树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna wangii?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 12 子房每室有胚珠5-6颗；花梗纤细，长0.5-1厘米；果椭圆形；叶下面疏被紧贴的短硬毛，在脉上较密。——[薄叶玉心花](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna gracilipes?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~子房每室有胚珠2颗；花梗较粗，长4-7毫米；果近球形；叶下面散生近乳突状的短柔毛或近无毛，常稍粗糙，在脉上被毛稍长和稍密。——[海南乌口树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna tsangii?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 13 花冠外面无毛；叶下面无毛或被疏毛。——(14)
* ~花冠外面被柔毛；叶下面密被柔毛。——[云南乌口树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna yunnanensis?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 14 花萼裂片三角形，无毛，长0.5-1.5毫米；叶两面无毛或下面被极疏的短柔毛。——(15)
* ~花萼裂片三角状披针形，有短柔毛，长约1.5毫米；叶上面无毛或沿中脉被疏短毛，罕有极疏的短伏毛，下面除中脉和侧脉被短柔毛外，其余部分常被疏短柔毛或乳突状毛，有时无毛。——[尖萼乌口树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna acutisepala?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 15 子房每室有胚珠6-9颗；萼管被疏短柔毛，萼裂片长0.8-1.5毫米；叶纸质或膜质，侧脉6-7对。——[华南乌口树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna austrosinensis?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~子房每室有胚珠约17颗；萼管无毛，萼裂片长约0.5毫米；叶革质，侧脉7-9对。——[多籽乌口树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna polysperma?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 16 叶上面无毛；花梗几无或长2-5毫米。——[假桂乌口树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna attenuata?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~叶上面被疏糙伏毛；花梗长1.8-3厘米。——[长梗乌口树](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Tarenna sinica?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)
