狭叶果寄生 **Taxillus liquidambaricola var. neriifolius** H. S. Kiu

> [Loranthaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Loranthaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Loranthaceae.pdf) >> [Taxillus](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Taxillus?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Taxillus.pdf)


> **Comment** : 
> Recorded hosts include *Daphniphyllum* *oldhamii*, *Myrsine* *seguinii*, *Nerium* *indicum*, and *Osmanthus* *fragrans*.

## Description

Leaf blade sublanceolate to narrowly oblong, 4-7.5 × 1.5-2.8 cm, lateral veins prominent. Umbels 2- or 3-flowered. Corolla scarlet, 2.2-2.5 cm. Berry 7-8 × ca. 5 mm. Fl. Aug-Oct, fr. Oct-Nov.


> **Habait** : 
>* Forests, mountain slopes, valleys; 500-700 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Hainan, SE Yunnan.
