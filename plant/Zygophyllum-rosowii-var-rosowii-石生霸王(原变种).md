> [Zygophyllaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Zygophyllaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Zygophyllaceae.pdf)>>[Zygophyllum](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Zygophyllum?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Zygophyllum.pdf)

## Description

Plants 10-15 cm tall. Leaflet blades ovate, 0.8-1.8 cm, apex acuminate to obtuse. Capsule 1.8-2.5 × ca. 0.5 cm.

> **Habait** : 
> Gravel hills, alluvial gravel slopes, rocky steep slopes. Gansu, Xinjiang [Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Mongolia, Tajikistan].
