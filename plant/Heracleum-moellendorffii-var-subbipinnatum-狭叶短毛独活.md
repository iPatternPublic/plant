## Description

Basal and lower leaves almost 2-pinnate, primary pinnae 3–4 pairs, ultimate segments ovate-lanceolate.

> **Habait** : 
> Open forests, forest margins, alpine meadows; 1000–3000 m. Hebei, Heilongjiang, Jilin, Nei Mongol [Korea].
