> [Lamiaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Lamiaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Lamiaceae.pdf) >> [Phlomis](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Phlomis?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Phlomis.pdf)
## Description

Stems 30-50 cm tall, sparsely retrorse minutely hispid. Petiole persistent, 4.5-13 cm; stem leaf blades triangular-ovate, 14-19 &amp;#215; 7.5-11.5 cm, adaxially stellate hispidulous (hairs with long central arms), abaxially stellate pubescent, with scattered elevated glands, base obliquely cordate, margin dentate, apex acuminate. Verticillasters 1 or 2, 10-18-flowered; floral leaves with petiole ca. 8 mm, blade ca. 6.5 &amp;#215; 3 cm, base shallowly cordate, apex long acuminate; bracts linear-subulate, 4-6 mm, bristly glandular puberulent. Calyx campanulate, ca. 10 &amp;#215; 5.5 mm, densely stellate puberulent outside, veins spreading bristly; teeth semicircular, 1-1.5 mm, margin hirsute, apical spines rigid, 2-3 mm. Corolla yellow-white, ca. 1.4 cm; tube ca. 7 mm, glabrous except for partly pubescent back outside, pilose annulate inside; upper lip ca. 7 mm, margin denticulate to subentire, white villous outside; lower lip ca. 6 &amp;#215; 8 mm, margin pubescent; middle lobe obovate, ca. 5 &amp;#215; 4 mm, apex slightly undulate; lateral lobes ovate. Stamens included; filaments long hairy, posterior 2 with a long, reflexed, apically hooked basal appendage. Nutlets glabrous. Fl. Jul-Sep.

> **Habait** : 
>* *Abies* forests; 3600-3900 m. Sichuan, Xizang, Yunnan.

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第65(2)卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/65(2)) >> 460页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/65(2)/460.pdf)
**29．刺毛糙苏**

Phlomis setifera Bur. et Franch. in Journ. de Bot. 5: 149. 1891, non est Phlomis setigera Falc. ex Benth. 1848; Diets in Pedde, Repert. Sp. Nov. Beih. 12: 477. 1922; Kudo in Mem. Fac. Sci. Agr. Taihoku Univ. 2: 212. 1929, excl. syn. Phlomis megalantha Diels 1929; Hand. -Mazz. in Act. Hort. Gothob. 13: 347. 1939——Phlomis bracteosa auct. non Royle: Dunn in Notes Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 8: 169. 1913; 6: 187. 1915, p. p.

多年生草本。茎高30-50厘米，基部被宿存叶鞘，四棱形，具槽，被极疏向下的短硬毛。茎生叶三角状卵形，长14-19厘米，宽7.5-11.5厘米，先端渐尖，基部偏斜的心形，边缘为具胼胝尖的牙齿状，苞叶卵状披针形，长约6.5厘米，宽约3厘米，先端长渐尖，基部浅心形，叶片均较薄，近膜质，上面橄榄绿色，被中枝特长的星状短硬毛，下面较淡，被星状短柔毛及满布的隆起腺点。轮伞花序10-18花，1或2个生于主茎及分枝顶端；苞片线状钻形，长4-6毫米，被具节刺毛及腺微柔毛。花萼钟形，长约1厘米，宽约5.5毫米，外面脉上被平展具节刺毛，其余部分密被星状微柔毛，齿半圆形，长1-1.5毫米，端具长2-3毫米的坚硬刺尖，边缘被长硬毛。花冠黄白色，长约1.4厘米，冠筒长约7毫米，外面背部上方被短柔毛，余部无毛，内面在中部稍下有斜向间断毛环，冠檐二唇形，上唇长约7毫米，外面被白色长柔毛，边缘具小齿或近全缘，自内面被髯毛，下唇长约6毫米，宽约8毫米，外面除边缘外被短柔毛，3圆裂，中裂片倒卵形，长约5毫米，宽约4毫米，先端微波状，侧裂片卵形，较小。雄蕊内藏，花丝有长毛，后对基部即在毛环上面有向上反折的长附属器，其先端呈钩状。花柱先端极不等的2裂。小坚果无毛。花期7-9月。

产四川西部；生于冷杉林下，海拔3800-3900米。模式标本采自四川康定。
