> **Comment** : 
> * Grassy scrub on waste mountains; ca. 1300 m. Zhejiang (Suichang Xian).

## Description

Stipules semiovate, margin laciniate. Petiole 7--10(--15) mm. Drupe black.
> **Habait** : 
>* Grassy scrub on waste mountains; ca. 1300 m. Zhejiang (Suichang Xian).
