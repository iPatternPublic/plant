> In addition, the following five species have been recorded as cultivated in China (FRPS 9(3): 105–109. 1987): the mainly European *Elytrigia elongata* (Host) Nevski, *E. intermedia* (Host) Nevski, *E. juncea* (Linnaeus) Nevski, and *E. trichophora* (Link) Nevski, and the North American *E. smithii* (Rydberg) Nevski.
>
> These species are very valuable hay and pasture plants.

## Description

Plants perennial, with or without rhizomes. Leaf sheath of cauline leaves split almost to base. Spike erect or nodding, linear; rachis usually not disarticulating. Spikelets 1 per node, sessile, clearly laterally compressed, with 3–10 florets; rachilla disarticulating below glumes. Glumes oblong, ovate, or lanceolate, unequal, usually asymmetric, distinctly (3–)5–7-veined, with transverse scar at base. Lemma lanceolate, 5–7-veined, rounded abaxially or keeled only at apex; callus usually glabrous. Palea slightly shorter than lemma, ciliate along keels. Caryopsis usually adherent to lemma and palea, oblong, with longitudinal groove adaxially. *x* = 7.

### Statistics
About 40 species: subtropical and warm-temperate regions; two species in China.

### Synonym
*Braconotia* Godron, nom. illeg. superfl.

## Keylist

* 1 Plants with long rhizomes; culms not tufted; leaf blade 0.5–1 cm wide; spike 10–18 cm; lemma apex acuminate or with awn to 8 mm——1  [偃麦草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Elytrigia repens?t=foc)
* ~ Plants with short rhizomes; culms usually densely tufted; leaf blade to 0.3 cm wide; spike 7–11 cm; lemma apex with awn 15–23 mm.——2  [曲芒偃麦草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Elytrigia gmelinii?t=foc)

**偃麦草属 Elytrigia**

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第9(3)卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/9(3)) >> 104页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/9(3)/104y.pdf)

**10. 偃麦草属* ——Elytrigia Desv.**

Desv. Nouv. Bull. Soc. Philom. Paris 2: 191. 1810.

多年生，具根状茎。穗状花序直立，小穗含3-10余小花，两侧扁压，无柄，单生于穗轴之两侧，以其侧面对向穗轴的扁平面，顶生小穗则以其背腹面对向穗轴的扁平面；无芒或具短芒，成熟时通常自穗轴上整个脱落；颖披针形或长圆形，无脊，具(3)5-7(11)彼此接近的脉，光滑无毛或被柔毛，基部具横沟；外稃披针形，具5脉，无毛或被柔毛，基盘通常无毛。颖果长圆形，顶端有毛，腹面具纵沟。

> 模式种：Elvtrigia repens (Linn.) Nevski (Triticum repens Linn.) 产于欧洲。

本属全世界约有40种，分布于两半球的寒温带，我国连栽培者共6种，多为优良牧草，由于与小麦属亲缘关系较近，有些种已被用于小麦杂交育种的材料。

* 偃麦草属（分科检索表附录）, 属名字义不明，根据 Ascherson et Graebner 二氏谓可能系由 Elymus 与 Triticum 2属名之缩写，一如:“电报通信处”中所缩写之字。

* 1 颖与外稃先端渐尖、突尖或具短尖头。——(2)
* ~颖与外稃先端截平或钝圆形。——(3)

* 2 颖具5-7脉，边缘膜质，外稃长于内稃约1毫米：叶片质软，基部叶鞘常具向下柔毛。——[偃麦草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Elytrigia repens?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~颖具3-5脉，无膜质边缘，外稃与内稃几等长；叶片质硬，基部叶鞘常无毛。——[硬叶偃麦草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Elytrigia smithii?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 3 穗轴成熟后不断落，其侧棱具细刺毛；颖长在10毫米以下；第一外稃长10-12毫米。——(4)
* ~穗轴于成熟后易断落，其侧棱光滑无毛；颖长14-16毫米；第一外稃长约16毫米。——[脆轴偃麦草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Elytrigia juncea?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 4 颖先端钝圆，或较平而偏斜，叶鞘边缘具纤毛。——(5)
* ~颖先端平截；叶鞘边缘膜质，平滑无毛。——[长穗偃麦草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Elytrigia elongata?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 5 颖先端平截而偏斜，外稃长8-9毫米，颖及稃体平滑无毛。——[中间偃麦草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Elytrigia intermedia?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~颖先端钝圆，外稃长10-11毫米，颖及稃体密被糙毛。——[毛偃麦草](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Elytrigia trichophora?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)
