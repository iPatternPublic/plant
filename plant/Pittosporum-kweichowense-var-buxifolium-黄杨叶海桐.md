## Description

Capsule obovoid.


> **Habait** : 
> Limestone areas, slopes; 1500--1700 m. SE Yunnan (Guangnan Xian, Xichou Xian).

### Synonym
*Pittosporum buxifolium* K. M. Feng ex W. C. Yin, Fl. Yunnan. 3: 318. 1983.
