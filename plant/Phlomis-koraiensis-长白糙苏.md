> [Lamiaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Lamiaceae ?t=foc) - [PDF](http://iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Lamiaceae.pdf) >> [Phlomis](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Phlomis ?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Phlomis.pdf)
## Description

Stems ca. 44 cm tall, &amp;#177; terete, finely retrorse pilose, densely so on nodes. Petiole of basal leaves 8-11.5 cm, others ca. 2.5 cm; basal leaf blades broadly ovate, ca. 14 &amp;#215; 12 cm, adaxially corrugate, sparsely strigose, abaxially densely stellate and finely pilose on veins, base deeply cordate, margin crenate, apex obtuse or acute; stem leaf blades cordate, 5.5-8 &amp;#215; ca. 5 cm, margin crenate. Verticillasters ca. 8-flowered; floral leaves short petiolate to subsessile, ovate to lanceolate, 2.5-4.5 &amp;#215; 0.7-2.7 cm, base shallowly cordate to broadly cuneate, margin irregularly dentate-crenate or shallowly serrate, apex obtuse to acuminate; bracts bristlelike, 0.9-1.1 cm, densely stellate puberulent, ciliate. Calyx campanulate, 1.1-1.2 cm, densely stellate puberulent outside; teeth bearded, broad at base, subtruncate or emarginate, apical spines 2-3 mm. Corolla red-purple, ca. 2.2 cm, densely pubescent on limb, glabrous except on back outside, villous annulate inside; upper lip ca. 9 mm, deeply incised, bearded inside on margin; lower lip ca. 8 &amp;#215; 7 mm, middle lobe obcordate, ca. 5 &amp;#215; 6 mm, apex emarginate; lateral lobes ovate, ca. 2.5 mm. Stamens included; filaments villous, appendage long, reflexed at base above villous annulus of tube. Nutlets glabrous.

> **Habait** : 
> Alpine meadows; 2200 m. Jilin (Changbai Shan) [Korea].

**7．长白糙苏**
Phlomis koraiensis Nakai in Chosen-iho extra ed. 1915 (Rep. Veg. Mt. Waigalbon N. Chosen) 72, Jun. 1915, none. nud., et in Bot. Mag. Tokyo 31: 106. 1917, descr.
多年生草本。茎高约44厘米，近圆柱形，被向下的小疏柔毛，节上较密。基生叶阔心形，长约14厘米，宽约12厘米，先端钝圆形或急尖，基部深心形，边缘具圆齿，茎生叶心形，长5.5-8厘米，宽约5厘米，边缘具圆齿，苞叶卵形至披针形，长2.5-4.5厘米，宽0.7-2.7厘米，先端钝或渐尖，基部浅心形至阔楔形，边缘具不整齐的牙齿状圆齿或浅锯齿，叶片均上面橄榄绿色，疏被糙伏毛，具皱纹，下面网脉上密被星状及单生疏短柔毛，基生叶叶柄长8-11.5厘米，茎生叶叶柄长约2.5厘米，苞叶叶柄短或近无柄。轮伞花序约8花；苞片刺毛状，密被星状微柔毛及混生的缘毛，长9-11毫米，与萼近等长。花萼钟形，长11-12毫米，宽约5.5毫米，外面密被星状及单生微柔毛，齿基部宽，先端近截形或微缺，自内面两侧被髯毛，先端具长2-3毫米的刺尖。花冠红紫色，长约2.2厘米，外面唇瓣上密被短柔毛，冠筒除背部外无毛，内而具毛环，上唇长约9毫米，边缘缺刻极细而深，自内面具髯毛，下唇长约8毫米，宽约7毫米，3圆裂，中裂片倒心形，长约5毫米，宽约6毫米，先端微缺，侧裂片卵形，长约2.5毫米。雄蕊内藏，花丝被柔毛，后对花丝基部在毛环以上具细长向下的附属器。花柱先端几不等的2裂。小坚果无毛。
产吉林（长白山）；生于高山草原中，海拔2200米。朝鲜北部也有。模式标本采自朝鲜北部。
