> [Lamiaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Lamiaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Lamiaceae.pdf) >> [Paraphlomis](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Paraphlomis?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Paraphlomis.pdf)
## Description

Herbs erect, with yellowish indumentum. Stems densely retrorse pilose, leafless toward base, unbranched. Petiole 3-6 mm; leaf blade oblong to ovate-oblong, 5-8 &amp;#215; 2.4-3.4 cm, &amp;#177; leathery, densely puberulent, abaxially golden glandular, base obtuse to rounded, margin undulate-crenate, apex acute. Verticillasters 2-flowered; bracteoles minute, deciduous. Pedicel ca. 1 mm. Fruiting calyx obconical, ca. 6 mm, densely fine pilose, conspicuously 5-veined apically; teeth triangular, ca. 1.8 mm, puberulent inside, conspicuously extended into wings from veins, reflexed. Corolla unknown. Immature nutlets brown, triquetrous, oblong, ca. 2.5 &amp;#215; 1.5 mm, densely fine pilose, base attenuate, apex truncate.

**22．短叶假糙苏**

Paraphlomis brevifolia C. Y. Wu et H. W. Li in Act. Phytotax. Sin. 10 (1): 73. 1965.

直立草本。茎钝四棱形，极密被淡黄色倒向疏柔毛，向基部无叶，不分枝。叶长圆形或卵圆状长圆形，长5-8厘米，宽2.4-3.4厘米，先端急尖，基部钝至近圆形，边缘有波状圆齿，近革质，上面密生下面较密生淡黄色微柔毛，下面还杂有金色腺点，侧脉4-5对，与中肋在上面微下陷，下面突起；叶柄短，长3-6毫米，腹平背凸，密被淡黄色微柔毛。轮伞花序2花，着生于枝条上部叶腋内；小苞片细小，脱落；花梗短，长约1毫米，密被淡黄色微柔毛。果萼倒圆锥形，长约6毫米，外密生淡黄色小疏柔毛，内面在齿上被微柔毛，余部无毛，脉5，上部明显但下部不明显，齿5，三角形，长约1.75毫米，先端明显由脉向外呈翅状伸展，反折。花未见。未成熟小坚果褐色，长圆 状三棱形，长2.5毫米，宽1.5毫米，先端截平，密被小疏柔毛，基部渐狭。

产广西北部。
