## Description

Trees, to 15 m tall. Leaf blade ovate to lanceolate, 4-6.5 × 2.5-3.5 cm, abaxially with persistent trichomes, apex acute.


> **Habait** : 
>● Broad-leaved evergreen forests; ca. 600 m. S Hainan.
