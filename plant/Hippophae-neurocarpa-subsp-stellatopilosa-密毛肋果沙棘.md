## Description

New shoots pilose, glabrescent. Leaf blade abaxially with long hairs, margin flat. Fruit red to yellow, 5.5-6.5 mm.

> **Habait** : 
>● River banks and terraces; 3400-4400 m. Sichuan, Xizang.
