苦参(原变种) **Sophora flavescens var. flavescens**

> [Fabaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Fabaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Fabaceae.pdf) >> [Sophora](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Sophora?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Sophora.pdf)
## Description

Branchlets and leaflets pilose or glabrescent. Corolla white or pale yellow. Legume sparsely pubescent or glabrescent.

### Statistics
Scrub, hill slopes, farm fields; below 1500 m. All provinces [In-dia, Japan, Korea, Russia].

### Synonym
*Sophora angustifolia* Siebold & Zuccarini; *S. flavescens* f. *angustifolia* (Siebold & Zuccarini) Yakovlev; *S. flavescens* var. *angustifolia* (Siebold & Zuccarini) Kitagawa; *S. flavescens *var. *stenophylla *Hayata.
