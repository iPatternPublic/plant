> **Comment** : 
> About seven species: Asia, two species in China.

## Description

Subshrubs, glabrous or stellate, sparsely golden yellow glandular. Leaves entire or pinnatley lobed. Verticillasters 2-4(-6)-flowered, in panicles. Flowers sessile to short pedicellate. Calyx tubular-campanulate, 8-veined, &amp;#177; dilated in fruit, densely hairy or sometimes stellate, 2-lipped, upper lip &amp;#177; entire to obscurely 3-denticulate, lower lip 2-toothed. Corolla purple, rose, or yellowish, rarely white, ca. 2 &amp;#215; as long as calyx, funnelform, without or with imperfect hairy annulus inside; limb 2-lipped, spreading; upper lip 4-lobed, lobes unequal, middle 2 lobes smaller; lower lip elliptic-ovate, margin entire. Stamens 4, anterior 2 fertile, exserted, inserted on corolla throat, posterior 2 sterile, rudimentary; anther cells 2, linear, parallel, connectives small. Style exserted, apex 2-cleft; lobes wide, flat. Disc ringlike or fingerlike in front. Nutlets brown, obovoid, obtuse, glabrous.

## 中国植物志
**分药花属 Perovskia**

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第65(2)卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/65(2)) >> 199页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/66/199y.pdf)
**61.分药花属——Perovskia Karel.**

Karel. in Bull. Soc. Nat. Mosc. 14: 15. 1841.

半灌木，具全缘或有时羽状分裂的对生叶，无毛或被具节单毛或星状毛或仅被星状毛，满布金黄色圆形无柄腺点。花多数，无梗或具短梗，排列成轮伞花序，轮伞花序再组成圆锥花序。花萼管状钟形，果时多少增大，密被具节单毛及有时星状毛，具多数金黄色无柄圆腺点，上唇近全缘或具不明显的3齿，下唇具2齿。花冠紫，玫瑰红，浅黄或稀为白色，长为花萼的2倍，冠筒漏斗状，内面有不完全的毛环，冠檐二唇形，开展，上唇具4裂片，裂片不等大，中央2裂片较侧裂片小，下唇椭圆状卵圆形，全缘。雄蕊4，后对雄蕊能育，微露出或伸出，着生在花冠喉部，前对雄蕊不育，小，露出，着生在花冠上唇裂片基部；花药2室，室线形，平行，直立，药隔小。花柱露出或伸出花冠，先端2裂，裂片宽而扁平，不等大，锐尖。花盘环状或前方呈指状膨大。小坚果倒卵圆形，顶端钝，褐色或棕色，无毛。

约7种，产伊朗北部，巴基斯坦，阿富汗，印度西部，苏联及我国西藏西部。我国西藏可能有2种。
