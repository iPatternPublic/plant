膨果豆属 **Phyllolobium** Fischer in Sprengel Novi Provent. 33. 1818.

> [Fabaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Fabaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Fabaceae.pdf) >> [Phyllolobium](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Phyllolobium?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Phyllolobium.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> Twenty-two species: mostly in China, a small number in the Himalayas and one in Tajikistan; 21 species (17 endemic) in China.

## Description

Perennial herbs with mostly well-developed stems, with basifixed hairs. Stipules free from petiole and from each other, very rarely shortly connate behind stem. Racemes several, with a distinct peduncle; bracts persistent. Bracteoles present or more rarely absent. Calyx campanulate. Standard wide, mostly suborbicular to transversely elliptic, with a very short claw, emarginate at apex; keel and wing petals not interlocking due to absence of bosses on keel and matching sockets on wings. Style with a brush of straight, rigid, short hairs just below glabrous stigma. Legumes 1-locular or incompletely to completely 2-locular.

* ~ Stems mostly shorter, without horizontally spreading lateral branches, if sometimes as long as above, then with other kinds of hair——(2)
* 2 (1)Plants in all parts covered with bladderlike, scalelike hairs up to 0.1 mm.——4  [背扁膨果豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Phyllolobium chinense?t=foc)
* ~ Plants at least at calyx with slender, longer, not bladderlike hairs——(3)
* 3 (2) Petals yellow, rarely with violet-tipped keel——(4)
* ~ Petals blue or violet, rarely yellow at base only——(6)
* 4 (3) Plants 30-75 cm tall; calyx 7-12 mm, teeth 4-9 mm; keel as long as wings.——5  [芒齿膨果豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Phyllolobium dolichochaete?t=foc)
* ~ Plants 10-50 cm tall; calyx up to 10 mm, teeth up to 6 mm; keel longer than wings——(5)
* 5 (4) Leaflets in 7-13 pairs, 5-16 × 1-4 mm, distinctly more than 2 × as long as wide; peduncles 3-11 cm.——9  [黄绿膨果豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Phyllolobium flavovirens?t=foc)

* ~ Stipules up to 2.5 mm; leaflets 2-6 mm; bracts up to 2 mm; bracteoles mostly absent, if present, then ca. 0.3 mm (in P. milingense); calyx teeth up to 2.5 mm; legumes either much higher than wide or much wider than high——(10)
* 10 (9) Stems with appressed hairs; leaflets loosely to rather densely hairy adaxially; bracts 1.5-2 mm; legumes 15-20 mm, 8-12 mm high, strongly compressed laterally, without beak.——10  [毛柱膨果豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Phyllolobium heydei?t=foc)
* ~ Stems with partly spreading hairs; leaflets glabrous to subglabrous adaxially; bracts 0.6-1 mm; legumes 10-15 mm, 2.5-3 mm high and 5-7 mm wide, with a short beak.——13  [米林膨果豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Phyllolobium milingense?t=foc)
* 11 (6) Leaflets on both surfaces loosely to densely hairy——(12)
* ~ Leaflets adaxially glabrous or rarely sparsely hairy——(13)
* 12 (11) Stipules 2-3 mm; bracts 4-6 mm, with spreading hairs; calyx 7-8 mm, covered with spreading white and dark brown hairs.——3  [蔓生膨果豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Phyllolobium chapmanianum?t=foc)
* ~ Stipules 4-7 mm; bracts 6-10 mm, with appressed to ascending hairs; calyx 9-10 mm, covered with appressed to ascending white and shorter black hairs.——11  [拉萨膨果豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Phyllolobium lasaense?t=foc)
* 13 (11) Standard up to 10 mm; bracts up to 2 mm; bracteoles absent——(14)
* ~ Standard at least 12 mm; bracts mostly distinctly longer——(16)
* 14 (13) Plants up to 50 cm tall; calyx 7-8 mm; legumes 25-30 mm.——17  [耐旱膨果豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Phyllolobium siccaneum?t=foc)
* ~ Plants up to 30 cm tall; calyx up to 5.5 mm; legumes up to 13 mm——(15)
* 15 (14) Stem and peduncles loosely to rather densely hairy; leaflets narrowly elliptic, 3-11 × 1-2 mm, acute; keel as long as wings.——15  [奇异膨果豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Phyllolobium prodigiosum?t=foc)

* ~ Stem, rachises, and peduncles loosely to rather densely hairy, sometimes only sparsely hairy; leaflets up to 12 mm—— (17)

* ~ Stems without minute subglobose hairs; at least longer calyx teeth 3.5-6 mm; legumes with a beak 2-4 mm—— (18) 
* 18 (17) Stipules 1-3 mm, lower ones completely embracing stem but free from each other; keel shorter than wings.——6  [*P. donianum* 亚东膨果豆](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Phyllolobium donianum?t=foc)
