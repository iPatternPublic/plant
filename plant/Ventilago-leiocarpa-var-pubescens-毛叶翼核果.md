## Description

Branchlets, fruiting pedicel, and persistent calyx tube pubescent. Leaf blade abaxially densely pubescent at least on veins.

> **Habait** : 
>●  Sparse forests in valleys; 600-1000 m. W Guangxi, S Guizhou, SE Yunnan.
