四川清风藤(原变种) **Sabia schumanniana subsp. schumanniana** (Batalin) H. Hara

> [Sabiaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Sabiaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Sabiaceae.pdf) >> [Sabia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Sabia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Sabia.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> Water (Blumea 26: 25-27. 1980) treated this taxon as a synonym of *Sabia campanulata*.

## Description

Leaf blade light green abaxially. Cyme 1-3-flowered. Sepals, petals, filaments, and disk without red glandular dots.


> **Habait** : 
>●  Broad-leaved forests, mountain slopes, valleys, streamsides; 1100-2600 m. Chongqing, N and W Guizhou, Henan, Shaanxi, S Sichuan.

### Synonym
*Sabia schumanniana* subsp. *longipes* (Rehder & E. H. Wilson) C. Y. Chang; *S. schumanniana* var. *longipes* Rehder & E. H. Wilson.
