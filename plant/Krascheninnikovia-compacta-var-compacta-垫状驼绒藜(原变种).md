垫状驼绒藜**(原变种) **Krascheninnikovia compacta var. compacta**

> [Chenopodiaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Chenopodiaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Chenopodiaceae.pdf)>>[Krascheninnikovia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Krascheninnikovia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Krascheninnikovia.pdf)

## Description

Female floral tube abaxially shortly hairy in fruit.

> **Habait** : 
> Slopes, gravelly flats, high cold desert communities; 3500-5000 m. Gansu (Qilian Shan), Qinghai, Xinjiang, Xizang [Tajikistan].

### Synonym
*Eurotia* *compacta* Losina-Losinskaja, Izv. Akad. Nauk SSSR, Ser. 7, Otd. Fiz.-Mat. Nauk 9: 995. 1930; *Certoides* *compacta* (Losina-Losinskaja) C. P. Tsien & C. G. Ma.
