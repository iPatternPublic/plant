## Description

Petiole long, 3.5-5.5 cm, slender, wingless; leaf blade ovate, 2.5-3.8 × 1.5-2.4 cm, base rounded or cuneate. Fl. Jun-Nov.

### Statistics
● Open forests; 2000-3900 m. Taiwan, Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Ainsliaea reflexa* Merrill var. *subalpina* Handel-Mazzetti, Anz. Akad. Wiss. Wien, Math.-Naturwiss. Kl. 63: 12. 1926; *A.* *henryi* var. *ovatifolia* C. C. Chang; *A. latifolia* var. *nimborum* (Handel-Mazzetti) Kitamura; *A. morrisonicola* Hayata; *A. reflexa* f. *morrisonicola* (Hayata) Suzuki; *A. reflexa* var. *morrisonicola* (Hayata)Yamamoto; *A. reflexa* var. *nimborum* Handel-Mazzetti.
