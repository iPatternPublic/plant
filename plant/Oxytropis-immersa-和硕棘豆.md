## Description

Herbs, 4-11 cm tall, acaulescent, from a multiheaded cau-dex, cushion- or mat-forming, with whitish trichomes. Stipules narrowly triangular, 5-6 × ca. 3 mm, membranous, basally connate. Leaves (0.8-)2-8 cm, 15-21-foliolate; leaflet blades ovate, 1-5 × 1-2 mm, both surfaces with appressed trichomes. Racemes compact, few to ca. 10-flowered; peduncle 4-10 cm, as long as or somewhat longer than leaves, with appressed tri-chomes; bracts triangular, 2-3 × ca. 1 mm, scarious, pubescent. Calyx broadly cylindric, 3-7 × ca. 1 mm, with whitish and blackish trichomes; lobes subulate, 1-3 × ca. 0.5 mm, ca. 1/2 as long as tube. Corolla violet to purple; standard 0.9-1.3 cm, lamina orbicular, apex emarginate; wings ca. 9.5 mm, lamina obovate, apex rounded; keel as long as wings, beak ca. 1 mm. Legume stipitate; stipe 2-3 mm; body cylindric, 10-18 × 4-7 mm, with appressed blackish and whitish trichomes, beak 1-2 mm. Fl. and fr. Aug.

### Statistics
Sunny gravelly hill slopes, alpine meadows; 3600-4200 m. Xin-jiang, Xizang [Afghanistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Pakistan, Tajiki-stan, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan; SW Asia].

### Synonym
*Astragalus immersus* Baker ex Aitchison, J. Linn. Soc., Bot. 18: 45. 1880; *Oxytropis incanescens* Freyn; *O. pamirica* Danguy.

多年生草本。根粗壮，木质。茎缩短，茎基分枝多，为宿存的托叶所覆盖。羽状复叶长1.5-6厘米；托叶纸质，于高处与叶柄贴生，分离部分卵状披针形，长5-7毫米，无毛或边缘被疏柔毛；叶柄与叶轴微具沟，被贴伏柔毛；小叶9 (13) -19 (21)，椭圆形或长圆状卵形，长2-6毫米，宽1-2毫米，先端急尖，基部圆，全缘，两面被贴伏疏柔毛。头形短总状花序；总花梗细，较叶长0.5-1倍，被贴伏白色和黑色疏柔毛；苞片披针形，长2-3毫米，被疏柔毛毛；花长12毫米；花梗长1-1.5毫米；花萼狭钟形，长4.5-6毫米，被贴伏黑色和白色疏柔毛，萼齿线状钻形，长1-3毫米；花冠紫色，旗瓣长9.5-12毫米，瓣片圆心形，宽6毫米，先端凹，翼瓣长9-11毫米，先端凹，龙骨瓣长9-10毫米，喙长0.3-1毫米；子房柄长3毫米，胚珠9-10。荚果卵形，长12-15毫米，果喙披针状钻形，长2毫米，被贴伏黑色和白色柔毛，1室；果梗长3毫米。种子卵形，长约3毫米，暗褐色。花期6-8月，果期7-9月。

产新疆和硕县一带。生于海拔3700-4300米的高山石质山坡和高山草甸上。模式标本采自阿富汗 (Sikaram)。巴基斯坦、伊朗、阿富汗、哈萨克斯坦、乌兹别克斯坦、土库曼斯坦、吉尔吉斯斯坦和塔吉克斯坦也有分布。
