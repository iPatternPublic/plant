> [Athyriaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Athyriaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Athyriaceae.pdf)>>[Diplazium](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Diplazium?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Diplazium.pdf)
## Description

Rhizome ascending. Fertile fronds over 1 m; stipe dark stramineous, 30-40 cm, ca. 6 mm in diam., with slightly aculeate protuberances, slightly scaly to base, all with sparse soft hairs when young, then glabrous; scales brown, narrowly lanceolate, up to 1 cm, margin serrate; lamina lower part 2-pinnate, upper part 1-pinnate, deltoid-ovate, 50-70 × ca. 40 cm at base; pinnae ca. 10 pairs, alternate, ascending, lower pinnae with stalk ca. 1.2 cm; basal pinnae sessile, large, oblong-lanceolate or lanceolate, expanded, 4-6 × 1-1.5 cm, base symmetrical, broadly cuneate or subtruncate, margin subentire or repand, crenate, serrate to apex, apex shortly acuminate; veins abaxially prominent, 4 or 5 pairs per lobe, ascending, basal 1 or 2 pairs anastomosing with tip-deltoid aperture. Lamina herbaceous, both surfaces without hairs; rachis and costa adaxially with dense aculeate protuberances. Sori linear, 3 or 4 pairs per lobe, basal line abaxial, often paired on 1 vein; indusia brown, linear, membranous, persistent.

> **Habait** : 
> Dense on wet sandy riverbanks. Guangdong, Hainan [Sri Lanka].

### Synonym
*Anisogonium smithianum* (Baker) Beddome; *Asplenium smithianum* Baker; *Callipteris paradoxa* (Fée) T. Moore; *C. smithiana* (Baker) Beddome.

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第3(2)卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/3(2)) >> 479页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/3(2)/479.pdf)
**2. 刺轴菜蕨（海南植物志） 图版115: 4-6**
Callipteris paradoxa (Fee) Moore, Ind. Fil. 217. 1861; Ching et C. H. Wang in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 9: 351. 1964; Ching et al. in W. Y. Chun et al., Fl. Hainan. 1: 93. 1964. ——Diplazium paradoxa Fee, Mem. Fam. Foug. 5: 214. 1852; Sledge, Bull. Brit. Mus. (Nat. Hist.) Bot. 2: 310. 1962. ——Asplenium smithianum Bak. in Hook. et Bak., Syn. Fil. 245. 1867. ——Callipteris smithiana (Bak.) Bedd., Ferns Brit. Ind. t. 332. 1870.——Anisogonium smithianum (Bak.) Bedd., Handb. Ferns Brit. Ind. 192. 1883.
根状茎斜升。能育叶长达1米以上；叶柄长30-40厘米，直径约6毫米，深禾秆色，略有小刺状突起，下部略被鳞片，幼时全部略被绒毛，后变光滑；鳞片狭披针形，长达1厘米，褐色，边缘有锯齿；叶片三角状卵形，长50-70厘米，基部宽约40厘米，上部一回羽状，下部二回羽状；侧生羽片约10对，互生，斜展，下部的柄长约1.2厘米；基部一对较大，矩圆披针形，长约40厘米，宽约15厘米，顶部羽裂渐尖，基部平截；小羽片8-10对，近对生，平展，无柄，披针形，长4-6厘米，宽1-1.5厘米，先端短渐尖，基部对称并为阔锲形或近平截，边缘近全缘或有波状圆齿，向顶部有细锯齿；叶脉下面明显，每组4-5对，斜向上，下部1-2对联结成尖三角形的网孔。叶为坚草质，两面均无毛；叶轴及羽轴下面有较密的小刺状突起。孢子囊群线形，每组3-4对，基部上侧一条往往双生一脉上；囊群盖线形，褐色，膜质，缩存。
分布于广东南部、海南（澄迈白石岭）。斯里兰卡也有分布。丛生于潮湿的河边沙质土上。模式标本采自斯里兰卡。
