## Description

Shrubs or small trees to 5 m tall; bark smooth or rough, fibrous; crown globose to columnar, dense or sparse; branchlets not arranged in a plane, ultimate ones 4-angled, ca. 1 mm in diam. Leaves green, not glaucous, without a conspicuous abaxial gland, apex acute. Pollen cones 3-4 ×  1.5-2 mm; microsporophylls each with 3-6 pollen sacs. Seed cones grayish brown, not glaucous, globose, 1-2.5(-3) ×  1-1.5 cm; cone scales 6-10, each fertile scale with numerous seeds. Seeds 3-4(-5) mm.


> **Habait** : 
> Cultivated. Jiangsu [native to W United States]

**4.加利福尼亚柏木（植物分类学报）**

Cupressus goveniana Gord．in Journ．Hort．Soc. London 4: 295．f.1849; Parl．in DC．Prodr．16 (2): 472．1868; Kent, Veitch's Man．Conif．ed．2．204．1900; Dallimore and Jackson, Handb. Conif．ed．3. 271．t．45．1948, rev. Harrson, Handb. Conif. and Ginkgo. ed. 4. 205. t. 40. 1966; Bailey, Man．Cult．Pl．rev. 121. 1949;郑万钧等, 植物分类学报13 (4): 73. 1975.

乔木，在原产地高达20米；树皮褐色或灰褐色，粗糙；生鳞叶的小枝不排成平面，末端鳞叶枝四棱形，径约1毫米，有芳香气味。鳞叶淡绿色或微带黄绿色，先端尖，无白粉，背部无明显的腺点。球果圆球形，较小，径1-1.5厘米，深褐色，有光泽，无白粉；种鳞3-5对，顶部有一尖头，发育种鳞具多数种子。

原产美国加利福尼亚州，广为栽培作庭园树。我国南京等地引种栽培，长势很旺。
