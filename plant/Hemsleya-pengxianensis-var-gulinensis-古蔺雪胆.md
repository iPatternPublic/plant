## Description

Corolla Chinese lanternlike, 10-12 mm in diam. Fruit elliptic.

> Reference: 
>*Hemsleya pengxianensis* var. *gulinensis* is not a new combination based on *H. gulinensis* (L. D. Shen & W. J. Chang, Acta Acad. Med. Sichuan. 11(2): 103. 1980), although both names have the same type.*Hemsleya pengxianensis* var. *gulinensis* is not a new combination based on *H. gulinensis* (L. D. Shen & W. J. Chang, Acta Acad. Med. Sichuan. 11(2): 103. 1980), although both names have the same type.

### Statistics
● Broad-leaved forests. Sichuan.

### Synonym
*Hemsleya gulinensis* L. D. Shen & W. J. Chang.

**16c. 古蔺雪胆（植物分类学报）（变种）**

var. gulinensis L. T. Shen et W. J. Chang, 植物分类学报21 (2): 188, 图3: 6-8. 1983.

花冠较大，径1-1.2厘米；果实倒圆锥形或倒卵形，长2.5-3厘米，直径1.5-2厘米而与原变种相区别。

产四川东南部。生于海拔1500-2000米的林缘及山谷灌丛中。原模式标本采自四川古蔺。
