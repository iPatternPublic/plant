## Description

Branchlets and petioles pubescent. Leaves all opposite. Fruiting perianth rhomboid or ± rounded, ca. 1.2 mm, base stipitate. Fl. Mar-Aug, fr. Jul-Oct.

> **Habait** : 
>* Forests; 300-1000 m. Taiwan.
