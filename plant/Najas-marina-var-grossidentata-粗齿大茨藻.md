## Description
 "
Stems stout, with only 1 or 2 spines under nodes. Margins of leaves with 2 or 4 teeth, abaxial side of midvein with sparse spines. Style and stigma 2-3 mm; anthers 3.8-4 mm. Seeds 4-4.5 × 2.2-2.4 mm.

### Statistics
Ponds, lakes. Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning [Korea].
