## Description

Leaflet blades papery, both surfaces silvery pubescent but glabrescent.

### Statistics
Sparse thickets; 200-1300 m. W Guangxi, S Yunnan [Laos, Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam].

### Synonym
*Adinobotrys filipes* Dunn, Bull. Misc. Inform. Kew 1911: 195. 1911; *Padbruggea filipes* (Dunn) Craib; *Whitfordioden-dron filipes* (Dunn) Dunn.
