## Description

Herbs perennial, 25-50 cm tall. Stem slender, hairy when young. Leaves paripinnate; stipules linear-lanceolate; leaflets 2-5-paired, linear, 25-30 × 2-3 mm, apex mucronate; lateral veins not obvious; tendril unbranched. Raceme longer than leaf, 2- or 3(or 4)-flowered; rachis 7-8 cm. Calyx campanulate. Corolla blue-purple or purple, 16-26 mm; standard obovate-oblong, longer than wings and keel, apex retuse. Ovary long stalked; ovules many. Legume not seen. Fl. Jun-Jul. 2*n* = 14.

### Statistics
Grasslands on riverbanks. Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mon-gol [Mongolia, Russia (E Siberia)].

**33. 索伦野豌豆（东北草本植物志）**

Vicia geminiflora Trautv. in Act. Hort. Petrop 3: 42. 1875; B. Fedtsch. in Kom. Fl. URSS 13: 443. tab. 25, fig. 5. 1948; 东北草本植物志5: 141. 1976.

多年生草本，高25-50厘米。根茎细，茎细柔，斜升，植株嫩时被毛，后渐脱落。偶数羽状复叶顶端卷须单一；托叶线披针形，小叶2-5对，线形，长2.5-3厘米，宽0.2-0.3厘米，先端平截或圆，具短尖头，基部披针形，叶脉不明显。总状花序长于叶，具花2-3 (-4) 朵，聚集着生于长7-8厘米的花序轴顶端；萼钟状，萼齿三角形至线披针形；花冠蓝紫色或紫色，长1.6-2.6厘米，旗瓣倒卵长圆形，先端微凹，翼瓣短于旗瓣，龙骨瓣短于旗瓣；子房柄较长，花柱上部被黄白色绢毛，胚珠多数。花期6-7。

产东北、内蒙古。生于河岸草丛。俄罗斯西伯利亚地区亦有。
