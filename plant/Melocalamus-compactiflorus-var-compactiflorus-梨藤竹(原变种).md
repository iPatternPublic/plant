梨籐竹**(原变种) **Melocalamus compactiflorus var. compactiflorus**

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf)>>[Melocalamus](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Melocalamus?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Melocalamus.pdf)

## Description

Plant taller, with thinner culms. Culm sheath ligule much shorter, not fimbriate; leaves smaller.


> **Habait** : 
> River valleys in tropical rain forests; 400–1000 m. S Yunnan [Bangladesh, NE India, Myanmar].

### Synonym
*Pseudostachyum compactiflorum* Kurz, J. Asiat. Soc. Bengal, Pt. 2, Nat. Hist. 42: 252. 1873; *Dinochloa compacti-flora* (Kurz) McClure.
