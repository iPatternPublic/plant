## Description

Herbs robust, ascending. Stems ca. 70 cm tall, densely gray tomentose. Leaf blade elliptic to obovate-elliptic, 14-24 &amp;#215; 5-11 cm, adaxially with short stellate, dusty hairs, abaxially densely gray stellate tomentose, base obliquely cuneate, decurrent, margin widely spaced serrulate, apex obtuse to acute. Cymes axillary, many flowered, densely stellate tomentose, ca. 3 &amp;#215; 2.5 cm, subsessile; bracts and bracteoles ovate-lanceolate to linear-lanceolate, ca. 1.2 &amp;#215; 3 mm, 1- or 3-veined. Calyx narrowly campanulate, 8-10 mm, densely stellate tomentose, veins comparatively conspicuous; teeth linear to triangular lanceolate, ca. as long as calyx tube. Corolla yellow, rarely white to purplish, exserted part 1.1-1.3(-1.5) cm, pubescent, somewhat dilated, ca. 2 mm wide at base, ca. 3.5 mm wide at throat; upper lip ovate, ca. 5 &amp;#215; 5 mm; lower lip ca. 4 &amp;#215; 6 mm. Nutlets brown, glabrous, smooth or striate. Fl. Jun.


> **Habait** : 
> Densely forested valleys, shady areas in dense forests; 600-1500 m. Yunnan [India, Malaysia, Myanmar, Thailand]

**3．小花锥花**

Gomphostemma parviflorum Wall. Cat. n. 2158. 1830. nom. nud.; ex Benth. in Wall. Pl. Asiat. Rar.: 12. 1831. p. p.: Prain in Ann. Bot. Gard. Calcutta 3: 252，pl. 86. 1891 (var. typicum); Backer & Bakh. f. Fl. Java 2: 619. 1965; H. Keng in G ard. Bull. Singapore 24: 83. 1969.

**3a. 小花锥花（原变种）图版21：13-14**

Gomphostemma parviflorum Wall. var. parviflorum

草本粗壮，上升。茎高约70厘米，钝四棱形，槽不明显，密被厚的灰色绒毛。叶椭圆形至倒卵状椭圆形，长14-24厘米，宽5-11厘米，先端钝至急尖，基部偏斜的楔形，下延至叶柄，边缘具小疏齿，草质，上面干后橄榄绿色，中肋及侧脉上有较密的星状毛，其余部分为尘状短星状毛及单毛，下面密被星状绒毛，呈灰色；叶柄长约2厘米，密被星状长绒毛。聚伞花序腋生，多花，密集，被星状绒毛，长3厘米，宽2.5厘米，几无梗；苞片及小苞片线状披针形，长约1.2厘米，宽约3毫米，脉仅1条。花萼狭钟形，长8-10毫米，密被灰色星状绒毛，肋有5条比较明显，萼齿线形，基部略宽，与萼筒等长或略超出。花冠黄色，稀白色至浅紫色，超出萼部分长11-13（15）毫米，外面被短柔毛，冠筒基部宽约2毫米，喉部宽3.5毫米，冠檐二唇形，上唇卵圆形，长5毫米，宽3毫米，下唇长4毫米，宽6毫米，3裂，中裂片较大。花柱扁平，无毛，与雄蕊及花冠上唇等长，先端不相等2浅裂。小坚果褐色，无毛，平滑。花期6月。

产云南西南部；生于密林下荫处，海拔840米。印度，马来西亚也有分布。模式标本采白印度。

**3b. 被粉变种 图版21:1-12**

Gomphostemma parviflorum Wall. var. farinosum Prain in Ann. Bot. Gard. Calcutta 3: 252. 1891——Gomphostemma parviflorum Wall. Pl. Asiat. Rar. 2: 12. 1831, p. p. ——Gomphostemma parviflorum Wall. auct. non Wall. ex Benth.: Dunn in Notes Bot. Gard. Edinburgh 6: 189. 1915; Kudo in Mein. Fac. Sci. Agr. Taihoku Univ. 2: 273. 1929 Leonurus farinosus Buch. -Ham. ex Mukerj. in Rec. Bot. Surv. India 14: 208. 1940, in syn.

这一变种与原变种不同在于苞片及小苞片卵状披针形至披针形，具三出脉；花萼狭钟形，萼齿线状披针形或三角状披针形，略短于萼筒；小坚果有细纹。

产云南南部；生于沟谷杂木林下或林缘等近水边荫湿处，海拔600-1500米。印度，缅甸，泰国有分布。模式标本采自印度。
