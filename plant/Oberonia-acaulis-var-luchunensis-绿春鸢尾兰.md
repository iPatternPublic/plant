## Description

Lateral lobes of lip with nearly entire margins; apical lobules of mid-lobe nearly ovate-lanceolate, apex acuminate. Fl. Oct.

### Statistics
&amp;#9679; Epiphytic on trees along forest margins; ca. 2400 m. S Yunnan.
