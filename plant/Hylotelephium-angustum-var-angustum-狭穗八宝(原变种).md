## Description

Axillary inflorescences short cymes, forming 2 or more dense whorls, contiguous and concealing peduncles.

> **Habait** : 
>* Woodlands, sides of ravines near thickets, rocky slopes; 1800-3500 m. Gansu, Hubei, Ningxia, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan.
