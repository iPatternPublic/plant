## Description

Sepals and petals yellowish green.


> **Habait** : 
> Grassy slopes, wet places, by streams; 200--2400 m. Gansu, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Hubei, Jilin, Liaoning, Nei Mongol, Ningxia, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi [Japan, Mongolia, Russia (Siberia)].
