## Description

Shrubs, 2-3 m tall; branches densely grayish yellow hispidulous or strigillose becoming glabrescent with age. Petiole 6-23 mm, densely strigillose; leaf blade drying papery and dark yellowish brown or blackish brown, oblong-elliptic or oblong-lanceolate, 5-15 × 1.5-6 cm, sparsely hispidulous to strigillose adaxially, sparsely to densely hispidulous or strigillose abaxially, base cuneate or acute, apex acuminate; secondary veins 6-8 pairs; stipules generally persistent, shortly united around stem, broadly triangular, 1.5-3, densely strigillose, acute. Inflorescences cymose to corymbose and somewhat lax, 6-10 cm, densely grayish yellow strigillose, pedunculate; peduncle ca. 1 cm; bracts triangular, 1-3 mm; pedicels 2-13 mm. Flowers pedicellate. Calyx densely strigillose; hypanthium portion cylindrical, ellipsoid, or urceolate, 2-2.5 mm; limb 1-2 mm, lobed for ca. 1/2; lobes ovate-triangular to triangular, apex acute. Corolla white, outside densely strigillose; tube 5-7 mm, villous in throat; lobes narrowly lanceolate to spatulate, 11-12 mm, acute to obtuse. Ovules ca. 15 per cell. Berry subglobose, 5-6 mm in diam., strigillose. Fl. May-Jul, fr. Jul-Feb.

### Statistics
● Forests on mountains; ca. 700 m. Hainan (Sanya).

**7. 崖州乌口树（中国高等植物图鉴）毛冠乌口树（海南植物志）图版99: 11-14**

Tarenna laui Merr. in Lingnan Sci. Journ. 14: 59. 1935; 海南植物志3: 319. 1974; 中国高等植物图鉴4: 232, 图5877. 1975.

灌木，高2-3米；小枝密被灰黄色紧贴的短硬毛，成长时毛渐脱落。叶纸质，长圆状椭圆形或长圆状披针形，长5-15厘米，宽1.5-6厘米，顶端渐尖，基部楔形或短尖，干时暗黄褐色或黑褐色，上面被极稀疏的紧贴的短硬毛，下面被毛较上面稍密，沿脉上更密；侧脉6-8对，与中脉在叶上面均平坦，在下面凸起；叶柄长6-23毫米，密被紧贴的短硬毛；托叶宽三角形，长约3毫米，外面被紧贴的短硬毛。伞房状的聚伞花序顶生，稍疏散，长6-10厘米，密被灰黄色紧贴的短硬毛；花芳香，花梗长2-8毫米，与花萼和花冠外面均密被灰黄色紧贴的短硬毛；萼管圆筒状壶形，长3-4毫米，裂片5，小，卵状三角形，顶端短尖，长约0.5毫米；花冠白色，冠管长5-7毫米，喉部被长柔毛，裂片5，披针形，长约12毫米，宽2-2.5毫米，开放时外反；雄蕊5枚，花丝长1-3毫米，花药线形，长约7毫米；子房2室，每室约有胚珠15颗，花柱纤细，长1.5-2厘米，下部有短柔毛，柱头伸出。果近球形，直径5-6毫米，有紧贴的短硬毛，果柄纤细，长7-13毫米；种子多数。花期5-7月，果期7月至翌年2月。

产于海南崖县；生于山地林中。模式标本采自海南崖县羊令山。
