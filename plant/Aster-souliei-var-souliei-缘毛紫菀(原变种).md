## Description

Phyllaries abaxially glabrous, margin densely pilose-ciliate or eciliate.

### Statistics
W Sichuan, Xizang, NW Yunnan [Bhutan, N Myanmar].

### Synonym
*Aster ganlun* Kitamura.
