稻田荸荠 **Eleocharis pellucida var. japonica** (Miquel) Tang & F. T. Wang Fl. Reipubl. Popularis Sin. 11: 58. 1961.

> [Cyperaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Cyperaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Cyperaceae.pdf)>>[Eleocharis](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Eleocharis?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Eleocharis.pdf)

## Description

Culms usually very short, hairlike. Perianth bristles shorter than nutlet, laxly retrorsely spinulose. Nutlet 0.8-0.9 mm; persistent style base usually ± elongate. Fl. and fr. Mar-Oct.

### Statistics
Paddy fields, shallow water margins; 200-1700 m. Anhui, Fujian, Guizhou, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Yunnan, Zhejiang [Japan, Korea, N Thailand].

### Synonym
*Eleocharis japonica* Miquel, Ann. Mus. Bot. Lugduno-Batavi 2: 142. 1865; *Scirpus japonicus* (Miquel) Franchet & Savatier, not (Maximowicz) Fernald.
