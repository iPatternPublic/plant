## Description

Leaflet blades narrowly elliptic, 2-3.5(-6) cm, adaxially pubescent but glabrescent, midvein adaxially sunken, secondary veins 8 or 9 on each side of midvein and curved toward apex, both ends acute but sometimes apex retuse, margins slightly revolute. Calyx teeth cuspidate.

### Statistics
● Grasslands, sparse woodlands; 800-1500 m. Guangxi, Yunnan.

### Synonym
*Millettia chenkangensis* Hu.

本变种与原变种不同为：小叶通常8-9对，椭圆形，两端狭尖或微凹陷，侧脉6-7对，明显向尖端弧曲，叶缘稍下卷，柔毛易脱净；萼齿截平而急尖。

产广西、云南。
