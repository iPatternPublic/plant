## Description

Bark gray, with sparse spots. Leaf blade base cuneate or obtuse.

> **Habait** : 
>●  Dense forests. SW Guangxi.

### Synonym
Basionym: *Sterculia nobilis* Smith var. *subspontanea* H. H. Hsue & S. J. Xu, J. S. China Agric. Univ. 8(3): 3. 1987.
