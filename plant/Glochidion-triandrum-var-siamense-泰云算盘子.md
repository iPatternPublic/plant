## Description

Leaf blade thickly papery, densely pubescent abaxially. Capsules larger, ca. 10 mm in diam.; fruiting pedicels robust, very short. Fl. May-Aug, fr. Aug-Oct.

> **Habait** : 
> Evergreen broad-leaved forests; 1700-1800 m. Yunnan (Gengma, Simao) [N Thailand].

### Synonym
*Glochidion acuminatum* Müller Argoviensis var. *siamense* Airy Shaw, Kew Bull. 26(2): 273. 1972.

**9b. 泰云算盘子（植物分类学报）思茅渐尖算盘子（云南种子植物名录）**

Glochidion triandrum (Blanco) C. B. Rob. var. siamense (Airy Shaw) P. T. Li in Acta Phytotax. Sin. 26: 62. 1988. ——Glochidion auminatum Muell.-Arg. var. siamense Airy Shaw in Kew Bull. 26 (2): 273. 1972.

本变种与原变种的不同，在于本变种的叶片厚纸质，密被短柔毛，蒴果较大，直径1厘米以上，果梗粗壮等不同。

产于云南（思茅、耿马等地），生于海拔达1750米山地常绿阔叶林中。泰国也有。模式标本采自泰国北部。
