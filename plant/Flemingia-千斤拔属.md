> [Fabaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Fabaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Fabaceae.pdf) >> [Flemingia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Flemingia.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> About 30 species: tropical Asia, Africa, Oceania; 15 species (two endemic) in China.

## Description

Shrubs or subshrubs, rarely herbs, erect or trailing. Leaves digitately 3-foliolate or simple; stipules persistent or caducous; stipels absent; leaflets usually with sessile glands abaxially. Inflorescence axillary or terminal, racemose or compound racemose, rarely paniculate or capitate. Bracts 2-columned; bracteoles absent. Calyx 5-lobed; lobes narrow and long, lower one longest; tube short. Corolla longer than calyx or included; standard oblong or elliptic, base clawed, with auricles; wings very narrow, auriculate. Stamens diadelphous; vexillary stamen free; anthers uniform. Ovary subsessile; ovules 2; style filiform, glabrous or slightly hairy; stigma small, capitate. Legume elliptic, dehiscent, inflated, not septate. Seeds 1 or 2, almost orbicular, without strophiole.

> The generic synonym *Maughania* is very often written incorrectly as *\"Moghania.\"*The generic synonym *Maughania* is very often written incorrectly as *\"Moghania.\"*

## Keylist

* 1 Leaves simple——(2)
* ~ Leaves digitately 3-foliolate——(5)

* 2 (1)Inflorescence a raceme or panicle; bracts small, ovate to ovate-lanceolate.——4  [锥序千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia paniculata?t=foc)
* ~ Inflorescence a thyrse of cymelets, each initially enclosed by large overlapping incurved bracts——(3)

* 3 (2) Leaflets orbicular-cordate; standard with lobe as long as broad, contracted above auricles, and obovate or obcordate.——1  [墨江千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia chappar?t=foc)
* ~ Leaflets ovate, narrowly ovate, elliptic, or oblong; standard with lobes not contracted above auricles, transversely elliptic or broadly orbicular; wings much narrower than keel——(4)

* 4 (3) Leaves 3-7 cm wide, base rounded or slightly cordate, petiole usually 5-15 mm.——3  [球穗千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia strobilifera?t=foc)
* ~ Leaves 1.5-2.5 cm wide, base cuneate, subsessile or petiole usually 1-5 mm.——2  [河边千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia fluminalis?t=foc)

* 5 (1) Prostrate herbs; stipules persistent; inflorescence compact——(6)
* ~ Erect shrubs; stipules usually deciduous (persistent in F. stricta)——(7)

* ~ Leaflets 1-5 cm, oblong or almost obovate; stipules ovate; inflorescence 2-10 cm.——14  [矮千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia procumbens?t=foc)

* 7 (5) Inflorescence a long-pedunculate capitulum; bracts 1.5-2 cm, persistent.——15  [总苞千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia involucrata?t=foc)
* ~ Inflorescence peduncle extremely short, racemes or panicles; bracts 0.4-1.2 cm, persistent or deciduous——(8)

* 8 (7) Peduncle conspicuous, axis slender.——5  [细叶千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia lineata?t=foc)
* ~ Peduncle inconspicuous, axis robust——(9)

* 9 (8) Leaflets usually more than 20 cm; stipules persistent; standard strongly contracted, narrowed above auricles.——6  [长叶千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia stricta?t=foc)
* ~ Leaflets less than 20 cm; stipules deciduous; standard not contracted——(10)

* 10 (9) Leaflets usually 8-19 cm, lateral veins not grooved adaxially——(11)
* ~ Leaflets usually 2-10 cm, lateral veins ± grooved adaxially——(13)

* 11 (10) Whole plant densely rusty tomentose; bracts elliptic to elliptic-lanceolate, apex obtuse.——7  [宽叶千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia latifolia?t=foc)
* ~ Whole plant densely gray or grayish brown pubescent or silky villous; bracts ovate to triangular——(12)

* 12 (11) Petiole with narrow wings; leaflets usually glabrous except for veins; inflorescence lax.——8  [大叶千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia macrophylla?t=foc)
* ~ Petiole without wings; leaflets densely pubescent, especially in lower part; inflorescence congested.——9  [勐板千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia mengpengensis?t=foc)

* 13 (10) Branchlets without obvious lenticels; leaflets abaxially glabrous or almost glabrous except for veins.——12  [贵州千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia kweichowensis?t=foc)
* ~ Branchlets with obvious lenticels; leaflets abaxially densely tomentose——(14)

* 14 (13) Branchlets and abaxial surface of leaflets gray to grayish brown tomentose.——10  [*F. wallichii* 云南千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia wallichii?t=foc)
* ~ Branchlets and both sides of leaflets brown tomentose.——11  [*F. grahamiana* 绒毛千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia grahamiana?t=foc)

**千斤拔属 Flemingia**

* [《中国植物志》](http://www.iplant.cn/frps)- [第41卷](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/vol/41) >> 313页 [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/frps/pdf/41/313y)

**76.千斤拔属 Flemingia Roxb. ex W. T. Ait.**

Roxb. ex W. T. Ait. Hort. Kew ed. 2, 4: 349. 1812. (nom. cons.); Rudd in Taxon 19: 294. 1970; Verdc. in Gillett et al. Fl. Trop. E. Afr. Legum. Pap. (2): 805. 1971; Thuan in Aubrev. Fl. Camb. Laos Vietn. 17: 138. 1979. ——Moghania J. St. Hil. in Desv. Journ. Bot. 1: 61. 1813; H. L. Li in Amer. Journ. Bot. 31: 224. 1944;广州植物志360. 1956; Hutch. Gen. Fl. Pl. 422. 1964: 海南植物志2: 310. 1965.

灌木或亚灌木，稀为草本。茎直立或蔓生。叶为指状3小叶或单叶，下面常有腺点；托叶宿存或早落；小托叶缺。花序腋生或顶生，为总状或复总状花序，或为小聚伞花序包藏于贝状苞片内，复再排成总状或复总状花序式；稀为圆锥花序或头状花序；苞片2列，小苞片缺；花萼5裂，裂片狭长，下面1枚最长，萼管短；花冠伸出萼外或内藏；雄蕊二体（9+1），对旗瓣的1枚离生，其余合生，花药一式；子房近无柄，有胚珠2颗，花柱丝状，无毛或基部略被毛，柱头小，头状。荚果椭圆形，膨胀，果瓣内无隔膜，有种子1-2颗；种子近圆形，无种阜。

约40种，分布于热带亚洲、非洲和大洋洲。我国产16种及1变种，分布于西南、中南和东南各省区。

属模式种：Flemingia strobilifera (Linn.) Ait. (Hedysarum strobiliferum Linn.) (Typ. Cons)

* 13 叶柄长3-6厘米，具狭翅；小叶两面除脉上外，通常无毛：总状花序较长，不密集成团；花萼长4-8毫米，被丝质柔毛。——[大叶千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia macrophylla?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~叶柄长6-13厘米，无翅；小叶两面密被柔毛，尤以下面为甚；总状花序较短，长约3厘米，3-6个簇生于叶腋，花与花序密集呈团；花萼长1.5-1.7厘米， 密被粗长伏毛。——[勐板千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia mengpengensis?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 14 小枝具明显皮孔；小叶下面密被绒毛。——(15)
* ~小枝无明显皮孔；小叶下面除脉上被毛外，无毛或近无毛。——[贵州千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia kweichowensis?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 15 幼枝、小叶两面密被棕色绒毛。——[绒毛千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia grahamiana?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~幼枝、叶下面密被灰色至灰褐色绒毛。——[云南千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia wallichii?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* 16 小叶长，椭圆形或卵状披针形，长4-7厘米，宽1.7-3厘米；托叶线状披针形。——[千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia philippinensis?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)

* ~小叶短，长圆形或近倒卵形，长1-5厘米，宽0.5-2.5厘米；托叶卵形。——[矮千斤拔](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Flemingia procumbens?t=z'  style='color:#0000ff)
