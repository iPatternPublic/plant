落芒草(原变种) **Piptatherum munroi var. munroi**

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf) >> [Piptatherum](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Piptatherum?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Piptatherum.pdf)
## Description

Glumes 5–7 mm; lemma 4–5 mm; awn 3–7 mm; anthers 2–2.5 mm. Fl. and fr. Jun–Aug.


> **Habait** : 
> Alpine scrub, mountain meadows, roadsides, farmlands; 2200–5000 m. Gansu, Guizhou, Qinghai, Sichuan, Xinjiang, Xizang, Yunnan [E Afghanistan, Bhutan, N India, Kashmir, Nepal, N Pakistan].

### Synonym
*Oryzopsis munroi* Stapf in J. D. Hooker, Fl. Brit. India 7: 234. 1896 [\"1897\"]; *O. geminiramula* Ohwi.
