## Description

Leaves similar to subsp. *denticulatum*. Involucre 8-9 mm. Inner phyllaries usually distinctly crested below apex. Achene 3-4 mm including a 0.3-0.6 mm beak. Pappus 4.5-5.5 mm. Fl. and fr. (?Sep-)Jan-Mar.

### Statistics
● Field margins; 400-1000 m. Fujian, Guangdong, Jiangxi.

### Synonym
Basionym: *Ixeris denticulata* subsp. *longiflora* Stebbins, J. Bot. 75: 48. 1937.
