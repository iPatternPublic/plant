## Description

Leaves 0.8-2 cm wide. Involucral bracts 1-1.6(-1.9) cm wide. Spikelets (1 or)2-5 digitately arranged at raylets or tertiary raylets, 7-14 mm, 10-22-flowered. Glumes rusty brown on both surfaces, middle abaxially grayish green. Fl. and fr. Aug-Sep.


> Reference: 
>*Cyperus diffusus* var. *latifolius* appears to be well differentiated from other variants of this widespread paleotropical species.*Cyperus diffusus* var. *latifolius* appears to be well differentiated from other variants of this widespread paleotropical species.

**宽叶多脈莎草（变种）图版L，7-8**

Cyperus diffusus var. latifolius L. K. Dai, var. nov.

叶片一般较宽，最宽达2厘采。苞片最宽达16毫米；小穗数目较多，通常2-5个（少数1个）成指状排列于辐射枝顶端，长7-14毫米，宽约2毫米；通常具10-22朵花（少数花较少）；鳞片背面较宽部分为暗灰绿色，两侧锈色。花果期8-9月。

产于广东；生长于潮湿处或高草丛中。
