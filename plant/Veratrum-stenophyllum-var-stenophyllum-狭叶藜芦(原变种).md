## Description

Leaf blade glabrous. Tepals 5--7 × 3--4 mm.

> **Habait** : 
>* Grassy slopes, shaded places in forests; 2000--4000 m. W Sichuan, NW Yunnan.

### Synonym
* Veratrum yunnanense* Loesener.
