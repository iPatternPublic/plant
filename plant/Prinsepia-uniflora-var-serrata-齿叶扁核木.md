## Description

Leaf blade ovate-lanceolate to ovate-oblong on sterile shoots, oblong to narrowly elliptic on flowering shoots, margin conspicuously serrate. Pedicel 5–15 mm.


> **Habait** : 
> Slopes, ravines in valleys; 800--2200 m. Gansu, Ningxia, E Qinghai, S Shaanxi, S Shanxi, W Sichuan.
