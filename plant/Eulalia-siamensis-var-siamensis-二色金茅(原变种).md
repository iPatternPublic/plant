二色金茅(原变种) **Eulalia siamensis var. siamensis**

> [Poaceae](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Poaceae?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Poaceae.pdf)>>[Eulalia](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Eulalia?t=foc) - [PDF](http://www.iplant.cn/foc/pdf/Eulalia.pdf)

> **Comment** : 
> This variety is reported from SW Yunnan, but its presence in China has not been confirmed.

## Description

Basal sheaths woolly with cream or golden hairs; leaf blades subglabrous to villous. Upper lemma 2-toothed in upper 1/5 or less.

> **Habait** : 
> Open woodlands. ?SW Yunnan [Myanmar, Thailand].
