## Description

Herbs perennial, low, rhizomatous, softly tomentose. Leaves mostly radical; leaf blade interrupted pinnatisect; lobes obovate, margin crenate; cauline leaves sessile among connate stipules and entire or 3-fid. Inflorescence erect, few-flowered, bracteate. Hypanthium obconic, eventually elongated, 10-ribbed. Sepals 5, valvate, persistent; epicalyx segments minute. Petals 5, yellow or white, larger than sepals. Stamens numerous, in 2 or 3 series; filaments free, persistent in fruit. Disk lining hypanthium, glabrous. Carpels numerous, inserted on short receptacle; ovule ascending from base of locule; style subterminal, erect, deciduous. Achenes numerous on columnar receptacle, included in hypanthium, compressed, rugose.

### Statistics
Five species: China, Mongolia, Russia; four species (all endemic) in China.

**33.无尾果属——Coluria R. Br.**

R. Br. in Parry, Voy. Suppl. App. 276. 1824; Benth. et Hook. f. Gen. Pl. 1: 619. 1865: Focke in Engler ＆ Prantl, Nat. Pflanzenfam. 3 (3): 36. 1888; W. E. Evans in Not. Roy. Bot. Gard. Edinb. 15: 47-54. Pl. 213-214. 1925; Hutch. Gen. Flow. Pl. 1: 202. 1964.

多年生草本，有柔毛，具根茎。基生叶为羽状复叶或大头羽状复叶，边缘有锯齿；托叶合生。花茎直立，有少数花，具苞片；副萼片5，常小形；花萼宿存，萼筒倒圆锥形，花后延长，有10肋，萼片5，镊合状排列；花瓣5，黄色或白色，比萼片长；雄蕊多数，成2-3组，花丝离生，在果期宿存；花盘环绕萼筒，无毛；心皮多数，生在短花托上，花柱近顶生，直立，脱落；胚珠1，着生在子房基部。瘦果多数，扁平，包在宿存萼筒内，有1种子。

本属的模式种：Coluria geoides（Pall.）Ldb.

约4种，分布在亚洲北部及南部，我国产3种。

## 检索表

* 1 基生叶为羽状复叶，但不为大头羽状。——(2)
* ~基生叶为大头羽状复叶，顶生小叶最大，宽卵形或卵形，基部心形；花直径2-2.5厘米；花瓣黄色或白色；心皮多数；心皮及瘦果有显明乳头状疣。——[大头叶无尾果](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Coluria henryi?t=z)

* 2 基生叶为不间断羽状复叶，小叶9-20对，上部者较大，下部者渐小；花直径1. 5-2.5厘米；花瓣黄色；心皮数个；心皮及瘦果平滑，无毛。——[无尾果](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Coluria longifolia?t=z)

* ~基生叶为参差间断羽状复叶，小叶4-5对，大小不等，倒卵状扇形至卵形，基部宽楔形；花直径约1厘米；花瓣白色；心皮4；心皮及瘦果有显明乳头状疣。——[汶川无尾果](http://www.iplant.cn/info/Coluria oligocarpa?t=z)
